Door; gate; entrance; opening; way in or out; family or school of thought.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                 |
| -------------- | -------------------- |
| Pinyin         | mén                  |
| Core meaning   | door; gate; entrance |
| Part of speech | noun, measure word   |
| Tone           | second tone (rising) |

## Visual Breakdown

门 is a **pictographic representation of a traditional Chinese door or gate**.

| Component | Visual Description                                      |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **门**    | Two panels of a traditional double door or gate opening |

## Mnemonic

Think of 门 as **two door panels that can open in the middle**:

- The character looks like a traditional Chinese gate or door
- Two vertical elements represent the door panels or posts
- The horizontal connection shows the frame or lintel
- Like looking at a traditional courtyard gate from the front
- Shows the opening that allows passage in and out

This creates the image: **a traditional doorway providing access and security**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

门 represents **entrances, barriers, and access points**. It's used:

1. **Physical doors**: 开门 (kāi mén) - "open the door"
2. **Entrances**: 大门 (dà mén) - "main gate; front door"
3. **Access**: 出门 (chū mén) - "go out; leave home"
4. **Categories**: 专门 (zhuān mén) - "specialized; specifically"

## Grammar Functions

- **Noun**: 这扇门 (zhè shàn mén) - "this door"
- **Measure word**: 一门课 (yī mén kè) - "one course/subject"
- **Modifier**: 门外 (mén wài) - "outside the door"

## Cultural Context

门 in Chinese culture represents:

- **家族观念** (jiāzú guānniàn) - Family and lineage concepts
- **社会等级** (shèhuì děngjí) - Social hierarchy and status
- **隐私保护** (yǐnsī bǎohù) - Privacy and boundaries
- **通道象征** (tōngdào xiàngzhēng) - Symbolic passages in life

## Philosophy and Symbolism

门 in Chinese thought:

- **入世出世** (rùshì chūshì) - Entering and leaving the world
- **修行门径** (xiūxíng mén jìng) - Paths of spiritual cultivation
- **学问门第** (xuéwèn mén dì) - Academic traditions and schools
- **人生关口** (rénshēng guānkǒu) - Important thresholds in life

The character represents both the physical barrier that controls access and the metaphorical concept
of opportunities, social boundaries, and pathways to knowledge or achievement.
