**🗣️ Pronunciation of 门 (mén)**

- **Pinyin:** mén
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "moon"
- **én** sounds like **"en"** in "pen" but with a rising tone
- **mén** sounds like **"men"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up: **"mén?"** — like asking "Door?" when looking for an entrance.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 门 (mén) - "door, gate"
- 门口 (mén kǒu) - "doorway, entrance"
- 门票 (mén piào) - "entrance ticket"
- 出门 (chū mén) - "go out, leave home"
- 开门 (kāi mén) - "open the door"

**📝 Special Notes:**

The character 门 also serves as a radical in many characters related to doors, gates, and enclosed
spaces.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you approach a **door**, you might wonder "Door?" with a rising tone of curiosity — that's
**mén**!
