**🗣️ Pronunciation of 表 (biǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** biǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **i** sounds like **"ee"** in "see"
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **biǎo** sounds like **"bee-ow"** with a dip-then-rise on the "ow"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 表 (biǎo) - "express; show"
- 表示 (biǎo shì) - "indicate; express"
- 表达 (biǎo dá) - "express; convey"
- 表现 (biǎo xiàn) - "performance; show"
- 手表 (shǒu biǎo) - "wristwatch"
- 表格 (biǎo gé) - "table; form"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you "express" something, your voice naturally dips and rises with emotion - just like the third
tone of 表!
