**🗣️ Pronunciation of 忽 (hū)**

- **Pinyin:** hū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "food" but with first tone → steady high pitch
- **hū** sounds like **"hoo"** with a steady high tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're making a long, steady sound: **"hū..."** — that's the tone pattern of **hū**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 忽 (hū) - "suddenly"
- 忽然 (hū rán) - "suddenly, all of a sudden"
- 忽视 (hū shì) - "ignore, overlook"
- 忽略 (hū lüè) - "neglect, overlook"

**💡 Special Notes:**

忽 is most commonly used in compound words, especially 忽然 (hū rán) meaning "suddenly". It's rarely used alone in modern Chinese.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Suddenly** with the steady first tone — like the sound "whoosh" when something appears out of nowhere!
