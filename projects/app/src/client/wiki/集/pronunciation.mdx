**🗣️ Pronunciation of 集 (jí)**

- **Pinyin:** jí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but softer, more like "gee")
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with second tone → rising up
- **jí** sounds like **"gee"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 集 (jí) - "gather"
- 集合 (jí hé) - "gather together"
- 收集 (shōu jí) - "collect"
- 集中 (jí zhōng) - "concentrate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"gee?"** with a rising tone when people gather and ask "Are we all here?"
