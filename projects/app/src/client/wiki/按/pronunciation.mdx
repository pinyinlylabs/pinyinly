**🗣️ Pronunciation of 按 (àn)**

- **Pinyin:** àn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ā** (similar to **"ah"** in "father")
- **n** like **"n"** in "nice"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn!"** with a sharp downward fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm instruction: **"àn!"** — start high and drop down decisively.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 按 (àn) - "to press; to push; according to"
- 按时 (àn shí) - "on time; punctually"
- 按照 (àn zhào) - "according to; in accordance with"
- 按钮 (àn niǔ) - "button (to press)"
- 按摩 (àn mó) - "massage"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **pressing** a button firmly and decisively — that's the sharp, falling tone of **àn**.
