**🗣️ Pronunciation of 马 (mǎ)**

- **Pinyin:** mǎ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "mother"
- **ǎ** sounds like **"ah"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **mǎ** sounds like **"mah"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone: Say it like you're thinking or being
thoughtful: **"mǎ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **mǎ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 马 (mǎ) - "horse"
- 马上 (mǎ shàng) - "immediately"
- 马路 (mǎ lù) - "road; street"
- 骑马 (qí mǎ) - "ride a horse"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a horse's **up and down** galloping motion — that matches the falling-rising tone of 马!
