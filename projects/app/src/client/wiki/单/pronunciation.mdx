**🗣️ Pronunciation of 单 (dān)**

- **Pinyin:** dān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady musical note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "day"
- **ān** sounds like **"ahn"** but with first tone → high and steady
- **dān** sounds like **"dahn"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high flat** tone: Keep it steady and high, like humming a single note:
**"dān"**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 单 (dān) - "single"
- 单个 (dān gè) - "individual"
- 单独 (dān dú) - "alone"
- 简单 (jiǎn dān) - "simple"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

单 means "single" — one steady tone, just like being **single** and independent!
