### ⚠️ Watch out for: 全 vs 都

- **都** means “all” for plural subjects
- **全** adds emphasis to **completeness**

They’re often used together:  
→ 我们**全都**来了。(_We all came._)

### 🧠 Tip to remember

Imagine a whole cake with no slices missing — that’s **全**.
