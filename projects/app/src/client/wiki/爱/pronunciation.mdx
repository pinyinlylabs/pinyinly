**🗣️ Pronunciation of 爱 (ài)**

- **Pinyin:** ài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ai** sounds like **"i"** in "eye" but with a sharp falling tone
- **ài** sounds like **"eye!"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Say it like you're declaring "Love!" with conviction and passion — **"ài!"** — that strong,
definitive drop is the fourth tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 爱 (ài) - "love; to love"
- 爱人 (ài rén) - "lover; spouse"
- 爱好 (ài hào) - "hobby; interest"
- 爱心 (ài xīn) - "love; compassion"
- 恋爱 (liàn ài) - "to be in love"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you declare your love for someone, you say it with strong conviction and a falling tone — just
like the fourth tone's decisive pattern in 爱!
