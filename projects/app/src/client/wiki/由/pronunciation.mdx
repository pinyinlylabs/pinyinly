**🗣️ Pronunciation of 由 (yóu)**

- **Pinyin:** yóu
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"What?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **óu** sounds like **"o"** in "so", but with second tone → rising upward
- **yóu** sounds like **"yo?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 由 (yóu) - "from; by; cause"
- 由于 (yóu yú) - "due to; because of"
- 自由 (zì yóu) - "freedom"
- 理由 (lǐ yóu) - "reason"
- 由来 (yóu lái) - "origin; source"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Second tone rises like asking **"From where?"** - your voice goes **up** throughout **yóu**.
