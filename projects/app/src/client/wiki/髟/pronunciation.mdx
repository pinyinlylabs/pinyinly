**🗣️ Pronunciation of 髟 (biāo)**

- **Pinyin:** biāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note held high

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **iāo** sounds like **"yow"** but with first tone → steady and high
- **biāo** sounds like **"byow"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone: Keep your voice steady and high, like hair
flowing in the wind: **"biāo"** — that's the flowing tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 髟 (biāo) - "long hair" (radical)
- 髦 (máo) - "bangs; fringe" (contains 髟)
- 鬓 (bìn) - "temples (hair)" (contains 髟)

**💡 Special Notes:**

髟 is primarily used as a **radical** in other characters related to hair. It's rarely used as a
standalone character in modern Chinese, but appears in many hair-related words.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **long hair** flowing **high** in the breeze — that matches the high, steady tone of 髟!
