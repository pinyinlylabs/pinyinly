**🗣️ Pronunciation of 卖 (mài)**

- **Pinyin:** mài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "make"
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **mài** sounds like **"my!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a sharp **falling** tone: Start high and drop sharply down, like saying
"No!" firmly — that's the tone pattern of **mài**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 卖 (mài) - "sell"
- 卖家 (mài jiā) - "seller"
- 买卖 (mǎi mài) - "buying and selling"
- 出卖 (chū mài) - "betray; sell out"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

卖 means "sell" — merchants often call out with a **sharp, commanding** tone to attract customers!
