**🗣️ Pronunciation of 接 (jiē)**

- **Pinyin:** jiē
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but with tongue position closer to "ch")
- **iē** sounds like **"yeh"**, but held steady and high
- **jiē** sounds like **"jyeh"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like singing a steady high note: **"jiē~~~"** — that's the flat, steady tone of **jiē**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 接 (jiē) - "receive; pick up"
- 接电话 (jiē diàn huà) - "answer the phone"
- 接受 (jiē shòu) - "accept; receive"
- 接下来 (jiē xià lái) - "next; following"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"接"** as **"jyeh"** held steady — like steadily receiving or connecting something!
