**🗣️ Pronunciation of 楚 (chǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** chǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **chǔ** sounds like **"choo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking clearly: **"chǔ..."** — that thoughtful, understanding tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 楚 (chǔ) - "clear"
- 清楚 (qīng chǔ) - "clear/obvious"
- 楚楚 (chǔ chǔ) - "neat/tidy"
- 痛楚 (tòng chǔ) - "pain/suffering"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **chǔ** as clarity dawning on you — the dip-and-rise tone is like confusion clearing up!
