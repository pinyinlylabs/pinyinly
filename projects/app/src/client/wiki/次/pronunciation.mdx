**🗣️ Pronunciation of 次 (cì)**

- **Pinyin:** cì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats"
- **ì** sounds like **"uh"** with a sharp falling tone
- **cì** sounds like **"tsuh"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're counting occurrences firmly: **"cì!"** — that's the sharp downward tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 一次 (yí cì) - "one time; once"
- 下次 (xià cì) - "next time"
- 这次 (zhè cì) - "this time"
- 多次 (duō cì) - "many times"
- 次数 (cì shù) - "number of times"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

次 means "occasion/time" — count each occurrence with that **sharp falling** tone: "First 次!
Second 次!"
