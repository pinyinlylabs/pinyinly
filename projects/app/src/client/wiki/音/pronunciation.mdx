**🗣️ Pronunciation of 音 (yīn)**

- **Pinyin:** yīn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **īn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen" but held steady and high
- **yīn** sounds like **"yin"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and steady** tone. Say **"yīn"** like you're holding a musical
note at a high, constant pitch — fitting for the word "sound"!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 音 (yīn) - "sound"
- 音乐 (yīn yuè) - "music"
- 音响 (yīn xiǎng) - "sound system"
- 录音 (lù yīn) - "recording"
- 发音 (fā yīn) - "pronunciation"
- 语音 (yǔ yīn) - "speech sound"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

音 means "sound" — pronounce it with a high, steady tone like a pure musical note: **yīn**!
