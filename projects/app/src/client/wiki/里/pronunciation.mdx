**🗣️ Pronunciation of 里 (lǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** lǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "lee"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **lǐ** sounds like **"lee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're looking inside something thoughtfully: **"lǐ..."** — that's the tone pattern of
**lǐ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 里 (lǐ) - "inside; within; in"
- 里面 (lǐ miàn) - "inside; interior"
- 里头 (lǐ tou) - "inside; within"
- 里边 (lǐ biān) - "inside; interior side"
- 家里 (jiā lǐ) - "at home; inside the house"
- 心里 (xīn lǐ) - "in one's heart; in one's mind"

**💡 Note:**

里 is also used as a unit of distance (Chinese mile), approximately 500 meters. However, the
pronunciation remains the same (lǐ) in both meanings - location and measurement.
