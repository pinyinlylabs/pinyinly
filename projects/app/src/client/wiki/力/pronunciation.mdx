**🗣️ Pronunciation of 力 (lì)**

- **Pinyin:** lì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "lee"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **lì** sounds like **"lee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're exerting force with determination: **"lì!"** — that's the tone pattern of **lì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 力 (lì) - "strength; force; power"
- 力量 (lì liàng) - "strength; power"
- 努力 (nǔ lì) - "effort; work hard"
- 能力 (néng lì) - "ability; capability"
- 压力 (yā lì) - "pressure; stress"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

力 means "strength" — imagine the forceful **falling** tone as the decisive power behind physical or
mental strength!
