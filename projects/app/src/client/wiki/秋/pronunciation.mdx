**🗣️ Pronunciation of 秋 (qiū)**

- **Pinyin:** qiū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but with lips more rounded)
- **iū** sounds like **"yo"** in "yo-yo", but with first tone → steady and high
- **qiū** sounds like **"chyo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're stating something clearly: **"qiū"** — hold that high, steady pitch like
**"qiūūū"**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 秋 (qiū) - "autumn; fall"
- 秋天 (qiū tiān) - "autumn; fall season"
- 秋季 (qiū jì) - "autumn season"
- 中秋 (zhōng qiū) - "Mid-Autumn (Festival)"

**💡 Cultural Note:**

秋 represents the harvest season in Chinese culture. The character combines 禾 (grain)
with 火 (fire), symbolizing the time when crops are ready and the weather becomes cooler.
