## Core Meaning & Usage

耳 represents **hearing and auditory perception**:

1. **Anatomy**: "左耳" - "left ear"
2. **Hearing**: "听不见" - "can't hear"
3. **Attention**: "耳朵" - "ear; ears"
4. **Reception**: "入耳" - "pleasant to hear"

## Examples

- **耳朵** (ěr duo) - "ear; ears"
- **耳机** (ěr jī) - "headphones; earphones"
- **耳环** (ěr huán) - "earring"
- **中耳** (zhōng ěr) - "middle ear"

## Cultural Context

耳 in Chinese culture represents not just physical hearing but wisdom gained through listening. The
concept emphasizes the importance of careful listening as a path to understanding and learning.
