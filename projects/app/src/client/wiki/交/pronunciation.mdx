**🗣️ Pronunciation of 交 (jiāo)**

- **Pinyin:** jiāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jar"
- **iāo** sounds like **"yow"** in "yowl", but with first tone → steady and high
- **jiāo** sounds like **"jyow"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 交 (jiāo) - "hand over"
- 交朋友 (jiāo péng yǒu) - "make friends"
- 交通 (jiāo tōng) - "traffic"
- 交流 (jiāo liú) - "exchange"
- 交给 (jiāo gěi) - "hand over to"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 交 as **"jyow"** said with authority and confidence (first tone) - like when you
confidently hand something over to someone!
