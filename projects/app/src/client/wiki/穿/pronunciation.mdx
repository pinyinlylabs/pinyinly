**🗣️ Pronunciation of 穿 (chuān)**

- **Pinyin:** chuān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church"
- **uān** sounds like **"wan"** in "wander", but with first tone → steady and high
- **chuān** sounds like **"chwan"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and steady** tone:

Keep your voice high and flat throughout: **"chuān"** — like holding a steady musical note.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 穿 (chuān) - "wear; put on"
- 穿衣服 (chuān yī fu) - "put on clothes"
- 穿鞋 (chuān xié) - "put on shoes"
- 穿过 (chuān guò) - "go through; pass through"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"chwan"** as the sound of putting on clothes — steady and continuous, like the first tone!
