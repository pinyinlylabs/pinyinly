**🗣️ Pronunciation of 送 (sòng)**

- **Pinyin:** sòng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "song"
- **òng** sounds like **"ong"** in "gong" but with a sharp falling tone
- **sòng** sounds like **"song!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying **"Here!"** when giving something: **"sòng!"** — that's the
tone pattern of **sòng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 送 (sòng) - "to give as a gift; to deliver"
- 送给 (sòng gěi) - "to give to"
- 送到 (sòng dào) - "to deliver to"
- 送礼 (sòng lǐ) - "to give gifts"
- 运送 (yùn sòng) - "to transport"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Giving** something is a definitive action — the falling tone shows the decisiveness of the giving
gesture!
