A period of 100 consecutive years; an era or age spanning a hundred years.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                                    |
| -------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | shìjì                                   |
| Core meaning   | century; era; 100-year period          |
| Part of speech | noun; time unit                         |
| Tone           | fourth + fourth tones                   |

## Word Breakdown

世纪 combines generational time with recording:

| Component     | Meaning                                        |
| ------------- | ---------------------------------------------- |
| **世** (shì)  | Generation, world, era, lifetime               |
| **纪** (jì)   | Record, discipline, era, chronicle             |

## Understanding

Think of 世纪 as **"recorded generational time"**:

- Like chapters in the book of human history
- Each century contains multiple generations of lives
- A unit for measuring historical progress and change
- Helps organize the flow of time into manageable periods
- Connects individual lifetimes to larger historical patterns

## Usage Contexts

### **Historical Periods**
- **20世纪** (èrshí shìjì) - "20th century"
- **21世纪** - "21st century"

### **Time References**
- **上个世纪** - "last century"
- **本世纪** - "this century"

### **Historical Analysis**
- **世纪之交** - "turn of the century"
- **跨世纪** - "spanning centuries"

### **Cultural/Social Context**
- **世纪工程** - "project of the century"
- **世纪发现** - "discovery of the century"

## Examples

- **我们生活在21世纪。**
  - "We live in the 21st century."

- **20世纪是一个变化巨大的时代。**
  - "The 20th century was an era of tremendous change."

- **这座教堂建于15世纪。**
  - "This church was built in the 15th century."

- **计算机是20世纪最重要的发明之一。**
  - "Computers are one of the most important inventions of the 20th century."

- **新世纪带来了新的挑战。**
  - "The new century brings new challenges."

- **这是本世纪最大的发现。**
  - "This is the biggest discovery of this century."

## Cultural Context

世纪 reflects Chinese historical consciousness:

- **Long-term perspective** - thinking in generational spans
- **Historical awareness** - connecting present to past
- **Progress measurement** - tracking development over time
- **Cultural continuity** - understanding how traditions evolve

## Grammar Notes

世纪 is typically used with:

1. **Numbers**: 19世纪 ("19th century")
2. **Demonstratives**: 这个世纪 ("this century")
3. **Ordinals**: 第一个世纪 ("first century")
4. **Modifiers**: 新世纪 ("new century")

**Common patterns:**
- **...世纪...年代** - "[X] century, [Y] decade"
- **世纪末** - "end of century"
- **世纪初** - "beginning of century"

| 下世纪     | next century               | 下世纪的计划     |
| 跨世纪     | cross-century, spanning    | 跨世纪工程       |

## Historical Significance

In Chinese culture, 世纪 often appears in contexts of:
- **Historical milestones** - marking important events
- **Generational change** - comparing different eras
- **Future planning** - projecting into coming centuries
- **Cultural reflection** - analyzing historical patterns

世纪 provides **essential temporal vocabulary** for discussing history, progress, and long-term perspectives.
