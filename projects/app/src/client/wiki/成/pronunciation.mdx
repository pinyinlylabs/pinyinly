**🗣️ Pronunciation of 成 (chéng)**

- **Pinyin:** chéng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Chéng?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church" but softer
- **éng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung" but with rising tone
- **chéng** sounds like **"chung"** with a rising pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "成?" (Did it succeed?) — that's the questioning, rising tone pattern of
**chéng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 成 (chéng) - "become; succeed"
- 成功 (chéng gōng) - "success; succeed"
- 成为 (chéng wéi) - "become; turn into"
- 完成 (wán chéng) - "complete; finish"
- 成长 (chéng zhǎng) - "grow up; develop"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Become** — the rising second tone suggests growth and progression, like becoming something better!
