**🗣️ Pronunciation of 目 (mù)**

- **Pinyin:** mù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "me"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with a sharp falling tone
- **mù** sounds like **"moo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly pointing at your eye: **"mù!"** — that decisive, falling tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 目 (mù) - "eye"
- 目标 (mù biāo) - "target; goal"
- 目前 (mù qián) - "currently; at present"
- 目的 (mù dì) - "purpose; objective"
- 节目 (jié mù) - "program; show"
- 题目 (tí mù) - "topic; title"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

目 means "eye" — the sharp falling fourth tone sounds like someone commanding attention: **"Look!"**
