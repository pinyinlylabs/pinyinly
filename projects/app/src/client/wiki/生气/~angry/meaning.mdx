To become annoyed or displeased; angry; mad; upset; irritated.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                              |
| -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | shēngqì                           |
| Core meaning   | angry; mad; upset; get angry      |
| Part of speech | verb, adjective                   |
| Tone           | shēng (1st), qì (4th)             |

## Word Breakdown

生气 combines concepts of producing and energy to represent anger formation.

| Component | Meaning                                               |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **生**    | Give birth; produce; generate - representing creation |
| **气**    | Energy; breath; spirit - representing vital force     |

Together they create: "generate energy/spirit" or "produce vital force" - specifically the intense energy of anger.

## Mnemonic

Think of 生气 as **"generating intense energy when provoked"**:

- 生 (shēng) represents producing or generating something
- 气 (qì) shows the energy, breath, or vital force
- Together: producing intense energy when something triggers you
- Like a fire suddenly flaring up with new fuel
- The body generating powerful energy in response to irritation

This creates the image: **the body generating intense energy in response to provocation**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

生气 represents **anger, irritation, and emotional upset**. It's used:

1. **Getting angry**: 生气了 (shēngqì le) - "got angry"
2. **Being mad**: 很生气 (hěn shēngqì) - "very angry"
3. **Feeling upset**: 为什么生气 (wèishénme shēngqì) - "why are you angry"
4. **Irritation**: 别生气 (bié shēngqì) - "don't be angry"

## Examples

- **很生气** (hěn shēngqì) - "very angry"
- **生气了** (shēngqì le) - "got angry"
- **别生气** (bié shēngqì) - "don't be angry"
- **不要生气** (bùyào shēngqì) - "don't get angry"
- **生气地说** (shēngqì de shuō) - "say angrily"

## Causes of Anger

生气 in various situations:

- **误解生气** (wùjiě shēngqì) - "angry due to misunderstanding"
- **被欺骗生气** (bèi qīpiàn shēngqì) - "angry about being deceived"
- **不公平生气** (bù gōngpíng shēngqì) - "angry about unfairness"
- **迟到生气** (chídào shēngqì) - "angry about being late"

## Expressing Anger

生气 in emotional expression:

- **生气地喊** (shēngqì de hǎn) - "shout angrily"
- **生气得脸红** (shēngqì de liǎn hóng) - "face red with anger"
- **生气得说不出话** (shēngqì de shuō bù chū huà) - "too angry to speak"
- **生气地走开** (shēngqì de zǒukāi) - "walk away angrily"

## Levels of Anger

Different degrees of 生气:

- **有点生气** (yǒudiǎn shēngqì) - "a little angry"
- **比较生气** (bǐjiào shēngqì) - "quite angry"
- **非常生气** (fēicháng shēngqì) - "very angry"
- **特别生气** (tèbié shēngqì) - "especially angry"

## Managing Anger

Dealing with 生气:

- **别生气了** (bié shēngqì le) - "stop being angry"
- **消气** (xiāoqì) - "calm down; stop being angry"
- **不值得生气** (bù zhídé shēngqì) - "not worth getting angry about"
- **控制生气** (kòngzhì shēngqì) - "control anger"

## Cultural Context

生气 in Chinese culture represents:

**Emotional Regulation:**
- **克制** (kèzhì) - Self-control and restraint
- **冷静** (lěngjìng) - Staying calm and rational
- **和谐** (héxié) - Maintaining harmony despite conflicts
- **忍耐** (rěnnài) - Patience and tolerance

**Social Harmony:**
- **避免冲突** (bìmiǎn chōngtū) - Avoiding conflicts
- **面子** (miànzi) - Saving face and maintaining dignity
- **和解** (héjiě) - Reconciliation after anger

## Common Expressions

- **气死人了** (qì sǐ rén le) - "makes one furious" (literally "angers one to death")
- **生闷气** (shēng mènqì) - "sulk; be quietly angry"
- **气不过** (qì bù guò) - "can't get over the anger"
- **生气也没用** (shēngqì yě méiyòng) - "being angry is useless"

## Interpersonal Conflicts

生气 in relationships:

- **和朋友生气** (hé péngyǒu shēngqì) - "angry with friends"
- **夫妻生气** (fūqī shēngqì) - "married couple angry"
- **父母生气** (fùmǔ shēngqì) - "parents are angry"
- **老师生气** (lǎoshī shēngqì) - "teacher is angry"

## Reasons for Anger

Common triggers for 生气:

- **不尊重** (bù zūnzhòng) - "disrespect"
- **说谎** (shuōhuǎng) - "lying"
- **不守时** (bù shǒushí) - "not being punctual"
- **不公平对待** (bù gōngpíng duìdài) - "unfair treatment"

## Physical Manifestations

生气 showing physically:

- **生气得发抖** (shēngqì de fādǒu) - "shake with anger"
- **生气得头疼** (shēngqì de tóu téng) - "headache from anger"
- **生气得吃不下饭** (shēngqì de chī bù xià fàn) - "too angry to eat"

## Resolution and Reconciliation

After 生气:

- **道歉** (dàoqiàn) - "apologize"
- **和好** (hé hǎo) - "make up; reconcile"
- **原谅** (yuánliàng) - "forgive"
- **冷静下来** (lěngjìng xiàlái) - "calm down"

## Grammar Functions

- **Verb**: 我生气了 (wǒ shēngqì le) - "I got angry"
- **Adjective**: 很生气的样子 (hěn shēngqì de yàngzi) - "looking very angry"
- **Adverb**: 生气地说 (shēngqì de shuō) - "say angrily"

## Related Emotions

生气 and similar feelings:

- **愤怒** (fènnù) - "furious; indignant"
- **恼火** (nǎohuǒ) - "annoyed; irritated"
- **烦躁** (fánzào) - "irritable; restless"
- **不高兴** (bù gāoxìng) - "unhappy; displeased"

## Advice and Wisdom

Traditional wisdom about 生气:

- **生气伤身** (shēngqì shāng shēn) - "anger harms the body"
- **退一步海阔天空** (tuì yī bù hǎi kuò tiān kōng) - "step back and the world opens up"
- **忍一时风平浪静** (rěn yī shí fēng píng làng jìng) - "patience brings peace"

## Modern Context

生气 in contemporary situations:

- **网络争论生气** (wǎngluò zhēnglùn shēngqì) - "angry about online arguments"
- **交通堵塞生气** (jiāotōng dǔsè shēngqì) - "angry about traffic jams"
- **工作压力生气** (gōngzuò yālì shēngqì) - "angry due to work pressure"

## Learning Notes

生气 is essential because:

- Fundamental emotion word for expressing negative feelings
- Essential for describing interpersonal conflicts and reactions
- Key to understanding emotional expression and social harmony
- Important for conflict resolution and relationship management
- Demonstrates how compound words express complex emotional states

生气 reflects the Chinese understanding that anger is a powerful energy that must be generated within but should be managed wisely to maintain social harmony and personal well-being!
