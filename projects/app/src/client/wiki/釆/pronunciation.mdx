**🗣️ Pronunciation of 釆 (biàn)**

- **Pinyin:** biàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **biàn** sounds like **"byen!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying "Choose!" decisively: **"biàn!"** — that's the tone pattern
of **biàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 釆 (biàn) - "to distinguish; to choose; to pick"

**💡 Note:**

釆 is primarily used as a radical in other characters related to distinguishing or separating, such
as:

- 辨 (biàn) - "to distinguish; to discern"
- 辩 (biàn) - "to argue; to debate"
- 瓣 (bàn) - "petal; segment"

The character 釆 itself is rarely used independently in modern Chinese, but understanding its
pronunciation helps with characters that contain this radical.
