**🗣️ Pronunciation of 康 (kāng)**

- **Pinyin:** kāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "king" but with stronger aspiration
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** held steady and high
- **kāng** sounds like **"kahng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Keep your voice high and steady, like singing a sustained note. **kāng** should sound confident and
stable.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 健康 (jiàn kāng) - "healthy; health"
- 康复 (kāng fù) - "recover; rehabilitation"
- 小康 (xiǎo kāng) - "moderately prosperous"
- 康乐 (kāng lè) - "health and happiness"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

康 means "peaceful/healthy" — the **steady, stable** first tone reflects the **calm peace** and
**steady health** that the character represents!
