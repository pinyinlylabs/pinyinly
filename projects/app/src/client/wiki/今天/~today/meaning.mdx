Today; the current day; this day; the present day we are experiencing right now.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                  |
| -------------- | --------------------- |
| Pinyin         | jīntiān               |
| Core meaning   | today; this day; now  |
| Part of speech | noun, time word       |
| Tone           | jīn (1st), tiān (1st) |

## Word Breakdown

今天 combines concepts of present moment and day to represent today.

| Component | Meaning                            |
| --------- | ---------------------------------- |
| **今**    | Now; present; current; this moment |
| **天**    | Day; sky; heaven; 24-hour period   |

Together they create: "the current day" or "this present day" - the day we are currently
experiencing.

## Mnemonic

Think of 今天 as **"the day that is happening right now"**:

- 今 (jīn) captures the immediacy and presence of "right now"
- 天 (tiān) represents the full 24-hour period we call a day
- Together: the specific day that's currently unfolding around us
- Like pointing to today on a calendar and saying "this is it"
- The present moment stretched into a complete day experience

This creates the image: **the present moment expanded into the full day we're living through**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

今天 represents **the current day and present time**. It's used:

1. **Time reference**: 今天是星期一 (jīntiān shì xīngqī yī) - "today is Monday"
2. **Daily activities**: 今天去购物 (jīntiān qù gòuwù) - "go shopping today"
3. **Present state**: 今天的天气 (jīntiān de tiānqì) - "today's weather"
4. **Current scheduling**: 今天有空吗 (jīntiān yǒu kòng ma) - "are you free today?"

## Examples

- **今天早上** (jīntiān zǎoshang) - "this morning"
- **今天下午** (jīntiān xiàwǔ) - "this afternoon"
- **今天晚上** (jīntiān wǎnshang) - "tonight; this evening"
- **今天的工作** (jīntiān de gōngzuò) - "today's work"
- **就是今天** (jiù shì jīntiān) - "it's today; right today"

## Time Periods

今天 with specific times:

- **今天凌晨** (jīntiān língchén) - "this dawn"
- **今天中午** (jīntiān zhōngwǔ) - "this noon"
- **今天傍晚** (jīntiān bàngwǎn) - "this evening"
- **今天深夜** (jīntiān shēnyè) - "tonight late"

## Present Focus

今天 emphasizing immediacy:

- **就在今天** (jiù zài jīntiān) - "right today"
- **今天开始** (jīntiān kāishǐ) - "starting today"
- **今天完成** (jīntiān wánchéng) - "complete today"
- **今天决定** (jīntiān juédìng) - "decide today"

## Cultural Context

今天 in Chinese culture represents:

**Present Moment Philosophy:**

- **活在当下** (huó zài dāngxià) - "live in the present moment"
- **今日事今日毕** (jīnrì shì jīnrì bì) - "today's work, today's completion"
- **把握今天** (bǎwò jīntiān) - "seize today; make the most of today"
- **珍惜今天** (zhēnxī jīntiān) - "treasure today"

**Time Management:**

- **今天的计划** (jīntiān de jìhuà) - "today's plan"
- **今天的目标** (jīntiān de mùbiāo) - "today's goals"
- **今天优先** (jīntiān yōuxiān) - "today is priority"

## Daily Planning

今天 in scheduling:

- **今天的日程** (jīntiān de rìchéng) - "today's schedule"
- **今天的任务** (jīntiān de rènwù) - "today's tasks"
- **今天的会议** (jīntiān de huìyì) - "today's meetings"
- **今天有什么** (jīntiān yǒu shénme) - "what's there today"

## Common Expressions

- **今天天气真好** (jīntiān tiānqì zhēn hǎo) - "the weather is really nice today"
- **今天是个好日子** (jīntiān shì gè hǎo rìzi) - "today is a good day"
- **今天怎么样** (jīntiān zěnmeyàng) - "how is today; how are things today"
- **今天不同** (jīntiān bùtóng) - "today is different"

## Work and Activities

今天 in daily life:

- **今天上班** (jīntiān shàngbān) - "work today"
- **今天休息** (jīntiān xiūxi) - "rest today"
- **今天学习** (jīntiān xuéxí) - "study today"
- **今天运动** (jīntiān yùndòng) - "exercise today"

## Current Events

今天 for immediate news:

- **今天的新闻** (jīntiān de xīnwén) - "today's news"
- **今天发生** (jīntiān fāshēng) - "happened today"
- **今天的事情** (jīntiān de shìqing) - "today's events"
- **今天听说** (jīntiān tīngshuō) - "heard today"

## Emotions and Mood

今天 with feelings:

- **今天很开心** (jīntiān hěn kāixīn) - "very happy today"
- **今天心情好** (jīntiān xīnqíng hǎo) - "in a good mood today"
- **今天感觉** (jīntiān gǎnjué) - "feel today"
- **今天状态** (jīntiān zhuàngtài) - "today's condition"

## Comparative Usage

今天 with other time words:

- **昨天、今天、明天** (zuótiān, jīntiān, míngtiān) - "yesterday, today, tomorrow"
- **今天比昨天** (jīntiān bǐ zuótiān) - "today compared to yesterday"
- **从今天开始** (cóng jīntiān kāishǐ) - "starting from today"

## Grammar Functions

- **Time adverb**: 今天我很忙 (jīntiān wǒ hěn máng) - "I'm busy today"
- **Noun modifier**: 今天的报纸 (jīntiān de bàozhǐ) - "today's newspaper"
- **Subject**: 今天是周末 (jīntiān shì zhōumò) - "today is weekend"

## Learning Notes

今天 is essential because:

- Most frequently used time word for present day reference
- Essential for daily conversation, planning, and scheduling
- Key to understanding Chinese emphasis on present-moment awareness
- Important for news, weather, and current event discussions
- Demonstrates the Chinese cultural value of making the most of the present day

今天 reflects the Chinese understanding that the present day is a precious opportunity that should
be fully embraced, planned for, and lived with intention and awareness!
