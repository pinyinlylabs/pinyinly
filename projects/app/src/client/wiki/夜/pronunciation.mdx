**🗣️ Pronunciation of 夜 (yè)**

- **Pinyin:** yè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"Yeah!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **yè** sounds like **"yeah!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're definitively stating the time: **"yè!"** — that firm, conclusive tone is the
**fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 夜 (yè) - "night; evening"
- 夜里 (yè lǐ) - "at night"
- 夜晚 (yè wǎn) - "nighttime"
- 半夜 (bàn yè) - "midnight"
- 昨夜 (zuó yè) - "last night"
- 今夜 (jīn yè) - "tonight"

**💡 Special Notes:**

夜 represents the dark hours when the sun has set. It's commonly used to describe nighttime
activities, late hours, and the period of rest. The character often appears in poetic and literary
contexts describing the beauty or mystery of night.
