**🗣️ Pronunciation of 似 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shop"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **shì** sounds like **"she"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like making a definitive comparison: **"shì!"** — that sharp drop down shows the fourth tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 似 (shì) - "resemble; be like"
- 似乎 (sì hū) - "seem; appear"
- 相似 (xiāng sì) - "similar; alike"
- 类似 (lèi sì) - "similar; analogous"

**💡 Special Note:**

似 can also be pronounced **sì** (fourth tone) in some compounds like 似乎 (sì hū) and 相似 (xiāng
sì).

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"she"** with a decisive falling tone — like pointing out a clear resemblance!
