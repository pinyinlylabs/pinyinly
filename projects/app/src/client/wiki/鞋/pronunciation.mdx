**🗣️ Pronunciation of 鞋 (xié)**

- **Pinyin:** xié
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **ié** sounds like **"yeah"** but with rising tone
- **xié** sounds like **"shyeah?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone, like when you ask "Really?" Say **"xié"** with a
curious, questioning inflection.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 鞋 (xié) - "shoe"
- 鞋子 (xié zi) - "shoes"
- 运动鞋 (yùn dòng xié) - "sneakers"
- 皮鞋 (pí xié) - "leather shoes"
- 拖鞋 (tuō xié) - "slippers"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of asking "What **shoe** is this?" with a rising intonation — that's the second tone of
**xié**!
