**🗣️ Pronunciation of 并 (bìng)**

- **Pinyin:** bìng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bing"
- **ìng** sounds like **"eeng"** but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **bìng** sounds like **"beeng"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** is a **sharp falling** tone, like giving a firm command:

Say it like firmly stating things are **together**: **"bìng!"** — that decisive drop is the **fourth
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 并 (bìng) - "together; and; furthermore"
- 并且 (bìng qiě) - "and; moreover"
- 合并 (hé bìng) - "merge; combine"
- 并列 (bìng liè) - "side by side; parallel"
- 并非 (bìng fēi) - "not; it is not the case that"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **并** as firmly joining things **together** with that decisive **fourth tone** authority!
