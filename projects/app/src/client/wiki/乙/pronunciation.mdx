**🗣️ Pronunciation of 乙 (yǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** yǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǐ** sounds like **"yee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it thoughtfully, like you're considering something — **"yǐ..."** — that contemplative tone fits
the meaning of **"second"** as in ranking.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 乙 (yǐ) - "second (in order), B-grade"
- 甲乙 (jiǎ yǐ) - "A and B, first and second"
- 乙级 (yǐ jí) - "B-grade, second class"

**📍 Traditional Usage:**

乙 is the **second** of the ten traditional Chinese stems (甲乙丙丁...), often used for:

- **Grading**: 乙级 (B-grade)
- **Ordering**: 甲方乙方 (Party A, Party B)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Second place** gets the thoughtful third tone — like you're pondering **"Hmm, second..."**
