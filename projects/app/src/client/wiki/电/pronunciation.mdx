**🗣️ Pronunciation of 电 (diàn)**

- **Pinyin:** diàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Stop!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "do"
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** in "yen", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **diàn** sounds like **"dyen!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 电 (diàn) - "electricity"
- 电话 (diàn huà) - "telephone"
- 电脑 (diàn nǎo) - "computer"
- 电视 (diàn shì) - "television"
- 电影 (diàn yǐng) - "movie"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Fourth tone is like the sharp **"Zap!"** of electricity - your voice **drops sharply** throughout
**diàn**.
