**🗣️ Pronunciation of 血 (xuè)**

- **Pinyin:** xuè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep" (but with tongue curled back slightly)
- **uè** sounds like **"way"** but shorter, with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **xuè** sounds like **"shway!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making an urgent statement: **"xuè!"** — that's the tone pattern of **xuè**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 血 (xuè) - "blood"
- 血液 (xuè yè) - "blood (medical term)"
- 流血 (liú xuè) - "bleed"
- 血压 (xuè yā) - "blood pressure"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **xuè** as urgently mentioning blood in a medical context: "Blood!" — the falling tone
sounds serious and important.
