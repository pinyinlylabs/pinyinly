**🗣️ Pronunciation of 觉 (jué)**

- **Pinyin:** jué
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but softer
- **ué** sounds like **"way"** but with second tone → rising up
- **jué** sounds like **"jway"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're discovering something: **"jué?"** — that upward rise captures the sense of
realization.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 觉 (jué) - "feel"
- 觉得 (jué de) - "feel; think"
- 感觉 (gǎn jué) - "feeling"
- 睡觉 (shuì jiào) - "sleep" (note: different tone here)
- 自觉 (zì jué) - "conscious; aware"

**💡 Special Note:**

觉 has two pronunciations:

- **jué** (second tone) - "feel; sense"
- **jiào** (fourth tone) - "sleep" (as in 睡觉)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 觉 as "feeling" something new — when you have a realization, your voice naturally rises
with curiosity, just like the second tone!
