**🗣️ Pronunciation of 刚 (gāng)**

- **Pinyin:** gāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Gaaang"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** in "gang", but with first tone → steady and high
- **gāng** sounds like **"gang"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Hold it steady and high like singing a single note: **"gāng..."** — that's the tone pattern of
**gāng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 刚 (gāng) - "just" (recently)
- 刚才 (gāng cái) - "just now"
- 刚刚 (gāng gāng) - "just now"
- 刚好 (gāng hǎo) - "just right"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

刚 means "just" (recently) — imagine someone saying "I **just** did that" with a firm, steady tone!
