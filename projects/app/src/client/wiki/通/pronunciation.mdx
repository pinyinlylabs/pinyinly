**🗣️ Pronunciation of 通 (tōng)**

- **Pinyin:** tōng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **ōng** sounds like **"ong"** in "gong" but with a high flat tone
- **tōng** sounds like **"tong"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Keep it steady and high, like a clear signal going through: **"tōnggg"** — that's the tone pattern
of **tōng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 通 (tōng) - "to communicate; to pass through"
- 通过 (tōng guò) - "to pass through; via"
- 通知 (tōng zhī) - "to notify; notice"
- 通话 (tōng huà) - "phone call"
- 通常 (tōng cháng) - "usually"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Good **communication** flows smoothly and clearly — just like the steady, uninterrupted first tone!
