**🗣️ Pronunciation of 路 (lù)**

- **Pinyin:** lù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "look"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "mood" but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **lù** sounds like **"loo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're decisively pointing down a path: **"lù!"** — that's the tone pattern of **lù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 路 (lù) - "road; path; way"
- 路上 (lù shàng) - "on the road; on the way"
- 马路 (mǎ lù) - "street; road"
- 走路 (zǒu lù) - "walk; go on foot"
- 路口 (lù kǒu) - "intersection; crossroads"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

路 means "road" — the **sharp falling** tone is like the decisive direction of a clear path!
