**🗣️ Pronunciation of 动 (dòng)**

- **Pinyin:** dòng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Dong!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dong"
- **òng** sounds like **"ong"** in "long" but with a sharp falling tone
- **dòng** sounds like **"dong!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're commanding something to move: **"dòng!"** — firm and decisive.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 动 (dòng) - "move"
- 动作 (dòng zuò) - "action, movement"
- 动物 (dòng wù) - "animal"
- 动力 (dòng lì) - "power, motivation"
- 动人 (dòng rén) - "moving, touching"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **dòng** like the deep sound of a "dong" — something powerful moving or striking!
