**🗣️ Pronunciation of 机 (jī)**

- **Pinyin:** jī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but more like "gee")
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **jī** sounds like **"gee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like humming a steady high note: **"jī"** — that even, high pitch is the first tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 机 (jī) - "machine, desk"
- 飞机 (fēi jī) - "airplane"
- 手机 (shǒu jī) - "cell phone"
- 机会 (jī huì) - "opportunity"
- 电脑机 (diàn nǎo jī) - "computer"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **机** as the steady hum of a "machine" — the constant first tone matches the consistent
operation of mechanical devices!
