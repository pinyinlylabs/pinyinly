**🗣️ Pronunciation of 邮 (yóu)**

- **Pinyin:** yóu
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **óu** sounds like **"oh"** but with second tone → rising pitch
- **yóu** sounds like **"yo?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start mid-pitch and rise up, like asking "Really?" curiously: **"yóu?"** — that's the tone pattern
of **yóu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 邮 (yóu) - "postal; mail"
- 邮件 (yóu jiàn) - "email; mail"
- 邮票 (yóu piào) - "postage stamp"
- 邮箱 (yóu xiāng) - "mailbox; email inbox"
- 邮局 (yóu jú) - "post office"

**💡 Note:**

邮 is commonly used in modern contexts related to postal services and electronic mail. The character
contains the 邑 radical, indicating its connection to places and services.
