**🗣️ Pronunciation of 外 (wài)**

- **Pinyin:** wài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"Why!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **wài** sounds like **"why!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're pointing out something beyond: **"wài!"** — that definitive tone is the **fourth
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 外 (wài) - "outside; external"
- 外面 (wài miàn) - "outside"
- 外国 (wài guó) - "foreign country"
- 外语 (wài yǔ) - "foreign language"
- 外交 (wài jiāo) - "diplomacy"
- 意外 (yì wài) - "unexpected"

**💡 Special Notes:**

外 indicates something that is outside, external, or foreign. It's commonly used to describe
locations, nationalities, and things that are beyond or separate from a reference point.
