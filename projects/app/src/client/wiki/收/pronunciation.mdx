**🗣️ Pronunciation of 收 (shōu)**

- **Pinyin:** shōu
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ōu** sounds like **"oh"** in "show", but held steady and high
- **shōu** sounds like **"show"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 收 (shōu) - "receive"
- 收到 (shōu dào) - "receive, get"
- 收入 (shōu rù) - "income"
- 收费 (shōu fèi) - "charge a fee"
- 收拾 (shōu shi) - "tidy up"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **收** as gathering things toward you - the steady first tone reflects the controlled
action of receiving or collecting.
