**🗣️ Pronunciation of 号 (hào)**

- **Pinyin:** hào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "how" but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **hào** sounds like **"how"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're announcing a number: **"hào!"** — that's the authoritative tone pattern of
**hào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 号 (hào) - "number; size; date"
- 电话号码 (diàn huà hào mǎ) - "phone number"
- 几号 (jǐ hào) - "what number/date?"
- 号码 (hào mǎ) - "number"
- 信号 (xìn hào) - "signal"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"how"** with a sharp drop — when calling out a **number**, you often use a strong,
definitive tone!
