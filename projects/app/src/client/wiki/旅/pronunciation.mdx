**🗣️ Pronunciation of 旅 (lǚ)**

- **Pinyin:** lǚ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **ǚ** sounds like **"yu"** in "you" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **lǚ** sounds like **"lyu"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're contemplating a journey: **"lǚ..."** — that thoughtful dip-and-rise suggests
planning a trip!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 旅 (lǚ) - "trip, journey, travel"
- 旅游 (lǚ yóu) - "to travel, tourism"
- 旅行 (lǚ xíng) - "to travel, journey"
- 旅客 (lǚ kè) - "passenger, traveler"
- 旅馆 (lǚ guǎn) - "hotel, inn"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Planning a **trip** requires thoughtful consideration — just like the contemplative third tone of
**lǚ**!

**📍 Cultural Note:**

旅 is fundamental to travel vocabulary in Chinese. 旅游 (tourism) and 旅行 (travel) are among the
most common travel-related terms.
