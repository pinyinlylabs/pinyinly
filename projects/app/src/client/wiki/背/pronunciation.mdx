**🗣️ Pronunciation of 背 (bèi)**

- **Pinyin:** bèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "boy"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "day", but with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **bèi** sounds like **"bay!"** with a firm falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're pointing something out firmly: **"bèi!"** — that's the definitive tone pattern of
**bèi**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 背 (bèi) - "back; backside"
- 背后 (bèi hòu) - "behind; at the back"
- 背包 (bèi bāo) - "backpack"
- 背景 (bèi jǐng) - "background"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

背 refers to the back or backside, and the strong fourth tone emphasizes the solid, supporting
nature of one's back!
