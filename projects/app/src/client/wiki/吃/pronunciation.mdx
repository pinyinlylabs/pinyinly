**🗣️ Pronunciation of 吃 (chī)**

- **Pinyin:** chī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "cheese" but softer
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **chī** sounds like **"chee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **steady and high**:

Say it like you're holding a long musical note — that's the energy of **chī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 吃饭 (chī fàn) - "eat rice/meal"
- 吃药 (chī yào) - "take medicine"
- 好吃 (hǎo chī) - "delicious"
- 吃早餐 (chī zǎo cān) - "eat breakfast"
