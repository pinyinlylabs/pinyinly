**🗣️ Pronunciation of 神 (shén)**

- **Pinyin:** shén
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: "Really?"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **én** sounds like **"en"** in "pen" but with second tone → rising up
- **shén** sounds like **"shen?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're in awe or asking: **"shén?"** — that's the tone pattern of **shén**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 神 (shén) - "spirit; god"
- 精神 (jīng shén) - "spirit; energy"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When encountering something **spiritual** or divine, you might speak with awe and wonder — that
rising, questioning tone perfectly captures the reverence of **shén**!
