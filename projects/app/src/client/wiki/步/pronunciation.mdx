**🗣️ Pronunciation of 步 (bù)**

- **Pinyin:** bù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** with a sharp falling tone
- **bù** sounds like **"boo"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're taking a decisive step: **"bù!"** — that's the sharp downward tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 步行 (bù xíng) - "to walk"
- 跑步 (pǎo bù) - "to run; jogging"
- 散步 (sàn bù) - "to take a walk"
- 进步 (jìn bù) - "progress"
- 脚步 (jiǎo bù) - "footsteps"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

步 means "step" — take each step with that **sharp, decisive** tone: "bù!"
