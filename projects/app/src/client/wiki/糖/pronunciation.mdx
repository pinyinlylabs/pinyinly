**🗣️ Pronunciation of 糖 (táng)**

- **Pinyin:** táng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Tang?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea" but with a slight puff of air
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with second tone → rising
- **táng** sounds like **"tahng?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "want some sugar?" — **"táng?"** — that rising, offering tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 糖 (táng) - "sugar; candy"
- 糖果 (táng guǒ) - "candy; sweets"
- 白糖 (bái táng) - "white sugar"
- 红糖 (hóng táng) - "brown sugar"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **糖** with the second tone rising like your energy level after eating sugar — that upward
boost matches the rising tone!
