**🗣️ Pronunciation of 疒 (nè)**

- **Pinyin:** nè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "net"
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** in "meh", but with a sharp falling tone
- **nè** sounds like **"neh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly stating something's wrong: **"nè!"** — that decisive, falling tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 疒 (nè) - "sickness" (radical)
- 疼 (téng) - "pain" (contains 疒)
- 病 (bìng) - "illness" (contains 疒)
- 痛 (tòng) - "pain" (contains 疒)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

疒 is the "sickness" radical — the sharp falling fourth tone sounds like someone collapsing from
illness!

**📝 Note:**

疒 is primarily used as a radical component in characters related to sickness, disease, and physical
ailments. It's not commonly used independently in modern Chinese.
