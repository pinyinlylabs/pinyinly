**🗣️ Pronunciation of 结 (jié)**

- **Pinyin:** jié
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but slightly softer)
- **ié** sounds like **"yeh"** with a rising tone
- **jié** sounds like **"jyeh?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and go up, like asking "Really?" — **"jié?"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 结果 (jié guǒ) - "result; outcome"
- 结婚 (jié hūn) - "to get married"
- 结束 (jié shù) - "to end; to finish"
- 结合 (jié hé) - "to combine"
- 结实 (jié shi) - "sturdy; solid"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of tying a **knot** — you pull it tight with a rising motion, like the rising tone!
