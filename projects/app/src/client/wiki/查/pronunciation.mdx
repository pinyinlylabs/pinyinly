**🗣️ Pronunciation of 查 (chá)**

- **Pinyin:** chá
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church"
- **á** sounds like **"ah"**, but with second tone → rise up
- **chá** sounds like **"chah?"** with a rising tone

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 查 (chá) - "investigate; check; look up"
- 检查 (jiǎn chá) - "inspect; examine"
- 调查 (diào chá) - "survey; investigate"
- 查看 (chá kàn) - "check; look at"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you **investigate**, you're asking questions and searching higher — that's the rising second
tone of **chá**!
