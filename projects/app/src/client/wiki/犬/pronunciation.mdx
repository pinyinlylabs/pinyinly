**🗣️ Pronunciation of 犬 (quǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** quǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" but with lips rounded
- **uǎn** sounds like **"wahn"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **quǎn** sounds like **"chwahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about a dog: **"quǎn..."** — that thoughtful dip-and-rise is the **third
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 犬 (quǎn) - "dog" (formal/literary)
- 猎犬 (liè quǎn) - "hunting dog"
- 警犬 (jǐng quǎn) - "police dog"
- 牧羊犬 (mù yáng quǎn) - "sheepdog"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **犬** as thoughtfully considering a dog: **"quǎn..."** — that contemplative tone matches
the **third tone**!

**📍 Usage Note:**

犬 is a more formal/literary word for dog. In everyday speech, 狗 (gǒu) is more commonly used.
