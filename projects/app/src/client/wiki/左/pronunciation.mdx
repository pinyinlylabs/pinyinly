**🗣️ Pronunciation of 左 (zuǒ)**

- **Pinyin:** zuǒ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"dz"** in "adds" but softer
- **uǒ** sounds like **"waw"** with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **zuǒ** sounds like **"dzwaw"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're pointing left while thinking: **"zuǒ..."** — that thoughtful dip-and-rise is the
**third tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 左 (zuǒ) - "left"
- 左右 (zuǒ yòu) - "left and right; about"
- 左边 (zuǒ biān) - "left side"
- 向左 (xiàng zuǒ) - "to the left"
- 左手 (zuǒ shǒu) - "left hand"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **左** as your left hand hesitating — that's the **third tone** contemplative dip and rise!
