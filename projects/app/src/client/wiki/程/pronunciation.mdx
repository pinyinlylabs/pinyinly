**🗣️ Pronunciation of 程 (chéng)**

- **Pinyin:** chéng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church"
- **éng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung", but with second tone → rise up
- **chéng** sounds like **"chung"** with a rising tone, like asking "chung?"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking: **"chéng?"** — that's the questioning rise of **chéng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 程 (chéng) - "sequence; process; journey"
- 程度 (chéng dù) - "degree; extent"
- 过程 (guò chéng) - "process"
- 课程 (kè chéng) - "course; curriculum"
- 工程 (gōng chéng) - "engineering; project"
- 路程 (lù chéng) - "distance; journey"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the rising second tone as representing **progress** — like going **up** through the steps
of a process or journey, which matches the meaning of 程.
