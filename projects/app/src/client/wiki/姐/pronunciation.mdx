**🗣️ Pronunciation of 姐 (jiě)**

- **Pinyin:** jiě
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but softer, more like "zy" in "as you"
- **iě** sounds like **"yeah"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **jiě** sounds like **"jyeah"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're fondly acknowledging your older sister: **"jiě..."** — that affectionate,
thoughtful tone is the **third tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 姐 (jiě) - "older sister"
- 姐姐 (jiě jie) - "older sister" (more familiar)
- 大姐 (dà jiě) - "eldest sister"
- 表姐 (biǎo jiě) - "older female cousin"
- 姐夫 (jiě fu) - "brother-in-law" (sister's husband)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The third tone's dip-and-rise is like respectfully greeting your **older sister** — you dip down to
show respect, then rise up warmly!
