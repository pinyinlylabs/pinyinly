**🗣️ Pronunciation of 乂 (yì)**

- **Pinyin:** yì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Yee!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **yì** sounds like **"yee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it with decisive action: **"yì!"** — like the sharp motion of cutting grass!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 乂 (yì) - "to mow grass; to cut" (radical meaning)
- 艺 (yì) - "art; skill" (different character, same sound)
- 意 (yì) - "meaning; intention" (different character, same sound)
- 易 (yì) - "easy" (different character, same sound)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the **X-shaped** stroke pattern (乂) as **cutting** or **mowing** — the sharp fourth tone
**yì** matches the decisive cutting action!
