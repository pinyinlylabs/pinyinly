**🗣️ Pronunciation of 圭 (guī)**

- **Pinyin:** guī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uī** sounds like **"way"**, but with first tone → steady and high
- **guī** sounds like **"gway"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like singing a steady high note: **"guī¯"** — that's the tone pattern of **guī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 圭 (guī) - "jade tablet; ancient ceremonial jade"
- 圭表 (guī biǎo) - "sundial; gnomon"

**💡 Special Notes:**

**圭** is primarily used in historical and ceremonial contexts, referring to ancient jade tablets
used in rituals. It's also used as a component in other characters and appears in specialized terms
related to astronomy and ancient Chinese culture.

**🏛️ Cultural Note:**

In ancient China, 圭 represented ceremonial jade tablets that symbolized authority and were used in
imperial ceremonies.
