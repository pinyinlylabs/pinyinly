**🗣️ Pronunciation of 取 (qǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** qǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" but more aspirated
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **qǔ** sounds like **"choo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it thoughtfully, like considering your choice: **"qǔ..."** — that's the deliberate, dipping
pattern of **qǔ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 取得 (qǔ dé) - "obtain"
- 取消 (qǔ xiāo) - "cancel"
- 争取 (zhēng qǔ) - "strive for"
- 录取 (lù qǔ) - "admit"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **取** as **take** — the dipping and rising tone suggests the careful action of reaching
down to take something!
