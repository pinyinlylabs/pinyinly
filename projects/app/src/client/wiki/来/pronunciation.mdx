**🗣️ Pronunciation of 来 (lái)**

- **Pinyin:** lái
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "let"
- **ái** sounds like **"eye"**, but with second tone → rise up
- **lái** sounds like **"lie?"** with a rising tone

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 来 (lái) - "come"
- 来到 (lái dào) - "arrive at"
- 来自 (lái zì) - "come from"
- 本来 (běn lái) - "originally"
- 将来 (jiāng lái) - "future"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When someone **comes**, their voice rises with excitement — that's the rising second tone of
**lái**!
