## Core Meaning & Usage

处 represents **specific locations and positions**:

1. **Physical places**: "这个处" - "this place"
2. **Situations**: "处境" - "situation; circumstances"
3. **Handling**: "处理" - "handle; deal with"
4. **Positions**: "高处" - "high place"

## Examples

- **处理** (chǔ lǐ) - "handle; deal with; process"
- **到处** (dào chù) - "everywhere"
- **好处** (hǎo chù) - "benefit; advantage"
- **住处** (zhù chù) - "residence; dwelling place"

## Cultural Context

处 embodies Chinese concepts of proper placement and situational awareness. The character appears in
many words related to handling situations appropriately and finding the right position. It reflects
cultural values of positioning oneself correctly in social and physical environments.
