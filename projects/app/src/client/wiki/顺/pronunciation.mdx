**🗣️ Pronunciation of 顺 (shùn)**

- **Pinyin:** shùn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **ùn** sounds like **"oon"** in "moon" but with sharp falling tone
- **shùn** sounds like **"shoon!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone. Say **"shùn"** like you're giving a firm
command to obey: "**Obey**!"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 顺 (shùn) - "obey/smooth"
- 顺利 (shùn lì) - "smooth/successful"
- 顺序 (shùn xù) - "sequence/order"
- 顺便 (shùn biàn) - "by the way"
- 顺从 (shùn cóng) - "obedient"
- 一路顺风 (yí lù shùn fēng) - "bon voyage"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you order someone to **obey**, you use a commanding, falling tone — that's the fourth tone of
**shùn**!
