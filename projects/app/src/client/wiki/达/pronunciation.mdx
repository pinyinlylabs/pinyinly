**🗣️ Pronunciation of 达 (dá)**

- **Pinyin:** dá
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **á** sounds like **"ah"** but with second tone → starts low and rises up
- **dá** sounds like **"dah?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're surprised you arrived: **"dá?"** — that's the rising tone pattern of **dá**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 到达 (dào dá) - "to arrive; to reach"
- 达到 (dá dào) - "to achieve; to attain"
- 表达 (biǎo dá) - "to express"
- 传达 (chuán dá) - "to convey; to communicate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

达 means **to arrive/reach** — pronounce it with a rising **dá** like asking "did I make it there?"
