**🗣️ Pronunciation of 室 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **shì** sounds like **"shee!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like pointing out a specific room: **"shì!"** — that definitive drop identifies the **room**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 室 (shì) - "room; chamber"
- 教室 (jiào shì) - "classroom"
- 办公室 (bàn gōng shì) - "office"
- 实验室 (shí yàn shì) - "laboratory"
- 卧室 (wò shì) - "bedroom"
- 会议室 (huì yì shì) - "conference room"

**📍 Usage Note:**

室 is typically used for formal or specific types of rooms, especially in institutional or
professional settings. For general "room," 房间 (fáng jiān) is more commonly used.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Room** — the sharp falling fourth tone is like the definitive sound of entering a specific
**room**!
