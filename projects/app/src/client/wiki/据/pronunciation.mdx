**🗣️ Pronunciation of 据 (jù)**

- **Pinyin:** jù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but with tongue position closer to "ch")
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **jù** sounds like **"joo"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're stating a fact firmly: **"jù!"** — that's the decisive falling tone of **jù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 据 (jù) - "according to"
- 据说 (jù shuō) - "it is said that"
- 根据 (gēn jù) - "according to; based on"
- 数据 (shù jù) - "data"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"据"** as **"joo"** with a sharp drop — like firmly stating evidence or data!
