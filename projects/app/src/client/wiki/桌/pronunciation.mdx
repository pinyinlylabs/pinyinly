**🗣️ Pronunciation of 桌 (zhuō)**

- **Pinyin:** zhuō
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **uō** sounds like **"woh"** held steady and high
- **zhuō** sounds like **"jwoh"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like steadily naming furniture: **"zhuō—"** — flat and clear.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 桌 (zhuō) - "table"
- 桌子 (zhuō zi) - "table"
- 书桌 (shū zhuō) - "desk"
- 餐桌 (cān zhuō) - "dining table"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **zhuō** as the steady, flat surface of a table — the high flat tone matches the table's
level top!
