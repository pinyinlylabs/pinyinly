**🗣️ Pronunciation of 名 (míng)**

- **Pinyin:** míng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "mom"
- **íng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing" but with a rising tone
- **míng** sounds like **"ming?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) **rises** like asking a question:

Say it like you're asking "Name?" — that's the energy of **míng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 名字 (míng zi) - "name"
- 有名 (yǒu míng) - "famous"
- 姓名 (xìng míng) - "full name"
- 名牌 (míng pái) - "brand name"
- 名单 (míng dān) - "name list"
