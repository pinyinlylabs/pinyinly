**🗣️ Pronunciation of 邑 (yì)**

- **Pinyin:** yì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **yì** sounds like **"yee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying "No!" firmly: **"yì!"** — that's the tone pattern of
**yì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 邑 (yì) - "city; settlement"
- 城邑 (chéng yì) - "city; town"
- 都邑 (dū yì) - "capital city"

**💡 Note:**

邑 is often used as a radical in other characters related to places and settlements, such
as 都 (capital), 部 (department), and 郊 (suburbs).
