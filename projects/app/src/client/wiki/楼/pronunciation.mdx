**🗣️ Pronunciation of 楼 (lóu)**

- **Pinyin:** lóu
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "low"
- **óu** sounds like **"oh"** in "go" but with a rising tone
- **lóu** sounds like **"low?"** with an upward inflection

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're looking up at a tall building: **"lóu?"** — with that upward gaze.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 楼 (lóu) - "building/floor"
- 楼上 (lóu shàng) - "upstairs"
- 楼下 (lóu xià) - "downstairs"
- 大楼 (dà lóu) - "large building"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **lóu** as looking up at floors of a building — the rising tone follows your gaze going
upward!
