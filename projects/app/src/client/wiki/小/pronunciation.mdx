**🗣️ Pronunciation of 小 (xiǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** xiǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like a soft **"sh"** sound, but your tongue is much closer to your teeth
- **iǎo** sounds like **"yow"** in "yowl" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **xiǎo** sounds like **"shyow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"xiǎo..."** — that's the tone pattern of
**xiǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 小 (xiǎo) - "small"
- 小孩 (xiǎo hái) - "child"
- 小姐 (xiǎo jiě) - "miss"
- 小心 (xiǎo xīn) - "be careful"
- 小时 (xiǎo shí) - "hour"
