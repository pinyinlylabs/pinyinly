**🗣️ Pronunciation of 友 (yǒu)**

- **Pinyin:** yǒu
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǒu** sounds like **"oh"** in "go", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǒu** sounds like **"yo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it thoughtfully, like considering: **"yǒu..."** — that's the contemplative, dipping pattern of
**yǒu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 朋友 (péng yǒu) - "friend"
- 友好 (yǒu hǎo) - "friendly"
- 友谊 (yǒu yì) - "friendship"
- 男朋友 (nán péng yǒu) - "boyfriend"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **友** as **friend** — the dipping and rising tone is like the warm, thoughtful way you'd
say someone's name when greeting a close friend!
