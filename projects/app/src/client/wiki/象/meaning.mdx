## Usage Examples

- **大象** (dà xiàng) - "elephant" (big elephant)
- **象征** (xiàng zhēng) - "symbol; symbolize"
- **现象** (xiàn xiàng) - "phenomenon; appearance"
- **印象** (yìn xiàng) - "impression"
- **象样** (xiàng yàng) - "decent; proper; presentable"
