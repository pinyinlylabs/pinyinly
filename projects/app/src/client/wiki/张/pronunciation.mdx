**🗣️ Pronunciation of 张 (zhāng)**

- **Pinyin:** zhāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Zhaaa"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" (retroflex sound)
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with first tone → steady and high
- **zhāng** sounds like **"jahng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high flat** tone:

Say it like you're stretching something taut: **"zhāng"** — that's the steady, extended tone of
**zhāng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 张 (zhāng) - "open/expand" or surname "Zhang"
- 张开 (zhāng kāi) - "open up/spread"
- 紧张 (jǐn zhāng) - "nervous/tense"
- 夸张 (kuā zhāng) - "exaggerate"
- 张三 (Zhāng Sān) - "Zhang San" (common name)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

张 means "open/expand" — the steady first tone is like the controlled motion of **opening** or
**expanding** something!
