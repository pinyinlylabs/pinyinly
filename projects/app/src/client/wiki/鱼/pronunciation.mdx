**🗣️ Pronunciation of 鱼 (yú)**

- **Pinyin:** yú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with rising tone
- **yú** sounds like **"yoo?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone: Say it like you're excited to see a fish: **"yú?"** —
that's the upward, questioning tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 鱼 (yú) - "fish"
- 金鱼 (jīn yú) - "goldfish"
- 鱼类 (yú lèi) - "fish (category)"
- 钓鱼 (diào yú) - "fishing"
- 鲤鱼 (lǐ yú) - "carp"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a fish **jumping up** out of the water — that upward motion matches the rising tone of 鱼!
