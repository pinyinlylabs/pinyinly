**🗣️ Pronunciation of 印 (yìn)**

- **Pinyin:** yìn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ìn** sounds like **"een"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **yìn** sounds like **"yeen!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a sharp **falling** tone: Start high and drop sharply down, like stamping
firmly: **"yìn!"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 印 (yìn) - "print; seal; stamp"
- 印象 (yìn xiàng) - "impression"
- 打印 (dǎ yìn) - "print"
- 复印 (fù yìn) - "photocopy"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

印 means "print" — when you **stamp** something, you press down with a sharp, decisive motion, like
the falling tone!
