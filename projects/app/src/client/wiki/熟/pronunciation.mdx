**🗣️ Pronunciation of 熟 (shú)**

- **Pinyin:** shú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a rising tone
- **shú** sounds like **"shoo"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Cooked?" when checking if food is ready — **"shú?"** — that questioning
upward inflection is the second tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 熟 (shú) - "ripe; cooked; familiar"
- 熟人 (shú rén) - "acquaintance; familiar person"
- 成熟 (chéng shú) - "mature; ripe"
- 熟悉 (shú xī) - "familiar with"

**💡 Special Note:**

熟 can also be pronounced **shóu** (second tone) in some contexts, especially in northern dialects,
but **shú** is the standard pronunciation.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When checking if fruit is ripe, you ask "Ready?" with a rising tone — just like the second tone
pattern in 熟!
