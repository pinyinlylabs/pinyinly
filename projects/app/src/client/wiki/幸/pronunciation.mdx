**🗣️ Pronunciation of 幸 (xìng)**

- **Pinyin:** xìng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **ìng** sounds like **"eeng"** but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **xìng** sounds like **"sheeng"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** is a **sharp falling** tone, like giving a firm command:

Say it like declaring good **fortune**: **"xìng!"** — that decisive drop is the **fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 幸 (xìng) - "fortunate; lucky"
- 幸福 (xìng fú) - "happiness; well-being"
- 幸运 (xìng yùn) - "lucky; fortunate"
- 不幸 (bù xìng) - "unfortunate; misfortune"
- 幸好 (xìng hǎo) - "fortunately; luckily"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **幸** as declaring good **fortune** with that strong, decisive **fourth tone** confidence!
