**🗣️ Pronunciation of 拍 (pāi)**

- **Pinyin:** pāi
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pat" (with a puff of air)
- **āi** sounds like **"eye"** but held steady and high
- **pāi** sounds like **"pie"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high, steady** tone:

Keep your voice high and level: **"pāi"** — like singing a sustained high note.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 拍 (pāi) - "to clap; to pat; to take (a photo)"
- 拍手 (pāi shǒu) - "to clap hands; to applaud"
- 拍照 (pāi zhào) - "to take a photo"
- 拍电影 (pāi diàn yǐng) - "to make a movie"
- 拍子 (pāi zi) - "beat; rhythm; racket"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sound of **clapping** hands has that sharp, clear quality — just like the crisp, high tone of
**pāi**.
