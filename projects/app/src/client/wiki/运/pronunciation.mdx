**🗣️ Pronunciation of 运 (yùn)**

- **Pinyin:** yùn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Move!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ùn** sounds like **"oon"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling tone
- **yùn** sounds like **"yoon!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a command to transport something: **"yùn!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone
pattern of **yùn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 运动 (yùn dòng) - "sports; exercise; movement"
- 运输 (yùn shū) - "transportation"
- 运气 (yùn qì) - "luck; fortune"
- 运用 (yùn yòng) - "to use; to apply"
- 运行 (yùn xíng) - "to operate; to run"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

运 means **to move/transport** — pronounce it with a decisive **yùn** like commanding something to
"move!"
