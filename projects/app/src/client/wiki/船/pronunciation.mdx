**🗣️ Pronunciation of 船 (chuán)**

- **Pinyin:** chuán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking about a journey

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "choose"
- **uán** sounds like **"wan"** in "want", but with second tone → rises up
- **chuán** sounds like **"chwan"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise smoothly, like asking **"Going by boat?"**: **"chuán?"** — that upward motion.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 船 (chuán) - "ship; boat; vessel"
- 小船 (xiǎo chuán) - "small boat"
- 大船 (dà chuán) - "big ship"
- 划船 (huá chuán) - "to row a boat"
- 船只 (chuán zhī) - "vessels; ships"
- 轮船 (lún chuán) - "steamship"

**💡 Size Note:**

船 (chuán) typically refers to larger vessels compared to 舟 (zhōu). It's the modern word for ships
and boats of various sizes.
