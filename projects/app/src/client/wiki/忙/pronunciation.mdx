**🗣️ Pronunciation of 忙 (máng)**

- **Pinyin:** máng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Mahng?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "may"
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with second tone → rising up
- **máng** sounds like **"mahng?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking if someone is busy: **"máng?"** — that's the questioning tone pattern of
**máng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 忙 (máng) - "busy"
- 很忙 (hěn máng) - "very busy"
- 忙碌 (máng lù) - "busy; bustling"
- 帮忙 (bāng máng) - "to help; to lend a hand"
- 连忙 (lián máng) - "hurriedly; hastily"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"mahng?"** with a questioning rise — when asking if someone is **busy**, you naturally
raise your voice like the second tone!
