Indicates something or someone that is not included.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                        |
| -------------- | --------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | yǐ wài                      |
| Core meaning   | except; besides; apart from |
| Part of speech | preposition                 |
| Tone           | 3rd + 4th tone              |

## Usage Examples

- **除了他以外** (chú le tā yǐ wài) - "except for him"
- **这些以外** (zhè xiē yǐ wài) - "besides these"
- **工作以外** (gōng zuò yǐ wài) - "apart from work"
- **意料之外** (yì liào zhī wài) - "beyond expectation"
- **此外** (cǐ wài) - "besides; in addition"

## Grammar Patterns

以外 typically:

- **With 除了**: 除了...以外 - "except for..."
- **Exclusion**: 他们以外的人 - "people other than them"
- **Addition**: 这个以外还有 - "besides this there's also"
- **Boundary setting**: 规定以外 - "outside the regulations"

## Cultural Context

以外 reflects Chinese categorical thinking:

- **Clear boundaries**: 以外 helps define what belongs and what doesn't
- **Group definition**: Important for understanding in-group vs. out-group
- **Systematic organization**: 以外 used to organize information and people
- **Exception handling**: 以外 allows for nuanced categorization
- **Inclusive exclusion**: Even exclusion (以外) can be done respectfully
