**🗣️ Pronunciation of 状 (zhuàng)**

- **Pinyin:** zhuàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **uàng** sounds like **"wahng"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **zhuàng** sounds like **"jwahng!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're describing a definite form: **"zhuàng!"** — that sharp, decisive drop is the
**fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 状 (zhuàng) - "form; shape; condition"
- 状况 (zhuàng kuàng) - "situation; condition"
- 状态 (zhuàng tài) - "state; condition"
- 形状 (xíng zhuàng) - "shape; form"
- 症状 (zhèng zhuàng) - "symptom"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **状** as definitively describing a shape: **"zhuàng!"** — that sharp emphasis matches the
**fourth tone**!
