**🗣️ Pronunciation of 双 (shuāng)**

- **Pinyin:** shuāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **shu** like **"shoe"** but more rounded
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"**, held steady and high
- **shuāng** sounds like **"shwahng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high flat** tone:

Say it steadily and evenly: **"shuāng"** — that's the consistent, high pattern of **shuāng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 双方 (shuāng fāng) - "both sides"
- 双手 (shuāng shǒu) - "both hands"
- 双重 (shuāng chóng) - "double"
- 双胞胎 (shuāng bāo tāi) - "twins"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **双** as a **pair** — the steady first tone represents the balance and equality of two
things together!
