**🗣️ Pronunciation of 长**

长 has **two different pronunciations** depending on its meaning:

**📍 cháng (second tone) - "long (length)"**

- **Pinyin:** cháng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair" but with tongue curled back
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** in "song" but with a rising tone
- **cháng** sounds like **"chahng?"** with a questioning rise

**📍 zhǎng (third tone) - "to grow, leader"**

- **Pinyin:** zhǎng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **ǎng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with the third tone → dip down and rise up
- **zhǎng** sounds like **"jahng"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

**cháng** (second tone) = **rising** ↗️ like something getting longer and longer **zhǎng** (third
tone) = **fall-then-rise** ↘️↗️ like growth going up and down, then up

**📝 Usage Guide:**

**长 (cháng) - "long (length)":**

- 很**长** (hěn cháng) - "very long"
- **长**城 (cháng chéng) - "Great Wall" (literally "long wall")
- **长**度 (cháng dù) - "length"
- **长**期 (cháng qī) - "long-term"

**长 (zhǎng) - "to grow, leader":**

- **长**大 (zhǎng dà) - "to grow up"
- 队**长** (duì zhǎng) - "team leader"
- 校**长** (xiào zhǎng) - "principal"
- **长**的很快 (zhǎng de hěn kuài) - "grows very fast"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**cháng** (long) = rises steadily ↗️ like measuring length **zhǎng** (grow) = dips then rises ↘️↗️
like a plant growing
