**🗣️ Pronunciation of 夬 (guài)**

- **Pinyin:** guài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uài** sounds like **"why"**, but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **guài** sounds like **"gwhy!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a definitive statement: **"guài!"** — that's the sharp, decisive drop of
the **fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 夬 (guài) - "parted" (rare standalone usage)
- 决 (jué) - contains the 夬 component, meaning "decide"
- 快 (kuài) - contains the 夬 component, meaning "fast"

**💡 Special Notes:**

夬 is primarily used as a component in other characters rather than as a standalone word. It
represents the concept of "parted" or "divided" and appears in characters like 决 (decide)
and 快 (fast). While you may not encounter 夬 alone in everyday conversation, understanding its
pronunciation helps with recognizing it in compound characters.

**📍 Character Component:**

As a radical component, 夬 often relates to concepts of separation, decision, or swiftness in the
characters it appears in.
