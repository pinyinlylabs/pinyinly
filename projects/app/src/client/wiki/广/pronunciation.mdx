**🗣️ Pronunciation of 广 (yǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** yǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǎn** sounds like **"yahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Third tone: ˇ)**

The **third tone** is a **fall–then-rise** tone, like being thoughtful:

Say it like contemplating something **broad**: **"yǎn..."** — that thoughtful dip-and-rise is the
**third tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 广 (yǎn) - "broad; wide"
- 广阔 (guǎng kuò) - "broad; vast" (note: different pronunciation when in compounds)

**📍 Special Note:**

广 has two main pronunciations:

- **yǎn** (third tone) - when used alone meaning "broad"
- **guǎng** (third tone) - in most compound words like 广场, 广告

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **广** as contemplating something **broad** with that thoughtful **third tone** quality!
