**🗣️ Pronunciation of 闻 (wén)**

- **Pinyin:** wén
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **én** sounds like **"en"** in "pen" but with a rising tone
- **wén** sounds like **"when"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up: **"wén?"** — like asking "Smell?" when you detect something.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 闻 (wén) - "smell, hear, news"
- 新闻 (xīn wén) - "news"
- 闻到 (wén dào) - "smell (something)"
- 听闻 (tīng wén) - "hear (news/information)"

**📝 Special Notes:**

闻 has multiple related meanings:

- **To smell** (detect odors)
- **To hear** (receive information)
- **News** (information heard/received)

The character contains the door radical 门, suggesting that news and smells both "come through" to
us.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you **smell** something or hear **news**, you might react with a rising "Wén?" of curiosity!
