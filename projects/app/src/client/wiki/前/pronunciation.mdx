**🗣️ Pronunciation of 前 (qián)**

- **Pinyin:** qián
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but with more air)
- **ián** sounds like **"yen"**, but with second tone → rising
- **qián** sounds like **"chyen?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're pointing ahead and asking "over there?": **"qián?"** — that's the tone pattern of
**qián**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 前 (qián) - "front; before"
- 前面 (qián miàn) - "in front; ahead"
- 前天 (qián tiān) - "day before yesterday"
- 以前 (yǐ qián) - "before; previously"
- 前进 (qián jìn) - "advance; move forward"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

前 means "before/front" — imagine the **rising** tone as looking ahead to the future or what's in
front!
