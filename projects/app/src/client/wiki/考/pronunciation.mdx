**🗣️ Pronunciation of 考 (kǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** kǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when thinking

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "kind"
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "how", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **kǎo** sounds like **"cow"** with a contemplative dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about a difficult test: **"kǎo..."** — that's the contemplative tone
pattern of **kǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 考 (kǎo) - "test, examine"
- 考试 (kǎo shì) - "exam, test"
- 考虑 (kǎo lǜ) - "consider, think over"
- 考生 (kǎo shēng) - "test taker, examinee"
- 参考 (cān kǎo) - "reference, consult"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When facing a test, you think deeply about it — use that contemplative third tone for **kǎo**!
