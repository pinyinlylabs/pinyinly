**🗣️ Pronunciation of 金 (jīn)**

- **Pinyin:** jīn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **īn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen", but held steady and high
- **jīn** sounds like **"jeen"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high flat** tone:

Keep your voice high and steady throughout: **"jīn"** — like saying a musical note.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 金 (jīn) - "gold"
- 金牌 (jīn pái) - "gold medal"
- 金钱 (jīn qián) - "money"
- 金属 (jīn shǔ) - "metal"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **gold** as precious and valuable — keep your voice high and steady like a pure golden
note!
