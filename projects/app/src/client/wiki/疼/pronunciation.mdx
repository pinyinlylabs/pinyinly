**🗣️ Pronunciation of 疼 (téng)**

- **Pinyin:** téng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top" but **stronger and more aspirated** — with a clear puff of air
- **éng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung" but with a **rising tone**
- **téng** sounds like **"tung?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) **rises** like asking a question:

Say it like you're asking "Does it hurt?": **"téng?"** — that's the rising energy of **téng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 疼 (téng) - "pain; ache"
- 疼痛 (téng tòng) - "pain; ache"
- 头疼 (tóu téng) - "headache"
- 肚子疼 (dù zi téng) - "stomachache"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

疼 means "pain" — the rising second tone sounds like someone's voice going up when they're in pain:
**"Ow, it hurts!"**
