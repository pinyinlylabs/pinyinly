**🗣️ Pronunciation of 活 (huó)**

- **Pinyin:** huó
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uó** sounds like **"wo"** in "wall", but with second tone → rise up
- **huó** sounds like **"hwo?"** with a rising inflection

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"huó?"** — that's the tone pattern of **huó**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 活 (huó) - "alive/living"
- 活动 (huó dòng) - "activity"
- 生活 (shēng huó) - "life/lifestyle"
- 活跃 (huó yuè) - "active/lively"
- 活着 (huó zhe) - "living/alive"
- 干活儿 (gàn huó r) - "work/do work"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

活 (huó) contains the water radical (氵) on the left — water is essential for life, so it makes
sense that this character means "alive"!
