**🗣️ Pronunciation of 桥 (qiáo)**

- **Pinyin:** qiáo
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheap" but with more air
- **iáo** sounds like **"yow"** with a rising tone
- **qiáo** sounds like **"chyow?"** with an upward inflection

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're pointing up at an arched bridge: **"qiáo?"** — with that upward curve.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 桥 (qiáo) - "bridge"
- 大桥 (dà qiáo) - "big bridge"
- 石桥 (shí qiáo) - "stone bridge"
- 过桥 (guò qiáo) - "cross the bridge"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **qiáo** as the rising arch of a bridge — the rising tone follows the bridge's upward
curve!
