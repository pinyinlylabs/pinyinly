**🗣️ Pronunciation of 鼠 (shǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** shǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **shǔ** sounds like **"shoo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Start low, dip even lower, then rise up: **"shǔ..."** — imagine the sound you make when you're
thinking about something.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 鼠 (shǔ) - "mouse"
- 老鼠 (lǎo shǔ) - "mouse/rat"
- 鼠标 (shǔ biāo) - "computer mouse"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a mouse squeaking with that characteristic **dip-then-rise** sound: **"shǔ"**!
