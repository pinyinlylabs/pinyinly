**🗣️ Pronunciation of 教 (jiāo)**

- **Pinyin:** jiāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but softer, closer to "zy")
- **iāo** sounds like **"ee-ow"**, but held steady and high
- **jiāo** sounds like **"jee-ow"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 教 (jiāo) - "teach"
- 教书 (jiāo shū) - "teach (books)"
- 教学 (jiāo xué) - "teaching"
- 老师 (lǎo shī) teaches → 教师 (jiāo shī) - "teacher"
- 教室 (jiāo shì) - "classroom"

**🎧 Special Note:**

教 has another pronunciation **jiào** (fourth tone) meaning "to call" or "religion", but for the
meaning "teach", it's always **jiāo** (first tone).

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The steady first tone reflects the patience and consistency needed for **teaching**.
