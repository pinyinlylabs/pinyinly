**🗣️ Pronunciation of 眼 (yǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** yǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǎn** sounds like **"yahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're focusing and examining: **"yǎn..."** — that contemplative tone pattern of
**yǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 眼 (yǎn) - "eye"
- 眼睛 (yǎn jīng) - "eyes"
- 眼镜 (yǎn jìng) - "glasses; eyeglasses"
- 眼前 (yǎn qián) - "in front of one's eyes"
- 闭眼 (bì yǎn) - "close one's eyes"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"yahn"** with a contemplative dip-then-rise — when you use your **eyes** to examine
something, you naturally focus with that thoughtful tone!
