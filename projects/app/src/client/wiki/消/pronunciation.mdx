**🗣️ Pronunciation of 消 (xiāo)**

- **Pinyin:** xiāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (but softer, more like "hs")
- **iāo** sounds like **"yow"** in "yowl", but with first tone → high and steady
- **xiāo** sounds like **"shee-ow"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady note: **"xiāo"** — that's the tone pattern of **xiāo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 消 (xiāo) - "vanish/disappear"
- 消息 (xiāo xi) - "news/information"
- 消费 (xiāo fèi) - "consume/consumption"
- 消失 (xiāo shī) - "disappear/vanish"
- 消化 (xiāo huà) - "digest/digestion"
- 取消 (qǔ xiāo) - "cancel"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

消 (xiāo) contains the water radical (氵) on the left. Think of water evaporating and vanishing into
thin air — just like how 消 means to vanish or disappear!
