**🗣️ Pronunciation of 欢 (huān)**

- **Pinyin:** huān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uān** sounds like **"wahn"** held steady and high
- **huān** sounds like **"hwahn"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're expressing joy steadily: **"huāaaaan"** — keep it high and level.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 欢迎 (huān yíng) - "welcome"
- 欢乐 (huān lè) - "joy; happiness"
- 喜欢 (xǐ huan) - "to like" (note: becomes neutral tone in compound)
- 欢庆 (huān qìng) - "to celebrate"
- 欢声 (huān shēng) - "cheerful sounds"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

欢 means "happy" — express that joy with a **high, steady** tone like sustained laughter: "huāaan!"
