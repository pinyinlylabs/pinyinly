**🗣️ Pronunciation of 住 (zhù)**

- **Pinyin:** zhù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" (but with tongue curled back)
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **zhù** sounds like **"joo"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like deciding where to settle: **"zhù!"** — that sharp drop down shows the fourth tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 住 (zhù) - "live; reside"
- 住房 (zhù fáng) - "housing"
- 住院 (zhù yuàn) - "be hospitalized"
- 居住 (jū zhù) - "reside; dwell"
- 住址 (zhù zhǐ) - "address"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"joo"** with a firm falling tone — like making a decisive choice about where to live!
