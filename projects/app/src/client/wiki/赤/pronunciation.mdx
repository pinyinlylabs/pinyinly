**🗣️ Pronunciation of 赤 (chì)**

- **Pinyin:** chì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Chee!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church" (with tongue curled back)
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **chì** sounds like **"chee!"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're pointing out something bright red: **"chì!"** — that's the sharp falling tone of
**chì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 赤色 (chì sè) - "red color"
- 赤道 (chì dào) - "equator"
- 赤脚 (chì jiǎo) - "barefoot"
- 赤字 (chì zì) - "deficit; red ink"
