**🗣️ Pronunciation of 这 (zhè)**

- **Pinyin:** zhè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"This!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "joke" but with tongue curled back
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling tone
- **zhè** sounds like **"juh!"** with a sharp drop and curled tongue

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like pointing at something specific: **"zhè!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone pattern of
**zhè**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 这个 (zhè ge) - "this one"
- 这里 (zhè lǐ) - "here"
- 这样 (zhè yàng) - "this way; like this"
- 这些 (zhè xiē) - "these"
- 这边 (zhè biān) - "this side; over here"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

这 means **this** — pronounce it with a definitive **zhè** like pointing directly at something!
