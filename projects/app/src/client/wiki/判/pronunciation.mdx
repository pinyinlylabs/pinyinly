**🗣️ Pronunciation of 判 (pàn)**

- **Pinyin:** pàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pan"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"**, but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **pàn** sounds like **"pahn!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a firm judgment: **"pàn!"** — that's the tone pattern of **pàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 判 (pàn) - "judge; decide"
- 判断 (pàn duàn) - "judge; determine"
- 裁判 (cái pàn) - "referee; judge"
- 审判 (shěn pàn) - "trial; judgment"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

判 means "judge" — imagine the decisive **falling** tone as a judge making a final verdict!
