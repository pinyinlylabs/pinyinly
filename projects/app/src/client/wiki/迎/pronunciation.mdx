**🗣️ Pronunciation of 迎 (yíng)**

- **Pinyin:** yíng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **íng** sounds like **"eeng"** but with second tone → starts low and rises up
- **yíng** sounds like **"yeeng?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like welcoming someone with surprise: **"yíng?"** — that's the rising tone pattern of
**yíng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 欢迎 (huān yíng) - "welcome"
- 迎接 (yíng jiē) - "to meet; to greet"
- 迎来 (yíng lái) - "to welcome; to usher in"
- 迎面 (yíng miàn) - "head-on; face to face"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

迎 means **to receive/welcome** — pronounce it with a rising **yíng** like greeting someone with
pleasant surprise!
