**🗣️ Pronunciation of 影 (yǐng)**

- **Pinyin:** yǐng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǐng** sounds like **"ing"** in "ring" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǐng** sounds like **"ying"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're pondering a shadow's shape: **"yǐng..."** — that's the contemplative tone pattern
of **yǐng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 影 (yǐng) - "shadow; reflection"
- 影响 (yǐng xiǎng) - "to influence; influence"
- 影片 (yǐng piàn) - "film; movie"
- 影视 (yǐng shì) - "film and television"
- 电影 (diàn yǐng) - "movie; film"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"ying"** with a thoughtful dip-rise — when you notice a **shadow**, you often pause and
look at it contemplatively, just like the third tone's pattern!
