**🗣️ Pronunciation of 检 (jiǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** jiǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **iǎn** sounds like **"yen"** with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **jiǎn** sounds like **"jyen"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully examining something: **"jiǎn..."** — that's the contemplative tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 检 (jiǎn) - "inspect"
- 检查 (jiǎn chá) - "check/inspect"
- 体检 (tǐ jiǎn) - "medical exam"
- 检验 (jiǎn yàn) - "test/examine"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **jiǎn** as carefully examining something — the dip-and-rise tone is like looking down,
then up with understanding!
