**🗣️ Pronunciation of 挂 (guà)**

- **Pinyin:** guà
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uà** sounds like **"wah"**, but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **guà** sounds like **"gwah!"** with a sharp downward fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm instruction: **"guà!"** — start high and drop down decisively.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 挂 (guà) - "to hang; to suspend"
- 挂电话 (guà diàn huà) - "to hang up the phone"
- 挂号 (guà hào) - "to register (at hospital)"
- 挂在 (guà zài) - "to hang on/at"
- 挂念 (guà niàn) - "to worry about; to be concerned"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **hanging up** the phone with a decisive click — that's the sharp, falling tone of **guà**.
