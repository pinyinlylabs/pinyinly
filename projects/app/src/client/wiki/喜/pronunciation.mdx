**🗣️ Pronunciation of 喜 (xǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** xǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep" but softer and more forward
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up
- **xǐ** sounds like **"shee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're expressing pleasant surprise: **"xǐ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **xǐ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 喜 (xǐ) - "to like; joy; happiness"
- 喜欢 (xǐ huan) - "to like; to enjoy"
- 喜悦 (xǐ yuè) - "joy; delight"
- 惊喜 (jīng xǐ) - "pleasant surprise"
- 欢喜 (huān xǐ) - "happy; joyful"

**💡 Special Notes:**

喜 represents joy, happiness, and liking something. It's commonly used in 喜欢 (xǐ huan), one of the
most important verbs for expressing preferences. The character often appears in celebrations and
positive contexts, symbolizing good fortune and happiness in Chinese culture.
