**🗣️ Pronunciation of 念 (niàn)**

- **Pinyin:** niàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "new"
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **niàn** sounds like **"nyen!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a sharp **falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly stating something: **"niàn!"** — that's the tone pattern of **niàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 念 (niàn) - "read aloud"
- 念书 (niàn shū) - "study, read books"
- 念头 (niàn tou) - "thought, idea"
- 纪念 (jì niàn) - "commemorate"
- 概念 (gài niàn) - "concept"

**💡 Special Notes:**

念 can mean both "to read aloud" and "to think about/miss". The pronunciation remains **niàn**
(fourth tone) in all contexts.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Read aloud** with the sharp fourth tone — like a teacher firmly saying "Read!" to students!
