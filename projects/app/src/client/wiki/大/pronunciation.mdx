**🗣️ Pronunciation of 大 (dà)**

- **Pinyin:** dà
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"Dah!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **à** sounds like **"ah"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **dà** sounds like **"dah!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're emphatically describing something impressive: **"dà!"** — that strong, definitive
tone is the **fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 大 (dà) - "big; large; great"
- 大家 (dà jiā) - "everyone"
- 大学 (dà xué) - "university"
- 大人 (dà rén) - "adult"
- 多大 (duō dà) - "how big/old"
- 长大 (zhǎng dà) - "to grow up"

**💡 Special Notes:**

大 is a fundamental adjective meaning "big" or "large." It's also used in many compound words to
indicate importance, seniority, or scale. The character is essential for describing size, age (in
some contexts), and significance.
