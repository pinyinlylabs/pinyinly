**🗣️ Pronunciation of 司 (sī)**

- **Pinyin:** sī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "sun"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **sī** sounds like **"see"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **steady and high**:

Say it like you're holding a long musical note — that's the energy of **sī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 司机 (sī jī) - "driver"
- 司令 (sī lìng) - "commander"
- 公司 (gōng sī) - "company"
- 司法 (sī fǎ) - "judicial"
