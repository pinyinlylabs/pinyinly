**🗣️ Pronunciation of 块 (kuài)**

- **Pinyin:** kuài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Fast!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **uài** sounds like **"why"**, but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **kuài** sounds like **"kwy!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're counting money decisively: **"kuài!"** — that's the falling tone of **kuài**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 块 (kuài) - "yuan (currency); piece; chunk"
- 一块钱 (yī kuài qián) - "one yuan"
- 几块 (jǐ kuài) - "a few yuan"
- 石块 (shí kuài) - "stone; rock"
- 肥皂块 (féi zào kuài) - "bar of soap"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **块** (yuan/piece) like stating a price firmly: **"kuài!"** — that's the **fourth tone**
definitive declaration!

**💰 Currency Note:**

块 is the colloquial term for 元 (yuán), the official Chinese currency unit.
