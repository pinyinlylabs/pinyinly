**🗣️ Pronunciation of 照 (zhào)**

- **Pinyin:** zhào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "jump" but with tongue tip curled back
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with a sharp falling tone
- **zhào** sounds like **"jow!"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Say it like you're commanding someone to "Look!" when taking a photo — **"zhào!"** — that sharp,
decisive drop is the fourth tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 照 (zhào) - "photograph; to shine on"
- 照片 (zhào piàn) - "photograph; picture"
- 照相 (zhào xiàng) - "to take a photo"
- 照顾 (zhào gù) - "to take care of"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When taking a photo, you say "Smile!" with authority and decisiveness — just like the fourth tone's
sharp falling pattern in 照!
