**🗣️ Pronunciation of 远 (yuǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** yuǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **uǎn** sounds like **"wahn"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yuǎn** sounds like **"ywahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like pondering distance: **"yuǎn..."** — that's the contemplative dip-and-rise tone pattern
of **yuǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 远方 (yuǎn fāng) - "distant place; far away"
- 远程 (yuǎn chéng) - "long-distance; remote"
- 遥远 (yáo yuǎn) - "distant; far-off"
- 远离 (yuǎn lí) - "to be far from; to keep away from"
- 长远 (cháng yuǎn) - "long-term; long-range"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

远 means **far** — pronounce it with a contemplative **yuǎn** like thinking about something in the
distance!
