**🗣️ Pronunciation of 费 (fèi)**

- **Pinyin:** fèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Fay!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fee"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "hay" but with a sharp falling tone
- **fèi** sounds like **"fay!"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're announcing a cost: **"fèi!"** — that's the sharp falling tone of **fèi**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 费用 (fèi yòng) - "cost; expense"
- 学费 (xué fèi) - "tuition fee"
- 浪费 (làng fèi) - "waste; squander"
- 消费 (xiāo fèi) - "consume; spend"
