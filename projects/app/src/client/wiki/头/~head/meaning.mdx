Head; the uppermost part of the body; leader; beginning; first.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                   |
| -------------- | ---------------------- |
| Pinyin         | tóu                    |
| Core meaning   | head; top; leader      |
| Part of speech | noun, measure word     |
| Tone           | second tone (rising)   |

## Visual Breakdown

头 combines **big person + dot** to represent the head with its prominent features.

| Component | Visual Description                                      |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **大**    | Big person (大) - shows a person with arms outstretched |
| **丶**    | Dot (丶) - represents the head or most important part   |

## Mnemonic

Think of 头 as **a person with their head clearly marked**:

- The big person component (大) shows the human body
- The dot (丶) sits at the top, marking the location of the head
- Like drawing a simple stick figure and putting a dot for the head
- Shows the head as the most important part that needs to be marked
- The dot emphasizes the head's central role in the body

This creates the image: **the head marked as the most important part of a person**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

头 represents **the head, leadership, and beginnings**. It's used:

1. **Body part**: 我的头很疼 (wǒ de tóu hěn téng) - "my head hurts"
2. **Beginning**: 从头开始 (cóng tóu kāi shǐ) - "start from the beginning"
3. **Leader**: 老板是头 (lǎo bǎn shì tóu) - "the boss is the head"
4. **First**: 头一次 (tóu yī cì) - "the first time"

## Examples

- **头发** (tóu fa) - "hair" (literally "head hair")
- **头脑** (tóu nǎo) - "mind; brain; intelligence"
- **头部** (tóu bù) - "head (anatomical region)"
- **回头** (huí tóu) - "turn around; look back"
- **点头** (diǎn tóu) - "nod one's head"
- **摇头** (yáo tóu) - "shake one's head"

## Leadership and Authority

头 indicating leadership:

- **头儿** (tóur) - "boss; leader" (colloquial)
- **头目** (tóu mù) - "ringleader; chief"
- **领头** (lǐng tóu) - "lead; take the lead"
- **出头** (chū tóu) - "get ahead; emerge as leader"

## Beginning and First

头 expressing sequence:

- **头等** (tóu děng) - "first-class; top grade"
- **头号** (tóu hào) - "number one; top priority"
- **头班车** (tóu bān chē) - "first bus/train"
- **年头** (nián tóu) - "beginning of the year"

## Physical Descriptions

头 in body-related terms:

- **低头** (dī tóu) - "lower one's head; bow"
- **抬头** (tái tóu) - "raise one's head; look up"
- **转头** (zhuǎn tóu) - "turn one's head"
- **埋头** (mái tóu) - "bury one's head; focus intently"

## Measure Word Usage

头 as a measure word:

- **一头牛** (yī tóu niú) - "one (head of) cow"
- **几头猪** (jǐ tóu zhū) - "several pigs"
- **三头大象** (sān tóu dà xiàng) - "three elephants"
- Used specifically for large animals and livestock

## Common Expressions

- **头头是道** (tóu tóu shì dào) - "logical and well-organized"
- **头重脚轻** (tóu zhòng jiǎo qīng) - "top-heavy; unbalanced"
- **焦头烂额** (jiāo tóu làn é) - "terribly busy; in a terrible mess"
- **头疼医头，脚疼医脚** - "treat symptoms, not the root cause"

## Directional Usage

头 in spatial contexts:

- **前头** (qián tou) - "front; ahead"
- **后头** (hòu tou) - "back; behind"
- **上头** (shàng tou) - "above; superior"
- **里头** (lǐ tou) - "inside"

## Time and Sequence

头 in temporal expressions:

- **头些天** (tóu xiē tiān) - "the first few days"
- **头年** (tóu nián) - "the first year"
- **头半年** (tóu bàn nián) - "first half of the year"
- **到头来** (dào tóu lái) - "in the end; finally"

## Modern Usage

头 in contemporary contexts:

- **头像** (tóu xiàng) - "profile picture; avatar"
- **头条** (tóu tiáo) - "headline; top news"
- **镜头** (jìng tóu) - "camera lens"
- **源头** (yuán tóu) - "source; origin"

## Grammar Functions

- **Noun**: 头很大 (tóu hěn dà) - "big head"
- **Measure word**: 两头牛 (liǎng tóu niú) - "two cows"
- **Prefix**: 头等舱 (tóu děng cāng) - "first-class cabin"
- **Suffix**: 年头 (nián tou) - "period of years"

## Cultural Context

头 in Chinese culture represents:

- **智慧中心** (zhì huì zhōng xīn) - The center of wisdom and intelligence
- **尊严象征** (zūn yán xiàng zhēng) - Symbol of dignity and respect
- **领导能力** (lǐng dǎo néng lì) - Leadership and authority
- **思维器官** (sī wéi qì guān) - The organ of thought and consciousness

## Traditional Medicine

头 in health contexts:

- **头痛** (tóu tòng) - "headache"
- **头晕** (tóu yūn) - "dizzy; lightheaded"
- **头火** (tóu huǒ) - "head heat" (traditional medicine concept)
- **清头目** (qīng tóu mù) - "clear the head and brighten the eyes"

The character represents both the physical head as the body's command center and the metaphorical concept of leadership, beginning, and primacy in various contexts.
