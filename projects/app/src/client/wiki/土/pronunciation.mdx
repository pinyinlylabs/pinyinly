**🗣️ Pronunciation of 土 (tǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** tǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "too", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **tǔ** sounds like **"too"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about soil: **"tǔ..."** — that thoughtful dip-and-rise is the **third
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 土 (tǔ) - "earth; soil; land"
- 土地 (tǔ dì) - "land; territory"
- 土豆 (tǔ dòu) - "potato"
- 本土 (běn tǔ) - "native; local"
- 土著 (tǔ zhù) - "indigenous"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **土** (earth) like contemplating the ground beneath you — that's the **third tone**
thoughtful quality!
