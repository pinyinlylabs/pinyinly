**🗣️ Pronunciation of 办 (bàn)**

- **Pinyin:** bàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Ban!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "ban"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"** in "john" but with a sharp falling tone
- **bàn** sounds like **"bahn!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving a firm instruction: **"bàn!"** — that's the energy of
fourth tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 办 (bàn) - "handle"
- 办法 (bàn fǎ) - "method, way"
- 办公室 (bàn gōng shì) - "office"
- 办理 (bàn lǐ) - "to handle, process"
- 举办 (jǔ bàn) - "to organize, hold"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **bàn** as "ban" with authority — you're handling or managing something with decisiveness!
