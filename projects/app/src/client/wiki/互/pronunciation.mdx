**🗣️ Pronunciation of 互 (hù)**

- **Pinyin:** hù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Stop!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **hù** sounds like **"hoo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** is a sharp **falling** tone, like making a firm statement:

Say it like you're establishing a strong connection: **"hù!"** — that's the decisive tone of **hù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 互 (hù) - "mutual; each other"
- 互相 (hù xiāng) - "mutually; each other"
- 互联网 (hù lián wǎng) - "internet"
- 互动 (hù dòng) - "interaction"
- 互助 (hù zhù) - "mutual help"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **互** as two sides firmly connecting to each other — that's the **fourth tone**
decisiveness!

**📍 Usage Note:**

互 primarily appears in compound words expressing mutual or reciprocal relationships. It's rarely
used as a standalone word in modern Chinese.
