**🗣️ Pronunciation of 饿 (è)**

- **Pinyin:** è
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: "Uh!"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **è** sounds like **"uh"** in "duh" but with a sharp falling tone
- **è** is pronounced like **"eh"** with a decisive downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone. Start high and drop down sharply, like expressing
frustration or urgency.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 饿 (è) - "hungry"
- 饿了 (è le) - "I'm hungry"
- 挨饿 (ái è) - "to go hungry, to starve"
- 饿死 (è sǐ) - "to starve to death"
- 又饿又渴 (yòu è yòu kě) - "both hungry and thirsty"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you're really hungry and urgently declare "I need food!" - that sharp, demanding tone matches
the falling fourth tone of **è**.
