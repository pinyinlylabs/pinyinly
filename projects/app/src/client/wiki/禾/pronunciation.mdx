**🗣️ Pronunciation of 禾 (hé)**

- **Pinyin:** hé
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **é** sounds like **"eh"** but with second tone → rise up
- **hé** sounds like **"heh"** with a rising tone, like asking "huh?"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking: **"hé?"** — that's the questioning rise of **hé**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 禾 (hé) - "grain; rice plant"
- 禾苗 (hé miáo) - "rice seedlings"

**💡 Cultural Note:**

禾 is the radical for grain/rice and appears in many characters related to farming and crops. It
represents one of the most important food sources in Chinese culture and historically symbolizes
prosperity and sustenance.
