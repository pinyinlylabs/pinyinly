**🗣️ Pronunciation of 田 (tián)**

- **Pinyin:** tián
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"What?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea"
- **ián** sounds like **"yen"** in "yen", but with second tone → rising upward
- **tián** sounds like **"tyen?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 田 (tián) - "field"
- 田地 (tián dì) - "farmland"
- 田野 (tián yě) - "field; countryside"
- 水田 (shuǐ tián) - "rice field"
- 油田 (yóu tián) - "oil field"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Second tone rises like asking **"Field?"** - your voice goes **up** throughout **tián**.
