**🗣️ Pronunciation of 散 (sàn)**

- **Pinyin:** sàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"**, but with a sharp falling tone
- **sàn** sounds like **"sahn!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 散 (sàn) - "scatter, disperse"
- 散步 (sàn bù) - "take a walk"
- 散开 (sàn kāi) - "spread out"
- 解散 (jiě sàn) - "dissolve, disband"
- 分散 (fēn sàn) - "disperse"

**🎧 Special Note:**

散 has another pronunciation **sǎn** (third tone) meaning "loose" or "casual", but for
"scatter/disperse", it's **sàn** (fourth tone).

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sharp falling tone mimics things **scattering** or spreading out quickly and decisively.
