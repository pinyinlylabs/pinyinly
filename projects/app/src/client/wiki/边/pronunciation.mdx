**🗣️ Pronunciation of 边 (biān)**

- **Pinyin:** biān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bet"
- **iān** sounds like **"ee-ahn"** but with first tone → steady and high
- **biān** sounds like **"bee-ahn"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like pointing to something: **"biān!"** — that's the steady, high tone pattern of **biān**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 边界 (biān jiè) - "border; boundary"
- 旁边 (páng biān) - "beside; next to"
- 这边 (zhè biān) - "this side; over here"
- 那边 (nà biān) - "that side; over there"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

边 means **edge/border** — pronounce it with a clear, defined **biān** like drawing a straight line!
