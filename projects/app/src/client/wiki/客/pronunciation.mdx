**🗣️ Pronunciation of 客 (kè)**

- **Pinyin:** kè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** in "meh", but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **kè** sounds like **"keh!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like formally announcing a visitor: **"kè!"** — that decisive drop shows respect for the
**guest**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 客 (kè) - "guest; visitor; customer"
- 客人 (kè rén) - "guest; visitor"
- 顾客 (gù kè) - "customer; client"
- 游客 (yóu kè) - "tourist; visitor"
- 旅客 (lǚ kè) - "passenger; traveler"
- 请客 (qǐng kè) - "to invite someone (to dinner)"
- 做客 (zuò kè) - "to be a guest; visit"
- 客观 (kè guān) - "objective; impartial"

**📍 Cultural Note:**

The concept of 客 is very important in Chinese culture. Treating guests well is a fundamental value,
and there are many customs around hospitality.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Guest** — the sharp falling fourth tone is like respectfully bowing to welcome a **guest**!
