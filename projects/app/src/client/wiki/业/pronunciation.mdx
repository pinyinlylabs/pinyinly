**🗣️ Pronunciation of 业 (yè)**

- **Pinyin:** yè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** in "bed", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **yè** sounds like **"yeh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a firm statement: **"yè!"** — that's the tone pattern of **yè**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 业 (yè) - "profession; industry; business"
- 专业 (zhuān yè) - "major; profession; specialty"
- 事业 (shì yè) - "career; undertaking"
- 工业 (gōng yè) - "industry"
- 商业 (shāng yè) - "business; commerce"

**💡 Special Notes:**

业 relates to professions, industries, or careers. It's commonly used in business and educational
contexts to describe fields of work, academic majors, or industrial sectors. The character is
essential for discussing professional and economic topics.
