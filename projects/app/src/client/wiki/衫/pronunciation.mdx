**🗣️ Pronunciation of 衫 (shān)**

- **Pinyin:** shān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like holding a steady note: **"Ahhhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shirt"
- **ān** sounds like **"ahn"** but held steady and high
- **shān** sounds like **"shahn"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 衫 (shān) - "shirt; unlined upper garment"
- 衬衫 (chèn shān) - "shirt; dress shirt"
- 汗衫 (hàn shān) - "undershirt; T-shirt"
- 毛衫 (máo shān) - "sweater"
- 背心衫 (bèi xīn shān) - "vest"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Remember "shirt" starts with "sh" just like 衫, and the steady first tone is like confidently saying
"This is my shirt!" in a clear, steady voice.
