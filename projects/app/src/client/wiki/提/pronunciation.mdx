**🗣️ Pronunciation of 提 (tí)**

- **Pinyin:** tí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea" (but with a slight puff of air)
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with second tone → rise up
- **tí** sounds like **"tee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking **"tea?"** with a rising intonation — that's the rising tone of **tí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 提 (tí) - "carry; lift"
- 提高 (tí gāo) - "improve; raise"
- 提问 (tí wèn) - "ask a question"
- 提前 (tí qián) - "in advance; ahead of time"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"提"** as **"tea?"** with a rising tone — like asking if you should lift or carry the
tea!
