**🗣️ Pronunciation of 导 (dǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** dǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "how" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **dǎo** sounds like **"dow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"dǎo..."** — that's the tone pattern of **dǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 导 (dǎo) - "direct"
- 导演 (dǎo yǎn) - "director"
- 指导 (zhǐ dǎo) - "guide"
- 领导 (lǐng dǎo) - "leader"
- 导师 (dǎo shī) - "mentor"
