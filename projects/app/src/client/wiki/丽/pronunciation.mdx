**🗣️ Pronunciation of 丽 (lì)**

- **Pinyin:** lì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Lee!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "like"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **lì** sounds like **"lee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it decisively and with emphasis: **"lì!"** — like you're declaring something beautiful!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 丽 (lì) - "beautiful; pretty"
- 美丽 (měi lì) - "beautiful"
- 华丽 (huá lì) - "magnificent; gorgeous"
- 艳丽 (yàn lì) - "gorgeous; colorful"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of something so **beautiful** that you exclaim **"Lee!"** with that sharp, admiring fourth
tone — like seeing a stunning view!
