**🗣️ Pronunciation of 钅 (jīn)**

- **Pinyin:** jīn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **īn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen", but held steady and high
- **jīn** sounds like **"jeen"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high flat** tone:

Keep your voice high and steady throughout: **"jīn"** — like saying a musical note.

**📝 Note on Usage:**

钅 is the **metal radical** — it appears on the left side of characters related to metals and
metallic objects. It's the simplified form of 金 when used as a radical.

**📝 Common Examples:**

Characters that use the 钅 radical:

- 钟 (zhōng) - "bell"
- 钱 (qián) - "money"
- 铁 (tiě) - "iron"
- 银 (yín) - "silver"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Remember that 钅 has the same pronunciation as 金 (gold) — both are **"jīn"** with first tone,
representing the metallic connection!
