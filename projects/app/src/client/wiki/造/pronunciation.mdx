**🗣️ Pronunciation of 造 (zào)**

- **Pinyin:** zào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"z"** in "zero"
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with a sharp falling tone
- **zào** sounds like **"zow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying **"Build!"** with determination: **"zào!"** — that's the
tone pattern of **zào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 造 (zào) - "to make; to build"
- 造成 (zào chéng) - "to cause; to result in"
- 制造 (zhì zào) - "to manufacture"
- 建造 (jiàn zào) - "to construct"
- 创造 (chuàng zào) - "to create"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Making** or building something requires decisive action — the falling tone shows that determined
effort to create!
