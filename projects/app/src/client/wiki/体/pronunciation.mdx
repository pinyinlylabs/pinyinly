**🗣️ Pronunciation of 体 (tǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** tǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **tǐ** sounds like **"tee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking thoughtfully: **"tǐ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **tǐ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 体 (tǐ) - "body"
- 身体 (shēn tǐ) - "body; health"
- 体重 (tǐ zhòng) - "body weight"
- 体验 (tǐ yàn) - "experience"
- 体育 (tǐ yù) - "physical education; sports"
