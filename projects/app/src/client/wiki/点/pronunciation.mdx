**🗣️ Pronunciation of 点 (diǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** diǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **iǎn** sounds like **"yen"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **diǎn** sounds like **"dyen"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're checking the time thoughtfully: **"diǎn..."** — that contemplative tone pattern
of **diǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 点 (diǎn) - "o'clock; point; dot"
- 一点 (yì diǎn) - "a little; one o'clock"
- 两点 (liǎng diǎn) - "two o'clock"
- 重点 (zhòng diǎn) - "key point; emphasis"
- 地点 (dì diǎn) - "location; place"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When checking what time it is, you thoughtfully say **"Hmm, what time...?"** — that contemplative
tone matches the third tone of **diǎn**!
