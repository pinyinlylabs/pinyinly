**🗣️ Pronunciation of 婚 (hūn)**

- **Pinyin:** hūn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ūn** sounds like **"oon"** in "moon" but held steady and high
- **hūn** sounds like **"hoon"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like you're solemnly discussing marriage: **"hūn"** — that steady, elevated tone is the
**first tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 婚 (hūn) - "marriage; wedding"
- 结婚 (jié hūn) - "to get married"
- 婚姻 (hūn yīn) - "marriage; matrimony"
- 婚礼 (hūn lǐ) - "wedding ceremony"
- 离婚 (lí hūn) - "to divorce"
- 新婚 (xīn hūn) - "newly married"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The first tone's steady high pitch is like the solemn, continuous tone used when discussing the
important topic of **marriage**!
