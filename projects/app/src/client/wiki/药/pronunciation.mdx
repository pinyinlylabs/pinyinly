**🗣️ Pronunciation of 药 (yào)**

- **Pinyin:** yào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow", but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **yào** sounds like **"yow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"yào!"** — that's the tone pattern of **yào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 药 (yào) - "medicine"
- 药店 (yào diàn) - "pharmacy"
- 药片 (yào piàn) - "pill"
- 吃药 (chī yào) - "take medicine"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **yào** as a doctor's firm command: "Take your medicine!" — the falling tone sounds
authoritative and urgent.
