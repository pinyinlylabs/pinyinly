**🗣️ Pronunciation of 伤 (shāng)**

- **Pinyin:** shāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shop"
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** (like "ah" + "ng"), but with first tone → high and steady
- **shāng** sounds like **"shahng"** held high and flat

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like expressing pain steadily: **"shāng~~"** — that high, level pitch shows the first tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 伤 (shāng) - "injury; wound"
- 伤心 (shāng xīn) - "sad; heartbroken"
- 受伤 (shòu shāng) - "get injured"
- 伤害 (shāng hài) - "harm; damage"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"shahng"** said steadily — like describing an injury in a calm, matter-of-fact way.
