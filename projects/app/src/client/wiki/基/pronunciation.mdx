**🗣️ Pronunciation of 基 (jī)**

- **Pinyin:** jī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Jee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but more forward tongue position)
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **jī** sounds like **"jee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like holding a steady, high musical note: **"jī"** — maintain that high pitch throughout.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 基 (jī) - "foundation"
- 基本 (jī běn) - "basic"
- 基础 (jī chǔ) - "foundation"
- 基金 (jī jīn) - "fund"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **基** as a solid **foundation** — the steady, high first tone represents something **firm
and unchanging** at the base!
