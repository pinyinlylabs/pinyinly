**🗣️ Pronunciation of 包 (bāo)**

- **Pinyin:** bāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Baooo"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bow"
- **āo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but held steady and high
- **bāo** sounds like **"bow"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like announcing something you're carrying: **"bāo!"** — keep that high, steady tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 包 (bāo) - "bag"
- 包子 (bāo zi) - "steamed bun"
- 书包 (shū bāo) - "school bag"
- 皮包 (pí bāo) - "leather bag"
- 钱包 (qián bāo) - "wallet"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **bāo** as "bow" said steadily — like you're proudly presenting a bag or package!
