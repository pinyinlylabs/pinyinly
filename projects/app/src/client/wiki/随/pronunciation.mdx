**🗣️ Pronunciation of 随 (suí)**

- **Pinyin:** suí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **uí** sounds like **"way"** but with second tone → rising pitch
- **suí** sounds like **"sway?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Should I follow?": **"suí?"** — that inquiring, rising tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 随 (suí) - "follow, go along with"
- 随便 (suí biàn) - "casual, whatever"
- 随时 (suí shí) - "anytime, at any time"
- 跟随 (gēn suí) - "follow, accompany"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of asking someone if you should follow them - that questioning second tone matches the
uncertainty of **随** meaning "follow"!
