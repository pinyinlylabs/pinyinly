**🗣️ Pronunciation of 古 (gǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** gǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **gǔ** sounds like **"goo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're pondering something old: **"gǔ..."** — that's the thoughtful tone pattern of
**gǔ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 古 (gǔ) - "ancient; old"
- 古代 (gǔ dài) - "ancient times"
- 古老 (gǔ lǎo) - "ancient; old"
- 考古 (kǎo gǔ) - "archaeology"
- 复古 (fù gǔ) - "retro; vintage"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"goo"** with a contemplative rise — when thinking about **ancient** times, we often
reflect thoughtfully, just like the third tone's pattern!
