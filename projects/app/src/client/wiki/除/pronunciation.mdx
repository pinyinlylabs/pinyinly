**🗣️ Pronunciation of 除 (chú)**

- **Pinyin:** chú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church" (aspirated sound)
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "food" but with second tone → rising pitch
- **chú** sounds like **"choo?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Remove this?": **"chú?"** — that inquiring, rising tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 除 (chú) - "eliminate, remove, except"
- 除了 (chú le) - "except for, besides"
- 消除 (xiāo chú) - "eliminate, remove"
- 除非 (chú fēi) - "unless"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of questioning whether to remove something - that rising second tone matches the consideration
involved in **除** meaning "eliminate/remove"!
