**🗣️ Pronunciation of 飞 (fēi)**

- **Pinyin:** fēi
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: "Fay"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fun"
- **ēi** sounds like **"ay"** in "say" but held at a high, steady pitch
- **fēi** sounds like **"fay"** at a constant high tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**. Keep your voice at the same high pitch throughout, like
singing a sustained musical note.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 飞 (fēi) - "to fly"
- 飞机 (fēi jī) - "airplane"
- 飞行 (fēi xíng) - "flight, aviation"
- 起飞 (qǐ fēi) - "to take off"
- 飞速 (fēi sù) - "at flying speed"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of an airplane flying smoothly at cruising altitude - that steady, level flight matches the
flat first tone of **fēi**.
