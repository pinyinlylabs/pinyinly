**🗣️ Pronunciation of 城 (chéng)**

- **Pinyin:** chéng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Cheng?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church" (but with tongue curled back)
- **éng** sounds like **"ung"** with a rising tone
- **chéng** sounds like **"chung"** with an upward inflection

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start mid-level and rise up like asking **"chéng?"** — like you're questioning "city?"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 城 (chéng) - "city"
- 城市 (chéng shì) - "city"
- 长城 (cháng chéng) - "Great Wall"
- 进城 (jìn chéng) - "enter the city"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the rising tone like walking **up** to the city gates — the pitch rises as you approach the
towering **城**!
