**🗣️ Pronunciation of 谈 (tán)**

- **Pinyin:** tán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea"
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** but with a rising tone
- **tán** sounds like **"tahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is **rising**, like when you're starting a conversation:

Say it like you're suggesting **"Let's talk?"**: **"tán?"** — that upward inflection is the **second
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 谈 (tán) - "talk; discuss; chat"
- 谈话 (tán huà) - "conversation; talk"
- 谈判 (tán pàn) - "negotiate; negotiation"
- 交谈 (jiāo tán) - "converse; chat"
- 谈论 (tán lùn) - "discuss; talk about"
- 访谈 (fǎng tán) - "interview"

**💡 Usage Note:**

谈 (tán) is used for various types of verbal communication, from casual chats to formal discussions
and negotiations. It emphasizes the interactive nature of conversation.
