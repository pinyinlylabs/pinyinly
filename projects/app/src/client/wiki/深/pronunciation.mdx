**🗣️ Pronunciation of 深 (shēn)**

- **Pinyin:** shēn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **ēn** sounds like **"un"** in "fun", but with first tone → high and steady
- **shēn** sounds like **"shun"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady note: **"shēn"** — that's the tone pattern of **shēn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 深 (shēn) - "deep"
- 深度 (shēn dù) - "depth"
- 深刻 (shēn kè) - "profound/deep"
- 深入 (shēn rù) - "go deep into"
- 深色 (shēn sè) - "dark color"
- 深夜 (shēn yè) - "late at night"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

深 (shēn) contains the water radical (氵) on the left. Think of deep water — the deeper you go into
water, the more profound and mysterious it becomes, just like the meaning of 深!
