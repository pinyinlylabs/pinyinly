**🗣️ Pronunciation of 后 (hòu)**

- **Pinyin:** hòu
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **òu** sounds like **"oh"** in "oh no" but with a sharp falling tone
- **hòu** sounds like **"hoh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving a command — that's the energy of **hòu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 后面 (hòu miàn) - "behind, back"
- 后来 (hòu lái) - "later, afterwards"
- 然后 (rán hòu) - "then, after that"
- 后天 (hòu tiān) - "day after tomorrow"
- 前后 (qián hòu) - "front and back"
