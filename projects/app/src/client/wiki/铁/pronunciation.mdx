**🗣️ Pronunciation of 铁 (tiě)**

- **Pinyin:** tiě
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea"
- **iě** sounds like **"yeah"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **tiě** sounds like **"t-yeah"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're being thoughtful: **"tiě..."** — dip down then come back up.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 铁 (tiě) - "iron"
- 铁路 (tiě lù) - "railway"
- 地铁 (dì tiě) - "subway, metro"
- 钢铁 (gāng tiě) - "steel"

**📝 Special Notes:**

The character 铁 contains the metal radical 钅, which makes sense since iron is a fundamental metal.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **iron** being strong but flexible — like the **third tone** that bends down then comes
back up!
