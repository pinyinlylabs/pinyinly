The eastern region or part of a location; eastern part; eastern region; east side.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                         |
| -------------- | ---------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | dōng bù                      |
| Core meaning   | eastern part; eastern region |
| Part of speech | noun                         |
| Tone           | 1st + 4th                    |

## Usage Examples

- **中国东部** (Zhōng guó dōng bù) - "eastern China"
- **城市东部** (chéng shì dōng bù) - "eastern part of the city"
- **东部地区** (dōng bù dì qū) - "eastern region"
- **东部沿海** (dōng bù yán hǎi) - "eastern coastal area"
- **东部时间** (dōng bù shí jiān) - "Eastern Time"

## Grammar Patterns

- **...的东部** - "the eastern part of..."
- **位于东部** - "located in the eastern part"
- **东部地区** - "eastern region/area"
- **在东部** - "in the eastern part"

## Regional Directions

- **东部** (dōng bù) - eastern part
- **西部** (xī bù) - western part
- **南部** (nán bù) - southern part
- **北部** (běi bù) - northern part
- **中部** (zhōng bù) - central part

## Cultural Context

东部 holds special significance in Chinese geography:

- **Economic development**: Eastern China is traditionally more developed economically
- **Coastal advantage**: 东部 often refers to coastal regions with major ports
- **Population centers**: Most major Chinese cities are in the eastern regions
- **Historical importance**: Eastern areas have been centers of Chinese civilization
- **Modern significance**: 东部 regions often lead in technology and business development
