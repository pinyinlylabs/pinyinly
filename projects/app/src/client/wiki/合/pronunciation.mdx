**🗣️ Pronunciation of 合 (hé)**

- **Pinyin:** hé
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **é** sounds like **"uh"** but with a rising tone
- **hé** sounds like **"huh?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) **rises** like asking a question:

Say it like you're asking "What?" — that's the energy of **hé**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 合作 (hé zuò) - "cooperate"
- 合适 (hé shì) - "suitable"
- 合理 (hé lǐ) - "reasonable"
- 联合 (lián hé) - "unite"
- 合同 (hé tóng) - "contract"
