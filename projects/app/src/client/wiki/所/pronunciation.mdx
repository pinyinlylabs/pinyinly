**🗣️ Pronunciation of 所 (suǒ)**

- **Pinyin:** suǒ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **uǒ** sounds like **"woh"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **suǒ** sounds like **"swoh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're contemplating a place: **"suǒ..."** — that's the thoughtful tone pattern of
**suǒ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 所 (suǒ) - "place; that which"
- 所以 (suǒ yǐ) - "so; therefore"
- 所有 (suǒ yǒu) - "all; every"
- 厕所 (cè suǒ) - "toilet; restroom"
- 场所 (chǎng suǒ) - "place; location"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Place** — the dipping third tone suggests looking around contemplatively at a location!
