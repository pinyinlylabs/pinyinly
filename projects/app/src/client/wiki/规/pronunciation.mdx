**🗣️ Pronunciation of 规 (guī)**

- **Pinyin:** guī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uī** sounds like **"way"** but with first tone → high and steady
- **guī** sounds like **"gway"** with a high, flat pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Say it like you're stating a rule clearly: **"guī"** — maintain that steady, authoritative high
pitch.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 规 (guī) - "rules"
- 规定 (guī dìng) - "rule; regulation"
- 规范 (guī fàn) - "standard; norm"
- 法规 (fǎ guī) - "laws and regulations"
- 校规 (xiào guī) - "school rules"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 规 as establishing "rules" — when you announce rules, you speak with steady authority, just
like the unwavering first tone!
