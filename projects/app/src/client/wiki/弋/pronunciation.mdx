**🗣️ Pronunciation of 弋 (yì)**

- **Pinyin:** yì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **yì** sounds like **"yee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a decisive statement: **"yì!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone of
**yì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 弋 (yì) - "shoot/hunt" (archaic usage, mainly in compounds)
- 弋阳 (Yì yáng) - place name
- 游弋 (yóu yì) - "cruise/patrol"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

弋 originally meant "shoot with a bow" — the sharp fourth tone sounds like the decisive action of
**shooting** an arrow!
