**🗣️ Pronunciation of 速 (sù)**

- **Pinyin:** sù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **sù** sounds like **"soo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying **"Fast!"** urgently: **"sù!"** — that's the tone pattern
of **sù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 速 (sù) - "quick; speed"
- 速度 (sù dù) - "speed; velocity"
- 快速 (kuài sù) - "fast; rapid"
- 高速 (gāo sù) - "high speed"
- 加速 (jiā sù) - "to accelerate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Speed** and quickness are urgent concepts — the sharp falling tone captures that sense of urgency
and swiftness!
