**🗣️ Pronunciation of 思 (sī)**

- **Pinyin:** sī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with first tone → steady high pitch
- **sī** sounds like **"see"** with a steady high tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Say it like you're thinking steadily and clearly: **"sī..."** — that steady, contemplative tone
matches the meaning of "think."

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 思 (sī) - "think; thought"
- 思想 (sī xiǎng) - "thought; thinking; ideology"
- 思考 (sī kǎo) - "think; ponder; reflect"
- 意思 (yì si) - "meaning; intention"
- 思念 (sī niàn) - "miss; long for"

**💡 Special Notes:**

思 is often used in compound words related to thinking and mental processes. The steady first tone
gives it a contemplative, intellectual quality.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The steady, high first tone **sī** sounds like a long, thoughtful "hmm..." — perfect for the
character meaning "think"!
