**🗣️ Pronunciation of 布 (bù)**

- **Pinyin:** bù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Boo!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "boot" with a sharp falling tone
- **bù** sounds like **"boo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're firmly describing fabric: **"bù!"** — that's the decisive energy of **bù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 布 (bù) - "cloth; fabric"
- 公布 (gōng bù) - "announce; publish"
- 宣布 (xuān bù) - "announce; declare"
- 分布 (fēn bù) - "distribute; spread"
- 帆布 (fān bù) - "canvas"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **布** as spreading out fabric or information — that sharp **fourth tone** shows the
definitive action of laying out or announcing!

**📍 Note on Usage:**

布 has two main meanings:

- **Cloth/fabric** (noun) - as in 这块布 (this piece of cloth)
- **To announce/spread** (verb) - as in 公布 (to announce publicly)
