**🗣️ Pronunciation of 效 (xiào)**

- **Pinyin:** xiào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (but more like a soft "hs" sound)
- **iào** sounds like **"ee-ow"**, but with a sharp falling tone
- **xiào** sounds like **"shee-ow!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 效 (xiào) - "result, effect"
- 效果 (xiào guǒ) - "effect, result"
- 有效 (yǒu xiào) - "effective"
- 效力 (xiào lì) - "effectiveness"
- 模仿 (mó fǎng) + 效 → 效仿 (xiào fǎng) - "imitate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sharp fourth tone emphasizes the decisive nature of achieving a **result** or **effect**.
