**🗣️ Pronunciation of 压 (yā)**

- **Pinyin:** yā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ā** sounds like **"ah"** in "father", but held steady and high
- **yā** sounds like **"yah"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high flat** tone:

Say it with steady confidence, like stating a fact: **"yā"** — that's the even, high pattern of
**yā**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 压力 (yā lì) - "pressure"
- 血压 (xuè yā) - "blood pressure"
- 压迫 (yā pò) - "oppress"
- 镇压 (zhèn yā) - "suppress"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **压** as applying steady **pressure** — the flat first tone mirrors the constant, even
force being applied!
