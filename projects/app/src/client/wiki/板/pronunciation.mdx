**🗣️ Pronunciation of 板 (bǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** bǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "ban"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"**, but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **bǎn** sounds like **"bahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 板 (bǎn) - "board; plank"
- 黑板 (hēi bǎn) - "blackboard"
- 老板 (lǎo bǎn) - "boss"
- 键盘 (jiàn pǎn) - "keyboard"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

A **board** bends slightly when pressed — that's the dip-then-rise third tone of **bǎn**!
