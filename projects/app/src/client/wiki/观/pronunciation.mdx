**🗣️ Pronunciation of 观 (guān)**

- **Pinyin:** guān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **u** like **"oo"** in "book"
- **ān** sounds like **"ahn"** with first tone → high and steady
- **guān** sounds like **"gwahn"** with a high, flat pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Say it like you're announcing something clearly: **"guān"** — maintain that steady, high pitch
throughout.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 观 (guān) - "observe"
- 观看 (guān kàn) - "watch"
- 观察 (guān chá) - "observe"
- 参观 (cān guān) - "visit; tour"
- 客观 (kè guān) - "objective"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 观 as "observing" from a high vantage point — you maintain that steady, elevated
perspective, just like the high flat first tone!
