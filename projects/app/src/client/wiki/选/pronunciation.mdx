**🗣️ Pronunciation of 选 (xuǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** xuǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" but more like **"hs"**
- **uǎn** sounds like **"wan"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **xuǎn** sounds like **"shwan"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking carefully before deciding: **"xuǎn..."** — that's the tone pattern of
**xuǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 选 (xuǎn) - "to choose; to select"
- 选择 (xuǎn zé) - "to choose; choice"
- 选手 (xuǎn shǒu) - "contestant; player"
- 选举 (xuǎn jǔ) - "election"
- 挑选 (tiāo xuǎn) - "to pick and choose"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Choosing** requires careful thought — the dipping and rising tone reflects the mental process of
weighing options!
