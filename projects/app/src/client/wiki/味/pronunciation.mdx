**🗣️ Pronunciation of 味 (wèi)**

- **Pinyin:** wèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Way!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way", but with a sharp falling tone
- **wèi** sounds like **"way!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're being decisive: **"wèi!"** — let your voice drop sharply.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 味 (wèi) - "taste; flavor"
- 味道 (wèi dào) - "taste; flavor"
- 口味 (kǒu wèi) - "taste preference"
- 调味 (tiáo wèi) - "season; flavor"
- 香味 (xiāng wèi) - "fragrance; aroma"
- 甜味 (tián wèi) - "sweet taste"
- 酸味 (suān wèi) - "sour taste"
