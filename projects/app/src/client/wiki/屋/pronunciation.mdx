**🗣️ Pronunciation of 屋 (wū)**

- **Pinyin:** wū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady hum: **"Woooo"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "wood"
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "soon" but held steady and high
- **wū** sounds like **"woo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and steady**:

Stay **high and flat** throughout — like humming contentedly while sitting in a cozy house:
**"wūūū"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 屋 (wū) - "house; room"
- 屋子 (wū zi) - "room; house"
- 房屋 (fáng wū) - "house; building"
- 屋顶 (wū dǐng) - "roof"
- 屋里 (wū lǐ) - "inside the house"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The steady, high tone sounds like the satisfied "woooo" you might make when entering a warm, cozy
house on a cold day!
