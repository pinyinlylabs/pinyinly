**🗣️ Pronunciation of 冬 (dōng)**

- **Pinyin:** dōng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Oooh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "day"
- **ōng** sounds like **"ong"** but held steady and high
- **dōng** sounds like **"dong"** with steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — like the long, cold season: **"dōōōng"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 冬 (dōng) - "winter"
- 冬天 (dōng tiān) - "winter; wintertime"
- 过冬 (guò dōng) - "to spend the winter"
- 冬季 (dōng jì) - "winter season"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Winter is long and steady — just like the high, flat tone of **"dōng"** that stretches on like the
cold season!
