**🗣️ Pronunciation of 变 (biàn)**

- **Pinyin:** biàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "big"
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** (currency) but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **biàn** sounds like **"bee-yen"** with a sharp drop on the second part

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're announcing a transformation: **"biàn!"** — that's the definitive tone pattern of
**biàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 变 (biàn) - "change; transform"
- 变化 (biàn huà) - "change; transformation"
- 变成 (biàn chéng) - "become; turn into"
- 改变 (gǎi biàn) - "change; alter"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"bee + yen"** with a sharp drop — when things **change**, it's often sudden and decisive
like the fourth tone!
