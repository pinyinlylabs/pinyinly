**🗣️ Pronunciation of 酉 (yǒu)**

- **Pinyin:** yǒu
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǒu** sounds like **"oh"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǒu** sounds like **"yo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"yǒu..."** — that's the tone pattern of **yǒu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 酉 (yǒu) - "wine vessel; tenth earthly branch"
- 酉时 (yǒu shí) - "5-7 PM (traditional time period)"

**💡 Note:**

酉 is primarily used as a radical in characters related to alcohol and fermentation, such as:

- 酒 (jiǔ) - "alcohol; wine"
- 醉 (zuì) - "drunk"
- 醋 (cù) - "vinegar"

It also represents one of the twelve earthly branches in the traditional Chinese calendar system.
