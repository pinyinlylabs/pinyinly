**🗣️ Pronunciation of 刻 (kè)**

- **Pinyin:** kè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "kept"
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** in "get", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **kè** sounds like **"keh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're marking time precisely: **"kè!"** — that's the tone pattern of **kè**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 刻 (kè) - "quarter of an hour; moment"
- 一刻 (yí kè) - "a quarter of an hour; 15 minutes"
- 时刻 (shí kè) - "moment; time"
- 刻苦 (kè kǔ) - "hardworking; diligent"
- 深刻 (shēn kè) - "deep; profound"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

刻 means "quarter hour" — imagine the sharp **falling** tone as the precise tick of a clock marking
time!
