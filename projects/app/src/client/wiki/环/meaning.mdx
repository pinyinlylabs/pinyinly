## Core Meaning & Usage

环 represents **circular shapes, surrounding, and complete enclosure**. It's used:

1. **Jewelry**: 戒环 (jièhuán) - "ring (jewelry)"
2. **Environment**: 环境 (huánjìng) - "environment; surroundings"
3. **Circulation**: 环球 (huánqiú) - "around the globe"
4. **Linking**: 环节 (huánjié) - "link; connection"

- **环形** (huánxíng) - "ring-shaped; circular"

## Cultural Context

环 is fundamental in Chinese concepts of wholeness and completion. In feng shui and traditional
Chinese philosophy, circular shapes (环形) represent harmony and completeness. 环境 (environment)
emphasizes how surroundings completely encircle and influence us. The ring shape symbolizes eternal
cycles and continuity in Chinese culture.
