**🗣️ Pronunciation of 猪 (zhū)**

- **Pinyin:** zhū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "zoo" but held steady and high
- **zhū** sounds like **"joo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like calling a pig: **"zhū!"** — keep that high, steady tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 猪 (zhū) - "pig"
- 小猪 (xiǎo zhū) - "piglet; little pig"
- 猪肉 (zhū ròu) - "pork"
- 野猪 (yě zhū) - "wild boar"
- 猪年 (zhū nián) - "Year of the Pig"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **猪** as steadily calling out to a pig: **"zhū!"** — that high, sustained call matches the
**first tone**!
