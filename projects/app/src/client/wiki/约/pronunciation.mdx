**🗣️ Pronunciation of 约 (yuē)**

- **Pinyin:** yuē
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **uē** sounds like **"way"** but shorter, with first tone → steady and high
- **yuē** sounds like **"yweh"** held steady and high

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're making a definite appointment: **"yuē"** — steady and confident.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 约 (yuē) - "appointment"
- 大约 (dà yuē) - "approximately"
- 节约 (jié yuē) - "to save/economize"
- 约定 (yuē dìng) - "agreement"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When making an **appointment**, you speak with certainty and confidence — just like the steady, high
first tone of **yuē**!
