**🗣️ Pronunciation of 咖 (kā)**

- **Pinyin:** kā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "kite", but with a strong aspiration
- **ā** sounds like **"ah"** in "father", but held steady and high
- **kā** sounds like **"kah"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady musical note: **"kā..."** — keep the pitch high and level.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 咖 (kā) - "coffee" (used in compound words)
- 咖啡 (kā fēi) - "coffee"
- 咖啡店 (kā fēi diàn) - "coffee shop"
- 咖啡馆 (kā fēi guǎn) - "cafe"
- 咖啡豆 (kā fēi dòu) - "coffee beans"

**💡 Usage Note:**

咖 (kā) is **rarely used alone** — it's almost always part of the compound word 咖啡 (kā fēi)
meaning "coffee". The character itself doesn't have an independent meaning in modern Chinese.
