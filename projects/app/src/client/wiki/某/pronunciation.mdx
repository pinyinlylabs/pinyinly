**🗣️ Pronunciation of 某 (mǒu)**

- **Pinyin:** mǒu
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "more"
- **ǒu** sounds like **"oh"**, but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **mǒu** sounds like **"moh"** with a dip-then-rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 某 (mǒu) - "certain; some"
- 某人 (mǒu rén) - "someone; a certain person"
- 某个 (mǒu gè) - "some; certain"
- 某天 (mǒu tiān) - "one day; some day"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When we say "a **certain** someone," we're being thoughtful and uncertain — that's the hesitant
dip-then-rise third tone of **mǒu**!
