**🗣️ Pronunciation of 好 (hǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** hǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ǎo** sounds a bit like **"how"**, but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up.
- **hǎo** sounds like **"how"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"hǎo..."** — that's the tone pattern of **hǎo**.

**📝 Note on Usage:**

好 (hǎo) has two main meanings:

- **好 (hǎo)** - "good; nice; friendly" (third tone)
- **好 (hǎo)** - "to like; to enjoy" (third tone)

Both meanings use the same pronunciation **hǎo** with third tone. However, in some compound words
expressing preference/fondness, 好 may be pronounced with fourth tone (hào), such as in:

- **好奇** (hào qí) - "curious"
- **爱好** (ài hào) - "hobby"
- **好学** (hào xué) - "studious"
