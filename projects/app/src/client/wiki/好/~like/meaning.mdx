To like; to enjoy; to be fond of; to prefer.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                        |
| -------------- | --------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | hǎo                         |
| Core meaning   | like; enjoy; be fond of     |
| Part of speech | verb                        |
| Tone           | third tone (falling-rising) |

## Usage Examples

Note: While 好 as "to like" is pronounced hǎo (third tone), it appears in compound words where it
may be pronounced hào (fourth tone):

- **好奇** (hào qí) - "curious; inquisitive"
- **爱好** (ài hào) - "hobby; interest; what one likes"
- **好学** (hào xué) - "studious; eager to learn"
- **癖好** (pǐ hào) - "quirk; personal preference"

## Grammar Note

This meaning of 好 (hǎo/hào) appears primarily in compound words rather than standalone. It forms
part of words that describe preferences, interests, and tendencies, reflecting deeper character
traits and inclinations. The pronunciation may vary between hǎo and hào depending on the specific
compound.
