**🗣️ Pronunciation of 游 (yóu)**

- **Pinyin:** yóu
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **óu** sounds like **"oh"** in "oh" but with second tone → rising up
- **yóu** sounds like **"yo?"** with a rising pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking someone if they want to go swimming: **"yóu?"** — that questioning, upward
tone pattern of **yóu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 游 (yóu) - "swim"
- 游泳 (yóu yǒng) - "swim; swimming"
- 游客 (yóu kè) - "tourist"
- 游戏 (yóu xì) - "game"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of asking someone **"You wanna swim?"** — the rising tone in **"You?"** matches the second
tone of **yóu**!
