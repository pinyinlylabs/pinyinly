**🗣️ Pronunciation of 答 (dá)**

- **Pinyin:** dá
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **á** sounds like **"ah"** in "father", but with second tone → rising
- **dá** sounds like **"dah?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start lower and rise up: **"dá?"** — like asking "Answer?" in a questioning tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 答 (dá) - "answer; reply"
- 答应 (dā ying) - "agree; promise"
- 回答 (huí dá) - "reply; answer"
- 答案 (dá àn) - "answer; solution"
- 答题 (dá tí) - "answer questions"

**💡 Special Notes:**

In 答应 (dā ying), the character 答 is pronounced with first tone **dā** instead of the usual second
tone **dá**.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"dah?"** with the rising tone — like asking "Is this the answer?" with curiosity!
