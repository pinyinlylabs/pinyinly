**🗣️ Pronunciation of 视 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **ì** sounds like **"er"** in "her" but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **shì** sounds like **"sher"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're focusing intently: **"shì!"** — that sharp drop shows the intensity of looking.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 视 (shì) - "look at"
- 电视 (diàn shì) - "television"
- 重视 (zhòng shì) - "attach importance to"
- 忽视 (hū shì) - "ignore; overlook"
- 影视 (yǐng shì) - "film and television"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 视 as "looking" with sharp focus — when you really concentrate your gaze, there's that
decisive intensity, just like the fourth tone's sharp fall!
