**🗣️ Pronunciation of 共 (gòng)**

- **Pinyin:** gòng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **òng** sounds like **"ohng"** with a sharp falling tone
- **gòng** sounds like **"gohng!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"gòng!"** — that's the tone pattern of **gòng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 共 (gòng) - "together; total"
- 共同 (gòng tóng) - "together; jointly"
- 公共 (gōng gòng) - "public; common"
- 一共 (yí gòng) - "altogether; in total"
- 共有 (gòng yǒu) - "to have jointly"

**💡 Special Notes:**

共 consistently uses the fourth tone (gòng) and means "together" or "shared." It's often used to
express collective action, totality, or sharing. Note the difference from 公 (gōng, first tone)
which means "public."
