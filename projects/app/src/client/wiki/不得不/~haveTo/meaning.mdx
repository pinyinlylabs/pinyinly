To have no other choice but to do something; must; have to; compelled to.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                           |
| -------------- | ------------------------------ |
| Pinyin         | bù dé bù                       |
| Core meaning   | have to; must; no choice but   |
| Part of speech | auxiliary verb phrase          |
| Tone           | fourth + second + fourth tone  |

## Visual Breakdown

不得不 combines negation with inability and repeated negation:

| Component | Visual Description                                                     |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **不**    | Not - negation marker                                                  |
| **得**    | Able to/permitted - represents ability or permission                   |
| **不**    | Not - second negation, creating double negative structure              |

## Mnemonic

Think of 不得不 as **"not able to not do"** (double negative = must do):

- 不 (not) + 得 (able to) + 不 (not) = "not able to not [do something]"
- Like being trapped with no escape route - you cannot avoid doing it
- When circumstances force you into action despite your preferences
- The situation gives you no alternative but to proceed

This creates the meaning: **compelled by circumstances to do something**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

不得不 expresses **unwilling necessity or forced action**. It's used for:

1. **Reluctant obligation**: 不得不工作 (bù dé bù gōngzuò) - "have to work"
2. **Forced circumstances**: 不得不离开 (bù dé bù líkāi) - "have no choice but to leave"
3. **Unwilling admission**: 不得不承认 (bù dé bù chéngrèn) - "have to admit"
4. **Compelled action**: 不得不说 (bù dé bù shuō) - "must say; have to say"

## Examples

- **不得不放弃** (bù dé bù fàngqì) - "have no choice but to give up"
- **不得不同意** (bù dé bù tóngyì) - "have to agree"
- **不得不回去** (bù dé bù huíqù) - "have to go back"
- **不得不相信** (bù dé bù xiāngxìn) - "have to believe"
- **不得不考虑** (bù dé bù kǎolǜ) - "have to consider"

## Emotional Tone

不得不 carries implications of:
- **Reluctance** - you'd prefer not to do this
- **External pressure** - circumstances force the action
- **Inevitability** - there's no realistic alternative
- **Resignation** - accepting what must be done

## Comparison with Other "Must" Words

- **不得不**: reluctant necessity (external pressure)
- **必须** (bìxū): definitive requirement (rule/law)
- **应该** (yīnggāi): moral obligation (should)
- **得** (děi): practical necessity (informal)

不得不 emphasizes the unwilling nature of necessary actions.
