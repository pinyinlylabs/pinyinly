**🗣️ Pronunciation of 省 (shěng)**

- **Pinyin:** shěng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **ěng** sounds like **"ung"** in "lung" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **shěng** sounds like **"shung"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully considering: **"shěng..."** — that contemplative tone pattern of
**shěng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 省 (shěng) - "province"
- 省会 (shěng huì) - "provincial capital"
- 河北省 (Hé běi shěng) - "Hebei Province"
- 广东省 (Guǎng dōng shěng) - "Guangdong Province"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"shung"** with a contemplative dip-then-rise — when thinking about which **province**
something is in, you naturally pause thoughtfully!
