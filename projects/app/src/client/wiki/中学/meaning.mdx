School for children usually from ages 11 to 18; secondary school; middle school; high school.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                            |
| -------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | zhōng xué                       |
| Core meaning   | secondary school; middle school |
| Part of speech | noun                            |
| Tone           | 1st + 2nd                       |

## Usage Examples

- **上中学** (shàng zhōng xué) - "attend middle school"
- **中学生** (zhōng xué shēng) - "middle school student"
- **中学教师** (zhōng xué jiào shī) - "middle school teacher"
- **重点中学** (zhòng diǎn zhōng xué) - "key middle school"
- **中学阶段** (zhōng xué jiē duàn) - "middle school stage"

## Grammar Patterns

- **在中学** - "in middle school"
- **中学的...** - "middle school's..."
- **中学 + noun** - middle school-related terms

## School System

- **小学** (xiǎo xué) - elementary school
- **中学** (zhōng xué) - middle/secondary school
- **高中** (gāo zhōng) - high school
- **大学** (dà xué) - university

## Cultural Context

中学 in Chinese education system:

- **Academic pressure**: 中学 years are crucial for college entrance preparation
- **Competitive environment**: Students face intense competition for good high schools
- **Character development**: 中学 is seen as formative years for personal growth
- **Social importance**: 中学 education is highly valued in Chinese society
- **Pathway to success**: Good 中学 performance is essential for university admission
- **Comprehensive education**: Covers academics, character building, and practical skills
