**🗣️ Pronunciation of 必 (bì)**

- **Pinyin:** bì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Bee!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **bì** sounds like **"bee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're insisting something is necessary: **"bì!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern of
**bì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 必 (bì) - "must; have to"
- 必须 (bì xū) - "must; have to"
- 必要 (bì yào) - "necessary; essential"
- 必然 (bì rán) - "inevitable; certain"
- 不必 (bù bì) - "need not; not necessary"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"bee!"** with a sharp fall — when something is **necessary** or a **must**, you often say
it with firm conviction, just like the fourth tone's pattern!
