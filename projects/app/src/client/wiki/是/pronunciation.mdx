**🗣️ Pronunciation of 是 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **shì** sounds like **"she!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like stating a fact definitively: **"shì!"** — that sharp drop is the fourth tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 是 (shì) - "to be; yes"
- 是的 (shì de) - "yes; that's right"
- 不是 (bù shì) - "no; is not"
- 就是 (jiù shì) - "exactly; precisely"
