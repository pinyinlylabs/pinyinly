**🗣️ Pronunciation of 酒 (jiǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** jiǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but softer, more like "zy" sound)
- **iǔ** sounds like **"yo"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **jiǔ** sounds like **"jyo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're savoring something: **"jiǔ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **jiǔ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 酒 (jiǔ) - "alcohol; alcoholic drink; wine"
- 酒店 (jiǔ diàn) - "hotel"
- 喝酒 (hē jiǔ) - "to drink alcohol"
- 白酒 (bái jiǔ) - "Chinese liquor"
- 红酒 (hóng jiǔ) - "red wine"
- 啤酒 (pí jiǔ) - "beer"

**💡 Note:**

酒 contains the 酉 (wine vessel) radical and 水 (water) component, representing fermented beverages.
It's a general term for all alcoholic drinks in Chinese.
