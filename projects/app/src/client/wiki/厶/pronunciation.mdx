**🗣️ Pronunciation of 厶 (sī)**

- **Pinyin:** sī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", held steady and high
- **sī** sounds like **"see"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high flat** tone:

Say it steadily and evenly: **"sī"** — that's the consistent, high pattern of **sī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 私人 (sī rén) - "private"
- 私立 (sī lì) - "private (institution)"
- 自私 (zì sī) - "selfish"
- 私密 (sī mì) - "private/secret"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **厶** as representing something **private** — the steady first tone suggests something
kept constant and hidden!
