An institution for educating children or providing specialized instruction; school; educational
facility.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                                     |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | xuéxiào                                  |
| Core meaning   | school; educational institution; academy |
| Part of speech | noun                                     |
| Tone           | xué (2nd), xiào (4th)                    |

## Word Breakdown

学校 combines concepts of learning and institutional correction to represent educational
institutions.

| Component | Meaning                                                        |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **学**    | Learn; study; education - representing knowledge acquisition   |
| **校**    | School; correct; verify - representing institutional structure |

Together they create: "learning institution" or "place for educational correction" - where students
learn and knowledge is verified.

## Core Meaning & Usage

学校 represents **educational institutions and formal learning environments**. It's used:

1. **Educational facility**: 上学校 (shàng xuéxiào) - "go to school"
2. **Institution type**: 小学校 (xiǎo xuéxiào) - "elementary school"
3. **Learning environment**: 学校生活 (xuéxiào shēnghuó) - "school life"
4. **Educational system**: 学校教育 (xuéxiào jiàoyù) - "school education"

## Examples

- **在学校** (zài xuéxiào) - "at school"
- **学校老师** (xuéxiào lǎoshī) - "school teacher"

- **私立学校** (sīlì xuéxiào) - "private school"
- **公立学校** (gōnglì xuéxiào) - "public school"

## Types of Schools

Different levels of 学校:

- **高中学校** (gāozhōng xuéxiào) - "high school"
- **大学校** (dà xuéxiào) - "university" (though 大学 is more common)

## Cultural Significance

学校 in Chinese culture represents:

**Educational Values:**

- **尊师重教** (zūn shī zhòng jiào) - Respect teachers and value education
- **学而时习之** (xué ér shí xí zhī) - Learning and practicing regularly
- **知识改变命运** (zhīshi gǎibiàn mìngyùn) - Knowledge changes destiny
- **教育兴国** (jiàoyù xīng guó) - Education strengthens the nation

**Social Institution:**

- **社会进步** (shèhuì jìnbù) - Engine of social progress
- **人才培养** (réncái péiyǎng) - Talent cultivation
- **文化传承** (wénhuà chuánchéng) - Cultural inheritance
- **公平机会** (gōngpíng jīhuì) - Equal opportunity platform

## Learning Notes

学校 is essential because:

- Fundamental institution in Chinese society and individual development
- Essential for discussing education, career, and social mobility
- Key to understanding Chinese cultural values about learning
- Important for family, social, and policy conversations
- Demonstrates how compound words express institutional concepts

学校 reflects the Chinese understanding that education requires both dedicated learning (学) and
institutional structure (校) to effectively transmit knowledge, verify understanding, and cultivate
human potential!
