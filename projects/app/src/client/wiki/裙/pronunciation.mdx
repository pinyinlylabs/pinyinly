**🗣️ Pronunciation of 裙 (qún)**

- **Pinyin:** qún
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" but with lips more rounded
- **ún** sounds like **"wun"** in "won" but with rising tone
- **qún** sounds like **"chwun?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 裙 (qún) - "skirt"
- 裙子 (qún zi) - "skirt"
- 连衣裙 (lián yī qún) - "dress"
- 短裙 (duǎn qún) - "short skirt"
- 长裙 (cháng qún) - "long skirt"
- 百褶裙 (bǎi zhě qún) - "pleated skirt"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The rising second tone is like the swishing sound a skirt makes when someone spins around - it
starts low and rises up gracefully!
