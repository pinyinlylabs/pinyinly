A female romantic partner; girlfriend.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info            |
| -------------- | --------------- |
| Pinyin         | nǚ péng yǒu     |
| Core meaning   | girlfriend      |
| Part of speech | noun            |
| Tone           | 3rd + 2nd + 3rd |

## Visual Breakdown

女朋友 combines **woman + friend** to create the term for a female romantic partner.

| Component | Meaning       | Contribution to 女朋友      |
| --------- | ------------- | --------------------------- |
| **女**    | woman; female | Shows gender and femininity |
| **朋**    | friend        | Indicates companionship     |
| **友**    | friend        | Reinforces friendly bond    |

## Character Analysis

### 女 (woman)

- Shows a person in a kneeling position, representing femininity
- Forms the basis for many female-related words
- Clear gender indicator in the compound

### 朋友 (friend)

- **朋** shows two moons (月月) side by side, suggesting companionship
- **友** shows two hands coming together, indicating friendship
- Together they mean "friend" - someone close and trusted

## Mnemonic

Think of 女朋友 as **"a female friend who is special"**:

- 女 (woman) specifies the gender
- 朋友 (friend) shows the close, caring relationship
- The combination creates a romantic partner who is both friend and lover
- Picture a woman who started as a friend and became something more special

## Usage Examples

- **我的女朋友** (wǒ de nǚ péng yǒu) - "my girlfriend"
- **他有女朋友** (tā yǒu nǚ péng yǒu) - "he has a girlfriend"
- **找女朋友** (zhǎo nǚ péng yǒu) - "looking for a girlfriend"
- **女朋友很漂亮** (nǚ péng yǒu hěn piào liang) - "girlfriend is very pretty"
- **和女朋友约会** (hé nǚ péng yǒu yuē huì) - "dating with girlfriend"

## Grammar Patterns

- **我的 + 女朋友** - possessive structure "my girlfriend"
- **有 + 女朋友** - "to have a girlfriend"
- **和 + 女朋友 + verb** - "doing something with girlfriend"

## Cultural Context

女朋友 reflects modern Chinese relationship concepts:

- **Modern terminology**: More casual than traditional marriage terms
- **Western influence**: Adopted from Western dating culture
- **Social acceptance**: Now widely accepted in contemporary China
- **Relationship progression**: Often leads to consideration of marriage
- **Family introduction**: Bringing 女朋友 to meet parents is significant
