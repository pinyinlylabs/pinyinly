All human beings collectively; the human race.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                          |
| -------------- | ----------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | rén lèi                       |
| Core meaning   | humanity; mankind; human race |
| Part of speech | noun                          |
| Tone           | 2nd + 4th tone                |

## Usage Examples

- **人类历史** (rén lèi lì shǐ) - "human history"
- **人类文明** (rén lèi wén míng) - "human civilization"
- **人类社会** (rén lèi shè huì) - "human society"
- **全人类** (quán rén lèi) - "all humanity"
- **人类发展** (rén lèi fā zhǎn) - "human development"

## Grammar Patterns

人类 typically:

- **As subject**: 人类需要和平 - "humanity needs peace"
- **As modifier**: 人类的未来 - "humanity's future"
- **Universal scope**: 全人类 - "all of humanity"
- **In abstractions**: 人类精神 - "human spirit"

## Cultural Context

人类 reflects Chinese philosophical perspectives:

- **Species unity**: Emphasizes what unites all humans despite differences
- **Historical consciousness**: 人类 viewed across long spans of time
- **Collective responsibility**: Humans share responsibility for Earth and future
- **Evolutionary awareness**: Recognition of humans' place in natural order
- **Global thinking**: 人类 transcends national and cultural boundaries
