**🗣️ Pronunciation of 坐 (zuò)**

- **Pinyin:** zuò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Sit!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"dz"** in "adds" (but lighter)
- **uò** sounds like **"waw"**, but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **zuò** sounds like **"dzwaw!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're commanding someone to sit: **"zuò!"** — that's the falling tone of **zuò**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 坐 (zuò) - "sit; sit down"
- 坐下 (zuò xià) - "sit down"
- 坐车 (zuò chē) - "take a vehicle; ride"
- 坐船 (zuò chuán) - "take a boat"
- 坐飞机 (zuò fēi jī) - "take an airplane"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **坐** (sit) like giving a firm command to sit down: **"zuò!"** — that's the **fourth
tone** authoritative tone!
