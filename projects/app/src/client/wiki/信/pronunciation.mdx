**🗣️ Pronunciation of 信 (xìn)**

- **Pinyin:** xìn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (but with tongue tip down)
- **ìn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **xìn** sounds like **"sheen!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"xìn!"** — that's the tone pattern of **xìn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 信 (xìn) - "to believe; trust; letter"
- 相信 (xiāng xìn) - "to believe"
- 信任 (xìn rèn) - "to trust"
- 信息 (xìn xī) - "information"
- 信心 (xìn xīn) - "confidence"
