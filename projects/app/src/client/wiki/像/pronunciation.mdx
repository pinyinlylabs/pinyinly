**🗣️ Pronunciation of 像 (xiàng)**

- **Pinyin:** xiàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like a soft **"sh"** sound, but your tongue is much closer to your teeth
- **iàng** sounds like **"yahng"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **xiàng** sounds like **"shyahng!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"xiàng!"** — that's the tone pattern of **xiàng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 像 (xiàng) - "resemble"
- 好像 (hǎo xiàng) - "seem like"
- 像样 (xiàng yàng) - "proper"
- 肖像 (xiào xiàng) - "portrait"
