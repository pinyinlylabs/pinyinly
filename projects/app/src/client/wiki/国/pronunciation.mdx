**🗣️ Pronunciation of 国 (guó)**

- **Pinyin:** guó
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uó** sounds like **"waw"**, but with second tone → rising pitch
- **guó** sounds like **"gwaw?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) starts **low** and rises **up**:

Say it like you're asking about a country: **"guó?"** — that's the rising tone of **guó**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 国 (guó) - "country; nation"
- 国家 (guó jiā) - "country; nation"
- 中国 (zhōng guó) - "China"
- 美国 (měi guó) - "America; USA"
- 国际 (guó jì) - "international"
- 外国 (wài guó) - "foreign country"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **国** (country) like proudly asking "guó?" — questioning which nation someone's from!
