**🗣️ Pronunciation of 元 (yuán)**

- **Pinyin:** yuán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **yu** is pronounced as **"ü"** - start by saying **"ee"** (as in "see"), then round your lips as
  if saying **"oo"** but keep your tongue in the "ee" position
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** but with a rising tone
- **yuán** sounds like **"yü-ahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"yuán?"** — that's the tone pattern of **yuán**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 元 (yuán) - "yuan" (currency)
- 元素 (yuán sù) - "element"
- 单元 (dān yuán) - "unit"
- 公元 (gōng yuán) - "AD/CE"
