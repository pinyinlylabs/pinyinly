**🗣️ Pronunciation of 播 (bō)**

- **Pinyin:** bō
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bow"
- **ō** sounds like **"aw"** in "law", but held steady and high
- **bō** sounds like **"baw"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like singing a steady high note: **"bō~~~"** — that's the flat, steady tone of **bō**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 播 (bō) - "sow; broadcast"
- 播放 (bō fàng) - "play; broadcast"
- 播出 (bō chū) - "broadcast; air"
- 传播 (chuán bō) - "spread; disseminate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"播"** as **"baw"** held steady — like steadily broadcasting or sowing seeds!
