**🗣️ Pronunciation of 备 (bèi)**

- **Pinyin:** bèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Bay!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bay"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "bay" but with a sharp falling tone
- **bèi** sounds like **"bay"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is **authoritative and falling**:

Start **high and drop decisively** — like giving an order to prepare: **"bèi!"** (get ready!)

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 备 (bèi) - "prepare"
- 准备 (zhǔn bèi) - "prepare"
- 设备 (shè bèi) - "equipment"
- 装备 (zhuāng bèi) - "equipment"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the sharp falling tone like a drill sergeant commanding **"bèi!"** (prepare!) — the
decisive drop reflects the urgency of getting ready!
