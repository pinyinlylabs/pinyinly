**🗣️ Pronunciation of 百 (bǎi)**

- **Pinyin:** bǎi
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **ǎi** sounds like **"eye"** but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up
- **bǎi** sounds like **"buy"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully counting: **"bǎi..."** — that contemplative dip-then-rise when
reaching a hundred.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 百 (bǎi) - "hundred"
- 一百 (yì bǎi) - "one hundred"
- 百分之 (bǎi fēn zhī) - "percent"
- 老百姓 (lǎo bǎi xìng) - "common people"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

百 means "hundred" — the dip-then-rise third tone sounds like someone carefully counting up to a big
number!
