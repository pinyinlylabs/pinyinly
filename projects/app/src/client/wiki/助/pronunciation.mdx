**🗣️ Pronunciation of 助 (zhù)**

- **Pinyin:** zhù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Zhoo!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **zhù** sounds like **"joo!"** with a sharp drop and tongue curled back

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly offering help: **"zhù!"** — decisive and supportive.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 助 (zhù) - "help, assist"
- 帮助 (bāng zhù) - "help"
- 助手 (zhù shǒu) - "assistant"
- 协助 (xié zhù) - "assist"
- 资助 (zī zhù) - "financial aid"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **zhù** as a firm "choose!" — you're choosing to help someone decisively!
