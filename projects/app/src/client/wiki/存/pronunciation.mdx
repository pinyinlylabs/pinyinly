**🗣️ Pronunciation of 存 (cún)**

- **Pinyin:** cún
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** — similar to "ts" in "cats" but as one sound
- **ún** sounds like **"oon"** in "moon" but with a rising tone
- **cún** sounds like **"tsoon?"** with a questioning rise

**🎯 Mastering the Chinese "c" sound:**

The **c** in Chinese is **different** from English:

1. **Tongue tip to teeth** — press tongue tip against upper teeth
2. **Make a "ts" sound** — like "ts" in "hits" but as one sound
3. **Keep it unaspirated** — no strong puff of air
4. **Think "ts"** — crisp and clean

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is **rising like a question**:

Start **low and rise up** — like asking if something can be saved: **"cún?"** — that's the hopeful
second tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 存 (cún) - "to save; to store; to exist"
- 存在 (cún zài) - "to exist; existence"
- 保存 (bǎo cún) - "to preserve; to keep"
- 储存 (chǔ cún) - "to store; storage"
- 生存 (shēng cún) - "to survive; survival"
- 存钱 (cún qián) - "to save money"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The second tone's rising pattern is like hopefully asking **"Can we save it?"** — that questioning
rise matches the meaning of **存**!
