**🗣️ Pronunciation of 顾 (gù)**

- **Pinyin:** gù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "good" but with sharp falling tone
- **gù** sounds like **"goo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone. Say **"gù"** like you're firmly telling someone
to look back: "**Look back**!"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 顾 (gù) - "look back/care for"
- 顾客 (gù kè) - "customer"
- 照顾 (zhào gù) - "take care of"
- 顾问 (gù wèn) - "advisor/consultant"
- 回顾 (huí gù) - "look back/review"
- 不顾 (bù gù) - "regardless of"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you command someone to "**Look back**!" you use a sharp, decisive tone — that's the fourth tone
of **gù**!
