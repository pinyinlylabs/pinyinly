**🗣️ Pronunciation of 宣 (xuān)**

- **Pinyin:** xuān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like a soft **"sh"** sound, but your tongue is much closer to your teeth
- **uān** sounds like **"wan"** but held steady and high
- **xuān** sounds like **"shwan"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like making an important announcement: **"xuān!"** — that steady, high tone is perfect for
**declaring** something.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 宣 (xuān) - "to declare; announce; proclaim"
- 宣布 (xuān bù) - "to announce; declare"
- 宣传 (xuān chuán) - "to publicize; propaganda"
- 宣言 (xuān yán) - "declaration; manifesto"

**📍 Usage Note:**

宣 is often used in formal or official contexts when making public announcements or declarations. It
carries a sense of authority and formality.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Declare** — the steady first tone is like the confident voice of someone making an official
**announcement**!
