**🗣️ Pronunciation of 碰 (pèng)**

- **Pinyin:** pèng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: "Bang!"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pot"
- **èng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung" but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **pèng** sounds like **"pung!"** with a sharp falling sound

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like the sound of impact: **"pèng!"** — that's the tone pattern of **pèng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 碰 (pèng) - "bump; to touch"
- 碰到 (pèng dào) - "to run into; to encounter"
- 碰见 (pèng jiàn) - "to meet by chance"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sound of **bumping** into something is sharp and sudden — just like the falling fourth tone of
**pèng**!
