**🗣️ Pronunciation of 自 (zì)**

- **Pinyin:** zì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a confident statement

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"ds"** in "beds" (voiced z sound)
- **ì** sounds like **"er"** in "her", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **zì** sounds like **"dzuh"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it decisively, like saying **"Stop!"** with authority: **"zì!"** — that's the falling pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 自 (zì) - "self; from"
- 自己 (zì jǐ) - "oneself; myself"
- 自然 (zì rán) - "natural; nature"
- 来自 (lái zì) - "come from"
- 自由 (zì yóu) - "freedom; free"

**💡 Usage Note:**

自 (zì) is commonly used as a prefix meaning "self-" or "auto-" in compound words, and also means
"from" when indicating origin.
