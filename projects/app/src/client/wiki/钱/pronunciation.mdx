**🗣️ Pronunciation of 钱 (qián)**

- **Pinyin:** qián
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "choose" but more aspirated
- **ián** sounds like **"yen"** but with a rising tone
- **qián** sounds like **"chen"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up: **"qián?"** — like asking "Money?" with surprise.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 钱 (qián) - "money"
- 钱包 (qián bāo) - "wallet"
- 金钱 (jīn qián) - "money, wealth"
- 赚钱 (zhuàn qián) - "earn money"
- 花钱 (huā qián) - "spend money"

**📝 Special Notes:**

The character 钱 contains the metal radical 钅, reflecting the historical connection between money
and metal coins.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you ask **"Money?"** your voice naturally rises — that's the second tone of **qián**!
