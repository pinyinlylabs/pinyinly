**🗣️ Pronunciation of 条 (tiáo)**

- **Pinyin:** tiáo
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **iáo** sounds like **"yow"** (as in "wow"), but with second tone → rise up
- **tiáo** sounds like **"tyow?"** with a rising tone

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 条 (tiáo) - "strip; measure word for long thin things"
- 一条路 (yī tiáo lù) - "one road"
- 条件 (tiáo jiàn) - "condition; requirement"
- 面条儿 (miàn tiáor) - "noodles"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

A **strip** or **road** stretches upward like the rising second tone of **tiáo**!
