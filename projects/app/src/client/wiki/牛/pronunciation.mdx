**🗣️ Pronunciation of 牛 (niú)**

- **Pinyin:** niú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "new"
- **iú** sounds like **"yo"** but with a rising tone
- **niú** sounds like **"nyo?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about a cow: **"niú?"** — that questioning rise is the **second tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 牛 (niú) - "cow; bull; ox"
- 牛奶 (niú nǎi) - "milk"
- 水牛 (shuǐ niú) - "water buffalo"
- 牛肉 (niú ròu) - "beef"
- 公牛 (gōng niú) - "bull"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **牛** like questioning if you see a cow: **"niú?"** — that rising intonation matches the
**second tone**!
