**🗣️ Pronunciation of 类 (lèi)**

- **Pinyin:** lèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like categorizing: **"Type!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **lèi** sounds like **"lay!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is **authoritative and falling**:

Say it like you're definitively categorizing something: **"lèi!"** — that decisive tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 类 (lèi) - "type; kind; category"
- 类似 (lèi sì) - "similar; alike"
- 种类 (zhǒng lèi) - "variety; type"
- 人类 (rén lèi) - "human; mankind"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **类** as firmly placing something into a category — that sharp fourth tone drop is like
stamping a classification!
