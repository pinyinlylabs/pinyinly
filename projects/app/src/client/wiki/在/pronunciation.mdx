**🗣️ Pronunciation of 在 (zài)**

- **Pinyin:** zài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"There!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"dz"** in "adds" (but lighter)
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"**, but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **zài** sounds like **"dzye!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're pointing out a location: **"zài!"** — that's the decisive falling tone of
**zài**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 在 (zài) - "at; in; on; (located) at"
- 在家 (zài jiā) - "at home"
- 现在 (xiàn zài) - "now; at present"
- 存在 (cún zài) - "exist"
- 在线 (zài xiàn) - "online"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **在** (at/in) like firmly stating a location: **"zài!"** — that's the **fourth tone**
definitiveness!
