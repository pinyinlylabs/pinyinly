**🗣️ Pronunciation of 特 (tè)**

- **Pinyin:** tè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "take" but with a slight puff of air
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **tè** sounds like **"teh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're emphasizing something special: **"tè!"** — that sharp, decisive drop is the
**fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 特 (tè) - "special; particular"
- 特别 (tè bié) - "especially; particularly"
- 特点 (tè diǎn) - "characteristic; feature"
- 特色 (tè sè) - "special feature; characteristic"
- 特殊 (tè shū) - "special; particular"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **特** as emphatically declaring something special: **"tè!"** — that sharp emphasis matches
the **fourth tone**!
