**🗣️ Pronunciation of 常 (cháng)**

- **Pinyin:** cháng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "change"
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with rising tone
- **cháng** sounds like **"chahng"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is a **rising** tone, like asking a question:

Say it like asking "**often**?": **"cháng?"** — that rising, questioning tone is the **second
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 常 (cháng) - "often; frequently"
- 常常 (cháng cháng) - "often; frequently"
- 非常 (fēi cháng) - "very; extremely"
- 正常 (zhèng cháng) - "normal"
- 日常 (rì cháng) - "daily; everyday"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **常** as asking "**often**?" with that rising, curious tone — that's the **second tone**
quality!
