**🗣️ Pronunciation of 为**

为 has **two different pronunciations** depending on its usage:

**📍 wéi (second tone) - "to be; to become; for"**

- **Pinyin:** wéi
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **éi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way" but with a rising tone
- **wéi** sounds like **"way?"** with a questioning rise

**📍 wèi (fourth tone) - "for the sake of; because of"**

- **Pinyin:** wèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way" but with a sharp falling tone
- **wèi** sounds like **"way!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

**wéi** (second tone) = rising, like asking "why?" **wèi** (fourth tone) = falling, like stating
"because!"

**📝 Usage Guide:**

**为 (wéi) - "to be; to become" (more common):**

- 成为 (chéng wéi) - "to become"
- 为什么 (wèi shén me) - "why" (note: uses fourth tone here)
- 认为 (rèn wéi) - "to think; to consider"

**为 (wèi) - "for the sake of; because of":**

- 为了 (wèi le) - "in order to; for"
- 为什么 (wèi shén me) - "why"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **为** as asking **"why?"** (rising wéi) and then answering **"because!"** (falling wèi) —
it connects reasons and purposes!
