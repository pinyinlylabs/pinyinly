**🗣️ Pronunciation of 页 (yè)**

- **Pinyin:** yè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **yè** sounds like **"yeah!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone. Say **"yè"** like you're decisively pointing to
a page: "That **page**!"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 页 (yè) - "page"
- 页面 (yè miàn) - "page/webpage"
- 首页 (shǒu yè) - "homepage/front page"
- 下一页 (xià yí yè) - "next page"
- 网页 (wǎng yè) - "webpage"
- 页码 (yè mǎ) - "page number"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you command "Turn the **page**!" you use a sharp, falling tone — that's the fourth tone of
**yè**!
