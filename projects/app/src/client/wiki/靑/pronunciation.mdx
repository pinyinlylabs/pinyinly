**🗣️ Pronunciation of 靑 (qīng)**

- **Pinyin:** qīng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but with a puff of air)
- **īng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing", held steady and high
- **qīng** sounds like **"ching"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 靑 (qīng) - "blue" (traditional form)
- 靑天 (qīng tiān) - "blue sky"
- 靑色 (qīng sè) - "blue color"

**💡 Note:**

靑 is the traditional form of 青. In modern Chinese, 青 is more commonly used, but both have the
same pronunciation **qīng** with first tone.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **high flat tone** reflects the endless, steady blue of the sky — **qīng**
