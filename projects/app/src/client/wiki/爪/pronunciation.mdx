**🗣️ Pronunciation of 爪 (zhǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** zhǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "jump" but with tongue tip curled back
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **zhǎo** sounds like **"jow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about a cat's claw: **"zhǎo..."** — that thoughtful, dipping-then-rising
pattern is the third tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 爪 (zhǎo) - "claw; paw"
- 爪子 (zhǎo zi) - "paw; claw"
- 鸡爪 (jī zhǎo) - "chicken feet"
- 猫爪 (māo zhǎo) - "cat's paw"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you see a cat's claw, you might think "Hmm..." with that contemplative dip-and-rise tone — just
like the third tone pattern in 爪!
