**🗣️ Pronunciation of 救 (jiù)**

- **Pinyin:** jiù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but softer, closer to "zy")
- **iù** sounds like **"ee-oo"**, but with a sharp falling tone
- **jiù** sounds like **"jee-oo!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 救 (jiù) - "save, rescue"
- 救命 (jiù mìng) - "help! save me!"
- 救护车 (jiù hù chē) - "ambulance"
- 拯救 (zhěng jiù) - "rescue, save"
- 获救 (huò jiù) - "be rescued"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The urgent fourth tone matches the emergency nature of **救** - when you need to **save** someone,
it's decisive and urgent!
