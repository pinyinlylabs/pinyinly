**🗣️ Pronunciation of 浪 (làng)**

- **Pinyin:** làng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **àng** sounds like **"ong"** in "long", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **làng** sounds like **"long!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"làng!"** — that's the tone pattern of **làng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 浪 (làng) - "wave"
- 海浪 (hǎi làng) - "ocean waves"
- 浪费 (làng fèi) - "waste/squander"
- 浪漫 (làng màn) - "romantic"
- 流浪 (liú làng) - "wander/roam"
- 波浪 (bō làng) - "waves"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

浪 (làng) contains the water radical (氵) on the left, which makes perfect sense since waves are
made of water moving in the ocean or sea!
