**🗣️ Pronunciation of 站 (zhàn)**

- **Pinyin:** zhàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "junk" but with tongue curled back
- **àn** sounds like **"an"** in "can", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **zhàn** sounds like **"jahn!"** with a sharp falling command

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop sharply: **"zhàn!"** — like giving a firm command to stop and stand.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 站 (zhàn) - "stand; station"
- 站住 (zhàn zhù) - "stop; halt"
- 火车站 (huǒ chē zhàn) - "train station"
- 地铁站 (dì tiě zhàn) - "subway station"
- 网站 (wǎng zhàn) - "website"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of commanding someone to **"jahn!"** (stop and stand) — the sharp fourth tone matches the authority of the command!
