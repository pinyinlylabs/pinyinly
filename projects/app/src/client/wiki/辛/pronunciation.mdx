**🗣️ Pronunciation of 辛 (xīn)**

- **Pinyin:** xīn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **īn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen", but with first tone → steady and high
- **xīn** sounds like **"sheen"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like stating a fact confidently: **"xīn!"** — that's the steady, high tone pattern of
**xīn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 辛苦 (xīn kǔ) - "hard work; toil"
- 辛辣 (xīn là) - "spicy; pungent"
- 辛勤 (xīn qín) - "hardworking; industrious"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

辛 represents something **bitter** or **hard** — pronounce it with a steady, clear **xīn** like
stating something definitively.
