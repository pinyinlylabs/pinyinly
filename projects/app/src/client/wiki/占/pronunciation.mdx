**🗣️ Pronunciation of 占 (zhàn)**

- **Pinyin:** zhàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "jump" but with tongue curled back
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **zhàn** sounds like **"jahn!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a sharp **falling** tone: Start high and drop sharply down, like making a
firm statement: **"zhàn!"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 占 (zhàn) - "occupy; take up"
- 占领 (zhàn lǐng) - "occupy"
- 占据 (zhàn jù) - "hold; occupy"
- 占卜 (zhàn bǔ) - "divination"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

占 means "occupy" — when claiming territory, you make a **firm, decisive** statement with a sharp
falling tone!
