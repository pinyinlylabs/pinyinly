**🗣️ Pronunciation of 跳 (tiào)**

- **Pinyin:** tiào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea"
- **iào** sounds like **"yow"** (as in "ouch") but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **tiào** sounds like **"t-yow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're commanding someone to jump: **"tiào!"** — that's the tone pattern of **tiào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 跳 (tiào) - "jump; leap; dance"
- 跳舞 (tiào wǔ) - "dance"
- 跳高 (tiào gāo) - "high jump"
- 跳远 (tiào yuǎn) - "long jump"
- 心跳 (xīn tiào) - "heartbeat"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

跳 means "jump" — the **sharp falling** tone is like the decisive downward motion after a leap!
