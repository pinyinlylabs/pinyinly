**🗣️ Pronunciation of 年 (nián)**

- **Pinyin:** nián
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "now"
- **ián** sounds like **"yen"** but with rising tone
- **nián** sounds like **"nyen"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is a **rising** tone, like asking a question:

Say it like asking "which **year**?": **"nián?"** — that rising, questioning tone is the **second
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 年 (nián) - "year"
- 今年 (jīn nián) - "this year"
- 去年 (qù nián) - "last year"
- 明年 (míng nián) - "next year"
- 新年 (xīn nián) - "New Year"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **年** as asking about which **year** with that rising, curious tone — that's the **second
tone** quality!
