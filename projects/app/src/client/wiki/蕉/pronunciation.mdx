**🗣️ Pronunciation of 蕉 (jiāo)**

- **Pinyin:** jiāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but more like "gee" with tongue forward)
- **iāo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow", but with first tone → high and steady
- **jiāo** sounds like **"gee-ow"** held steady and high

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're stating a fact clearly: **"jiāo"** — that's the tone pattern of **jiāo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 蕉 (jiāo) - "banana"
- 香蕉 (xiāng jiāo) - "banana"
- 芭蕉 (bā jiāo) - "plantain/banana plant"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **jiāo** as confidently identifying a banana: "That's a banana!" — the high flat tone
sounds clear and certain.
