Feeling or showing grief or unhappiness; sad; upset; distressed; sorrowful.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                              |
| -------------- | --------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | nánguò                            |
| Core meaning   | sad; upset; distressed; difficult |
| Part of speech | adjective                         |
| Tone           | nán (2nd), guò (4th)              |

## Word Breakdown

难过 combines concepts of difficulty and passage to represent emotional pain.

| Component | Meaning                                             |
| --------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **难**    | Difficult; hard; challenging - representing struggle |
| **过**    | Pass; get through - representing enduring something  |

Together they create: "difficult to get through" or "hard to endure" - the emotional state that's challenging to bear.

## Mnemonic

Think of 难过 as **"struggling to get through difficult emotional terrain"**:

- 难 (nán) represents the difficulty and challenge of the situation
- 过 (guò) shows trying to pass through or endure the experience
- Together: the struggle of getting through emotionally difficult times
- Like walking through rough terrain that's hard to navigate
- The feeling of facing emotional obstacles that are hard to overcome

This creates the image: **struggling to navigate through emotionally difficult territory**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

难过 represents **sadness, emotional distress, and difficulty coping**. It's used:

1. **General sadness**: 很难过 (hěn nánguò) - "very sad"
2. **Emotional pain**: 感到难过 (gǎndào nánguò) - "feel upset"
3. **Grief**: 为此难过 (wèi cǐ nánguò) - "sad about this"
4. **Distress**: 难过极了 (nánguò jí le) - "extremely upset"

## Examples

- **很难过** (hěn nánguò) - "very sad"
- **真难过** (zhēn nánguò) - "really sad"
- **不要难过** (bùyào nánguò) - "don't be sad"
- **感到难过** (gǎndào nánguò) - "feel sad"
- **难过得哭了** (nánguò de kū le) - "so sad that (one) cried"

## Causes of Sadness

难过 in various contexts:

- **分离难过** (fēnlí nánguò) - "sad about separation"
- **失败难过** (shībài nánguò) - "sad about failure"
- **听到坏消息很难过** (tīngdào huài xiāoxi hěn nánguò) - "sad to hear bad news"
- **离别时很难过** (líbié shí hěn nánguò) - "sad when parting"

## Expressing Sadness

难过 in emotional expression:

- **难过地哭** (nánguò de kū) - "cry sadly"
- **难过地说** (nánguò de shuō) - "say sadly"
- **难过得说不出话** (nánguò de shuō bù chū huà) - "too sad to speak"
- **眼泪难过** (yǎnlèi nánguò) - "tears of sadness"

## Levels of Sadness

Different degrees of 难过:

- **有点难过** (yǒudiǎn nánguò) - "a little sad"
- **比较难过** (bǐjiào nánguò) - "quite sad"
- **非常难过** (fēicháng nánguò) - "very sad"
- **特别难过** (tèbié nánguò) - "especially sad"

## Comforting Others

Responses to 难过:

- **别难过** (bié nánguò) - "don't be sad"
- **不要太难过** (bùyào tài nánguò) - "don't be too sad"
- **安慰难过的人** (ānwèi nánguò de rén) - "comfort sad people"
- **理解你的难过** (lǐjiě nǐ de nánguò) - "understand your sadness"

## Opposite Emotions

**Opposite**: 开心 (kāixīn) - "happy"

Emotional contrasts:
- 难过 vs 开心 (sad vs happy)
- 难过 vs 快乐 (sad vs joyful)
- 难过 vs 高兴 (sad vs glad)

## Cultural Context

难过 in Chinese culture represents:

**Emotional Understanding:**
- **情感表达** (qínggǎn biǎodá) - Emotional expression is natural
- **同情心** (tóngqíngxīn) - Empathy and compassion for others
- **情感支持** (qínggǎn zhīchí) - Emotional support from community
- **时间治愈** (shíjiān zhìyù) - Time heals emotional wounds

**Social Response:**
- **安慰** (ānwèi) - Comforting those who are sad
- **陪伴** (péibàn) - Accompanying people through difficult times
- **理解** (lǐjiě) - Understanding others' emotional pain

## Common Expressions

- **难过一阵子** (nánguò yī zhènzi) - "be sad for a while"
- **难过得不得了** (nánguò de bùdéliǎo) - "incredibly sad"
- **为了...而难过** (wèile... ér nánguò) - "sad because of..."
- **难过的心情** (nánguò de xīnqíng) - "sad mood"

## Life Events

难过 in significant situations:

- **失去亲人很难过** (shīqù qīnrén hěn nánguò) - "very sad to lose loved ones"
- **考试失败难过** (kǎoshì shībài nánguò) - "sad about exam failure"
- **朋友搬走很难过** (péngyǒu bānzǒu hěn nánguò) - "sad that friends moved away"
- **宠物去世难过** (chǒngwù qùshì nánguò) - "sad about pet's death"

## Temporal Aspects

难过 over time:

- **刚开始难过** (gāng kāishǐ nánguò) - "sad at first"
- **一直难过** (yīzhí nánguò) - "continuously sad"
- **慢慢不难过了** (mànmàn bù nánguò le) - "gradually not sad anymore"
- **偶尔还会难过** (ǒu'ěr hái huì nánguò) - "occasionally still sad"

## Physical Manifestations

难过 showing physically:

- **难过得吃不下饭** (nánguò de chī bù xià fàn) - "too sad to eat"
- **难过得睡不着** (nánguò de shuì bù zháo) - "too sad to sleep"
- **难过得头疼** (nánguò de tóu téng) - "headache from sadness"

## Grammar Functions

- **Adjective**: 我很难过 (wǒ hěn nánguò) - "I am very sad"
- **Adverb**: 难过地哭 (nánguò de kū) - "cry sadly"
- **State**: 感到难过 (gǎndào nánguò) - "feel sad"

## Coping and Recovery

Dealing with 难过:

- **克服难过** (kèfú nánguò) - "overcome sadness"
- **走出难过** (zǒuchū nánguò) - "get out of sadness"
- **释放难过** (shìfàng nánguò) - "release sadness"
- **治愈难过** (zhìyù nánguò) - "heal sadness"

## Support Systems

难过 and help:

- **分享难过** (fēnxiǎng nánguò) - "share sadness"
- **倾诉难过** (qīngsù nánguò) - "confide sadness"
- **陪伴难过的朋友** (péibàn nánguò de péngyǒu) - "accompany sad friends"

## Learning Notes

难过 is essential because:

- Fundamental emotion word for expressing negative feelings
- Essential for describing personal struggles and emotional states
- Key to seeking and offering emotional support
- Important for understanding human emotional experiences
- Demonstrates how compound words express complex emotional concepts

难过 reflects the Chinese understanding that sadness is a difficult emotional terrain that requires endurance, support, and time to navigate through successfully!
