**🗣️ Pronunciation of 村 (cūn)**

- **Pinyin:** cūn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats"
- **ūn** sounds like **"oon"** in "soon", but with first tone → stay high and flat
- **cūn** sounds like **"tsoon"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 村 (cūn) - "village"
- 农村 (nóng cūn) - "countryside; rural area"
- 村子 (cūn zi) - "village"
- 村民 (cūn mín) - "villager"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

A **village** sits steadily on flat land — that's the steady first tone of **cūn**!
