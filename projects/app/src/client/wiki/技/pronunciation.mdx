**🗣️ Pronunciation of 技 (jì)**

- **Pinyin:** jì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but **lighter** and **more forward** in the mouth
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **jì** sounds like **"jee!"** with a sharp drop

**🔤 Mastering the "j" sound:**

The **j** in Chinese is **different** from English "j":

1. **Keep your tongue forward** — tip behind lower teeth
2. **Make it lighter** — less buzzy than English "j"
3. **More like "jee"** in "gee whiz" but softer

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like demonstrating a skill confidently: **"jì!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern of
**jì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 技 (jì) - "skill; technique"
- 技术 (jì shù) - "technology; technique"
- 科技 (kē jì) - "science and technology"
- 技能 (jì néng) - "skill; ability"
- 技巧 (jì qiǎo) - "skill; technique; trick"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Skill** — the sharp fourth tone shows the precision and decisiveness of mastered technique!
