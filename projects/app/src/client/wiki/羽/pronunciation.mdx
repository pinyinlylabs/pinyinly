**🗣️ Pronunciation of 羽 (yǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** yǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"ü"** (like German "ü" or French "u"), but with third tone → dip down and rise
  up
- **yǔ** sounds like **"yü"** with a contemplative dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're gently touching a feather: **"yǔ..."** — that's the soft, thoughtful tone pattern
of **yǔ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 羽 (yǔ) - "feather"
- 羽毛 (yǔ máo) - "feather, plumage"
- 羽毛球 (yǔ máo qiú) - "badminton" (literally "feather ball")
- 鸟羽 (niǎo yǔ) - "bird feathers"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

A feather is soft and delicate — pronounce **yǔ** with that gentle third tone, like you're admiring
its lightness!
