**🗣️ Pronunciation of 庆 (qìng)**

- **Pinyin:** qìng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a firm command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" but with a slight puff of air
- **ìng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing" but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **qìng** sounds like **"ching!"** with a sharp drop in pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like when you say "No!" decisively. **qìng** should sound firm and
definitive.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 庆祝 (qìng zhù) - "celebrate"
- 国庆 (guó qìng) - "National Day"
- 庆典 (qìng diǎn) - "celebration ceremony"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

庆 means "celebrate" — think of the **sharp, decisive** fourth tone as the **excitement** of
celebrating good news!
