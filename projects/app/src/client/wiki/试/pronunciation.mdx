**🗣️ Pronunciation of 试 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like a firm command: **"shr!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe" but with your **tongue curled back** further toward the roof of the
  mouth
- **ì** sounds like **"ur"** in "fur" but with a falling tone
- **shì** sounds like **"shr!"** with a decisive drop

**🎯 Mastering the "sh" sound:**

The **sh** in Chinese is **different** from English "sh":

1. **Curl your tongue back** — tip pointing toward the roof of your mouth
2. **Keep tongue sides touching** your upper teeth
3. **Make it deeper** — more throaty than English "sh"
4. **Think retroflex** — tongue curled back like you're making an "r" shape but saying "sh"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Start **high and drop down sharply** like giving a firm command: **"shì!"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 试 (shì) - "try; test; attempt"
- 考试 (kǎo shì) - "exam; test"
- 试验 (shì yàn) - "experiment; test"
- 试题 (shì tí) - "test question"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think **"sure!"** but with that curled-back Chinese "sh" sound and a falling tone — like firmly
saying "sure, I'll try it!"
