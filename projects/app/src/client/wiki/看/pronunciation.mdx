**🗣️ Pronunciation of 看 (kàn)**

- **Pinyin:** kàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **kàn** sounds like **"kahn!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a command to look: **"kàn!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern of **kàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 看 (kàn) - "see; look; watch"
- 看见 (kàn jiàn) - "see; catch sight of"
- 看书 (kàn shū) - "read a book"
- 看电视 (kàn diàn shì) - "watch TV"
- 好看 (hǎo kàn) - "good-looking; attractive"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"kahn!"** with a sharp drop — when you want someone to **look** at something, you use a
decisive, commanding tone!
