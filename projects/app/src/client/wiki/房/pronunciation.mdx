**🗣️ Pronunciation of 房 (fáng)**

- **Pinyin:** fáng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Fáng?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fan"
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with rising tone
- **fáng** sounds like **"fahng"** with a rising pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about a house: **"fáng?"** — that's the inquiring, rising tone pattern of
**fáng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 房 (fáng) - "house; room"
- 房子 (fáng zi) - "house"
- 房间 (fáng jiān) - "room"
- 房东 (fáng dōng) - "landlord"
- 住房 (zhù fáng) - "housing; residence"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**House** — the rising second tone is like the upward slope of a roof!
