**🗣️ Pronunciation of 抓 (zhuā)**

- **Pinyin:** zhuā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "jeer" but with tongue curled back
- **uā** sounds like **"wah"** but shorter
- **zhuā** sounds like **"jwah"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high, steady** tone:

Keep your voice high and level: **"zhuā"** — like singing a sustained high note.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 抓 (zhuā) - "to grab; to catch; to scratch"
- 抓住 (zhuā zhù) - "to catch; to seize"
- 抓紧 (zhuā jǐn) - "to grasp tightly; to hurry up"
- 抓痒 (zhuā yǎng) - "to scratch an itch"

**💡 Pronunciation Note:**

The **zh** sound is a retroflex consonant - curl your tongue tip back towards the roof of your mouth
when making the sound.
