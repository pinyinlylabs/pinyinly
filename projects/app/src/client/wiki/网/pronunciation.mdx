**🗣️ Pronunciation of 网 (wǎng)**

- **Pinyin:** wǎng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ǎng** sounds like **"ang"** in "hang", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **wǎng** sounds like **"wahng"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about a web or network: **"wǎng..."** — that contemplative dip and rise.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 网上 (wǎng shàng) - "online; on the internet"
- 网站 (wǎng zhàn) - "website"
- 网球 (wǎng qiú) - "tennis"
- 网络 (wǎng luò) - "network"
- 上网 (shàng wǎng) - "to go online"
- 互联网 (hù lián wǎng) - "internet"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

A **net** or **web** has that interconnected, thoughtful pattern — just like the dip-and-rise of the
third tone!
