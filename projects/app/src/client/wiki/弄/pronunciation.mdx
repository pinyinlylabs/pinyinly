**🗣️ Pronunciation of 弄 (nòng)**

- **Pinyin:** nòng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "now"
- **òng** sounds like **"ong"** with a sharp falling tone
- **nòng** sounds like **"nong!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving an order: **"nòng!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone
of **nòng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 弄 (nòng) - "do/make"
- 弄清楚 (nòng qīng chǔ) - "figure out clearly"
- 弄错 (nòng cuò) - "make a mistake"
- 弄好 (nòng hǎo) - "fix/arrange properly"
- 弄懂 (nòng dǒng) - "understand/figure out"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

弄 means "do/make" — the sharp fourth tone sounds decisive, like when you're determined to **get
something done**!
