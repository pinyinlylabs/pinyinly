**🗣️ Pronunciation of 做 (zuò)**

- **Pinyin:** zuò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"ds"** sound - tongue flat close to teeth, not as voiced as English "z"
- **uò** sounds like **"woh"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **zuò** sounds like **"dzwoh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"zuò!"** — that's the tone pattern of **zuò**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 做 (zuò) - "do"
- 做饭 (zuò fàn) - "cook"
- 做事 (zuò shì) - "do things"
- 做工作 (zuò gōng zuò) - "do work"
