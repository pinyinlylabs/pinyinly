**🗣️ Pronunciation of 介 (jiè)**

- **Pinyin:** jiè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **iè** sounds like **"yeah"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **jiè** sounds like **"jyeh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"jiè!"** — that's the decisive, falling tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 介绍 (jiè shào) - "introduce"
- 介于 (jiè yú) - "lie between"
- 介词 (jiè cí) - "preposition"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 介 as "in between" — it helps **introduce** or **lie between** things, just like its
meaning!
