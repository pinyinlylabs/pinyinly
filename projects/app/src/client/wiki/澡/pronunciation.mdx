**🗣️ Pronunciation of 澡 (zǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** zǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"ds"** in "kids" (but softer)
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **zǎo** sounds like **"dzow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about taking a relaxing bath: **"zǎo..."** — that contemplative tone
pattern of **zǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 澡 (zǎo) - "bath; wash"
- 洗澡 (xǐ zǎo) - "take a bath; shower"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you're thinking about a nice, relaxing **bath**, you might say **"Ahh..."** with that
contemplative, dip-then-rise tone — just like **zǎo**!
