**🗣️ Pronunciation of 儿 (ér)**

- **Pinyin:** ér
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **é** sounds like **"er"** in "her", but with a rising tone
- **ér** sounds like **"er?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"ér?"** — that's the tone pattern of **ér**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 儿 (ér) - "son"
- 儿子 (ér zi) - "son"
- 女儿 (nǚ ér) - "daughter"
- 小孩儿 (xiǎo hái er) - "child"

**💡 Special Note:**

儿 is often used as a suffix (儿化音) in Beijing dialect, where it's pronounced as a light "r" sound
attached to the previous syllable.
