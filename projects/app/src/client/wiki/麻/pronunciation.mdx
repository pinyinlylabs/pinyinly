**🗣️ Pronunciation of 麻 (má)**

- **Pinyin:** má
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "ma"
- **á** sounds like **"ah"** but with a rising tone
- **má** sounds like **"ma?"** with a questioning rise, similar to calling "Mom?"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question about hemp: **"má?"** — that rising, questioning intonation.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 麻 (má) - "hemp"
- 麻烦 (má fán) - "trouble; troublesome"
- 芝麻 (zhī má) - "sesame"
- 麻将 (má jiàng) - "mahjong"
- 麻油 (má yóu) - "sesame oil"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

麻 with its rising tone sounds like questioning "ma?" — imagine asking your mom (ma) if she knows
about hemp: **"má?"** with that rising, curious tone.
