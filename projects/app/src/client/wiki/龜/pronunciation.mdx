**🗣️ Pronunciation of 龜 (guī)**

- **Pinyin:** guī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uī** sounds like **"way"** but with the **"w"** sound softer, with first tone → high and steady
- **guī** sounds like **"gway"** held steady and high

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Keep it high and steady, like humming a consistent note: **"guī"** — that's the tone pattern of
**guī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 龜 (guī) - "turtle"
- 烏龜 (wū guī) - "tortoise; turtle"
- 龜速 (guī sù) - "turtle speed; very slow"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a turtle moving **steadily** and **consistently** slow with that flat, unchanging **"guī"**
tone!
