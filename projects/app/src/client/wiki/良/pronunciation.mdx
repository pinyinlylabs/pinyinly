**🗣️ Pronunciation of 良 (liáng)**

- **Pinyin:** liáng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "light"
- **iáng** sounds like **"yahng"** but with second tone → rise up
- **liáng** sounds like **"lee-yahng"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ´)**

The **second tone** is a **rising** tone, like asking a question:

Say it like you're asking: **"liáng?"** — that questioning rise is the **second tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 良 (liáng) - "good, fine"
- 良好 (liáng hǎo) - "good, favorable"
- 善良 (shàn liáng) - "kind, good-hearted"
- 良心 (liáng xīn) - "conscience"
- 优良 (yōu liáng) - "excellent, fine"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **良** as expressing something **good** with an upward, positive rising tone — that's the
**second tone** optimistic quality!

**📍 Cultural Note:**

良 appears in many positive expressions in Chinese culture, often relating to moral goodness and
quality, such as in 良师 (liáng shī) - "good teacher".
