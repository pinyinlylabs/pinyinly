**🗣️ Pronunciation of 经 (jīng)**

- **Pinyin:** jīng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but slightly softer)
- **īng** sounds like **"eeng"** with a high, steady tone
- **jīng** sounds like **"jeeng"** held high and flat

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and flat**:

Hold your voice steady and high, like singing a sustained note: **"jīng~~~"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 经过 (jīng guò) - "to pass through; to experience"
- 经常 (jīng cháng) - "often; frequently"
- 经济 (jīng jì) - "economy; economic"
- 经理 (jīng lǐ) - "manager"
- 经验 (jīng yàn) - "experience"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a **straight line** going through something — that's the meaning and the steady tone!
