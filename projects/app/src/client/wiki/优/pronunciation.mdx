**🗣️ Pronunciation of 优 (yōu)**

- **Pinyin:** yōu
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ōu** sounds like **"oh"** in "oh my", but with first tone → high and steady
- **yōu** sounds like **"yo"** held high and flat

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like expressing admiration: **"yōu~~"** — that high, level pitch shows the first tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 优 (yōu) - "superior; excellent"
- 优秀 (yōu xiù) - "excellent; outstanding"
- 优势 (yōu shì) - "advantage"
- 优点 (yōu diǎn) - "strong point; merit"
- 优美 (yōu měi) - "graceful; beautiful"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"yo"** said with admiration and held steady — like praising something superior!
