**🗣️ Pronunciation of 采 (cǎi)**

- **Pinyin:** cǎi
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats"
- **ǎi** sounds like **"eye"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **cǎi** sounds like **"tseye"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully picking something: **"cǎi..."** — that's the tone pattern of
**cǎi**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 采 (cǎi) - "to pick; to gather; to collect"
- 采取 (cǎi qǔ) - "to adopt; to take (measures)"
- 采用 (cǎi yòng) - "to adopt; to employ"
- 采购 (cǎi gòu) - "to purchase; to buy"
- 采访 (cǎi fǎng) - "to interview"
- 采花 (cǎi huā) - "to pick flowers"

**💡 Note:**

采 contains the 爪 (claw/hand) component on top, representing the action of picking or gathering
with hands. It's commonly used in contexts involving collection, selection, and adoption.
