**🗣️ Pronunciation of 内 (nèi)**

- **Pinyin:** nèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way" but with a sharp falling tone
- **nèi** sounds like **"nay!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is sharp and **falling**:

Say it like you're pointing inside somewhere: **"nèi!"** — decisive and clear.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 内 (nèi) - "inside; internal"
- 内容 (nèi róng) - "content"
- 内心 (nèi xīn) - "inner heart"
- 国内 (guó nèi) - "domestic; within the country"
- 室内 (shì nèi) - "indoor"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of pointing inside a room with authority — **"nèi!"** — the sharp falling tone shows you're
being direct!
