**🗣️ Pronunciation of 订 (dìng)**

- **Pinyin:** dìng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ìng** sounds like **"ing"** in "ring" but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **dìng** sounds like **"ding"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're finalizing a booking: **"dìng!"** — that firm drop shows the certainty of making
a reservation.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 订 (dìng) - "book; order; reserve"
- 预订 (yù dìng) - "book in advance"
- 订购 (dìng gòu) - "order goods"
- 订票 (dìng piào) - "book tickets"
- 订房 (dìng fáng) - "book a room"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 订 as "booking" something — when you confirm a reservation, you speak with firm decision,
just like the sharp falling fourth tone that seals the deal!
