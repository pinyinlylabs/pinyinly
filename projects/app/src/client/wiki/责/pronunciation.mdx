**🗣️ Pronunciation of 责 (zé)**

- **Pinyin:** zé
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Zay?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"dz"** in "adze" (voiced consonant)
- **é** sounds like **"ay"** in "hay" with a rising tone
- **zé** sounds like **"dzay?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is **rising**, like the end of a question:

Say it like you're asking "really?": **"zé?"** — that's the rising tone pattern of **zé**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 责任 (zé rèn) - "responsibility"
- 负责 (fù zé) - "responsible; in charge"
- 责备 (zé bèi) - "blame; reproach"
- 职责 (zhí zé) - "duty; responsibility"
