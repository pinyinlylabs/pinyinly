**🗣️ Pronunciation of 男 (nán)**

- **Pinyin:** nán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"What?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **án** sounds like **"an"** in "can", but with second tone → rising upward
- **nán** sounds like **"nahn?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 男 (nán) - "male; man"
- 男人 (nán rén) - "man"
- 男孩儿 (nán hái er) - "boy"
- 男朋友 (nán péng yǒu) - "boyfriend"
- 男生 (nán shēng) - "male student"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Second tone rises like asking **"Man?"** - your voice goes **up** throughout **nán**.
