**🗣️ Pronunciation of 吗 (ma)**

- **Pinyin:** ma
- **Tone: Neutral tone** — **light and quick**, unstressed

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "mom"
- **a** sounds like **"ah"** but very light and quick
- **ma** sounds like a quick **"mah"**

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **neutral tone** is **light and unstressed** — like a quick afterthought:

Say it lightly and quickly, like adding "huh?" to the end of a sentence — that's **ma**.

**📝 Special Notes:**

吗 (ma) is a **question particle** that turns statements into yes/no questions. It's always
pronounced with neutral tone and comes at the end of sentences.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 你好吗？ (nǐ hǎo ma?) - "How are you?" / "Are you well?"
- 你是学生吗？ (nǐ shì xué shēng ma?) - "Are you a student?"
- 这是你的吗？ (zhè shì nǐ de ma?) - "Is this yours?"
- 你喜欢吗？ (nǐ xǐ huān ma?) - "Do you like it?"
