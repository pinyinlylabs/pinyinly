**🗣️ Pronunciation of 买 (mǎi)**

- **Pinyin:** mǎi
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "my"
- **ǎi** sounds like **"eye"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **mǎi** sounds like **"my"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're considering a purchase — **"mǎi..."** — that thoughtful tone fits the meaning of
**"buy"**!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 买 (mǎi) - "buy, purchase"
- 买东西 (mǎi dōng xi) - "go shopping, buy things"
- 买票 (mǎi piào) - "buy tickets"
- 买房子 (mǎi fáng zi) - "buy a house"

**📍 Opposite:**

The opposite of 买 (mǎi) "buy" is 卖 (mài) "sell" — notice they sound very similar but with
different tones!

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Buy** — the contemplative third tone is perfect for thinking about whether to make a purchase!
