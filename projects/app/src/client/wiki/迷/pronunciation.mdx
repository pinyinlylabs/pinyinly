**🗣️ Pronunciation of 迷 (mí)**

- **Pinyin:** mí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "me"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a rising tone
- **mí** sounds like **"mee"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up, like saying **"Huh?"** when confused: **"mí?"** — that's the tone pattern of
**mí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 迷 (mí) - "fan; enthusiast"
- 球迷 (qiú mí) - "sports fan"
- 歌迷 (gē mí) - "music fan"
- 迷路 (mí lù) - "to get lost"
- 着迷 (zháo mí) - "fascinated"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Fans get **excited** about their interests — the rising tone shows that excitement and enthusiasm!
