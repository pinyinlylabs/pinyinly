**🗣️ Pronunciation of 顿 (dùn)**

- **Pinyin:** dùn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ùn** sounds like **"oon"** in "moon" but with sharp falling tone
- **dùn** sounds like **"done!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone. Say **"dùn"** like you're commanding a sudden
stop: "**Pause**!"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 顿 (dùn) - "pause/suddenly"
- 顿时 (dùn shí) - "immediately/at once"
- 顿号 (dùn hào) - "Chinese comma (、)"
- 一顿饭 (yí dùn fàn) - "a meal"
- 停顿 (tíng dùn) - "pause/stop"
- 顿悟 (dùn wù) - "sudden enlightenment"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you command someone to **pause**, you use a sharp, abrupt tone — that's the fourth tone of
**dùn**!
