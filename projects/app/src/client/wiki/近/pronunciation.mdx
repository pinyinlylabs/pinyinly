**🗣️ Pronunciation of 近 (jìn)**

- **Pinyin:** jìn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Close!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **ìn** sounds like **"een"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling tone
- **jìn** sounds like **"jeen!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like pointing out something close: **"jìn!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone pattern of
**jìn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 最近 (zuì jìn) - "recently; lately"
- 接近 (jiē jìn) - "to approach; to get close to"
- 附近 (fù jìn) - "nearby; in the vicinity"
- 近来 (jìn lái) - "recently; lately"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

近 means **near/close** — pronounce it with a sharp **jìn** like pointing out something that's right
there!
