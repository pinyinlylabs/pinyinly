**🗣️ Pronunciation of 可 (kě)**

- **Pinyin:** kě
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **ě** sounds like **"uh"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **kě** sounds like **"kuh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're considering possibility: **"kě..."** — that's the thoughtful tone pattern of
**kě**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 可 (kě) - "can; may; able to"
- 可以 (kě yǐ) - "can; may; possible"
- 可能 (kě néng) - "possible; maybe"
- 可是 (kě shì) - "but; however"
- 可爱 (kě ài) - "cute; lovely"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"kuh"** with a contemplative rise — when considering if something is **possible**, you
often reflect thoughtfully, just like the third tone's pattern!
