**🗣️ Pronunciation of 需 (xū)**

- **Pinyin:** xū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (Chinese x is similar to "sh")
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "shoe", held steady and high
- **xū** sounds like **"shoo"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 需 (xū) - "need"
- 需要 (xū yào) - "to need; require"
- 需求 (xū qiú) - "demand; requirement"
- 必需 (bì xū) - "necessary; essential"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **high flat tone** sounds urgent and steady, like when you really **need** something — **xū**
