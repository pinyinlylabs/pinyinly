**🗣️ Pronunciation of 社 (shè)**

- **Pinyin:** shè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: "Stop!"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** in "pet" but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **shè** sounds like **"sheh!"** with a sharp falling command

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a statement: **"shè!"** — that's the tone pattern of **shè**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 社 (shè) - "society; organization"
- 社会 (shè huì) - "society"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When talking about **society** or organizations, we often speak with authority and conviction — that
definitive fourth tone matches **shè** perfectly!
