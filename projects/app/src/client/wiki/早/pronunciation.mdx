**🗣️ Pronunciation of 早 (zǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** zǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"ds"** in "kids" — a buzzing sound
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "how", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **zǎo** sounds like **"dzow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully acknowledging the morning: **"zǎo..."** — that's the contemplative
tone pattern of **zǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 早 (zǎo) - "morning; early"
- 早上 (zǎo shang) - "morning"
- 早餐 (zǎo cān) - "breakfast"
- 早就 (zǎo jiù) - "already; long ago"
