**🗣️ Pronunciation of 羊 (yáng)**

- **Pinyin:** yáng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question "yes?"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** with a rising tone, similar to "young" but with second tone
- **yáng** sounds like **"yahng"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up, like you're asking "Really?" — that's the tone pattern of **yáng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 羊 (yáng) - "sheep"
- 山羊 (shān yáng) - "goat"
- 羊毛 (yáng máo) - "wool"
- 绵羊 (mián yáng) - "sheep" (literally "cotton sheep")

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a sheep going "baa?" with a rising intonation — that's the **yáng** tone!
