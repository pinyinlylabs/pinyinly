**🗣️ Pronunciation of 书 (shū)**

- **Pinyin:** shū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shush"
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "moon" but held steady and high
- **shū** sounds like **"shoo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — like the concentrated tone you'd use when reading a
**book**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 书 (shū) - "book"
- 书店 (shū diàn) - "bookstore"
- 书包 (shū bāo) - "school bag, backpack"
- 读书 (dú shū) - "read books, study"
- 书架 (shū jià) - "bookshelf"

**📍 Cultural Note:**

书 is fundamental in Chinese culture — **reading** and **learning** are highly valued, making this a
very important character.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Book** — the steady first tone is like the focused, continuous sound of reading!
