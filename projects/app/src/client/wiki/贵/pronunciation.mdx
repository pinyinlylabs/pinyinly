**🗣️ Pronunciation of 贵 (guì)**

- **Pinyin:** guì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Gway!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uì** sounds like **"way"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **guì** sounds like **"gway!"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're declaring something expensive: **"guì!"** — that's the sharp falling tone of
**guì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 贵 (guì) - "expensive"
- 宝贵 (bǎo guì) - "precious; valuable"
- 贵重 (guì zhòng) - "valuable; precious"
- 您贵姓 (nín guì xìng) - "what's your honorable surname?"
