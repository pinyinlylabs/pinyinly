The pair of organs of sight in humans and animals; eyes; vision organs; windows to the soul.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                       |
| -------------- | -------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | yǎnjīng                    |
| Core meaning   | eyes; vision organs; sight |
| Part of speech | noun                       |
| Tone           | yǎn (3rd), jīng (1st)      |

## Word Breakdown

眼睛 combines concepts of eye and clarity to represent the organs of vision.

| Component | Meaning                                               |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **眼**    | Eye - the basic organ of sight                        |
| **睛**    | Clear; bright; pupil - representing clarity of vision |

Together they create: "clear eyes" or "bright vision organs" - emphasizing both the physical organ
and its function of clear sight.

## Mnemonic

Think of 眼睛 as **"eyes that see with crystal clarity"**:

- 眼 (yǎn) represents the eye as the organ of sight
- 睛 (jīng) shows the clarity, brightness, and sharpness of vision
- Together: eyes that provide clear, bright vision of the world
- Like having perfectly clear windows to see through
- The combination of the physical organ with its clarity function

This creates the image: **clear, bright windows to perceive the world with perfect vision**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

眼睛 represents **the eyes and vision**. It's used:

1. **Body part**: 闭上眼睛 (bìshàng yǎnjīng) - "close your eyes"
2. **Vision**: 眼睛好 (yǎnjīng hǎo) - "good eyesight"
3. **Appearance**: 大眼睛 (dà yǎnjīng) - "big eyes"
4. **Function**: 保护眼睛 (bǎohù yǎnjīng) - "protect the eyes"

## Examples

- **眼睛疼** (yǎnjīng téng) - "eyes hurt"
- **美丽的眼睛** (měilì de yǎnjīng) - "beautiful eyes"
- **眼睛累** (yǎnjīng lèi) - "eyes are tired"
- **睁开眼睛** (zhēngkāi yǎnjīng) - "open the eyes"
- **揉眼睛** (róu yǎnjīng) - "rub the eyes"

## Vision and Sight

眼睛 related to seeing:

- **用眼睛看** (yòng yǎnjīng kàn) - "look with the eyes"
- **眼睛看见** (yǎnjīng kànjiàn) - "eyes see"
- **眼睛视力** (yǎnjīng shìlì) - "eyesight; vision"
- **保护视力** (bǎohù shìlì) - "protect vision"

## Eye Care and Health

眼睛 maintenance:

- **洗眼睛** (xǐ yǎnjīng) - "wash/rinse eyes"
- **眼睛检查** (yǎnjīng jiǎnchá) - "eye examination"
- **眼睛近视** (yǎnjīng jìnshì) - "nearsightedness"
- **戴眼镜** (dài yǎnjìng) - "wear glasses"

## Cultural Significance

眼睛 in Chinese culture represents:

**Spiritual and Emotional:**

- **心灵之窗** (xīnlíng zhī chuāng) - "windows to the soul"
- **眼睛会说话** (yǎnjīng huì shuōhuà) - "eyes can speak"
- **明亮的眼睛** (míngliàng de yǎnjīng) - Symbol of intelligence and alertness
- **炯炯有神** (jiǒngjiǒng yǒushén) - "bright and spirited eyes"

**Traditional Beliefs:**

- **眼观六路** (yǎn guān liù lù) - "eyes observe all directions" (be alert)
- **慧眼识珠** (huì yǎn shí zhū) - "wise eyes recognize pearls" (good judgment)
- **一见钟情** (yī jiàn zhōng qíng) - "love at first sight"

## Common Expressions

- **眼不见为净** (yǎn bù jiàn wéi jìng) - "out of sight, out of mind"
- **眼花缭乱** (yǎn huā liáo luàn) - "dazzled; overwhelmed by what one sees"
- **睁一只眼闭一只眼** (zhēng yī zhī yǎn bì yī zhī yǎn) - "turn a blind eye"
- **眼见为实** (yǎn jiàn wéi shí) - "seeing is believing"

## Eye Descriptions

眼睛 characteristics:

- **大眼睛** (dà yǎnjīng) - "big eyes"
- **小眼睛** (xiǎo yǎnjīng) - "small eyes"
- **黑眼睛** (hēi yǎnjīng) - "black eyes; dark eyes"
- **明亮眼睛** (míngliàng yǎnjīng) - "bright eyes"

## Modern Eye Health

眼睛 in contemporary context:

- **电脑眼** (diànnǎo yǎn) - "computer vision syndrome"
- **手机眼** (shǒujī yǎn) - "phone eye strain"
- **蓝光伤害** (lánguāng shānghài) - "blue light damage"
- **护眼模式** (hù yǎn móshì) - "eye protection mode"

## Eye Movements and Actions

眼睛 activities:

- **眨眼睛** (zhǎ yǎnjīng) - "blink eyes"
- **转眼睛** (zhuàn yǎnjīng) - "roll eyes"
- **瞪眼睛** (dèng yǎnjīng) - "stare; glare"
- **眯眼睛** (mī yǎnjīng) - "squint eyes"

## Emotional Expressions

眼睛 showing feelings:

- **哭红眼睛** (kū hóng yǎnjīng) - "cry until eyes are red"
- **眼睛发亮** (yǎnjīng fā liàng) - "eyes light up"
- **眼睛湿润** (yǎnjīng shīrùn) - "eyes become moist"
- **眼神温柔** (yǎnshén wēnróu) - "gentle look in eyes"

## Grammar Functions

- **Subject**: 眼睛很美 (yǎnjīng hěn měi) - "the eyes are beautiful"
- **Object**: 保护眼睛 (bǎohù yǎnjīng) - "protect the eyes"
- **Body part**: 我的眼睛 (wǒ de yǎnjīng) - "my eyes"

## Learning Notes

眼睛 is essential because:

- Fundamental body part for describing appearance and health
- Essential for expressing vision, sight, and perception
- Key to understanding Chinese cultural concepts about eyes and soul
- Important for health discussions and eye care
- Demonstrates how compound words specify body parts with enhanced meaning

眼睛 reflects the Chinese understanding that eyes are not just organs of sight but clear, bright
windows that reveal inner character and enable us to perceive the world with clarity and wisdom!
