**🗣️ Pronunciation of 十 (shí)**

- **Pinyin:** shí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a rising tone
- **shí** sounds like **"shee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking: **"shí?"** — that upward inflection like a question.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 十 (shí) - "ten"
- 十分 (shí fēn) - "very; completely"
- 十一 (shí yī) - "eleven"
- 二十 (èr shí) - "twenty"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

十 means "ten" — the rising tone sounds like counting up: "eight, nine, ten?" with that upward
questioning tone!
