**🗣️ Pronunciation of 下 (xià)**

- **Pinyin:** xià
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (softer than English "sh")
- **ià** sounds like **"yah"** with a sharp falling tone
- **xià** sounds like **"shyah!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a command: **"xià!"** — that's the tone pattern of **xià**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 下 (xià) - "under; below; down"
- 下面 (xià miàn) - "below; underneath"
- 下午 (xià wǔ) - "afternoon"
- 下雨 (xià yǔ) - "to rain"
- 下车 (xià chē) - "get off (vehicle)"

**💡 Special Notes:**

下 is a versatile directional word meaning "down," "under," or "below." It's also used in many
compound words and can function as a verb meaning "to go down" or "to get off." The character
visually represents the concept of "below" with its downward-pointing stroke.
