In or to a lower place or position; below; under; down.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                      |
| -------------- | ------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | xià                       |
| Core meaning   | below; under; down        |
| Part of speech | preposition; noun; verb   |
| Tone           | fourth tone (falling)     |

## Visual Breakdown

下 represents **the concept of "below" or "underneath"**.

| Component | Visual Description                                   |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **一**    | Horizontal line representing a reference point/surface |
| **丅**    | Downward pointing element showing direction below    |

## Mnemonic

Think of 下 as **an arrow pointing down below a line**:

- The top horizontal stroke (一) represents a surface or reference point
- The bottom part shows something pointing or moving downward
- Like an arrow on a sign pointing to the floor below
- Or water dripping down from a table surface
- The character visually shows the concept of "lower than" or "beneath"

## Usage Examples

- **楼下** (lóu xià) - "downstairs"
- **桌子下面** (zhuō zi xià miàn) - "under the table"
- **下雨** (xià yǔ) - "rain (literally: sky sends down water)"
- **下车** (xià chē) - "get off a vehicle"
- **下次** (xià cì) - "next time"

## Grammar Functions

下 serves multiple roles:

- **Spatial preposition**: 在...下 - "under/below..."
- **Direction indicator**: 往下走 - "go down"
- **Time sequence**: 下个月 - "next month"
- **Action prefix**: 下载 (download), 下降 (descend)

## Directional Concepts

下 in spatial relationships:

- **Physical position**: Below, underneath, beneath
- **Hierarchy**: Lower rank, subordinate position
- **Sequence**: Following, subsequent (下一个 - "next one")
- **Movement**: Downward motion, descent

## Cultural Context

下 reflects important Chinese concepts:

- **Humility**: "下" position shows respect and modesty
- **Order**: Understanding of hierarchical relationships
- **Flow**: Natural movement from high to low (like water)
- **Temporal sequence**: Orderly progression of time and events
