**🗣️ Pronunciation of 身 (shēn)**

- **Pinyin:** shēn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **ēn** sounds like **"un"** in "fun" but with first tone → steady and high
- **shēn** sounds like **"shun"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — like the constant presence of your **body**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 身 (shēn) - "body; oneself"
- 身体 (shēn tǐ) - "body; health"
- 身边 (shēn biān) - "beside one; at one's side"
- 身份 (shēn fèn) - "identity; status"
- 全身 (quán shēn) - "whole body"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

身 means "body" — the **steady** first tone represents the constant, unchanging presence of your physical self!
