**🗣️ Pronunciation of 诉 (sù)**

- **Pinyin:** sù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like a firm command: **"soo!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see" — crisp and clear
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "zoo" but with a falling tone
- **sù** sounds like **"soo!"** with a decisive drop

**🔊 Key Sound Tips:**

The **s** in Chinese is **clean and sharp** — make sure your tongue doesn't touch your teeth, keep
it behind your lower teeth for a crisp sound.

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Start **high and drop down sharply** like giving a firm command: **"sù!"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 诉 (sù) - "accuse; tell; complain"
- 告诉 (gào sù) - "tell; inform"
- 起诉 (qǐ sù) - "sue; prosecute"
- 诉说 (sù shuō) - "tell; relate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think **"sue!"** with that sharp falling tone — like firmly declaring you're going to sue someone!
