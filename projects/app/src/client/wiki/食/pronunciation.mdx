**🗣️ Pronunciation of 食 (shí)**

- **Pinyin:** shí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a rising tone
- **shí** sounds like **"she"** with an upward inflection

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone. Start lower and rise up, like when you're asking
"Really?" in a questioning voice.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 食物 (shí wù) - "food"
- 食品 (shí pǐn) - "food products"
- 美食 (měi shí) - "delicious food"
- 主食 (zhǔ shí) - "staple food"
- 食堂 (shí táng) - "dining hall, cafeteria"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you see delicious food and ask "What food is that?" - the questioning tone matches the rising
second tone of **shí**.
