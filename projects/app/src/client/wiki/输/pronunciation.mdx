**🗣️ Pronunciation of 输 (shū)**

- **Pinyin:** shū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "moon" but held steady and high
- **shū** sounds like **"shoo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — even when talking about losing, the tone stays constant.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 输 (shū) - "lose; be defeated"
- 输了 (shū le) - "lost; was defeated"
- 输入 (shū rù) - "input; enter"
- 输出 (shū chū) - "output; export"
- 运输 (yùn shū) - "transport; shipping"

**📍 Usage Note:**

输 has two main meanings: "lose/be defeated" in games/competitions, and "transport/transmit" in technical contexts.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

输 means "lose" — even though losing feels bad, the **steady** first tone keeps your voice composed!
