**🗣️ Pronunciation of 境 (jìng)**

- **Pinyin:** jìng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Jing!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but more forward tongue position)
- **ìng** sounds like **"ing"** with a sharp falling tone
- **jìng** sounds like **"jing"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is **authoritative and falling**:

Start **high and drop decisively** — like declaring a boundary: **"jìng!"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 境 (jìng) - "boundary"
- 环境 (huán jìng) - "environment"
- 境界 (jìng jiè) - "realm"
- 出境 (chū jìng) - "exit the country"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **境** as drawing a firm **boundary** — the sharp falling fourth tone represents the
decisive line being drawn!
