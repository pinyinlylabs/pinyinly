**🗣️ Pronunciation of 彐 (jì)**

- **Pinyin:** jì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **jì** sounds like **"jee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're pointing something out decisively: **"jì!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone of
**jì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 彐 (jì) - "snout" (radical form, rarely used alone)
- Used as a component in characters like:
- 寻 (xún) - "seek" (contains 彐)
- 当 (dāng) - "serve as" (contains 彐)

**💡 Special Notes:**

彐 is primarily used as a radical component in other characters rather than as a standalone
character. It represents the concept of a "snout" or protruding part.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

彐 represents a "snout" — the sharp fourth tone is like the decisive action of an animal **poking**
its snout forward!
