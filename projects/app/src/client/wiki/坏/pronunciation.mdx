**🗣️ Pronunciation of 坏 (huài)**

- **Pinyin:** huài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Bad!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uài** sounds like **"why"**, but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **huài** sounds like **"hwy!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're declaring something is bad: **"huài!"** — that's the falling tone of **huài**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 坏 (huài) - "bad; broken; spoiled"
- 坏人 (huài rén) - "bad person"
- 坏事 (huài shì) - "bad thing; trouble"
- 破坏 (pò huài) - "destroy; damage"
- 坏掉 (huài diào) - "broken; spoiled"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **坏** (bad) like firmly declaring something is wrong: **"huài!"** — that's the **fourth
tone** decisive judgment!
