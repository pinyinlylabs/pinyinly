**🗣️ Pronunciation of 脏 (zāng)**

- **Pinyin:** zāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"z"** in "zoo"
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** held steady and high
- **zāng** sounds like **"zahng"** with a level, sustained tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're stating something plainly: **"zāng"** — that's the steady, level tone pattern of
**zāng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 脏 (zāng) - "dirty; filthy"
- 脏话 (zāng huà) - "dirty words; profanity"
- 弄脏 (nòng zāng) - "make dirty; soil"
- 很脏 (hěn zāng) - "very dirty"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

脏 means dirty or filthy, and the flat first tone conveys the straightforward, unpleasant reality of
something being dirty!
