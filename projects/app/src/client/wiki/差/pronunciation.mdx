**🗣️ Pronunciation of 差 (chà)**

- **Pinyin:** chà
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Cha!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair" with a puff of air
- **à** sounds like **"ah"** with a sharp falling tone
- **chà** sounds like **"cha!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're expressing disappointment: **"chà!"** — that's the decisive energy of **chà**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 差 (chà) - "poor; bad; lacking"
- 差不多 (chà bu duō) - "almost; about the same"
- 差别 (chā bié) - "difference" (note: different tone here)
- 差点儿 (chà diǎn er) - "almost; nearly"
- 相差 (xiāng chà) - "to differ by"

**💡 Special Notes:**

差 has multiple pronunciations depending on meaning:

- **chà** (4th tone) - "poor; lacking"
- **chā** (1st tone) - "difference; to differ"
- **chāi** (1st tone) - "to send on errands"
