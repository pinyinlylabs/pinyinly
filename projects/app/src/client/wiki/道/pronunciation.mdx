**🗣️ Pronunciation of 道 (dào)**

- **Pinyin:** dào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "do"
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with a sharp falling tone
- **dào** sounds like **"dow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying **"Way!"** when pointing out a path: **"dào!"** — that's
the tone pattern of **dào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 道 (dào) - "path; road; way"
- 道路 (dào lù) - "road; path"
- 道理 (dào lǐ) - "reason; logic"
- 知道 (zhī dào) - "to know"
- 说道 (shuō dào) - "to say; to talk about"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

A **path** or **way** leads somewhere definite — the falling tone shows that clear direction and
purpose!
