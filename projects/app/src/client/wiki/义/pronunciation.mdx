**🗣️ Pronunciation of 义 (yì)**

- **Pinyin:** yì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Yes!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **yì** sounds like **"yee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **sharply**:

Say it with conviction, like declaring something important — that fits the meaning of **"justice"**!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 义 (yì) - "justice, righteousness"
- 义务 (yì wù) - "duty, obligation"
- 意义 (yì yì) - "meaning, significance"
- 正义 (zhèng yì) - "justice"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **justice** being declared with authority — that sharp falling tone matches the strength of
the concept!
