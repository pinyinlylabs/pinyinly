**🗣️ Pronunciation of 仅 (jǐn)**

- **Pinyin:** jǐn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jar"
- **ǐn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **jǐn** sounds like **"jeen"** with a dip-then-rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 仅 (jǐn) - "only", "merely"
- 仅仅 (jǐn jǐn) - "only", "just"
- 仅有 (jǐn yǒu) - "only have"
- 不仅 (bù jǐn) - "not only"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

仅 means **only** - say **"jeen"** with the thoughtful third tone, like you're carefully considering
something limited or exclusive!
