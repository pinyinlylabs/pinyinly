**🗣️ Pronunciation of 演 (yǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** yǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǎn** sounds like **"yahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully considering a performance: **"yǎn..."** — that contemplative tone
pattern of **yǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 演 (yǎn) - "perform; act"
- 演出 (yǎn chū) - "performance; show"
- 演员 (yǎn yuán) - "actor; performer"
- 演唱 (yǎn chàng) - "sing; perform songs"
- 表演 (biǎo yǎn) - "perform; performance"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When watching a **performance**, you might thoughtfully nod and say **"Yeah..."** — that
contemplative tone matches the third tone of **yǎn**!
