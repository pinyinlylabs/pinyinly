**🗣️ Pronunciation of 猫 (māo)**

- **Pinyin:** māo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "mouse"
- **āo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but held steady and high
- **māo** sounds like **"mow"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like a cat's meow: **"māo!"** — keep that high, steady tone like a cat calling.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 猫 (māo) - "cat"
- 小猫 (xiǎo māo) - "kitten; small cat"
- 猫咪 (māo mī) - "kitty" (cute/informal)
- 熊猫 (xióng māo) - "panda"
- 猫头鹰 (māo tóu yīng) - "owl"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **猫** as imitating a cat's high meow: **"māo!"** — that high, sustained sound matches the
**first tone**!
