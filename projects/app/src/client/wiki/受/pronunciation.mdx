**🗣️ Pronunciation of 受 (shòu)**

- **Pinyin:** shòu
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **òu** sounds like **"oh"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **shòu** sounds like **"show"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're being firm or definitive: **"shòu!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern of
**shòu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 受 (shòu) - "suffer; receive"
- 受伤 (shòu shāng) - "get injured"
- 受到 (shòu dào) - "receive; be subjected to"
- 接受 (jiē shòu) - "accept; receive"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"show"** with a sharp drop — when you **suffer** or **receive** something, it often comes
down on you suddenly!
