**🗣️ Pronunciation of 叫 (jiào)**

- **Pinyin:** jiào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but softer, like "gee")
- **iào** sounds like **"yow"** (like "ouch!") but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **jiào** sounds like **"jyow"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're calling out loudly: **"jiào!"** — that's the commanding tone pattern of **jiào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 叫 (jiào) - "call; shout; be called"
- 叫什么 (jiào shén me) - "what is it called?"
- 我叫... (wǒ jiào...) - "my name is..."
- 大叫 (dà jiào) - "shout loudly"
- 叫声 (jiào shēng) - "cry; call; shout"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"jyow"** with a sharp drop — when you **call** someone, you often use a strong, falling
intonation to get their attention!
