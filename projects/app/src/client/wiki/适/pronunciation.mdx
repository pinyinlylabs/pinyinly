**🗣️ Pronunciation of 适 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **shì** sounds like **"shee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying **"Fit!"** when something matches perfectly: **"shì!"** —
that's the tone pattern of **shì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 适 (shì) - "suitable; appropriate"
- 适合 (shì hé) - "to suit; to fit"
- 适应 (shì yìng) - "to adapt"
- 适用 (shì yòng) - "applicable"
- 适当 (shì dāng) - "appropriate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When something is **suitable**, it fits perfectly — the decisive falling tone shows that perfect
match!
