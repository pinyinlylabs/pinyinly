**🗣️ Pronunciation of 带 (dài)**

- **Pinyin:** dài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "day"
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"** but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **dài** sounds like **"dye"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** is a **sharp falling** tone, like giving a firm command:

Say it like instructing someone to **bring** something: **"dài!"** — that decisive drop is the
**fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 带 (dài) - "carry; bring; belt"
- 带来 (dài lái) - "bring"
- 带走 (dài zǒu) - "take away"
- 皮带 (pí dài) - "belt"
- 领带 (lǐng dài) - "necktie"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **带** as a firm command to **carry** something — that decisive **fourth tone** shows
authority!
