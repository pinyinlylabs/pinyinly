**🗣️ Pronunciation of 乡 (xiāng)**

- **Pinyin:** xiāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** — like **"sh"** but with tongue tip down and more hissing (see the "x" guide in 习)
- **iāng** sounds like **"yahng"** with a steady high tone
- **xiāng** sounds like **"shyahng"** held steady and high

**🎯 The "xiāng" combination:**

The **x + iāng** creates a unique sound:

- **x** is that hissing "sh" sound
- **iāng** has a nasal **"ahng"** ending
- Together: a steady, high **"shyahng"**

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — like singing a sustained note about your
**hometown**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 乡 (xiāng) - "township, countryside"
- 家乡 (jiā xiāng) - "hometown, home village"
- 乡村 (xiāng cūn) - "rural area, countryside"
- 老乡 (lǎo xiāng) - "fellow villager"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Hometown** — the steady first tone is like a long, fond memory of your **xiāng**!
