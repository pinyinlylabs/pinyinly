**🗣️ Pronunciation of 帮 (bāng)**

- **Pinyin:** bāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Bahng"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bang"
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** but held steady and high
- **bāng** sounds like **"bahng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (First tone: ˉ)**

The **first tone** is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like offering to **help**: **"bāng..."** — that steady, helpful tone is the **first tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 帮 (bāng) - "help; assist"
- 帮助 (bāng zhù) - "help; assistance"
- 帮忙 (bāng máng) - "help; give a hand"
- 帮派 (bāng pài) - "gang; faction"
- 帮工 (bāng gōng) - "helper; assistant"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **帮** as offering **help** with a steady, reliable voice — that's the **first tone**
dependability!
