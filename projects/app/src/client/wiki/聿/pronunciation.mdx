**🗣️ Pronunciation of 聿 (yù)**

- **Pinyin:** yù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **yù** sounds like **"yoo!"** with a decisive falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a firm statement: **"yù!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern of **yù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 聿 (yù) - "brush for writing"
- Used as a radical in other characters related to writing or recording

**💡 Memory Tip:**

聿 represents a writing brush, and the sharp fourth tone mimics the decisive stroke of putting brush
to paper!
