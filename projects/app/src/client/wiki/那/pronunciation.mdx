**🗣️ Pronunciation of 那 (nà)**

- **Pinyin:** nà
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "nice"
- **à** sounds like **"ah"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **nà** sounds like **"nah!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like pointing and saying "That one!" firmly: **"nà!"** — that's the
tone pattern of **nà**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 那 (nà) - "that"
- 那个 (nà ge) - "that one"
- 那里 (nà li) - "there"
- 那时候 (nà shí hou) - "at that time"
- 那边 (nà biān) - "over there"

**💡 Note:**

那 is one of the most frequently used demonstrative pronouns in Chinese, contrasting with 这 (zhè)
meaning "this".
