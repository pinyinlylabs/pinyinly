**🗣️ Pronunciation of 束 (shù)**

- **Pinyin:** shù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **shù** sounds like **"shoo!"** with a sharp falling tone

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 束 (shù) - "bundle; bunch"
- 一束花 (yī shù huā) - "a bouquet of flowers"
- 束缚 (shù fù) - "to restrain; to bind"
- 光束 (guāng shù) - "beam of light"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Bundling** requires a firm, decisive action — that's the sharp fourth tone of **shù**!
