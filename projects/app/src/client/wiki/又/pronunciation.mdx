**🗣️ Pronunciation of 又 (yòu)**

- **Pinyin:** yòu
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **òu** sounds like **"oh"** in "go", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **yòu** sounds like **"yo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Say it with emphasis, like making a point: **"yòu!"** — that's the definitive, falling pattern of
**yòu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 又是 (yòu shì) - "again"
- 又一次 (yòu yī cì) - "once again"
- 又大又好 (yòu dà yòu hǎo) - "both big and good"
- 又来了 (yòu lái le) - "here we go again"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **又** as **again** — the falling fourth tone emphasizes the repetition, like saying "There
it is **again**!"
