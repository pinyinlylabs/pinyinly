**🗣️ Pronunciation of 黄 (huáng)**

- **Pinyin:** huáng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **hu** like **"who"** but shorter
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** with a rising tone, similar to "hung" but with an "ah" sound
- **huáng** sounds like **"hwahng"** with a rising pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about the color: **"huáng?"** — that rising, questioning intonation when
you see something yellow.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 黄 (huáng) - "yellow"
- 黄色 (huáng sè) - "yellow color"
- 黄金 (huáng jīn) - "gold"
- 黄河 (huáng hé) - "Yellow River"
- 黄瓜 (huáng guā) - "cucumber"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

黄 sounds like "hwang" with a rising tone — imagine pointing at something yellow and asking
**"huáng?"** with that questioning rise, like "Yellow?"
