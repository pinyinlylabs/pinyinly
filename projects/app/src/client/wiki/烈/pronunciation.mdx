**🗣️ Pronunciation of 烈 (liè)**

- **Pinyin:** liè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **iè** sounds like **"yeh"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **liè** sounds like **"lyeh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're reacting to intense heat: **"liè!"** — that sharp, definitive falling tone
pattern of **liè**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 烈 (liè) - "fiery; intense; fierce"
- 强烈 (qiáng liè) - "strong; intense"
- 热烈 (rè liè) - "warm; enthusiastic"
- 烈日 (liè rì) - "scorching sun"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you feel something **fiery** or intense, you might exclaim **"Yikes!"** with that sharp,
falling tone — just like **liè**!
