**🗣️ Pronunciation of 高 (gāo)**

- **Pinyin:** gāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note held high

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **āo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with first tone → steady and high
- **gāo** sounds like **"gow"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone: Keep your voice steady and high, like reaching
for something up high: **"gāo"** — that's the elevated tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 高 (gāo) - "tall; high"
- 高兴 (gāo xìng) - "happy"
- 高中 (gāo zhōng) - "high school"
- 高级 (gāo jí) - "high level; advanced"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When something is **tall** or **high**, it reaches up — just like the high, steady tone of 高!
