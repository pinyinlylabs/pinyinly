**🗣️ Pronunciation of 区 (qū)**

- **Pinyin:** qū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but with rounded lips)
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "too" but held steady and high
- **qū** sounds like **"choo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're confidently stating: **"qū!"** — that level, sustained high tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 区 (qū) - "area"
- 地区 (dì qū) - "region"
- 区别 (qū bié) - "difference"
- 小区 (xiǎo qū) - "residential area"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

区 means "area" — the steady first tone is like clearly defining the boundaries of an area!
