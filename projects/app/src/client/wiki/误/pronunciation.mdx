**🗣️ Pronunciation of 误 (wù)**

- **Pinyin:** wù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like a firm command: **"woo!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way" — round your lips like you're going to whistle
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "zoo" but with a falling tone
- **wù** sounds like **"woo!"** with a decisive drop

**🔊 Key Sound Tips:**

The **w** in Chinese:

1. **Round your lips** — make a small circle with your lips, like starting to whistle
2. **Brief transition** — quickly move from "w" to the "ù" vowel
3. **Don't over-emphasize** — it's a gentle "w" sound, not too strong

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Start **high and drop down sharply** like giving a firm command: **"wù!"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 误 (wù) - "mistake; error; misunderstand"
- 错误 (cuò wù) - "mistake; error"
- 误会 (wù huì) - "misunderstanding"
- 耽误 (dān wù) - "delay; hold up"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think **"woops!"** but with that sharp falling tone — like firmly acknowledging "woops, I made a
mistake!"
