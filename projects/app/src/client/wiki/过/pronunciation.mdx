**🗣️ Pronunciation of 过 (guò)**

- **Pinyin:** guò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Go!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uò** sounds like **"woh"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling tone
- **guò** sounds like **"gwoh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a command to pass through: **"guò!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone pattern of
**guò**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 过去 (guò qù) - "past; to pass"
- 过来 (guò lái) - "to come over"
- 经过 (jīng guò) - "to pass through; experience"
- 通过 (tōng guò) - "to pass through; by means of"
- 过年 (guò nián) - "to celebrate New Year"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

过 means **to pass** — pronounce it with a decisive **guò** like commanding someone to "pass
through!"
