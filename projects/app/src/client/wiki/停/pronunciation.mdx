**🗣️ Pronunciation of 停 (tíng)**

- **Pinyin:** tíng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like the English **"t"** in "top", but **stronger and more aspirated** — with a clear puff
  of air
- **íng** sounds like **"eeng"** but with a rising tone
- **tíng** sounds like **"teeng?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"tíng?"** — that's the tone pattern of **tíng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 停 (tíng) - "stop"
- 停车 (tíng chē) - "park (a car)"
- 停止 (tíng zhǐ) - "cease"
- 停下 (tíng xià) - "stop (come to a halt)"
