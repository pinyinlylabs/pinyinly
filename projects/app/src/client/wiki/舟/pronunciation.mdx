**🗣️ Pronunciation of 舟 (zhōu)**

- **Pinyin:** zhōu
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady boat gliding on calm water

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" (retroflex zh sound)
- **ōu** sounds like **"oh"** in "go", held steady and high
- **zhōu** sounds like **"joe"** with a smooth, steady pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and flat**:

Keep it smooth and steady, like a boat floating peacefully: **"zhōūū"** — calm and even.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 舟 (zhōu) - "boat; small vessel"
- 小舟 (xiǎo zhōu) - "small boat"
- 扁舟 (piān zhōu) - "small flat boat"
- 舟楫 (zhōu jí) - "boat and oars"

**💡 Cultural Note:**

舟 (zhōu) refers to traditional boats, especially smaller vessels. It appears in classical Chinese
poetry and is also used as a radical in characters related to boats and water transport.
