**🗣️ Pronunciation of 修 (xiū)**

- **Pinyin:** xiū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (but with tongue tip down)
- **iū** sounds like **"yo"** in "yo-yo", but with first tone → steady and high
- **xiū** sounds like **"shyo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like singing a steady high note: **"xiū¯"** — that's the tone pattern of **xiū**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 修 (xiū) - "to repair; to fix; to study"
- 修理 (xiū lǐ) - "to repair"
- 修改 (xiū gǎi) - "to modify; to revise"
- 装修 (zhuāng xiū) - "to renovate"
- 修习 (xiū xí) - "to study; to practice"
