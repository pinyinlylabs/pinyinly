**🗣️ Pronunciation of 玉 (yù)**

- **Pinyin:** yù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like making a firm statement

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ù** sounds like **"yu"** in "yule" but with sharp falling tone
- **yù** sounds like **"yu!"** with decisive drop

**🎯 Mastering the "ù" final:**

The **ù** sound:

1. **Start with "u"** like "oo" in "boot"
2. **Keep it pure** — no glide or extra sounds
3. **Sharp fourth tone** — drop decisively from high to low
4. **Brief and crisp** — don't linger on the sound

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is **authoritative and falling**:

Start **high and drop sharply** — like stating a fact: **"yù!"** (It's jade!)

**📝 Practice words:**

- 玉 (yù) - "jade"
- 玉米 (yù mǐ) - "corn"
- 玉石 (yù shí) - "jade stone"
- 白玉 (bái yù) - "white jade"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of holding a precious piece of **jade** — you say "yù!" with authority and certainty, like
declaring its value with that sharp, falling tone.
