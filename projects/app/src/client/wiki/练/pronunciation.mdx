**🗣️ Pronunciation of 练 (liàn)**

- **Pinyin:** liàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "low"
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** in "yen", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **liàn** sounds like **"lyen!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command to practice: **"liàn!"** — that's the sharp falling pattern
of **liàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 练 (liàn) - "practice/train"
- 练习 (liàn xí) - "practice/exercise"
- 训练 (xùn liàn) - "training"
- 教练 (jiào liàn) - "coach"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you **practice**, you need discipline and determination — just like the firm, decisive fourth
tone of **liàn**!
