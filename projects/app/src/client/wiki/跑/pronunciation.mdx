**🗣️ Pronunciation of 跑 (pǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** pǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pat" (unaspirated)
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **pǎo** sounds like **"pow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about running: **"pǎo..."** — that's the tone pattern of **pǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 跑 (pǎo) - "run"
- 跑步 (pǎo bù) - "jog; go jogging"
- 跑车 (pǎo chē) - "sports car"
- 跑道 (pǎo dào) - "runway; track"
- 跑腿 (pǎo tuǐ) - "run errands"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

跑 means "run" — the **dipping and rising** tone mimics the up-and-down motion of running!
