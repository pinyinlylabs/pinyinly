**🗣️ Pronunciation of 欠 (qiàn)**

- **Pinyin:** qiàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but more aspirated)
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** with a sharp falling tone
- **qiàn** sounds like **"chyen"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're stating a debt firmly: **"qiàn!"** — that's the sharp downward tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 欠债 (qiàn zhài) - "to owe debt"
- 欠缺 (qiàn quē) - "to lack; to be deficient"
- 亏欠 (kuī qiàn) - "to owe; to be indebted"
- 欠款 (qiàn kuǎn) - "debt; money owed"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

欠 means "to owe" — say it with that **sharp falling** tone like you're acknowledging a serious
debt!
