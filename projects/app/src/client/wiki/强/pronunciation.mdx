**🗣️ Pronunciation of 强 (qiáng)**

- **Pinyin:** qiáng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (aspirated)
- **iáng** sounds like **"yahng"** but with second tone → rising up
- **qiáng** sounds like **"chahng?"** with a rising tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're impressed by someone's strength: **"qiáng?"** — that's the rising, admiring tone
of **qiáng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 强 (qiáng) - "strong/powerful"
- 强壮 (qiáng zhuàng) - "strong/robust"
- 坚强 (jiān qiáng) - "strong/resilient"
- 强调 (qiáng diào) - "emphasize"
- 增强 (zēng qiáng) - "strengthen"

**💡 Special Notes:**

强 can also be pronounced **qiǎng** (third tone) meaning "force/compel":

- 勉强 (miǎn qiǎng) - "reluctant/forced"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

强 means "strong" — the rising second tone sounds like expressing admiration for someone's
**impressive strength**!
