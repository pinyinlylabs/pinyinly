**🗣️ Pronunciation of 拉 (lā)**

- **Pinyin:** lā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **ā** sounds like **"ah"** in "father", held steady and high
- **lā** sounds like **"lah"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high, steady** tone:

Keep your voice high and level: **"lā"** — like singing a sustained high note or saying "la" in a
musical scale.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 拉 (lā) - "to pull; to drag"
- 拉手 (lā shǒu) - "to hold hands"
- 拉开 (lā kāi) - "to pull open"
- 拉小提琴 (lā xiǎo tí qín) - "to play the violin"
- 拉链 (lā liàn) - "zipper"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the steady effort needed when **pulling** something — that consistent, sustained tone is
like **lā**.
