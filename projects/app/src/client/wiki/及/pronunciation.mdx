**🗣️ Pronunciation of 及 (jí)**

- **Pinyin:** jí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with rising tone
- **jí** sounds like **"jee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're checking: **"jí?"** — that's the upward, questioning pattern of **jí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 及时 (jí shí) - "timely"
- 以及 (yǐ jí) - "as well as"
- 普及 (pǔ jí) - "popularize"
- 涉及 (shè jí) - "involve"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **及** as **extend** or reach — the rising tone suggests reaching upward or extending
outward!
