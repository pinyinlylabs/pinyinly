**🗣️ Pronunciation of 春 (chūn)**

- **Pinyin:** chūn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair" (but with tongue curled back)
- **ūn** sounds like **"oon"** in "spoon" but held steady and high
- **chūn** sounds like **"choon"** with a steady high pitch

**🎯 Mastering the "ch" sound:**

The **ch** in Chinese is different from English "ch":

1. **Curl your tongue back** — tip pointing toward the roof of your mouth
2. **More breath** — make it aspirated (you should feel air on your hand)
3. **It's closer to "chr"** than plain "ch"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 春 (chūn) - "spring"
- 春天 (chūn tiān) - "spring; springtime"
- 春节 (chūn jié) - "Spring Festival; Chinese New Year"
