**🗣️ Pronunciation of 剧 (jù)**

- **Pinyin:** jù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "zoo", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **jù** sounds like **"joo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're dramatically announcing something intense: **"jù!"** — that's the tone pattern of
**jù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 剧 (jù) - "drama; play; severe"
- 剧场 (jù chǎng) - "theater; playhouse"
- 电视剧 (diàn shì jù) - "TV drama"
- 话剧 (huà jù) - "stage play"
- 京剧 (jīng jù) - "Peking opera"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

剧 means "drama/severe" — imagine the dramatic **falling** tone as the intensity of a theatrical
performance!
