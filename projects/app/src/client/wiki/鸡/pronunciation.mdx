**🗣️ Pronunciation of 鸡 (jī)**

- **Pinyin:** jī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but softer, similar to "gee" without the hard "g" sound
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **jī** sounds like **"gee"** with a steady high pitch, like the sound a chicken makes: "ji ji ji!"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're imitating the sound a chicken makes: **"jī jī jī!"** — that steady, high-pitched
sound.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 鸡 (jī) - "chicken"
- 鸡蛋 (jī dàn) - "chicken egg"
- 公鸡 (gōng jī) - "rooster"
- 母鸡 (mǔ jī) - "hen"
- 小鸡 (xiǎo jī) - "chick"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

鸡 sounds exactly like the onomatopoeia for a chicken sound in Chinese: **"jī jī jī!"** — just like
how chickens cluck!
