**🗣️ Pronunciation of 持 (chí)**

- **Pinyin:** chí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair" but with tongue curled back
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with rising tone
- **chí** sounds like **"chee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Really?" in English: **"chí?"** — start lower and rise up.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 持 (chí) - "to hold; to maintain; to sustain"
- 持续 (chí xù) - "to continue; to persist"
- 保持 (bǎo chí) - "to maintain; to keep"
- 坚持 (jiān chí) - "to persist; to insist"
- 持有 (chí yǒu) - "to hold; to possess"

**💡 Pronunciation Note:**

The **ch** sound is a retroflex consonant - curl your tongue tip back towards the roof of your mouth
when making the sound.
