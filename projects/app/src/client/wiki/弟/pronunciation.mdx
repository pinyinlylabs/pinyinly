**🗣️ Pronunciation of 弟 (dì)**

- **Pinyin:** dì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "day"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **dì** sounds like **"dee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're calling out to someone: **"dì!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone of **dì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 弟 (dì) - "younger brother"
- 弟弟 (dì dì) - "little brother"
- 兄弟 (xiōng dì) - "brothers"
- 师弟 (shī dì) - "junior fellow student"
- 表弟 (biǎo dì) - "younger male cousin"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

弟 means "younger brother" — the sharp fourth tone sounds like calling out **"little brother!"** to
get his attention!
