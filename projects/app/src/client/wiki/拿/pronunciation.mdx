**🗣️ Pronunciation of 拿 (ná)**

- **Pinyin:** ná
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "nice"
- **á** sounds like **"ah"** in "father", but with rising tone
- **ná** sounds like **"nah?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Really?" in English: **"ná?"** — start lower and rise up.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 拿 (ná) - "to take; to hold; to carry"
- 拿出 (ná chū) - "to take out"
- 拿到 (ná dào) - "to get; to obtain"
- 拿走 (ná zǒu) - "to take away"
- 拿手 (ná shǒu) - "to be good at; specialty"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you **take** something, you might ask "Can I take this?" — that questioning, rising intonation
is like **ná**.
