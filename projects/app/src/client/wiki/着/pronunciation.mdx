**🗣️ Pronunciation of 着**

着 has **three different pronunciations** depending on its usage:

**📍 zhe (neutral tone) - continuous aspect particle**

- **Pinyin:** zhe (neutral tone)
- **Tone: Neutral tone** — **light and quick**, unstressed

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **e** sounds like **"uh"** but very light and quick
- **zhe** sounds like a quick **"juh"**

**📍 zháo (second tone) - "to touch, to catch"**

- **Pinyin:** zháo
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **áo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with a rising tone
- **zháo** sounds like **"jow?"** with a questioning rise

**📍 zhuó (second tone) - "to wear, to put on"**

- **Pinyin:** zhuó
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, same as above

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **uó** sounds like **"wo"** but with a rising tone
- **zhuó** sounds like **"jwo?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Usage Guide:**

**着 (zhe) - continuous aspect (most common):**

- 他看**着**电视 (tā kàn zhe diàn shì) - "He is watching TV"
- 等**着** (děng zhe) - "waiting" (ongoing action)
- 坐**着** (zuò zhe) - "sitting" (ongoing state)

**着 (zháo) - "to touch, to catch, to succeed":**

- 睡**着**了 (shuì zháo le) - "fell asleep"
- 找不**着** (zhǎo bù zháo) - "can't find"
- **着**火 (zháo huǒ) - "to catch fire"

**着 (zhuó) - "to wear, to put on":**

- **着**装 (zhuó zhuāng) - "to dress, attire"
- 穿**着** (chuān zhuó) - "wearing"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**zhe** = light grammatical particle (like -ing) **zháo** = "got it!" — successful
contact/catching  
**zhuó** = formal "wearing" — think "suit up"
