**🗣️ Pronunciation of 直 (zhí)**

- **Pinyin:** zhí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a rising tone
- **zhí** sounds like **"jee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Straight?" — **"zhí?"** — that's the rising tone pattern of **zhí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 直 (zhí) - "straight"
- 直接 (zhí jiē) - "directly"
- 直到 (zhí dào) - "until"
- 一直 (yì zhí) - "all along; continuously"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"jee?"** with a questioning rise — when asking if something is **"straight"**, you
naturally use a rising tone to question!
