**🗣️ Pronunciation of 其 (qí)**

- **Pinyin:** qí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but with more aspiration)
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a rising tone
- **qí** sounds like **"chee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"qí?"** — that's the tone pattern of **qí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 其 (qí) - "its; his; her; their"
- 其他 (qí tā) - "other; others"
- 其中 (qí zhōng) - "among them; among which"
- 其实 (qí shí) - "actually; in fact"
- 其次 (qí cì) - "secondly; next"

**💡 Special Notes:**

其 consistently uses the second tone (qí) and functions as a pronoun meaning "its," "his," "her," or
"their." It's commonly used in formal or written Chinese and appears in many set phrases and
expressions.
