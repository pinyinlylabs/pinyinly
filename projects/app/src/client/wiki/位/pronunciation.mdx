**🗣️ Pronunciation of 位 (wèi)**

- **Pinyin:** wèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way", but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **wèi** sounds like **"way"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like addressing someone formally: **"wèi!"** — that sharp drop down shows the fourth tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 位 (wèi) - "position; person (polite measure word)"
- 位置 (wèi zhì) - "position; location"
- 座位 (zuò wèi) - "seat"
- 各位 (gè wèi) - "everyone; ladies and gentlemen"
- 地位 (dì wèi) - "status; position"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"way"** with a respectful falling tone — like formally addressing someone's position!
