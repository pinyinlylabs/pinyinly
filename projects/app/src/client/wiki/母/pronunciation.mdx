**🗣️ Pronunciation of 母 (mǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** mǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "mother"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up
- **mǔ** sounds like **"moo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Third tone: ˇ)**

The **third tone** is a **fall–then-rise** tone, like being thoughtful:

Say it like you're thinking about your mother with love: **"mǔ..."** — that contemplative
dip-and-rise is the **third tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 母 (mǔ) - "mother"
- 母亲 (mǔ qīn) - "mother" (formal)
- 母语 (mǔ yǔ) - "mother tongue; native language"
- 母鸡 (mǔ jī) - "hen"
- 父母 (fù mǔ) - "parents"
- 祖母 (zǔ mǔ) - "grandmother (paternal)"

**💡 Special Notes:**

母 is one of the most fundamental family relationship words in Chinese. It's used both as a
standalone word and as a component in compound words related to motherhood, origin, or female
animals.
