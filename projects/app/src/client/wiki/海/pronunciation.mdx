**🗣️ Pronunciation of 海 (hǎi)**

- **Pinyin:** hǎi
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ǎi** sounds like **"eye"**, but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **hǎi** sounds like **"hi"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"hǎi..."** — that's the tone pattern of **hǎi**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 海 (hǎi) - "sea/ocean"
- 海边 (hǎi biān) - "seaside/coast"
- 海水 (hǎi shuǐ) - "seawater"
- 上海 (shàng hǎi) - "Shanghai"
- 海外 (hǎi wài) - "overseas"
- 海关 (hǎi guān) - "customs"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

海 (hǎi) contains the water radical (氵) on the left, which is perfect since the sea is made of
water! The character represents the vast expanse of ocean water.
