**🗣️ Pronunciation of 死 (sǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** sǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"uh"** with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **sǐ** sounds like **"suh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're contemplating mortality: **"sǐ..."** — that thoughtful dip-then-rise pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 死了 (sǐ le) - "died; dead"
- 生死 (shēng sǐ) - "life and death"
- 死亡 (sǐ wáng) - "death; to die"
- 死人 (sǐ rén) - "dead person"
- 该死 (gāi sǐ) - "damn it!" (curse word)

**💡 Character Structure:**

死 contains the 歹 (dǎi) radical, which represents "death/evil," plus 匕 (bǐ) meaning "dagger."
Together they form the concept of death.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

死 means "die" — contemplate it with that **thoughtful dip-and-rise** tone, reflecting the gravity
of the concept!
