**🗣️ Pronunciation of 短 (duǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** duǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **uǎn** sounds like **"wahn"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **duǎn** sounds like **"dwahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're measuring and considering: **"duǎn..."** — that contemplative tone pattern of
**duǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 短 (duǎn) - "short"
- 短期 (duǎn qī) - "short-term"
- 短信 (duǎn xìn) - "text message; SMS"
- 短裤 (duǎn kù) - "shorts"
- 短处 (duǎn chù) - "shortcoming; weakness"
- 很短 (hěn duǎn) - "very short"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"dwahn"** with a contemplative dip-then-rise — when measuring something **short**, you
naturally pause to consider its length!
