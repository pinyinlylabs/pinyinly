**🗣️ Pronunciation of 今 (jīn)**

- **Pinyin:** jīn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jar"
- **īn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen", but with first tone → steady and high
- **jīn** sounds like **"jeen"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 今 (jīn) - "now", "today"
- 今天 (jīn tiān) - "today"
- 今年 (jīn nián) - "this year"
- 今后 (jīn hòu) - "from now on"
- 如今 (rú jīn) - "nowadays"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

今 means **now** or **today** - say **"jeen"** with confidence and steadiness (first tone),
emphasizing the present moment!
