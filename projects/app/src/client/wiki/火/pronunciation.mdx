**🗣️ Pronunciation of 火 (huǒ)**

- **Pinyin:** huǒ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "house"
- **uǒ** sounds like **"wo"** in "whoa" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **huǒ** sounds like **"hwoh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're cautiously acknowledging fire: **"huǒ..."** — that careful, contemplative tone
pattern of **huǒ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 火 (huǒ) - "fire"
- 火车 (huǒ chē) - "train" (literally "fire vehicle")
- 火灾 (huǒ zāi) - "fire disaster"
- 生火 (shēng huǒ) - "make a fire"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you see **fire**, you might cautiously say **"Whoa..."** with that dip-then-rise tone — showing
both concern and acknowledgment, just like **huǒ**!
