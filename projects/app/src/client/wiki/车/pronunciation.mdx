**🗣️ Pronunciation of 车 (chē)**

- **Pinyin:** chē
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (aspirated)
- **ē** sounds like **"eh"** in "meh" but with first tone → steady and high
- **chē** sounds like **"cheh"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — like the constant hum of a **vehicle** engine.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 车 (chē) - "vehicle; car"
- 汽车 (qì chē) - "car; automobile"
- 火车 (huǒ chē) - "train"
- 自行车 (zì xíng chē) - "bicycle"
- 开车 (kāi chē) - "drive a car"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

车 means "vehicle" — the **steady** first tone is like the smooth, constant motion of driving!
