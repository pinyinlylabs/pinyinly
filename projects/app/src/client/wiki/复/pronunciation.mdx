**🗣️ Pronunciation of 复 (fù)**

- **Pinyin:** fù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"Foo!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "food"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **fù** sounds like **"foo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're assertively stating a return: **"fù!"** — that commanding tone is the **fourth
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 复 (fù) - "return; restore"
- 复习 (fù xí) - "to review"
- 复印 (fù yìn) - "to photocopy"
- 复杂 (fù zá) - "complex"
- 恢复 (huī fù) - "to recover"

**💡 Special Notes:**

复 relates to returning, restoring, or doing something again. It's commonly used in educational
contexts (复习 - review) and in words related to repetition or restoration.
