**🗣️ Pronunciation of 畏 (wèi)**

- **Pinyin:** wèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Stop!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **wèi** sounds like **"way!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 畏 (wèi) - "to fear; to be afraid of"
- 畏惧 (wèi jù) - "to fear; to dread"
- 敬畏 (jìng wèi) - "to revere; awe"
- 畏缩 (wèi suō) - "to cower; to shrink back"
- 无所畏惧 (wú suǒ wèi jù) - "fearless"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Fourth tone is like a sharp warning of **danger** - your voice **drops sharply** throughout **wèi**.
