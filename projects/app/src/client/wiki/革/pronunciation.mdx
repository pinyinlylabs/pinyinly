**🗣️ Pronunciation of 革 (gé)**

- **Pinyin:** gé
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **é** sounds like **"eh"** but with second tone → rising up
- **gé** sounds like **"geh?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 革 (gé) - "leather; hide"
- 改革 (gǎi gé) - "reform; revolution"
- 革命 (gé mìng) - "revolution"
- 皮革 (pí gé) - "leather; hide"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **rising tone** sounds like questioning the quality of leather: "Is this good leather?" — **gé**
