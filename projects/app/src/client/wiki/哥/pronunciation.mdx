**🗣️ Pronunciation of 哥 (gē)**

- **Pinyin:** gē
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Geee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go" (but softer, more like "k" sound)
- **ē** sounds like **"uh"** in "duh", held steady and high
- **gē** sounds like **"guh"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and level**:

Say it like calling your big brother affectionately: **"gē!"** — maintain that high, steady pitch.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 哥 (gē) - "older brother"
- 哥哥 (gē ge) - "older brother" (casual)
- 大哥 (dà gē) - "big brother"
- 表哥 (biǎo gē) - "male cousin (older)"
- 堂哥 (táng gē) - "male cousin (older, same surname)"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The character shows **two mouths (口)** stacked — like an older brother singing or speaking with
authority from above!
