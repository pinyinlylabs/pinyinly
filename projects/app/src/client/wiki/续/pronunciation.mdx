**🗣️ Pronunciation of 续 (xù)**

- **Pinyin:** xù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (but with tongue tip down)
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **xù** sounds like **"shoo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like making a firm commitment: **"xù!"** — start high and drop down decisively.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 继续 (jì xù) - "to continue"
- 连续 (lián xù) - "continuous; consecutive"
- 续集 (xù jí) - "sequel"
- 手续 (shǒu xù) - "procedure; formalities"
- 持续 (chí xù) - "to persist; to last"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

To **continue** something requires determination — that firm resolve matches the sharp falling tone!
