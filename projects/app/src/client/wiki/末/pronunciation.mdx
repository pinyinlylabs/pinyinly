**🗣️ Pronunciation of 末 (mò)**

- **Pinyin:** mò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "moon"
- **ò** sounds like **"aw"** in "raw", but with fourth tone → sharp downward fall
- **mò** sounds like **"maw!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're declaring something final: **"mò!"** — that decisive drop is the fourth tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 末 (mò) - "end, final part"
- 末尾 (mò wěi) - "end, tail end"
- 期末 (qī mò) - "end of term, final exam period"
- 年末 (nián mò) - "end of year"
- 周末 (zhōu mò) - "weekend"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **末** as the decisive "end" — the sharp falling tone perfectly matches the finality of
something coming to an end!
