**🗣️ Pronunciation of 言 (yán)**

- **Pinyin:** yán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** but with second tone → rising up
- **yán** sounds like **"yahn"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're about to speak: **"yán?"** — that upward rise shows the anticipation of speech.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 言 (yán) - "speech; words"
- 语言 (yǔ yán) - "language"
- 发言 (fā yán) - "speak; make a speech"
- 谣言 (yáo yán) - "rumor"
- 名言 (míng yán) - "famous saying"

**💡 Note on Radical:**

言 is also the "speech radical" (讠) when used as a component in other characters related to
speaking, talking, or language.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 言 as "speech" — when you're about to say something important, your voice naturally rises
with emphasis, just like the second tone!
