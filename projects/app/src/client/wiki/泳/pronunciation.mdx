**🗣️ Pronunciation of 泳 (yǒng)**

- **Pinyin:** yǒng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǒng** sounds like **"ong"** in "song", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǒng** sounds like **"yong"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"yǒng..."** — that's the tone pattern of
**yǒng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 泳 (yǒng) - "swim"
- 游泳 (yóu yǒng) - "swimming"
- 游泳池 (yóu yǒng chí) - "swimming pool"
- 蛙泳 (wā yǒng) - "breaststroke"
- 仰泳 (yǎng yǒng) - "backstroke"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

泳 (yǒng) contains the water radical (氵) on the left, which makes sense since it relates to
swimming in water!
