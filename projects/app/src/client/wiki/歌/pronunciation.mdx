**🗣️ Pronunciation of 歌 (gē)**

- **Pinyin:** gē
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ē** sounds like **"uh"** held steady and high
- **gē** sounds like **"guh"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're singing a sustained note: **"gēeeee"** — keep it high and level.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 唱歌 (chàng gē) - "to sing"
- 歌声 (gē shēng) - "singing voice"
- 歌手 (gē shǒu) - "singer"
- 歌迷 (gē mí) - "fan (of music)"
- 民歌 (mín gē) - "folk song"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

歌 means "song" — sing it with that **high, steady** tone like holding a musical note: "gēēē!"
