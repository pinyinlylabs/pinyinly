**🗣️ Pronunciation of 生 (shēng)**

- **Pinyin:** shēng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **ēng** sounds like **"ung"** in "sung", but with first tone → steady and high
- **shēng** sounds like **"shung"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 生 (shēng) - "life; born; raw"
- 生活 (shēng huó) - "life; to live"
- 生日 (shēng rì) - "birthday"
- 生产 (shēng chǎn) - "to produce"
- 学生 (xué shēng) - "student"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

First tone is like the steady beginning of **life** - keep your voice **flat and high** throughout
**shēng**.
