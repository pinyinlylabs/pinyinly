**🗣️ Pronunciation of 朋 (péng)**

- **Pinyin:** péng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question with enthusiasm

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pen"
- **éng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung", but with rising tone → goes up like asking a question
- **péng** sounds like **"pung?"** with an upward rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up high, like calling out to a friend with excitement: "Hey friend!"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 朋友 (péng you) - "friend"
- 老朋友 (lǎo péng you) - "old friend"
- 小朋友 (xiǎo péng you) - "little friend; child"
- 交朋友 (jiāo péng you) - "make friends"
- 男朋友 (nán péng you) - "boyfriend"
- 女朋友 (nǚ péng you) - "girlfriend"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

朋 means "friend" - when you see a friend and want to greet them, you naturally call out with that
bright, rising tone: **"péng!"** (like calling "Hey!")
