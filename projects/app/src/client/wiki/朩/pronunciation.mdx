**🗣️ Pronunciation of 朩 (děng)**

- **Pinyin:** děng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ěng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **děng** sounds like **"dung"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or considering: **"děng..."** — that dip and rise is the third tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 朩 (děng) - "rank, grade, level"
- 等级 (děng jí) - "grade, level, rank"
- 等等 (děng děng) - "and so on, etc."
- 等候 (děng hòu) - "to wait for"
- 平等 (píng děng) - "equal, equality"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **朩** as organizing things by "rank" — the thoughtful third tone matches the careful
consideration needed when ranking or grading things!
