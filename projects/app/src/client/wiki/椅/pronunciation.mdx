**🗣️ Pronunciation of 椅 (yǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** yǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǐ** sounds like **"yee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're settling into a chair: **"yǐ..."** — that relaxed, contemplative tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 椅 (yǐ) - "chair"
- 椅子 (yǐ zi) - "chair"
- 轮椅 (lún yǐ) - "wheelchair"
- 摇椅 (yáo yǐ) - "rocking chair"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **yǐ** as the relaxed sound you make when sitting down — the dip-and-rise tone is like
settling into comfort!
