**🗣️ Pronunciation of 夏 (xià)**

- **Pinyin:** xià
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"Shyah!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **ià** sounds like **"yah"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **xià** sounds like **"shyah!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're definitively naming the hottest season: **"xià!"** — that decisive tone is the
**fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 夏 (xià) - "summer"
- 夏天 (xià tiān) - "summer (season)"
- 夏日 (xià rì) - "summer day"
- 夏季 (xià jì) - "summer season"
- 立夏 (lì xià) - "beginning of summer"

**💡 Special Notes:**

夏 is one of the four seasons and represents the hottest time of year. It's also historically
significant as it refers to the Xia Dynasty, the first dynasty in traditional Chinese
historiography.
