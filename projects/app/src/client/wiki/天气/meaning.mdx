The state of the atmosphere, regarding heat, dryness, sunshine, wind, rain, etc.; weather;
atmospheric conditions.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                                     |
| -------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | tiānqì                                   |
| Core meaning   | weather; atmospheric conditions; climate |
| Part of speech | noun                                     |
| Tone           | tiān (1st), qì (4th)                     |

## Word Breakdown

天气 combines concepts of heaven/sky and energy to represent atmospheric conditions.

| Component | Meaning                                               |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **天**    | Heaven; sky; day - representing the atmospheric realm |
| **气**    | Energy; air; breath - representing atmospheric forces |

Together they create: "sky energy" or "atmospheric force" - the dynamic conditions in the sky that
affect daily life.

## Core Meaning & Usage

天气 represents **weather conditions and atmospheric patterns**. It's used:

1. **General weather**: 今天天气 (jīntiān tiānqì) - "today's weather"
2. **Conditions**: 好天气 (hǎo tiānqì) - "good weather"
3. **Forecasting**: 天气预报 (tiānqì yùbào) - "weather forecast"
4. **Discussion**: 谈论天气 (tánlùn tiānqì) - "discuss the weather"

## Examples

- **天气很好** (tiānqì hěn hǎo) - "the weather is very good"
- **天气不好** (tiānqì bù hǎo) - "the weather is bad"
- **天气预报** (tiānqì yùbào) - "weather forecast"
- **天气变化** (tiānqì biànhuà) - "weather changes"
- **恶劣天气** (èliè tiānqì) - "severe weather"

## Cultural Context

天气 in Chinese culture represents:

**Daily Life Impact:**

- **农业社会** (nóngyè shèhuì) - Agricultural society's dependence on weather
- **生活节奏** (shēnghuó jiézòu) - Weather affects daily rhythm and activities
- **心情影响** (xīnqíng yǐngxiǎng) - Weather influences mood and well-being
- **健康关注** (jiànkāng guānzhù) - Weather considerations for health

**Social Conversation:**

- **寒暄话题** (hánxuān huàtí) - Weather as small talk topic
- **关心表达** (guānxīn biǎodá) - Showing care through weather concerns
- **文化交流** (wénhuà jiāoliú) - Weather as universal conversation starter

## Common Expressions

## Learning Notes

天气 is essential because:

- Fundamental concept for daily conversation and planning
- Essential for travel, outdoor activities, and health considerations
- Key to understanding Chinese cultural attitudes toward nature
- Important for social interaction and small talk
- Demonstrates how compound words express natural phenomena

天气 reflects the Chinese understanding that weather is the sky's dynamic energy affecting all
aspects of human life, requiring awareness, adaptation, and respectful attention to natural
patterns!
