**🗣️ Pronunciation of 亥 (hài)**

- **Pinyin:** hài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"**, but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **hài** sounds like **"high!"** with a commanding drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 亥 (hài) - "12th terrestrial branch" (in Chinese zodiac/calendar)
- 亥时 (hài shí) - "9-11 PM" (traditional time period)
- 亥年 (hài nián) - "year of the pig"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

亥 represents the **pig** in the Chinese zodiac - imagine someone sharply calling **"high!"** (hài)
to get a pig's attention!
