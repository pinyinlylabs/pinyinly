**🗣️ Pronunciation of 候 (hòu)**

- **Pinyin:** hòu
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **òu** sounds like **"oh"** in "oh no", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **hòu** sounds like **"hoh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"hòu!"** — that's the tone pattern of **hòu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 候 (hòu) - "to wait; time; season"
- 等候 (děng hòu) - "to wait"
- 时候 (shí hou) - "time; moment"
- 候选人 (hòu xuǎn rén) - "candidate"
- 气候 (qì hòu) - "climate"

**📝 Special Note:**

In the common word 时候 (shí hou), the 候 is pronounced as a neutral tone **hou** (without tone
marking), not the fourth tone **hòu**.
