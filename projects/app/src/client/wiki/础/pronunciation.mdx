**🗣️ Pronunciation of 础 (chǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** chǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **chǔ** sounds like **"choo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"chǔ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **chǔ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 础 (chǔ) - "foundation"
- 基础 (jī chǔ) - "foundation; basis"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

A **foundation** is something you think deeply about — that thoughtful, contemplative tone pattern
matches perfectly with the third tone of **chǔ**!
