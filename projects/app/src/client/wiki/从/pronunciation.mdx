**🗣️ Pronunciation of 从 (cóng)**

- **Pinyin:** cóng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats"
- **óng** sounds like **"ong"** in "song" but with a rising tone
- **cóng** sounds like **"tsong?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "from where?" with curiosity: **"cóng?"** — that's the rising tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 从来 (cóng lái) - "always; all along"
- 从前 (cóng qián) - "before; in the past"
- 从小 (cóng xiǎo) - "from childhood"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

从 means "from" — like asking "**from** where?" with that rising, inquisitive tone!
