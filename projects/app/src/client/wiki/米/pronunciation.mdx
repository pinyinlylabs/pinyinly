**🗣️ Pronunciation of 米 (mǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** mǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "me"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **mǐ** sounds like **"mee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about rice: **"mǐ..."** — that thoughtful tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 米 (mǐ) - "rice; meter"
- 米饭 (mǐ fàn) - "rice (cooked)"
- 玉米 (yù mǐ) - "corn"
- 一米 (yī mǐ) - "one meter"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **米** as considering rice — the third tone dips down (thinking) then rises up (deciding to
eat)!
