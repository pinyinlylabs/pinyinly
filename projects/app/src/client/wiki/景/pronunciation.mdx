**🗣️ Pronunciation of 景 (jǐng)**

- **Pinyin:** jǐng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but softer, almost like "zh"
- **ǐng** sounds like **"ing"** in "ring", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **jǐng** sounds like **"jing"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Start mid-level, drop down low, then rise back up. Perfect for describing beautiful scenery with a
thoughtful tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 景色 (jǐng sè) - "scenery; landscape"
- 风景 (fēng jǐng) - "scenery; view"
- 景点 (jǐng diǎn) - "scenic spot; attraction"
- 背景 (bèi jǐng) - "background"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

景 means "scenery" - when you see beautiful scenery, you naturally pause thoughtfully and say
**"jǐng..."** with that contemplative third tone!
