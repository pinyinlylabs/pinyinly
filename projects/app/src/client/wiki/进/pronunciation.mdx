**🗣️ Pronunciation of 进 (jìn)**

- **Pinyin:** jìn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Enter!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **ìn** sounds like **"een"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling tone
- **jìn** sounds like **"jeen!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like commanding someone to enter: **"jìn!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone pattern of
**jìn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 进入 (jìn rù) - "to enter; to go into"
- 进来 (jìn lái) - "to come in"
- 进去 (jìn qù) - "to go in"
- 进步 (jìn bù) - "progress; to improve"
- 进行 (jìn xíng) - "to carry out; to conduct"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

进 means **to enter** — pronounce it with a commanding **jìn** like telling someone to "come in!"
