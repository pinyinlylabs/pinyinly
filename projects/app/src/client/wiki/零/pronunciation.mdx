**🗣️ Pronunciation of 零 (líng)**

- **Pinyin:** líng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "long"
- **íng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing", but with second tone → rising up
- **líng** sounds like **"ling"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 零 (líng) - "zero"
- 零下 (líng xià) - "below zero"
- 零食 (líng shí) - "snacks" (literally "zero food")
- 零件 (líng jiàn) - "spare parts"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When counting down to zero, your voice naturally rises with anticipation — **líng**
