**🗣️ Pronunciation of 皮 (pí)**

- **Pinyin:** pí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pat" but with a stronger puff of air
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with a **rising tone**
- **pí** sounds like **"pee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) **rises** like asking a question:

Say it like you're asking "Is that skin?": **"pí?"** — that's the rising energy of **pí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 皮 (pí) - "skin; leather; fur"
- 皮包 (pí bāo) - "leather bag"
- 皮肤 (pí fū) - "skin"
- 橘子皮 (jú zi pí) - "orange peel"
- 土豆皮 (tǔ dòu pí) - "potato skin"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

皮 means "skin" — the rising second tone sounds like someone exclaiming when they touch something
with an interesting texture!
