The week following the current one.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                       |
| -------------- | -------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | xià zhōu                   |
| Core meaning   | next week; following week  |
| Part of speech | noun (time expression)     |
| Tone           | fourth + first tone        |

## Visual Breakdown

下周 combines **next/down** (下) with **week** (周) to mean "the following week."

| Component | Visual Description                                          |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **下**    | Down/next - shows movement to following position           |
| **周**    | Week/cycle - represents a complete seven-day period        |

## Mnemonic

Think of 下周 as **"the week that comes after (下) this one"**:

- 下 shows progression to the next position in sequence
- 周 represents the weekly cycle
- Together: the week that follows the current week
- Like moving down a calendar to the next week

The concept captures temporal progression through weekly cycles.

## Usage Examples

- **下周一** (xià zhōu yī) - "next Monday"
- **下周见** (xià zhōu jiàn) - "see you next week"
- **下周的会议** (xià zhōu de huìyì) - "next week's meeting"
- **下周末** (xià zhōu mò) - "next weekend"

## Time Sequence Pattern

下周 fits into the time progression system:

- **上周** (shàng zhōu) - last week
- **这周** (zhè zhōu) - this week  
- **下周** (xià zhōu) - next week
- **下下周** (xià xià zhōu) - the week after next

## Grammar Patterns

下周 functions as a **time noun**:

- **下周 + [verb]**: next week + [verb]
- **下周的 + [noun]**: next week's + [noun]
- **在下周**: during next week
- **下周 + [specific day]**: next + [day of week]

## Cultural Context

下周 reflects Chinese time organization:
- **Weekly planning**: Essential for scheduling in Chinese work culture
- **Future orientation**: Shows planning ahead and preparation
- **Social coordination**: Important for making appointments and commitments
- **Temporal precision**: Specific time references valued in Chinese communication
