**🗣️ Pronunciation of 龙 (lóng)**

- **Pinyin:** lóng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "long"
- **óng** sounds like **"ong"** in "song", but with second tone → rising up
- **lóng** sounds like **"long?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and go up, like asking a question: **"lóng?"** — that's the tone pattern of **lóng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 龙 (lóng) - "dragon"
- 恐龙 (kǒng lóng) - "dinosaur"
- 龙舟 (lóng zhōu) - "dragon boat"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Imagine a dragon **rising** into the sky with that ascending **"lóng?"** sound — majestic and
powerful!
