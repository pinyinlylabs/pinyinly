**🗣️ Pronunciation of 六 (liù)**

- **Pinyin:** liù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **iù** sounds like **"ee-oo"** blended together with a sharp falling tone
- **liù** sounds like **"lee-oo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"liù!"** — that's the tone pattern of **liù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 六 (liù) - "six"
- 六月 (liù yuè) - "June"
- 十六 (shí liù) - "sixteen"
- 六十 (liù shí) - "sixty"
- 六点 (liù diǎn) - "six o'clock"

**💡 Special Notes:**

六 consistently uses the fourth tone (liù) as the number "six." The pronunciation combines the "l"
sound with a diphthong "iù" that flows from "ee" to "oo" with a falling tone.
