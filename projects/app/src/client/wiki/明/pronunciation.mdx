**🗣️ Pronunciation of 明 (míng)**

- **Pinyin:** míng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "me"
- **íng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing" but with second tone → rising upward
- **míng** sounds like **"ming?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about brightness or clarity: **"míng?"** — that upward rise is the second
tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 明 (míng) - "bright; clear"
- 明天 (míng tiān) - "tomorrow"
- 明白 (míng bai) - "to understand"
- 明星 (míng xīng) - "star; celebrity"
