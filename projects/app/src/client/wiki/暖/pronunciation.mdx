**🗣️ Pronunciation of 暖 (nuǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** nuǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "now"
- **uǎn** sounds like **"wahn"**, but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **nuǎn** sounds like **"nwahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Start mid-level, drop down low, then rise back up. Like the cozy, comfortable feeling of warmth that
settles in slowly.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 暖和 (nuǎn huo) - "warm; mild"
- 温暖 (wēn nuǎn) - "warm; cozy"
- 暖气 (nuǎn qì) - "heating; central heating"
- 取暖 (qǔ nuǎn) - "keep warm; heat"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

暖 means "warm" - when you feel that cozy warmth, you let out a satisfied **"nuǎn..."** with that
comfortable, settling third tone!
