**🗣️ Pronunciation of 色 (sè)**

- **Pinyin:** sè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **è** sounds like **"uh"** but with fourth tone → sharp fall down
- **sè** sounds like **"suh"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: `)**

The **fourth tone** is a **sharp falling** tone, like being decisive:

Say it like you're stating firmly: **"sè!"** — that sharp downward drop is the **fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 色 (sè) - "color"
- 颜色 (yán sè) - "color"
- 红色 (hóng sè) - "red color"
- 白色 (bái sè) - "white color"
- 绿色 (lǜ sè) - "green color"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **色** as pointing decisively at a color — that sharp, definitive gesture matches the
**fourth tone**!

**📍 Cultural Note:**

色 is fundamental in Chinese when describing colors and appears in countless color combinations,
from basic colors to complex shades.
