**🗣️ Pronunciation of 赛 (sài)**

- **Pinyin:** sài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Sigh!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "sit"
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **sài** sounds like **"sigh!"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're announcing a competition: **"sài!"** — that's the sharp falling tone of **sài**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 比赛 (bǐ sài) - "competition; match"
- 决赛 (jué sài) - "final; championship"
- 参赛 (cān sài) - "participate in a contest"
- 赛跑 (sài pǎo) - "race; running competition"
