**🗣️ Pronunciation of 师 (shī)**

- **Pinyin:** shī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Sheee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **shī** sounds like **"she"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (First tone: ˉ)**

The **first tone** is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like addressing someone respectfully: **"shī..."** — that steady, respectful tone is the
**first tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 师 (shī) - "teacher"
- 老师 (lǎo shī) - "teacher"
- 师傅 (shī fu) - "master; skilled worker"
- 师长 (shī zhǎng) - "military commander; superior"
- 导师 (dǎo shī) - "tutor; supervisor"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **师** as speaking to a **teacher** with respect — that steady, high tone shows respect and
attention!
