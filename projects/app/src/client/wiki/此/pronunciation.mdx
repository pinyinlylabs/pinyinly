**🗣️ Pronunciation of 此 (cǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** cǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"uh"** with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **cǐ** sounds like **"tsuh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're pointing thoughtfully: **"cǐ..."** — that dip-then-rise pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 此时 (cǐ shí) - "at this time"
- 此外 (cǐ wài) - "besides; in addition"
- 因此 (yīn cǐ) - "therefore; thus"
- 如此 (rú cǐ) - "like this; so"
- 从此 (cóng cǐ) - "from now on"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

此 means "this" — point to something with that **thoughtful dip-and-rise** tone: "cǐ..."
