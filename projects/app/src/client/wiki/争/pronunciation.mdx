**🗣️ Pronunciation of 争 (zhēng)**

- **Pinyin:** zhēng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **ēng** sounds like **"ung"** in "lung" but held steady and high
- **zhēng** sounds like **"jung"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (First tone: ˉ)**

The **first tone** is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like you're sustaining a high musical note: **"zhēnnnng"** — that's the tone pattern of
**zhēng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 争 (zhēng) - "compete; argue"
- 争取 (zhēng qǔ) - "strive for"
- 竞争 (jìng zhēng) - "competition"
- 争论 (zhēng lùn) - "debate; argue"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **争** as people reaching high and steady for the same prize — that's the **first tone**
energy!
