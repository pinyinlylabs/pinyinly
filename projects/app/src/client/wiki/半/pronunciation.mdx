**🗣️ Pronunciation of 半 (bàn)**

- **Pinyin:** bàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "ban"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with the **fourth tone** → sharp drop down
- **bàn** sounds like **"bahn!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're decisively stating: **"bàn!"** — that firm, definitive tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 半 (bàn) - "half"
- 一半 (yī bàn) - "half; one half"
- 半天 (bàn tiān) - "half a day; a long time"
- 半年 (bàn nián) - "half a year"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

半 means "half" — the sharp falling fourth tone sounds decisive, like cutting something exactly in
half!
