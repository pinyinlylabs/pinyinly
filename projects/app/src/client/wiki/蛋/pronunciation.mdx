**🗣️ Pronunciation of 蛋 (dàn)**

- **Pinyin:** dàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"**, but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **dàn** sounds like **"dahn!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're stating something definitively: **"dàn!"** — that's the tone pattern of **dàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 蛋 (dàn) - "egg"
- 鸡蛋 (jī dàn) - "chicken egg"
- 蛋糕 (dàn gāo) - "cake"
- 煮蛋 (zhǔ dàn) - "boiled egg"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **dàn** as confidently identifying an egg: "That's an egg!" — the falling tone sounds
decisive and clear.
