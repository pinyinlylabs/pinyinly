**🗣️ Pronunciation of 木 (mù)**

- **Pinyin:** mù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "moon"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp downward fall
- **mù** sounds like **"moo"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly stating something: **"mù!"** — that decisive downward drop is the fourth
tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 木 (mù) - "wood, tree"
- 木头 (mù tou) - "wood, lumber"
- 树木 (shù mù) - "trees, vegetation"
- 木材 (mù cái) - "timber, lumber"
- 木工 (mù gōng) - "carpenter, woodworker"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **木** as solid, sturdy "wood" — the sharp falling tone matches the firm, grounded nature
of trees and timber!
