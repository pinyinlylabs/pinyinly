**🗣️ Pronunciation of 补 (bǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** bǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **bǔ** sounds like **"boo"** with a dip-then-rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 补 (bǔ) - "supplement; patch"
- 补充 (bǔ chōng) - "supplement; add"
- 弥补 (mí bǔ) - "make up for; remedy"
- 补习 (bǔ xí) - "tutorial; supplementary study"
- 修补 (xiū bǔ) - "repair; fix"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The third tone's dip-and-rise pattern is like patching a hole - you go down into the problem, then
rise back up with the fix!
