**🗣️ Pronunciation of 材 (cái)**

- **Pinyin:** cái
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats"
- **ái** sounds like **"eye"**, but with second tone → rise up
- **cái** sounds like **"tsai"** with a rising tone

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 材 (cái) - "material"
- 材料 (cái liào) - "materials"
- 教材 (jiào cái) - "textbook"
- 人材 (rén cái) - "talent"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **material** going **up** in value — that's the rising second tone of **cái**!
