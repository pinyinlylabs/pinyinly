**🗣️ Pronunciation of 告 (gào)**

- **Pinyin:** gào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Go!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go", but **unaspirated** — no strong puff of air
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "how", but with a sharp falling tone
- **gào** sounds like **"gow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving a command: **"gào!"** — that's the energy of **gào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 告 (gào) - "tell; inform"
- 告诉 (gào sù) - "tell; inform"
- 告别 (gào bié) - "say goodbye"
- 广告 (guǎng gào) - "advertisement"
- 报告 (bào gào) - "report"
- 忠告 (zhōng gào) - "advice; warning"
