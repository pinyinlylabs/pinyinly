**🗣️ Pronunciation of 胜 (shèng)**

- **Pinyin:** shèng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **èng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung", but with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **shèng** sounds like **"shung!"** with a triumphant falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're declaring victory: **"shèng!"** — that's the victorious tone pattern of
**shèng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 胜 (shèng) - "win; victory"
- 胜利 (shèng lì) - "victory; triumph"
- 获胜 (huò shèng) - "win; be victorious"
- 战胜 (zhàn shèng) - "defeat; overcome"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

胜 means to win or achieve victory, and the powerful fourth tone captures the decisive moment of
triumph!
