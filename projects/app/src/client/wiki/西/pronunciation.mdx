**🗣️ Pronunciation of 西 (xī)**

- **Pinyin:** xī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like holding a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" but with tongue positioned lower
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **xī** sounds like **"shee"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 西 (xī) - "west"
- 东西 (dōng xi) - "thing; stuff"
- 西方 (xī fāng) - "the West; western"
- 西部 (xī bù) - "western part"
- 西边 (xī biān) - "west side"
- 西餐 (xī cān) - "Western food"
- 西医 (xī yī) - "Western medicine"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The steady, high first tone is like pointing confidently toward the west and saying "There!" - clear
and unwavering direction.
