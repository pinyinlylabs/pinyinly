**🗣️ Pronunciation of 夕 (xī)**

- **Pinyin:** xī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Shee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **xī** sounds like **"shee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're calmly observing the evening: **"xī..."** — that steady, peaceful tone is the
**first tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 夕 (xī) - "evening; dusk"
- 夕阳 (xī yáng) - "setting sun"
- 除夕 (chú xī) - "New Year's Eve"
- 朝夕 (zhāo xī) - "morning and evening"
- 夕照 (xī zhào) - "evening glow"

**💡 Special Notes:**

夕 represents the time of day when the sun is setting. It's often used poetically to describe the
peaceful, contemplative time of evening. The character also appears as a radical in other
characters.
