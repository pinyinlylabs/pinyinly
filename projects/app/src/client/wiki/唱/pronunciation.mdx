**🗣️ Pronunciation of 唱 (chàng)**

- **Pinyin:** chàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "change" (aspirated, with a puff of air)
- **àng** sounds like **"ahng"** in "gong", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **chàng** sounds like **"chahng"** with a sharp drop in pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp fall**:

Say it like confidently declaring you'll sing: **"chàng!"** — that decisive drop fits the action.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 唱 (chàng) - "to sing"
- 唱歌 (chàng gē) - "to sing songs"
- 演唱 (yǎn chàng) - "to perform (singing)"
- 演唱会 (yǎn chàng huì) - "concert"
- 合唱 (hé chàng) - "chorus; to sing together"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **mouth (口)** plus the **昌** component suggests using your mouth to make beautiful sounds —
that's **singing**!
