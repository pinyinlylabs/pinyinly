**🗣️ Pronunciation of 故 (gù)**

- **Pinyin:** gù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "good", but with a sharp falling tone
- **gù** sounds like **"goo!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 故 (gù) - "incident, reason"
- 故事 (gù shi) - "story"
- 故乡 (gù xiāng) - "hometown"
- 故意 (gù yì) - "on purpose"
- 事故 (shì gù) - "accident"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The decisive fourth tone reflects the definitive nature of an **incident** or the firmness of
something done on **purpose**.
