**🗣️ Pronunciation of 转 (zhuǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** zhuǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge"
- **uǎn** sounds like **"wan"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **zhuǎn** sounds like **"j-wan"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're contemplating a change of direction: **"zhuǎn..."** — that's the tone pattern of
**zhuǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 转 (zhuǎn) - "turn; rotate; change"
- 转身 (zhuǎn shēn) - "turn around"
- 转弯 (zhuǎn wān) - "turn a corner"
- 转换 (zhuǎn huàn) - "switch; convert"
- 转移 (zhuǎn yí) - "transfer; shift"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

转 means "turn" — the **dipping and rising** tone mimics the circular motion of turning!
