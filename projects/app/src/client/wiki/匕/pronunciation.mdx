**🗣️ Pronunciation of 匕 (bǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** bǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **bǐ** sounds like **"bee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully considering a utensil: **"bǐ..."** — that contemplative
dip-then-rise pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 匕 (bǐ) - "spoon, dagger" (archaic)
- 匕首 (bǐ shǒu) - "dagger"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **bǐ** as "bee" with hesitation — like you're carefully examining a small tool or utensil!

**📍 Special Note:**

匕 is an ancient character that originally referred to a spoon or small knife. In modern usage, it's
mainly seen in compounds like 匕首 (dagger) or used as a radical component in other characters.
