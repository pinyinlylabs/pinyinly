**🗣️ Pronunciation of 右 (yòu)**

- **Pinyin:** yòu
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **òu** sounds like **"oh"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **yòu** sounds like **"yo"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a direction: **"yòu!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern of **yòu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 右 (yòu) - "right (direction)"
- 右边 (yòu biān) - "right side"
- 左右 (zuǒ yòu) - "left and right; about"
- 右手 (yòu shǒu) - "right hand"
- 向右 (xiàng yòu) - "turn right"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"yo"** with a sharp drop — when giving directions to turn **right**, you often speak
decisively with a falling tone!
