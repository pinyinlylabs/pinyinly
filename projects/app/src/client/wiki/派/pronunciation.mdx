**🗣️ Pronunciation of 派 (pài)**

- **Pinyin:** pài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pay" (but with a puff of air)
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"**, but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **pài** sounds like **"pie!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"pài!"** — that's the tone pattern of **pài**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 派 (pài) - "send/dispatch"
- 派出所 (pài chū suǒ) - "police station"
- 派对 (pài duì) - "party"
- 派遣 (pài qiǎn) - "dispatch/send"
- 苹果派 (píng guǒ pài) - "apple pie"
- 学派 (xué pài) - "school of thought"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

派 (pài) contains the water radical (氵) on the left. Think of water flowing or being "sent" in a
direction — just like how 派 means to send or dispatch!
