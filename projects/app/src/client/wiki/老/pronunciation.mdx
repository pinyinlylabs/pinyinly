**🗣️ Pronunciation of 老 (lǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** lǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "hmm" when thinking

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "how", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **lǎo** sounds like **"low"** with a contemplative dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're respectfully referring to someone elderly: **"lǎo..."** — that's the thoughtful
tone pattern of **lǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 老 (lǎo) - "old, aged"
- 老师 (lǎo shī) - "teacher" (literally "old master")
- 老人 (lǎo rén) - "elderly person, old person"
- 老板 (lǎo bǎn) - "boss" (literally "old board")
- 老家 (lǎo jiā) - "hometown, ancestral home"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When respectfully addressing an elder or experienced person, use that thoughtful third tone —
**lǎo** shows respect!
