**🗣️ Pronunciation of 什 (shén)**

- **Pinyin:** shén
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **én** sounds like **"en"** in "pen", but with second tone → rising like a question
- **shén** sounds like **"shen?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 什么 (shén me) - "what"
- 什么样 (shén me yàng) - "what kind"
- 为什么 (wèi shén me) - "why"

**📍 Special Note:**

什 is rarely used alone in modern Chinese. It's almost always found in the compound **什么** (shén
me) meaning "what". The pronunciation fits perfectly with its questioning meaning!

**💡 Memory Tip:**

什 means **what** - say **"shen?"** with a curious, rising tone (second tone), perfect for asking
questions!
