**🗣️ Pronunciation of 排 (pái)**

- **Pinyin:** pái
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pay" (but with a slight puff of air)
- **ái** sounds like **"eye"**, but with second tone → rise up
- **pái** sounds like **"pie"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking **"pie?"** with a rising intonation — that's the rising tone of **pái**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 排 (pái) - "arrange; row"
- 排队 (pái duì) - "line up; queue"
- 排球 (pái qiú) - "volleyball"
- 安排 (ān pái) - "arrange; plan"

Think of **"排"** as **"pie?"** with a rising tone — like asking someone to arrange the pies in a
