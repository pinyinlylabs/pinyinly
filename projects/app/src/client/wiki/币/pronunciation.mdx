**🗣️ Pronunciation of 币 (bì)**

- **Pinyin:** bì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Bee!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** with a sharp falling tone
- **bì** sounds like **"bee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're firmly stating a price: **"bì!"** — that's the decisive energy of currency.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 币 (bì) - "currency; coin"
- 人民币 (rén mín bì) - "Chinese yuan (RMB)"
- 硬币 (yìng bì) - "coin"
- 货币 (huò bì) - "currency"
- 外币 (wài bì) - "foreign currency"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **币** as the definitive value of money — that sharp **fourth tone** shows the firm,
decisive nature of currency!

**📍 Cultural Note:**

币 is commonly seen in 人民币, the official currency of China, literally meaning "people's
currency."
