**🗣️ Pronunciation of 干 (gān)**

- **Pinyin:** gān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Gaahn"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ān** sounds like **"ahn"** but held steady and high
- **gān** sounds like **"gahn"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (First tone: ˉ)**

The **first tone** is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like stating something is **dry**: **"gān..."** — that steady, matter-of-fact tone is the
**first tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 干 (gān) - "dry"
- 干净 (gān jìng) - "clean"
- 干什么 (gān shén me) - "what are you doing?"
- 干杯 (gān bēi) - "cheers!"
- 干活儿 (gān huó er) - "work; do work"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **干** as describing something **dry** with a steady, factual voice — that's the **first
tone** certainty!
