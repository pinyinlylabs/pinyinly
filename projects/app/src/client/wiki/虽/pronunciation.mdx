**🗣️ Pronunciation of 虽 (suī)**

- **Pinyin:** suī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "sun"
- **uī** sounds like **"way"** but starting with "oo", with first tone → high and steady
- **suī** sounds like **"sway"** held steady and high

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're making a statement: **"suī"** — that's the tone pattern of **suī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 虽 (suī) - "although/though"
- 虽然 (suī rán) - "although/even though"
- 虽说 (suī shuō) - "although it is said"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **suī** as the beginning of "although..." in a calm, matter-of-fact way: "Although..." —
the steady high tone sets up a contrasting statement.
