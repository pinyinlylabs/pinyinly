**🗣️ Pronunciation of 课 (kè)**

- **Pinyin:** kè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"That's it!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "keep"
- **è** sounds like **"uh"** in "duh" but with a sharp falling tone
- **kè** sounds like **"kuh!"** with a sharp drop in pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** is **sharp and falling**, like making a firm statement:

Say it like you're announcing a class: **"kè!"** — that decisive, downward tone is the **fourth
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 课 (kè) - "lesson; class"
- 上课 (shàng kè) - "attend class"
- 课本 (kè běn) - "textbook"
- 课堂 (kè táng) - "classroom"
- 课程 (kè chéng) - "curriculum; course"
- 功课 (gōng kè) - "homework"

**💡 Usage Note:**

课 (kè) is fundamental in educational contexts, representing both individual lessons and the broader
concept of coursework or academic subjects.
