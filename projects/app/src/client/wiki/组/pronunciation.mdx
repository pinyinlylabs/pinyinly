**🗣️ Pronunciation of 组 (zǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** zǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"ds"** in "lids"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **zǔ** sounds like **"dzoo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully organizing a group: **"zǔ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **zǔ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 组 (zǔ) - "group"
- 小组 (xiǎo zǔ) - "small group"
- 组合 (zǔ hé) - "combination"
- 组成 (zǔ chéng) - "compose/form"
- 组长 (zǔ zhǎng) - "group leader"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When forming a **group**, you carefully consider who should be together — just like the thoughtful
dip-and-rise of the third tone in **zǔ**!
