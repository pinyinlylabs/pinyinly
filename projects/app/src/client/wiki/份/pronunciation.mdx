**🗣️ Pronunciation of 份 (fèn)**

- **Pinyin:** fèn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fun"
- **èn** sounds like **"en"** in "hen", but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **fèn** sounds like **"fen"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving an order: **"fèn!"** — that sharp drop down is the
fourth tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 份 (fèn) - "portion; share"
- 一份 (yī fèn) - "one portion/copy"
- 身份 (shēn fèn) - "identity"
- 年份 (nián fèn) - "year"
- 月份 (yuè fèn) - "month"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"fen"** with a sharp downward tone — like dividing something into portions with a firm
chop!
