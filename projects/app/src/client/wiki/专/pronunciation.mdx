**🗣️ Pronunciation of 专 (zhuān)**

- **Pinyin:** zhuān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Zhwahn"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" (but with tongue curled back)
- **uān** sounds like **"wahn"** with first tone → high and steady
- **zhuān** sounds like **"zhwahn"** with a high, flat pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady note: **"zhuān..."** — that's the tone pattern of **zhuān**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 专 (zhuān) - "monopolize; specialize"
- 专业 (zhuān yè) - "major; profession; specialty"
- 专家 (zhuān jiā) - "expert; specialist"
- 专门 (zhuān mén) - "specially; specifically"

**💡 Special Notes:**

专 relates to specialization, exclusivity, or focusing on one particular area. It's commonly used in
educational and professional contexts, especially when discussing academic majors, professional
specialties, or expert knowledge in a specific field.
