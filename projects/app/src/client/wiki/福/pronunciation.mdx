**🗣️ Pronunciation of 福 (fú)**

- **Pinyin:** fú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question with hope

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "for"
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "food", but with second tone → rise up
- **fú** sounds like **"foo"** with a rising tone, like asking "food?"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking with hope: **"fú?"** — that's the hopeful rising tone of **fú**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 福 (fú) - "blessing; fortune"
- 幸福 (xìng fú) - "happiness"
- 福利 (fú lì) - "welfare; benefits"
- 祝福 (zhù fú) - "to bless; blessing"

**💡 Cultural Note:**

福 is one of the most auspicious characters in Chinese culture, often displayed upside down during
Chinese New Year to symbolize that fortune has "arrived" (倒福).
