**🗣️ Pronunciation of 甜 (tián)**

- **Pinyin:** tián
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"What?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea"
- **ián** sounds like **"yen"** in "yen", but with second tone → rising upward
- **tián** sounds like **"tyen?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 甜 (tián) - "sweet"
- 甜品 (tián pǐn) - "dessert"
- 甜美 (tián měi) - "sweet and beautiful"
- 甜心 (tián xīn) - "sweetheart"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Second tone rises like asking **"Sweet?"** - your voice goes **up** throughout **tián**.
