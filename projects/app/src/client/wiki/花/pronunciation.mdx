**🗣️ Pronunciation of 花 (huā)**

- **Pinyin:** huā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uā** sounds like **"wah"** but with first tone → steady and high
- **huā** sounds like **"hwah"** held steady and high

**🎧 Tone tip (First tone: ˉ)**

The **first tone** is **high and flat**, like singing a steady note:

Say it like you're admiring a flower: **"huā"** — that steady, high pitch is the **first tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 花 (huā) - "flower"
- 花园 (huā yuán) - "garden"
- 鲜花 (xiān huā) - "fresh flowers"
- 花朵 (huā duǒ) - "flower blossom"
- 花费 (huā fèi) - "to spend, cost"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **花** as the beautiful, steady blooming of a flower — that constant, high beauty matches
the **first tone**!

**📍 Cultural Note:**

花 is beloved in Chinese culture, appearing in poetry, art, and festivals. Different flowers have
symbolic meanings, like 梅花 (plum blossom) representing resilience.
