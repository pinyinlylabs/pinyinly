**🗣️ Pronunciation of 反 (fǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** fǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fan"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **fǎn** sounds like **"fahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it with contemplation: **"fǎn..."** — that's the thoughtful, dipping pattern of **fǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 反对 (fǎn duì) - "oppose"
- 反应 (fǎn yìng) - "reaction"
- 反复 (fǎn fù) - "repeatedly"
- 相反 (xiāng fǎn) - "opposite"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **反** as **reverse** — the dipping and rising tone mimics the back-and-forth motion of
something being reversed!
