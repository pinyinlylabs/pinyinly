**🗣️ Pronunciation of 相 (xiāng)**

- **Pinyin:** xiāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like a soft **"sh"** sound, but your tongue is much closer to your teeth
- **iāng** sounds like **"yang"** held steady and high
- **xiāng** sounds like **"shyang"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like a steady note: **"xiāng—"** — that's the tone pattern of **xiāng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 相 (xiāng) - "mutual; each other"
- 相信 (xiāng xìn) - "believe; trust"
- 相同 (xiāng tóng) - "same; identical"
- 相互 (xiāng hù) - "mutual; reciprocal"
- 相关 (xiāng guān) - "related; relevant"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"shyang"** with a steady tone — **mutual** relationships require steady, consistent
interaction!
