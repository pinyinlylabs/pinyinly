**🗣️ Pronunciation of 媒 (méi)**

- **Pinyin:** méi
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "me"
- **éi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way" but with a rising tone
- **méi** sounds like **"may?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is **rising like a question**:

Start **low and rise up** — like asking if someone might serve as a matchmaker: **"méi?"** — that's
the questioning second tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 媒 (méi) - "matchmaker; go-between"
- 媒体 (méi tǐ) - "media"
- 媒人 (méi rén) - "matchmaker"
- 媒婆 (méi pó) - "matchmaker" (female)
- 传媒 (chuán méi) - "media; mass media"
- 做媒 (zuò méi) - "to act as matchmaker"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The second tone's rising pattern is like asking **"May I introduce you two?"** — that's what a
**matchmaker** does with a questioning, hopeful tone!
