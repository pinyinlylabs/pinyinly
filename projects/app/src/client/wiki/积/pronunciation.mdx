**🗣️ Pronunciation of 积 (jī)**

- **Pinyin:** jī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with first tone → steady and high
- **jī** sounds like **"jee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're stating something clearly: **"jī"** — hold that high, steady pitch like
**"jīīī"**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 积 (jī) - "to accumulate; to build up"
- 积极 (jī jí) - "active; positive"
- 积累 (jī lěi) - "to accumulate"
- 面积 (miàn jī) - "area; space"
- 体积 (tǐ jī) - "volume"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the steady first tone representing the **continuous process** of accumulation — like **"jī,
jī, jī"** steadily building up over time.
