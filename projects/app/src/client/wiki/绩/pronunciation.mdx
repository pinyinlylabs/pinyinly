**🗣️ Pronunciation of 绩 (jì)**

- **Pinyin:** jì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but slightly softer)
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **jì** sounds like **"jee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like announcing an achievement: **"jì!"** — start high and drop down with authority.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 成绩 (chéng jì) - "achievement; grade; result"
- 绩效 (jì xiào) - "performance; efficiency"
- 学绩 (xué jì) - "academic performance"
- 工绩 (gōng jì) - "work performance"
- 业绩 (yè jì) - "achievement; performance"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Merit** and achievements are announced with confidence — that decisive tone matches the sharp
fall!
