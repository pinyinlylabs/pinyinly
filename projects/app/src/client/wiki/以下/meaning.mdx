Refers to something that is below or less than a certain point or amount.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                    |
| -------------- | ----------------------- |
| Pinyin         | yǐ xià                  |
| Core meaning   | below; under; less than |
| Part of speech | preposition/adverb      |
| Tone           | 3rd + 4th tone          |

## Usage Examples

- **十八岁以下** (shí bā suì yǐ xià) - "under eighteen years old"
- **以下内容** (yǐ xià nèi róng) - "the following content"
- **一千元以下** (yī qiān yuán yǐ xià) - "under 1000 yuan"
- **中学以下学历** (zhōng xué yǐ xià xué lì) - "high school education or below"
- **请看以下说明** (qǐng kàn yǐ xià shuō míng) - "please see the following explanation"

## Grammar Patterns

以下 typically:

- **With numbers**: 五个以下 - "five or fewer"
- **Text reference**: 以下几点 - "the following points"
- **Age limits**: 十二岁以下免费 - "free for under 12"
- **Lists**: 以下人员 - "the following personnel"

## Cultural Context

以下 reflects Chinese organizational communication:

- **Hierarchical reference**: 以下 used to show subordinate levels
- **Document structure**: Essential for organizing information in formal writing
- **Age-based categories**: Important for policies affecting different age groups
- **Administrative precision**: Clear indication of limits and boundaries
- **Legal language**: Used in regulations to specify scope and applicability
