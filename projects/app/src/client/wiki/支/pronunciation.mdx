**🗣️ Pronunciation of 支 (zhī)**

- **Pinyin:** zhī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" (but with tongue curled back)
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but held steady and high
- **zhī** sounds like **"jee"** with a steady high pitch and retroflex "zh"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like singing a steady high note: **"zhī~~~"** — that's the flat, steady tone of **zhī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 支 (zhī) - "branch; support"
- 支持 (zhī chí) - "support"
- 支付 (zhī fù) - "pay"
- 一支笔 (yì zhī bǐ) - "a pen" (measure word)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"支"** as **"jee"** held steady — like a steady branch supporting something!
