**🗣️ Pronunciation of 裤 (kù)**

- **Pinyin:** kù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with sharp falling tone
- **kù** sounds like **"koo!"** with a sharp drop in pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 裤 (kù) - "trousers; pants"
- 裤子 (kù zi) - "trousers; pants"
- 短裤 (duǎn kù) - "shorts"
- 长裤 (cháng kù) - "long pants"
- 牛仔裤 (niú zǎi kù) - "jeans"
- 运动裤 (yùn dòng kù) - "sweatpants"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sharp, decisive fourth tone is like the sound of someone firmly pulling on their pants - it's a
quick, definitive action!
