**🗣️ Pronunciation of 原 (yuán)**

- **Pinyin:** yuán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **yu** like **"you"** but faster
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** but with rising tone
- **yuán** sounds like **"you-ahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking for confirmation: **"yuán?"** — that's the upward questioning pattern of
**yuán**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 原因 (yuán yīn) - "reason"
- 原来 (yuán lái) - "originally"
- 草原 (cǎo yuán) - "grassland"
- 原始 (yuán shǐ) - "original"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **原** as the **source** or beginning — the rising tone lifts up like tracing back to where
something started!
