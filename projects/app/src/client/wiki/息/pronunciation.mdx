**🗣️ Pronunciation of 息 (xī)**

- **Pinyin:** xī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Sheee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" but **lighter** and more **forward** in the mouth
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **xī** sounds like **"shee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎯 Mastering the "x" sound:**

The **x** in Chinese is **different** from English:

1. **Start with "sh"** like in "she"
2. **Keep tongue forward** — tip behind lower teeth, not curled back
3. **Make it lighter** — more airy, less harsh than English "sh"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're taking a deep, peaceful breath: **"xī..."** — that steady high tone is perfect
for 息 (rest).

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 息 (xī) - "rest; breath"
- 休息 (xiū xī) - "to rest; to take a break"
- 消息 (xiāo xī) - "news; information"
- 利息 (lì xī) - "interest (financial)"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the steady, peaceful sound of breathing — **"xī..."** — that high, flat tone captures the
calm feeling of rest!
