**🗣️ Pronunciation of 场 (chǎng)**

- **Pinyin:** chǎng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chart" (but with tongue tip down)
- **ǎng** sounds like **"ahng"**, but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **chǎng** sounds like **"chahng"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're considering a venue: **"chǎng..."** — that thoughtful dip-and-rise is the **third
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 场 (chǎng) - "site; venue; field; court"
- 场所 (chǎng suǒ) - "place; venue"
- 广场 (guǎng chǎng) - "square; plaza"
- 球场 (qiú chǎng) - "sports field; court"
- 机场 (jī chǎng) - "airport"
- 市场 (shì chǎng) - "market"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **场** (site/venue) like contemplating which place to go to — that's the **third tone**
thoughtful quality!
