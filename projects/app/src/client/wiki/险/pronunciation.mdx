**🗣️ Pronunciation of 险 (xiǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** xiǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (but with tongue position for "s")
- **iǎn** sounds like **"yen"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **xiǎn** sounds like **"shyen"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall-then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're cautiously considering danger: **"xiǎn..."** — that hesitant, wavering tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 险 (xiǎn) - "danger, risk"
- 危险 (wēi xiǎn) - "dangerous"
- 保险 (bǎo xiǎn) - "insurance"
- 冒险 (mào xiǎn) - "adventure, take risks"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of hesitating at the edge of something dangerous - that uncertain, wavering third tone
perfectly matches the caution of **险** meaning "danger"!
