**🗣️ Pronunciation of 破 (pò)**

- **Pinyin:** pò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: "Stop!"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pot"
- **ò** sounds like **"aw"** in "saw" but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **pò** sounds like **"paw!"** with a sharp falling command

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"pò!"** — that's the tone pattern of **pò**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 破 (pò) - "broken"
- 破坏 (pò huài) - "to destroy; to damage"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When something **breaks**, you might shout **"NO!"** with that sharp, falling tone of frustration —
just like **pò**!
