**🗣️ Pronunciation of 件 (jiàn)**

- **Pinyin:** jiàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **jiàn** sounds like **"jyen!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"jiàn!"** — that's the decisive, falling tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 一件 (yí jiàn) - "one piece/item"
- 件事 (jiàn shì) - "matter; affair"
- 条件 (tiáo jiàn) - "condition; requirement"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

件 is a **measure word** for pieces/items — like decisively counting "**one** piece!" with that firm
fourth tone!
