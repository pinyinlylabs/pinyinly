**🗣️ Pronunciation of 爬 (pá)**

- **Pinyin:** pá
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pat" (but with a slight puff of air)
- **á** sounds like **"ah"** with a rising tone, like saying "Huh?" when surprised
- **pá** sounds like **"pah"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Climb?" when someone suggests going up a mountain — **"pá?"** — that
upward questioning inflection is the second tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 爬 (pá) - "climb; crawl"
- 爬山 (pá shān) - "climb a mountain"
- 爬树 (pá shù) - "climb a tree"
- 爬虫 (pá chóng) - "reptile; crawler"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When someone asks if you want to go climbing, you respond with a questioning "Really?" tone — just
like the second tone's rising pattern in 爬!
