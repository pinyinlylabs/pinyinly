**🗣️ Pronunciation of 卡 (kǎ)**

- **Pinyin:** kǎ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "cat"
- **ǎ** sounds like **"ah"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **kǎ** sounds like **"kah"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone: Say it like you're thinking: **"kǎ..."** —
that's the tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 卡 (kǎ) - "card"
- 卡片 (kǎ piàn) - "card"
- 信用卡 (xìn yòng kǎ) - "credit card"
- 银行卡 (yín háng kǎ) - "bank card"

**💡 Special Note:**

卡 can also be pronounced **kǎ** (third tone) meaning "card" or **qiǎ** (third tone) meaning "stuck;
jammed", but the card meaning with **kǎ** is most common.
