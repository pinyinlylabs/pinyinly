**🗣️ Pronunciation of 线 (xiàn)**

- **Pinyin:** xiàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (but with tongue tip down)
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** in "yen", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **xiàn** sounds like **"shyen!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're pointing out a line: **"xiàn!"** — that's the sharp falling pattern of **xiàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 线 (xiàn) - "line/thread"
- 路线 (lù xiàn) - "route"
- 电线 (diàn xiàn) - "electrical wire"
- 直线 (zhí xiàn) - "straight line"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When drawing a **line**, you make a decisive stroke downward — just like the sharp, falling fourth
tone of **xiàn**!
