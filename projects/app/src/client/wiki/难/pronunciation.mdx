**🗣️ Pronunciation of 难 (nán)**

- **Pinyin:** nán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** but with second tone → rising pitch
- **nán** sounds like **"nahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're struggling and asking "This is hard?": **"nán?"** — that rising, questioning tone
of difficulty.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 难 (nán) - "difficult, hard"
- 难过 (nán guò) - "sad, upset"
- 困难 (kùn nán) - "difficulty, hardship"
- 难题 (nán tí) - "difficult problem"

**Special Note:**

难 can also be pronounced **nàn** (fourth tone) in some contexts, meaning "disaster" or "calamity,"
but **nán** (second tone) meaning "difficult" is much more common.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of your voice rising with frustration when something is difficult - that rising second tone
perfectly captures the struggle of **难** meaning "difficult"!
