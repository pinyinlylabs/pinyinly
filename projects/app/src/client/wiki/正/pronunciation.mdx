**🗣️ Pronunciation of 正 (zhèng)**

- **Pinyin:** zhèng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" (but with tongue curled back)
- **èng** sounds like **"ung"** with a sharp falling tone
- **zhèng** sounds like **"jung"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a firm correction: **"zhèng!"** — that's the sharp downward tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 正在 (zhèng zài) - "right now; currently"
- 正确 (zhèng què) - "correct; right"
- 正式 (zhèng shì) - "formal; official"
- 正常 (zhèng cháng) - "normal"
- 改正 (gǎi zhèng) - "to correct"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

正 means "just/correct" — say it with that **sharp falling** tone like you're making a definitive
correction!
