**🗣️ Pronunciation of 于 (yú)**

- **Pinyin:** yú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a rising tone
- **yú** sounds like **"yoo?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is a **rising** tone, like when you're curious or asking:

Say it like you're asking **"Where?"** or showing interest: **"yú?"** — that's the inquisitive
energy of **yú**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 于 (yú) - "at; in; on" (preposition)
- 关于 (guān yú) - "about; concerning"
- 由于 (yóu yú) - "due to; because of"
- 对于 (duì yú) - "as for; regarding"
- 基于 (jī yú) - "based on"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **于** as pointing to a location with curiosity — that's the **second tone** questioning
nature!

**📍 Usage Note:**

于 is primarily used as a preposition meaning "at," "in," or "on" in formal and written Chinese.
It's commonly seen in compound words and formal expressions.
