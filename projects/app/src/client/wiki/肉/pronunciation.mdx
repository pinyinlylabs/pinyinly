**🗣️ Pronunciation of 肉 (ròu)**

- **Pinyin:** ròu
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **r** like **"r"** in "red" (slight tongue curl)
- **òu** sounds like **"oh"** in "go", but with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **ròu** sounds like **"roe!"** with a firm falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're stating a fact firmly: **"ròu!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern of **ròu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 肉 (ròu) - "meat"
- 牛肉 (niú ròu) - "beef"
- 猪肉 (zhū ròu) - "pork"
- 鸡肉 (jī ròu) - "chicken"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

肉 refers to meat, and the firm fourth tone reflects the substantial, satisfying nature of meat!
