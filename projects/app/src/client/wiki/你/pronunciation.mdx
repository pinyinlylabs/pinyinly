**🗣️ Pronunciation of 你 (nǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** nǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **nǐ** sounds like **"nee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"nǐ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **nǐ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 你 (nǐ) - "you"
- 你好 (nǐ hǎo) - "hello"
- 你们 (nǐ men) - "you (plural)"
- 你的 (nǐ de) - "your; yours"
- 你们好 (nǐ men hǎo) - "hello everyone"
