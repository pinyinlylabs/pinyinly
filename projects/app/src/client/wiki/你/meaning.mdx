## Quick Reference

## Usage Examples

- **你好** (nǐ hǎo) - "hello; how are you"
- **你是谁？** (nǐ shì shéi?) - "Who are you?"
- **你们** (nǐ men) - "you all; you (plural)"
- **你的** (nǐ de) - "your; yours"

## Cultural Context

你 is fundamental for addressing others in Chinese:

- Essential for greetings, questions, and conversations
- Used in both formal and informal situations (though 您 is more formal)
- The basis for understanding Chinese social interaction
- Critical for showing respect and acknowledgment of others
- Foundation for building relationships and communication
