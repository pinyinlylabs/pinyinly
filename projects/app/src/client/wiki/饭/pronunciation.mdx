**🗣️ Pronunciation of 饭 (fàn)**

- **Pinyin:** fàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: "Fan!"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fun"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **fàn** sounds like **"fahn"** with a decisive downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone. Start high and drop down sharply, like saying "No!"
firmly or giving a command.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 饭 (fàn) - "rice, meal"
- 米饭 (mǐ fàn) - "cooked rice"
- 吃饭 (chī fàn) - "to eat (a meal)"
- 晚饭 (wǎn fàn) - "dinner"
- 午饭 (wǔ fàn) - "lunch"
- 早饭 (zǎo fàn) - "breakfast"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When someone calls "Time for dinner!" with authority, that firm, commanding tone matches the sharp
falling fourth tone of **fàn**.
