**🗣️ Pronunciation of 换 (huàn)**

- **Pinyin:** huàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uàn** sounds like **"wan"** in "wander", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **huàn** sounds like **"hwan"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"huàn!"** — that's the decisive falling tone of
**huàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 换 (huàn) - "exchange; change"
- 交换 (jiāo huàn) - "to exchange"
- 换钱 (huàn qián) - "exchange money"
- 换车 (huàn chē) - "change vehicles"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"换"** as **"hwan"** with a sharp drop — like dropping something to exchange it for
something else!
