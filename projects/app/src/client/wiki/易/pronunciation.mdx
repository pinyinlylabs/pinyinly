**🗣️ Pronunciation of 易 (yì)**

- **Pinyin:** yì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **yì** sounds like **"yee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like stating something is simple: **"yì!"** — that sharp drop is the fourth tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 易 (yì) - "easy; simple"
- 容易 (róng yì) - "easy"
- 简易 (jiǎn yì) - "simple; easy"
- 交易 (jiāo yì) - "transaction; trade"
