**🗣️ Pronunciation of 数 (shǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** shǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "good", but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up
- **shǔ** sounds like **"shoo"** with a dip-then-rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 数 (shǔ) - "count"
- 数字 (shù zì) - "number, digit"
- 数学 (shù xué) - "mathematics"
- 数量 (shù liàng) - "quantity"
- 计数 (jì shǔ) - "count"

**🎧 Special Note:**

数 has another pronunciation **shù** (fourth tone) when used as a noun meaning "number" or
"mathematics", but as a verb meaning "count", it's **shǔ** (third tone).

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The third tone's rhythm reflects the methodical up-and-down process of **counting**.
