**🗣️ Pronunciation of 旁 (páng)**

- **Pinyin:** páng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "park"
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** in "hang" but with second tone → rising tone
- **páng** sounds like **"pahng?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're pointing to something beside you: **"páng?"** — that rising tone suggests looking
to the side!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 旁 (páng) - "side, beside, next to"
- 旁边 (páng biān) - "beside, next to"
- 路旁 (lù páng) - "roadside"
- 身旁 (shēn páng) - "beside one's body, at one's side"
- 旁人 (páng rén) - "other people, bystanders"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you look to the **side**, your voice naturally rises in curiosity — just like the rising second
tone of **páng**!

**📍 Usage Note:**

旁 is commonly used to indicate location or position relative to something else, especially in the
compound 旁边 (beside).
