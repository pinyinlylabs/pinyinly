**🗣️ Pronunciation of 群 (qún)**

- **Pinyin:** qún
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question "huh?"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but more back in the throat)
- **ún** sounds like **"oon"** in "moon", but with second tone → rise up
- **qún** sounds like **"chün"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up, like you're asking "A group?" — that's the tone pattern of **qún**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 群 (qún) - "group, crowd"
- 人群 (rén qún) - "crowd of people"
- 群体 (qún tǐ) - "group, collective"
- 羊群 (yáng qún) - "flock of sheep"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you see a group forming, you might ask "qún?" with that rising, curious tone!
