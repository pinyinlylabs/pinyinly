**🗣️ Pronunciation of 鬥 (dòu)**

- **Pinyin:** dòu
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "do"
- **òu** sounds like **"oh"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **dòu** sounds like **"doh!"** with a sharp falling pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone: Say it like you're shouting in battle:
**"dòu!"** — that's the forceful, decisive tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 鬥 (dòu) - "fight; struggle"
- 战斗 (zhàn dòu) - "battle; combat"
- 斗争 (dòu zhēng) - "struggle; fight"

**💡 Special Notes:**

鬥 is the traditional form. In simplified Chinese, it's written as 斗. Both have the same
pronunciation and meaning.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you **fight**, you strike with force and determination — that matches the sharp, falling tone
of 鬥!
