**🗣️ Pronunciation of 族 (zú)**

- **Pinyin:** zú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"z"** in "zebra"
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "zoo" but with second tone → rising tone
- **zú** sounds like **"zoo?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about someone's family background: **"zú?"** — that rising tone suggests
inquiry about kinship!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 族 (zú) - "clan, ethnic group, family"
- 民族 (mín zú) - "ethnic group, nationality"
- 家族 (jiā zú) - "family clan"
- 种族 (zhǒng zú) - "race, ethnicity"
- 族人 (zú rén) - "clanspeople, ethnic group members"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When asking about someone's **clan** or ethnic background, your voice naturally rises with curiosity
— just like the rising second tone of **zú**!

**📍 Cultural Note:**

族 is important in Chinese culture and politics, especially in terms like 中华民族 (Chinese nation)
and referring to China's 56 recognized ethnic groups.
