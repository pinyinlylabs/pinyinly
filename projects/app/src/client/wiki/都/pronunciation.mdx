**🗣️ Pronunciation of 都 (dōu)**

- **Pinyin:** dōu
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "do"
- **ōu** sounds like **"oh"** but with first tone → steady high pitch
- **dōu** sounds like **"doh"** with a steady high tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Keep it steady and high, like singing a sustained note: **"dōu"** — that's the tone pattern of
**dōu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 都 (dōu) - "all; both; entirely"
- 都是 (dōu shì) - "all are; both are"
- 都有 (dōu yǒu) - "all have"
- 都会 (dōu huì) - "all can; everyone can"
- 我们都 (wǒ men dōu) - "all of us"

**💡 Special Note:**

都 has two pronunciations:

- **都 (dōu)** - "all; both" (first tone) - more common
- **都 (dū)** - "capital city" (first tone) as in 首都 (shǒu dū) "capital"

The pronunciation in this context focuses on the "all" meaning.
