**🗣️ Pronunciation of 户 (hù)**

- **Pinyin:** hù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "moon" but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **hù** sounds like **"hoo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like firmly closing a door: **"hù!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern of **hù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 户 (hù) - "door; household"
- 户口 (hù kǒu) - "household registration"
- 用户 (yòng hù) - "user; customer"
- 住户 (zhù hù) - "resident; household"
- 开户 (kāi hù) - "open an account"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Door** — the sharp fourth tone is like the firm sound of a door closing!
