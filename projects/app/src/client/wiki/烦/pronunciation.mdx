**🗣️ Pronunciation of 烦 (fán)**

- **Pinyin:** fán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fun"
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** with a rising tone, similar to saying "Huh?" when confused
- **fán** sounds like **"fahn"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Really?" — **"fán?"** — that questioning upward inflection is the second
tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 烦 (fán) - "bother; annoying"
- 烦人 (fán rén) - "annoying person"
- 麻烦 (má fán) - "troublesome; to bother"
- 不烦 (bù fán) - "not bothersome"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 烦 as the feeling when someone keeps asking you questions — that rising, annoyed "What?!"
tone matches the second tone perfectly!
