**🗣️ Pronunciation of 被 (bèi)**

- **Pinyin:** bèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "day" but with sharp falling tone
- **bèi** sounds like **"bay!"** with a sharp drop in pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 被 (bèi) - "used in passive voice; by"
- 被子 (bèi zi) - "quilt; blanket"
- 被动 (bèi dòng) - "passive"
- 被迫 (bèi pò) - "be forced to"
- 被告 (bèi gào) - "defendant; accused"

**📝 Note on Usage:**

被 has two main meanings:

- **被 (bèi)** - passive voice marker: "被雨淋了" (bèi yǔ lín le) - "got caught in the rain"
- **被 (bèi)** - quilt/blanket: "盖被子" (gài bèi zi) - "cover with a blanket"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sharp falling tone sounds decisive, like firmly pulling a blanket over yourself or definitively
stating something was done TO someone (passive voice)!
