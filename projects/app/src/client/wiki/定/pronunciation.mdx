**🗣️ Pronunciation of 定 (dìng)**

- **Pinyin:** dìng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ìng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing", but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **dìng** sounds like **"ding!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like making a firm decision: **"dìng!"** — that decisive drop is perfect for
**settle/decide**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 定 (dìng) - "to settle; decide; fix"
- 决定 (jué dìng) - "to decide; decision"
- 一定 (yí dìng) - "definitely; certainly"
- 确定 (què dìng) - "to confirm; certain"
- 规定 (guī dìng) - "regulation; to stipulate"
- 制定 (zhì dìng) - "to formulate; establish"
- 定期 (dìng qī) - "regular; periodic"

**📍 Usage Note:**

定 is commonly used in words related to decisions, certainty, and establishing something fixed or
regular.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Settle** — the sharp falling fourth tone is like the firmness of making a **definite decision**!
