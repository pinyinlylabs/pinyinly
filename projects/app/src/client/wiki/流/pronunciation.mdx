**🗣️ Pronunciation of 流 (liú)**

- **Pinyin:** liú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **iú** sounds like **"yo"** in "yo-yo", but with second tone → rise up
- **liú** sounds like **"lee-yo?"** with a rising inflection

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"liú?"** — that's the tone pattern of **liú**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 流 (liú) - "flow"
- 流水 (liú shuǐ) - "flowing water"
- 流行 (liú xíng) - "popular/trendy"
- 流利 (liú lì) - "fluent"
- 流传 (liú chuán) - "pass down/spread"
- 交流 (jiāo liú) - "exchange/communicate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

流 (liú) contains the water radical (氵) on the left, which perfectly represents the concept of
water flowing — the core meaning of this character!
