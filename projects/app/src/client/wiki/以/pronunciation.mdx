**🗣️ Pronunciation of 以 (yǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** yǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǐ** sounds like **"yee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"yǐ..."** — that's the contemplative tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 以前 (yǐ qián) - "before; previously"
- 以后 (yǐ hòu) - "after; later"
- 可以 (kě yǐ) - "can; may"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

以 means "by means of" — like thoughtfully considering "**by** what means?" with that reflective
third tone!
