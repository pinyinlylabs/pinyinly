**🗣️ Pronunciation of 格 (gé)**

- **Pinyin:** gé
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **é** sounds like **"uh"** in "duh" but with a rising tone
- **gé** sounds like **"guh?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about a pattern: **"gé?"** — with that upward inflection.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 格 (gé) - "pattern/grid"
- 格子 (gé zi) - "squares/grid"
- 性格 (xìng gé) - "personality"
- 价格 (jià gé) - "price"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **gé** as questioning a pattern — the rising tone is like asking "what pattern is this?"
