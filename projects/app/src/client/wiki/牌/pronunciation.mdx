**🗣️ Pronunciation of 牌 (pái)**

- **Pinyin:** pái
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pie" but with a slight puff of air
- **ái** sounds like **"eye"** but with a rising tone
- **pái** sounds like **"pie?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about a sign: **"pái?"** — that questioning rise is the **second tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 牌 (pái) - "signboard; sign"
- 牌子 (pái zi) - "sign; brand"
- 招牌 (zhāo pai) - "signboard; shop sign"
- 品牌 (pǐn pái) - "brand"
- 扑克牌 (pū kè pái) - "playing cards"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **牌** as pointing up at a sign: **"pái?"** — that upward questioning gesture matches the
**second tone**!
