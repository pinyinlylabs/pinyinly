World Cup; the FIFA World Cup; the premier international soccer/football tournament held every four
years featuring national teams from around the globe.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                                         |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | shìjiè bēi                                   |
| Core meaning   | World Cup; international soccer championship |
| Part of speech | proper noun                                  |
| Tone           | fourth + fourth + first tone                 |

## Word Breakdown

世界杯 combines global scope with the concept of a championship trophy.

| Component | Meaning                                   |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------- |
| **世界**  | World; global; international; all nations |
| **杯**    | Cup; trophy; championship award           |

## Understanding

Think of 世界杯 as **"the cup that represents the entire world"**:

- 世界 (shìjiè) emphasizes the global nature - all countries participate
- 杯 (bēi) refers to the actual trophy that winners receive
- It's THE championship that every soccer nation dreams of winning
- Represents the pinnacle of international soccer competition
- Unites the entire world around one sport every four years

## Cultural Significance

### **Global Phenomenon**

The 世界杯 is more than just a sports event:

- **Most watched sporting event** in the world
- **Brings nations together** in peaceful competition
- **Cultural celebration** beyond just soccer
- **National pride** and identity on display
- **Economic impact** on host countries

### **In Chinese Culture**

- **足球热** (zúqiú rè) - "soccer fever" sweeps China during World Cup years
- **国家荣誉** (guójiā róngyù) - represents national honor and achievement
- **全民关注** (quánmín guānzhù) - captures attention of entire population
- **体育强国梦** (tǐyù qiángguó mèng) - reflects China's dream of becoming a sports powerhouse

## Usage Examples

### **Watching and Following**

- **看世界杯** (kàn shìjiè bēi) - "watch the World Cup"
- **世界杯决赛** (shìjiè bēi juésài) - "World Cup final"
- **支持世界杯** (zhīchí shìjiè bēi) - "support/follow the World Cup"

### **Participation and Competition**

- **进入世界杯** (jìnrù shìjiè bēi) - "qualify for the World Cup"
- **赢得世界杯** (yíngdé shìjiè bēi) - "win the World Cup"
- **世界杯冠军** (shìjiè bēi guànjūn) - "World Cup champion"

### **Event Organization**

- **举办世界杯** (jǔbàn shìjiè bēi) - "host the World Cup"
- **世界杯申办** (shìjiè bēi shēnbàn) - "bid for the World Cup"

## Related Soccer Terms

- **足球** (zúqiú) - "soccer/football" (the sport)
- **国际足联** (guójì zúlián) - "FIFA" (international soccer federation)
- **预选赛** (yùxuǎnsài) - "qualifying rounds"
- **小组赛** (xiǎozǔsài) - "group stage"
- **淘汰赛** (táotàisài) - "knockout stage"

## Historical Context

### **China and the World Cup**

- **中国队** (Zhōngguó duì) qualified only once (2002 Korea/Japan)
- **世界杯梦想** (shìjiè bēi mèngxiǎng) - "World Cup dream" remains strong in China
- **足球改革** (zúqiú gǎigé) - ongoing efforts to improve Chinese soccer
- **青训计划** (qīngxùn jìhuà) - youth development programs to reach World Cup

## Social Impact

The 世界杯 creates unique social phenomena:

- **全球狂欢** (quánqiú kuánghuān) - "global carnival" atmosphere
- **文化交流** (wénhuà jiāoliú) - cultural exchange between nations
- **和平竞争** (hépíng jìngzhēng) - peaceful competition on global stage
- **体育外交** (tǐyù wàijiāo) - sports diplomacy between countries

## Modern Relevance

Recent World Cups have showcased:

- **技术创新** (jìshù chuàngxīn) - technological innovations (VAR, goal-line technology)
- **全媒体转播** (quán méitǐ zhuǎnbō) - multi-media broadcasting reaching billions
- **社交媒体时代** (shèjiāo méitǐ shídài) - social media era changing how fans engage

The 世界杯 represents humanity's ability to unite around **shared passion, peaceful competition, and
collective celebration**.
