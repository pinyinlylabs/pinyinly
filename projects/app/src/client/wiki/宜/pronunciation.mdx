**🗣️ Pronunciation of 宜 (yí)**

- **Pinyin:** yí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with second tone → rising upward
- **yí** sounds like **"yee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're suggesting something suitable: **"yí?"** — that's the rising tone pattern of
**yí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 宜 (yí) - "suitable; appropriate; should"
- 便宜 (pián yi) - "cheap; inexpensive"
- 不宜 (bù yí) - "not suitable; inadvisable"
- 适宜 (shì yí) - "suitable; appropriate"

**📍 Usage Note:**

宜 is often used in formal or written Chinese to indicate what is appropriate or suitable. It's
commonly seen in signs, instructions, and formal recommendations.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Suitable** — the rising second tone is like raising a suggestion about what's **appropriate**!
