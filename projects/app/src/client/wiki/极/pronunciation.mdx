**🗣️ Pronunciation of 极 (jí)**

- **Pinyin:** jí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but softer, like "gee")
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with second tone → rise up
- **jí** sounds like **"jee?"** with a rising tone

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 极 (jí) - "extreme; very"
- 极了 (jí le) - "extremely; very much"
- 积极 (jī jí) - "active; positive"
- 太极 (tài jí) - "Tai Chi"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Something **extreme** reaches higher and higher — that's the rising second tone of **jí**!
