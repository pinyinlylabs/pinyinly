**🗣️ Pronunciation of 空 (kōng)**

- **Pinyin:** kōng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **ōng** sounds like **"ong"** in "long", but with first tone → steady and high
- **kōng** sounds like **"kong"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and steady** tone:

Keep your voice high and flat throughout: **"kōng"** — like holding a steady musical note.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 空 (kōng) - "empty"
- 空气 (kōng qì) - "air"
- 空调 (kōng tiáo) - "air conditioning"
- 天空 (tiān kōng) - "sky"

**💡 Special Notes:**

空 can also be pronounced **kòng** (fourth tone) in some contexts, meaning "free time" or "spare":

- 有空 (yǒu kòng) - "have free time"
- 空儿 (kòng er) - "spare time"

But the most common pronunciation is **kōng** (first tone) meaning "empty."
