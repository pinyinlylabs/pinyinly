**🗣️ Pronunciation of 仍 (réng)**

- **Pinyin:** réng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **r** like **"r"** in "ring" (but softer, almost like "zh")
- **éng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung" but with a rising tone
- **réng** sounds like **"rung?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "still?" with curiosity: **"réng?"** — that's the rising tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 仍然 (réng rán) - "still; yet"
- 仍旧 (réng jiù) - "still; as before"
- 仍是 (réng shì) - "still is"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

仍 means "still" — like asking "**still?**" with that rising, questioning tone!
