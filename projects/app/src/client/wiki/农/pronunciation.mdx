**🗣️ Pronunciation of 农 (nóng)**

- **Pinyin:** nóng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **óng** sounds like **"ong"** but with a rising tone
- **nóng** sounds like **"nong?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is **rising**:

Say it like you're asking about farming: **"nóng?"** — with that upward inflection.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 农 (nóng) - "agriculture; farming"
- 农业 (nóng yè) - "agriculture"
- 农村 (nóng cūn) - "countryside; rural area"
- 农民 (nóng mín) - "farmer"
- 农场 (nóng chǎng) - "farm"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of asking someone about their farming work — the rising tone sounds like curiosity:
**"nóng?"**
