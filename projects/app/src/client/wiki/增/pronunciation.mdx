**🗣️ Pronunciation of 增 (zēng)**

- **Pinyin:** zēng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Zeng"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"ds"** at the end of "kids" — but as a starting sound
- **ēng** sounds like **"ung"** but held steady and high
- **zēng** sounds like **"dzung"** with a steady high pitch

**🎯 Mastering the Chinese "z" sound:**

The **z** in Chinese is like:

1. **Say "ds"** from the end of "kids"
2. **Use it to start** a syllable instead of end it
3. **Keep tongue flat** — tip against lower teeth
4. **Brief and clean** — quick "dz" sound

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like holding a steady, high musical note: **"zēng"** — maintain that high pitch throughout.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 增 (zēng) - "increase"
- 增加 (zēng jiā) - "increase"
- 增长 (zēng zhǎng) - "grow"
- 增强 (zēng qiáng) - "strengthen"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **增** as a steady **increase** — the high, flat first tone represents continuous,
unwavering growth!
