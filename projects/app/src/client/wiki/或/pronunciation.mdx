**🗣️ Pronunciation of 或 (huò)**

- **Pinyin:** huò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uò** sounds like **"woh"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **huò** sounds like **"hwoh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like presenting an option decisively: **"huò!"** — that's the definitive tone pattern of
**huò**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 或 (huò) - "or; perhaps"
- 或者 (huò zhě) - "or; either... or"
- 或许 (huò xǔ) - "perhaps; maybe"
- 或是 (huò shì) - "or; either"
- 生或死 (shēng huò sǐ) - "life or death"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Or** — the sharp fourth tone cuts between options like making a decisive choice!
