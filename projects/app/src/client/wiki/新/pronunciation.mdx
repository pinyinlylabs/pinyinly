**🗣️ Pronunciation of 新 (xīn)**

- **Pinyin:** xīn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Sheen"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep" (but slightly more front-of-mouth)
- **īn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen" but with first tone → high and steady
- **xīn** sounds like **"sheen"** with a high, flat pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're excited about something fresh and new: **"xīn..."** — that steady, bright tone is
perfect for "new"!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 新 (xīn) - "new, fresh"
- 新年 (xīn nián) - "New Year"
- 新的 (xīn de) - "new (adjective)"
- 新闻 (xīn wén) - "news"
- 最新 (zuì xīn) - "newest, latest"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**New** things shine bright — just like the bright, high first tone of **xīn**!

**📍 Cultural Note:**

新 is especially important during Chinese New Year (新年), when everything is about renewal, fresh
starts, and new beginnings.
