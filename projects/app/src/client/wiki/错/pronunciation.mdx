**🗣️ Pronunciation of 错 (cuò)**

- **Pinyin:** cuò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats"
- **uò** sounds like **"whoa"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **cuò** sounds like **"ts-whoa!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop sharply: **"cuò!"** — like saying "Wrong!" with authority.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 错 (cuò) - "wrong, mistake"
- 错了 (cuò le) - "wrong, made a mistake"
- 错误 (cuò wù) - "error, mistake"
- 不错 (bù cuò) - "not bad, pretty good"

**📝 Special Notes:**

The character 错 contains the metal radical 钅. The falling tone perfectly matches the meaning —
when something is **wrong**, we often say it with a sharp, definitive tone.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you realize something is **wrong**, you say "Wrong!" with a sharp falling tone — just like
**cuò**!
