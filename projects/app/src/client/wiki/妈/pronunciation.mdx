**🗣️ Pronunciation of 妈 (mā)**

- **Pinyin:** mā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "mother"
- **ā** sounds like **"ah"**, but with first tone → high and steady
- **mā** sounds like **"mah"** with a high, flat pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it warmly and clearly: **"mā..."** — that's the steady, loving tone you'd use to call your
mother.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 妈 (mā) - "mom, mother" (informal)
- 妈妈 (mā ma) - "mama, mommy"
- 老妈 (lǎo mā) - "old mom" (colloquial)
- 妈咪 (mā mī) - "mommy" (from English)
- 叫妈 (jiào mā) - "call (someone) mom"

**💡 Special Notes:**

妈 is the informal, affectionate term for "mother." It combines the "woman" radical (女)
with 马 (horse), though the connection is phonetic rather than semantic. This is one of the first
words children learn and is universally recognizable.

**📍 Formal vs. Informal:**

- 妈妈 (mā ma) - informal "mom"
- 母亲 (mǔ qīn) - formal "mother"
- 妈 (mā) - casual "mom"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sound "mā" is similar to "mama" in many languages worldwide, making it naturally easy to
remember!
