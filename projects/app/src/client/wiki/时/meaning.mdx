## Core Meaning & Usage

时 represents **specific time points, hours, and temporal moments**. It's used:

1. **Clock time**: 三点时 (sān diǎn shí) - "at three o'clock"
2. **Moments**: 这时 (zhè shí) - "at this time"
3. **Periods**: 平时 (píngshí) - "usually; in normal times"
4. **Seasons**: 夏时 (xiàshí) - "summer time"

## Examples

- **时间** (shíjiān) - "time (duration or moment)"
- **小时** (xiǎoshí) - "hour" (literally "small time")
- **时候** (shíhou) - "time; moment; when"
- **及时** (jíshí) - "timely; in time"
- **同时** (tóngshí) - "at the same time; simultaneously"
- **有时** (yǒushí) - "sometimes"

## Time Expressions

时 in temporal contexts:

- **什么时候** (shénme shíhou) - "when; what time"
- **那时** (nàshí) - "at that time; then"
- **此时** (cǐshí) - "at this moment; now"
- **战时** (zhànshí) - "wartime"
- **平时** (píngshí) - "peacetime; normally"

## Measure Word Usage

时 as a measure word:

- **几时** (jǐshí) - "what time" (formal)
- **一时** (yīshí) - "for a moment; temporarily"
- **二十四时** (èrshísì shí) - "24 hours" (formal time)

## Grammar Functions

- **Temporal noun**: 时间很宝贵 (shíjiān hěn bǎoguì) - "time is precious"
- **Time adverbial**: 学习时很认真 (xuéxí shí hěn rènzhēn) - "very serious when studying"
- **Measure word**: 三小时 (sān xiǎoshí) - "three hours"

## Common Collocations

- **按时** (ànshí) - "on time; punctually"
- **临时** (línshí) - "temporary; for the time being"
- **随时** (suíshí) - "at any time; anytime"
- **定时** (dìngshí) - "at a fixed time; timed"

## Cultural Context

时 reflects Chinese concepts of:

- **Temporal precision**: Importance of exact timing and punctuality
- **Seasonal awareness**: Recognition of appropriate times for activities
- **Ritual timing**: Proper timing for ceremonies and social functions
- **Opportunity**: 时机 (shíjī) - the right moment for action

## Time Philosophy

In Chinese thought, 时 represents:

- **天时** (tiānshí) - "heavenly timing" (natural/cosmic timing)
- **时运** (shíyùn) - "timing and fortune" (luck related to timing)
- **时势** (shíshì) - "trends of the times" (historical momentum)

The character emphasizes that time is not just duration but significant moments that require human
attention and respect for natural cycles.
