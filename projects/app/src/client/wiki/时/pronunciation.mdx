**🗣️ Pronunciation of 时 (shí)**

- **Pinyin:** shí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with second tone → rising upward
- **shí** sounds like **"she?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about time: **"shí?"** — that upward rise is the second tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 时 (shí) - "time; hour"
- 时间 (shí jiān) - "time"
- 时候 (shí hou) - "time; moment"
- 有时 (yǒu shí) - "sometimes"
