**🗣️ Pronunciation of 手 (shǒu)**

- **Pinyin:** shǒu
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shush"
- **ǒu** sounds like **"oh"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **shǒu** sounds like **"show"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're examining your hand: **"shǒu..."** — that's the contemplative tone pattern of
**shǒu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 手 (shǒu) - "hand"
- 手机 (shǒu jī) - "cell phone; mobile phone"
- 手表 (shǒu biǎo) - "wristwatch"
- 握手 (wò shǒu) - "shake hands"
- 手指 (shǒu zhǐ) - "finger"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Hand** — the dipping third tone is like the gesture of looking at your hand!
