**🗣️ Pronunciation of 寸 (cùn)**

- **Pinyin:** cùn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats"
- **ùn** sounds like **"oon"** in "moon" but with a sharp falling tone
- **cùn** sounds like **"tsoon!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"cùn!"** — that's the tone pattern of **cùn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 寸 (cùn) - "inch"
- 寸步 (cùn bù) - "inch by inch"
- 英寸 (yīng cùn) - "inch"
- 分寸 (fēn cùn) - "proper limits"
- 得寸进尺 (dé cùn jìn chǐ) - "give an inch, take a mile"
