**🗣️ Pronunciation of 姑 (gū)**

- **Pinyin:** gū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "goose" but held steady and high
- **gū** sounds like **"goo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like you're calling your aunt with respect: **"gū"** — that steady, elevated tone is the
**first tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 姑 (gū) - "father's sister; aunt"
- 姑姑 (gū gu) - "aunt" (more familiar)
- 姑娘 (gū niang) - "girl; young woman"
- 小姑 (xiǎo gū) - "father's younger sister"
- 大姑 (dà gū) - "father's elder sister"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The first tone's steady high pitch is like respectfully addressing your **father's sister** —
maintain that elevated, respectful tone throughout!
