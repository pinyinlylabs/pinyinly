**🗣️ Pronunciation of 题 (tí)**

- **Pinyin:** tí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with rising tone
- **tí** sounds like **"tea?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone, like when you ask a question. Say **"tí"** like you're
asking "What's the question?" — perfect for a word meaning "question"!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 题 (tí) - "question/topic"
- 题目 (tí mù) - "title/topic/question"
- 问题 (wèn tí) - "question/problem"
- 话题 (huà tí) - "topic of conversation"
- 主题 (zhǔ tí) - "theme/main topic"
- 题材 (tí cái) - "subject matter"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you ask "What's the **question**?" you naturally use a rising tone — that's the second tone of
**tí**!
