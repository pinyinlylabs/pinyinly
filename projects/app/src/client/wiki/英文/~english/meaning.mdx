The English language; English writing or text.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                        |
| -------------- | --------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | yīngwén                     |
| Core meaning   | English language; English  |
| Part of speech | noun                        |
| Tone           | first + second tone         |

## Visual Breakdown

英文 combines talent with written expression:

| Component | Visual Description                                                       |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **英**    | Outstanding/brave - represents excellence, talent, or distinguished     |
| **文**    | Writing/culture - represents written language, literature, and culture  |

## Mnemonic

Think of 英文 as **the outstanding written culture**:

- 英 (outstanding/excellent) + 文 (written culture) = "excellent written culture"
- Like the distinguished literary tradition of English-speaking peoples
- The "outstanding" form of written expression from England/Britain
- A written system that represents cultural excellence and global reach

This creates the meaning: **the written language and culture of English**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

英文 refers to **the English language, especially written English**. It's used for:

1. **Language identification**: 学英文 (xué yīngwén) - "study English"
2. **Written materials**: 英文书 (yīngwén shū) - "English book"
3. **Language skills**: 英文很好 (yīngwén hěn hǎo) - "English is very good"
4. **Academic context**: 英文专业 (yīngwén zhuānyè) - "English major"

## Examples

- **英文名字** (yīngwén míngzi) - "English name"
- **英文翻译** (yīngwén fānyì) - "English translation"
- **英文老师** (yīngwén lǎoshī) - "English teacher"
- **英文水平** (yīngwén shuǐpíng) - "English level"
- **英文字母** (yīngwén zìmǔ) - "English letters/alphabet"

## Cultural Context

英文 in Chinese education and society:
- **Global importance**: Essential for international communication
- **Educational priority**: Major subject in Chinese schools
- **Career advantage**: Important for many professional fields
- **Cultural bridge**: Represents connection to Western culture

## Comparison with Related Terms

- **英文** (yīngwén) - English language (especially written)
- **英语** (yīngyǔ) - English language (especially spoken)
- **英国** (yīngguó) - England/Britain (the country)
- **英国人** (yīngguórén) - British/English people

## Usage Patterns

- **英文 + Noun**: "English [thing]" (English book, English name, etc.)
- **学英文**: "study English"
- **用英文**: "use English; in English"

英文 is fundamental for discussing English language learning and international communication.
