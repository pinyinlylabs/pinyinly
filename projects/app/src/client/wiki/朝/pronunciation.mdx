**🗣️ Pronunciation of 朝 (cháo)**

- **Pinyin:** cháo
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair"
- **áo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow", but with second tone → rising up
- **cháo** sounds like **"chow?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "really?" with surprise: **"cháo?"** — that upward lift is the second tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 朝 (cháo) - "towards, facing"
- 朝向 (cháo xiàng) - "direction, orientation"
- 朝着 (cháo zhe) - "toward, in the direction of"
- 朝阳 (cháo yáng) - "facing the sun, morning sun"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **朝** as pointing "towards" something — the rising tone matches the upward motion of
directing attention toward a target!
