**🗣️ Pronunciation of 人 (rén)**

- **Pinyin:** rén
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **r** like **"r"** in "red" (but with tongue tip curled up)
- **én** sounds like **"en"** in "pen", but with second tone → rising like a question
- **rén** sounds like **"ren?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 人 (rén) - "person", "people"
- 人们 (rén men) - "people"
- 人民 (rén mín) - "people", "citizens"
- 人类 (rén lèi) - "humanity", "human race"
- 好人 (hǎo rén) - "good person"
- 别人 (bié rén) - "other people"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

人 means **person** - say **"ren?"** with a curious, rising tone (second tone), like asking
"person?" when meeting someone new!
