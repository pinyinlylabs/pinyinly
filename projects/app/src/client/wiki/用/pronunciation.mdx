**🗣️ Pronunciation of 用 (yòng)**

- **Pinyin:** yòng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Stop!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **òng** sounds like **"ong"** in "song", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **yòng** sounds like **"yong!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 用 (yòng) - "to use"
- 用法 (yòng fǎ) - "usage; method"
- 用心 (yòng xīn) - "to be attentive"
- 不用 (bù yòng) - "no need; don't have to"
- 常用 (cháng yòng) - "commonly used"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Fourth tone is like commanding someone to **"Use!"** - your voice **drops sharply** throughout
**yòng**.
