**🗣️ Pronunciation of 资 (zī)**

- **Pinyin:** zī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Zee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"dz"** in "adze" (voiced consonant)
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **zī** sounds like **"dzee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (First tone: ˉ)**

The **first tone** is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like you're sustaining a note: **"zī—"** — that's the tone pattern of **zī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 资格 (zī gé) - "qualification"
- 资金 (zī jīn) - "capital; funds"
- 投资 (tóu zī) - "invest; investment"
- 资料 (zī liào) - "material; data"
