**🗣️ Pronunciation of 千 (qiān)**

- **Pinyin:** qiān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but with rounded lips)
- **iān** sounds like **"yen"** in "yen" but held steady and high
- **qiān** sounds like **"chyen"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're confidently stating a large number: **"qiān!"** — that steady, impressive tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 千 (qiān) - "thousand"
- 千万 (qiān wàn) - "ten million; by all means"
- 千克 (qiān kè) - "kilogram"
- 一千 (yī qiān) - "one thousand"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

千 means "thousand" — the steady, high first tone sounds impressive and grand, like announcing a big
number!
