**🗣️ Pronunciation of 伟 (wěi)**

- **Pinyin:** wěi
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ěi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **wěi** sounds like **"way"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about something impressive: **"wěi..."** — that dip and rise shows the
third tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 伟 (wěi) - "great; mighty"
- 伟大 (wěi dà) - "great; magnificent"
- 伟人 (wěi rén) - "great person"
- 宏伟 (hóng wěi) - "grand; magnificent"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"way"** with a thoughtful dip-and-rise tone — like contemplating something truly great!
