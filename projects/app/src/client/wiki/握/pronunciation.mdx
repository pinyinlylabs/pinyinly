**🗣️ Pronunciation of 握 (wò)**

- **Pinyin:** wò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ò** sounds like **"aw"** in "awful", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **wò** sounds like **"waw"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly grasping something: **"wò!"** — that's the decisive falling tone of
**wò**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 握 (wò) - "grasp; hold"
- 握手 (wò shǒu) - "shake hands"
- 把握 (bǎ wò) - "grasp; control"
- 掌握 (zhǎng wò) - "master; grasp"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"握"** as **"waw"** with a sharp drop — like firmly grasping something in your hand!
