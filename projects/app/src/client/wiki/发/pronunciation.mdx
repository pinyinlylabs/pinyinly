**🗣️ Pronunciation of 发**

发 has **two different pronunciations** depending on its meaning:

**📍 fā (first tone) - "to send, to emit, to develop"**

- **Pinyin:** fā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "father"
- **ā** sounds like **"ah"** but held steady and high
- **fā** sounds like **"fah"** with a steady high pitch

**📍 fà (fourth tone) - "hair"**

- **Pinyin:** fà
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "father"
- **à** sounds like **"ah"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **fà** sounds like **"fah!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

**fā** (first tone) = **steady high** — like sending something far away **fà** (fourth tone) =
**sharp drop** ↘️ like hair falling down

**📝 Usage Guide:**

**发 (fā) - "to send, to emit, to develop":**

- **发**送 (fā sòng) - "to send, to deliver"
- **发**现 (fā xiàn) - "to discover"
- **发**展 (fā zhǎn) - "to develop"
- **发**出 (fā chū) - "to emit, to give out"

**发 (fà) - "hair":**

- 头**发** (tóu fà) - "hair (on head)"
- **发**型 (fà xíng) - "hairstyle"
- 理**发** (lǐ fà) - "to cut hair"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**fā** (send/develop) = stays high and steady — like launching something **fà** (hair) = falls down
sharply — like hair hanging down
