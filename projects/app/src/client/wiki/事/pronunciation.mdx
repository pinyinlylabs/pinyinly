**🗣️ Pronunciation of 事 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Stop!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **ì** sounds like **"ir"** in "shirt" but with a sharp falling tone
- **shì** sounds like **"shr!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** is a sharp **falling** tone, like giving a firm command:

Say it like you're decisively stating something important: **"shì!"** — that's the authoritative
tone of **shì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 事 (shì) - "matter; thing; affair"
- 事情 (shì qíng) - "matter; affair"
- 故事 (gù shì) - "story"
- 工作事 (gōng zuò shì) - "work matters"
- 没事 (méi shì) - "no problem; it's fine"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **事** as something important that demands attention — that's the **fourth tone**
decisiveness!
