**🗣️ Pronunciation of 鸟 (niǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** niǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **iǎo** sounds like **"ee-ow"** blended together with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **niǎo** sounds like **"nee-ow"** with a dip-then-rise, similar to "meow" but starting with "n"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully observing a bird: **"niǎo..."** — that contemplative tone pattern of
**niǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 鸟 (niǎo) - "bird"
- 小鸟 (xiǎo niǎo) - "little bird"
- 鸟儿 (niǎo er) - "birdie" (affectionate)
- 鸟类 (niǎo lèi) - "bird species"
- 飞鸟 (fēi niǎo) - "flying bird"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

鸟 sounds like a cat saying "meow" but with an "n" - imagine a bird making a sound that's almost
like a cat's meow, but more nasal: **"niǎo"**!
