**🗣️ Pronunciation of 般 (bān)**

- **Pinyin:** bān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady musical note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **ān** sounds like **"on"** in "upon", held steady and high
- **bān** sounds like **"bahn"** with a calm, even pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and flat**:

Keep it steady and neutral, like categorizing things: **"bāān"** — smooth and consistent.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 般 (bān) - "sort; kind; type"
- 一般 (yī bān) - "ordinary; general; usually"
- 这般 (zhè bān) - "this kind; such"
- 那般 (nà bān) - "that kind; such"
- 百般 (bǎi bān) - "in every possible way"

**💡 Usage Note:**

般 (bān) is often used as a classifier for types or kinds of things. It's commonly seen in the
word 一般 (yī bān) meaning "ordinary" or "general."
