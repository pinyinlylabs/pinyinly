**🗣️ Pronunciation of 管 (guǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** guǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uǎn** sounds like **"wan"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **guǎn** sounds like **"gwan"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're considering whether to manage something: **"guǎn..."** — that thoughtful tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 管 (guǎn) - "manage; control; tube"
- 管理 (guǎn lǐ) - "manage; administer"
- 不管 (bù guǎn) - "regardless; no matter"
- 管子 (guǎn zi) - "tube; pipe"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **管** as a manager thinking things through — the third tone dips down (considering) then
rises up (deciding to manage)!
