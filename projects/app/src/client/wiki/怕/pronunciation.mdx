**🗣️ Pronunciation of 怕 (pà)**

- **Pinyin:** pà
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pot" but with a strong puff of air
- **à** sounds like **"ah"** in "father" but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **pà** sounds like **"pah!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎯 Mastering the "p" sound:**

The **p** in Chinese is **heavily aspirated** (lots of air):

1. **Start with "p"** like in "pot"
2. **Add a strong puff of air** — hold your hand in front of your mouth and feel the breeze
3. **Make it explosive** — like spitting out a seed
4. **Think "p" + strong breath**

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a sharp **falling** tone:

Say it like you're expressing sudden fear: **"pà!"** — that sharp drop captures the feeling of being
startled or afraid.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 怕 (pà) - "afraid; fear"
- 怕人 (pà rén) - "scary; frightening"
- 害怕 (hài pà) - "to be afraid"
- 不怕 (bù pà) - "not afraid"
- 可怕 (kě pà) - "terrible; horrible"
- 恐怕 (kǒng pà) - "I'm afraid that...; probably"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sharp falling fourth tone **pà!** sounds like someone gasping in fear — perfect for remembering
this character means "afraid"!
