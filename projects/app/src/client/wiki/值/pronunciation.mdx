**🗣️ Pronunciation of 值 (zhí)**

- **Pinyin:** zhí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like the English **"j"** in "jam", but with the **tongue curled back**
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a rising tone
- **zhí** sounds like **"zhee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"zhí?"** — that's the tone pattern of **zhí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 值 (zhí) - "be worth"
- 值得 (zhí de) - "worthwhile"
- 价值 (jià zhí) - "value"
- 数值 (shù zhí) - "numerical value"
