**🗣️ Pronunciation of 负 (fù)**

- **Pinyin:** fù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"That's it!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "food"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "food" but with a sharp falling tone
- **fù** sounds like **"foo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** is **sharp and falling**, like taking on responsibility:

Say it like you're accepting a burden: **"fù!"** — that decisive, downward tone is the **fourth
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 负 (fù) - "bear; carry; shoulder (responsibility)"
- 负责 (fù zé) - "be responsible for"
- 负担 (fù dān) - "burden; bear (a load)"
- 负债 (fù zhài) - "be in debt"
- 负面 (fù miàn) - "negative (aspect)"
- 胜负 (shèng fù) - "victory or defeat"
- 正负 (zhèng fù) - "positive and negative"

**💡 Core Meanings:**

负 (fù) has several related meanings:

1. **Physical**: carry, bear (a load)
2. **Responsibility**: shoulder, take on
3. **Debt/Obligation**: owe, be indebted
4. **Mathematical/Emotional**: negative (opposite of positive)
5. **Competition**: lose, be defeated

All meanings connect to the idea of "carrying" or "bearing" something, whether physical, emotional,
or abstract.
