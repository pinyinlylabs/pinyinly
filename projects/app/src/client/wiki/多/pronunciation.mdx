**🗣️ Pronunciation of 多 (duō)**

- **Pinyin:** duō
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Dwoh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **uō** sounds like **"woh"** but held steady and high
- **duō** sounds like **"dwoh"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're steadily expressing abundance: **"duō..."** — that continuous, level tone is the
**first tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 多 (duō) - "many; much; a lot"
- 多少 (duō shao) - "how many; how much"
- 很多 (hěn duō) - "very many; a lot"
- 多么 (duō me) - "how (exclamatory)"
- 多数 (duō shù) - "majority"
- 多云 (duō yún) - "cloudy"

**💡 Special Notes:**

多 is a fundamental word expressing quantity and abundance. It can function as an adjective meaning
"many" or as an adverb meaning "how" in exclamatory expressions. It's essential for asking about
quantities and describing large amounts.
