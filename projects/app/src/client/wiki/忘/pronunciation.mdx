**🗣️ Pronunciation of 忘 (wàng)**

- **Pinyin:** wàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Wahng!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **àng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **wàng** sounds like **"wahng!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're exclaiming that you forgot something: **"wàng!"** — that's the decisive tone
pattern of **wàng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 忘 (wàng) - "to forget"
- 忘记 (wàng jì) - "to forget"
- 忘了 (wàng le) - "forgot"
- 难忘 (nán wàng) - "unforgettable"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"wahng!"** with a sharp fall — when you suddenly realize you **forgot** something, you
often exclaim with a sharp, decisive tone, just like the fourth tone's pattern!
