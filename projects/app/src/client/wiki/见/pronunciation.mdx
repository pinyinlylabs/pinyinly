**🗣️ Pronunciation of 见 (jiàn)**

- **Pinyin:** jiàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but softer
- **i** like **"ee"** in "see"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **jiàn** sounds like **"jee-ahn"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"jiàn!"** — that's the decisive drop of the fourth tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 见 (jiàn) - "see"
- 见面 (jiàn miàn) - "meet"
- 见到 (jiàn dào) - "see; meet"
- 再见 (zài jiàn) - "goodbye"
- 意见 (yì jiàn) - "opinion"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 见 as "seeing" someone important — you say their name with authority and decisiveness,
hence the sharp falling fourth tone!
