**🗣️ Pronunciation of 部 (bù)**

- **Pinyin:** bù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **bù** sounds like **"boo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying "Stop!" firmly: **"bù!"** — that's the tone pattern of
**bù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 部 (bù) - "department; section; part"
- 部门 (bù mén) - "department"
- 部分 (bù fèn) - "part; section"
- 部长 (bù zhǎng) - "minister; department head"
- 全部 (quán bù) - "all; entire"

**💡 Note:**

部 contains the 邑 radical on the right, which relates to places and administrative divisions. It's
commonly used in organizational and governmental contexts.
