**🗣️ Pronunciation of 低 (dī)**

- **Pinyin:** dī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with first tone → high and steady
- **dī** sounds like **"dee"** held high and flat

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like describing something steadily: **"dī~~"** — that high, level pitch shows the first tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 低 (dī) - "low; short"
- 低头 (dī tóu) - "lower one's head"
- 高低 (gāo dī) - "height; high and low"
- 低声 (dī shēng) - "in a low voice"
- 降低 (jiàng dī) - "reduce; lower"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"dee"** said steadily — like describing something low in a calm, level way!
