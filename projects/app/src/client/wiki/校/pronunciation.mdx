**🗣️ Pronunciation of 校 (xiào)**

- **Pinyin:** xiào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like a soft **"sh"** sound, but your tongue is much closer to your teeth
- **iào** sounds like **"yow"** with a sharp falling tone
- **xiào** sounds like **"shyow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like announcing your school with authority: **"xiào!"** — sharp and clear.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 校 (xiào) - "school"
- 学校 (xué xiào) - "school"
- 校园 (xiào yuán) - "campus"
- 校长 (xiào zhǎng) - "principal"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **xiào** as the bell ringing to announce school — the falling tone is like the
authoritative school bell!
