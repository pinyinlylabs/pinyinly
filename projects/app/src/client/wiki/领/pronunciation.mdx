**🗣️ Pronunciation of 领 (lǐng)**

- **Pinyin:** lǐng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "lead"
- **ǐng** sounds like **"ing"** in "ring" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **lǐng** sounds like **"ling"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone. Say **"lǐng"** like you're thoughtfully
considering who should lead.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 领 (lǐng) - "lead/receive"
- 领导 (lǐng dǎo) - "leader/leadership"
- 领袖 (lǐng xiù) - "leader"
- 领取 (lǐng qǔ) - "receive/collect"
- 领域 (lǐng yù) - "field/domain"
- 白领 (bái lǐng) - "white-collar worker"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of thoughtfully saying "**Ling**" when pondering leadership — that dip-and-rise tone pattern
is **lǐng**!
