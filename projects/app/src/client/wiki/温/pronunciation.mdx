**🗣️ Pronunciation of 温 (wēn)**

- **Pinyin:** wēn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ēn** sounds like **"un"** in "fun", but with first tone → high and steady
- **wēn** sounds like **"when"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady note: **"wēn"** — that's the tone pattern of **wēn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 温 (wēn) - "warm/lukewarm"
- 温度 (wēn dù) - "temperature"
- 温暖 (wēn nuǎn) - "warm"
- 温柔 (wēn róu) - "gentle/tender"
- 温泉 (wēn quán) - "hot spring"
- 气温 (qì wēn) - "air temperature"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

温 (wēn) contains the water radical (氵) on the left. Think of warm water — not too hot, not too
cold, but just the right lukewarm temperature that feels comfortable!
