**🗣️ Pronunciation of 竹 (zhú)**

- **Pinyin:** zhú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "junk" but with tongue curled back
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "too", but with second tone → rising
- **zhú** sounds like **"joo?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start lower and rise up: **"zhú?"** — like asking "Bamboo?" in a questioning tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 竹 (zhú) - "bamboo"
- 竹子 (zhú zi) - "bamboo"
- 竹笋 (zhú sǔn) - "bamboo shoots"
- 熊猫吃竹子 (xióng māo chī zhú zi) - "pandas eat bamboo"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of someone asking **"Joo?"** (bamboo?) with curiosity — the rising second tone matches the
questioning intonation!
