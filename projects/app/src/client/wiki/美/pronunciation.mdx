**🗣️ Pronunciation of 美 (měi)**

- **Pinyin:** měi
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "hmm" when thinking

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "me"
- **ěi** sounds like **"ay"** in "may", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **měi** sounds like **"may"** with a thoughtful dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're considering something beautiful: **"měi..."** — that's the contemplative tone
pattern of **měi**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 美 (měi) - "beauty, beautiful"
- 美国 (měi guó) - "America" (literally "beautiful country")
- 美丽 (měi lì) - "beautiful"
- 美好 (měi hǎo) - "wonderful, fine"
- 美术 (měi shù) - "fine arts"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you see something beautiful, you pause and say "měi..." with that contemplative third tone!
