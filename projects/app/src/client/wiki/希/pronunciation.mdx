**🗣️ Pronunciation of 希 (xī)**

- **Pinyin:** xī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Sheee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **xī** sounds like **"she"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (First tone: ˉ)**

The **first tone** is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like expressing a wish or hope: **"xī..."** — that steady, hopeful tone is the **first
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 希 (xī) - "hope"
- 希望 (xī wàng) - "hope; wish"
- 希腊 (xī là) - "Greece"
- 希奇 (xī qí) - "rare; strange"
- 希少 (xī shǎo) - "rare; scarce"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **希** as expressing **hope** with a steady, optimistic voice — that's the **first tone**
quality!
