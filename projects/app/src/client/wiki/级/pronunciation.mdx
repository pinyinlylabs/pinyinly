**🗣️ Pronunciation of 级 (jí)**

- **Pinyin:** jí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with second tone → rising up
- **jí** sounds like **"jee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about someone's level: **"jí?"** — that's the rising pattern of **jí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 级 (jí) - "level/grade"
- 年级 (nián jí) - "grade level"
- 班级 (bān jí) - "class"
- 高级 (gāo jí) - "advanced level"
- 初级 (chū jí) - "beginner level"
- 中级 (zhōng jí) - "intermediate level"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When asking about someone's **level** or **grade**, your voice naturally rises with curiosity — just
like the second tone of **jí**!
