**🗣️ Pronunciation of 创 (chuàng)**

- **Pinyin:** chuàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair"
- **uàng** sounds like **"wahng"**, but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **chuàng** sounds like **"chwahng!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a decisive statement: **"chuàng!"** — that's the tone pattern of
**chuàng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 创 (chuàng) - "begin; create"
- 创业 (chuàng yè) - "start a business"
- 创作 (chuàng zuò) - "create; compose"
- 创新 (chuàng xīn) - "innovate"
- 创造 (chuàng zào) - "create; invent"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

创 means "begin/create" — imagine the decisive **falling** tone as the moment you **create**
something new!
