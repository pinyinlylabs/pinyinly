**🗣️ Pronunciation of 筷 (kuài)**

- **Pinyin:** kuài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **uài** sounds like **"why"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **kuài** sounds like **"kwy!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving a command — that's the energy of **kuài**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 筷 (kuài) - "chopsticks"
- 筷子 (kuài zi) - "chopsticks"
- 一双筷子 (yī shuāng kuài zi) - "a pair of chopsticks"
- 木筷 (mù kuài) - "wooden chopsticks"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **kuài** as the quick, decisive motion of picking up food with chopsticks — that sharp
fourth tone matches the swift action!
