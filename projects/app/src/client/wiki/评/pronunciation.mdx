**🗣️ Pronunciation of 评 (píng)**

- **Pinyin:** píng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"ping?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "ping" but with a puff of air (aspirated)
- **íng** sounds like **"eeng"** with a rising tone
- **píng** sounds like **"peeng?"** with a questioning rise

**🔊 Key Sound Tips:**

The **p** in Chinese is **aspirated** — hold your hand in front of your mouth and feel a strong puff of air when you say it, like the "p" in "pin" or "pen".

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start **low and rise up** like you're asking a question or showing surprise: **"píng?"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 评 (píng) - "criticize; comment"
- 评价 (píng jià) - "evaluate; assess"
- 批评 (pī píng) - "criticize"
- 评论 (píng lùn) - "comment; review"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"ping"** with a questioning tone — like asking "ping?" when testing a connection, but with that rising intonation!
