**🗣️ Pronunciation of 病 (bìng)**

- **Pinyin:** bìng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **ìng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing", but with a sharp falling tone
- **bìng** sounds like **"bing!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're definitively stating someone is sick: **"bìng!"** — that firm, falling
declaration.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 病 (bìng) - "illness; disease; sick"
- 病人 (bìng rén) - "patient; sick person"
- 生病 (shēng bìng) - "to get sick"
- 看病 (kàn bìng) - "to see a doctor"
- 毛病 (máo bìng) - "problem; defect"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

病 means "illness" — the sharp falling fourth tone sounds like a doctor's serious diagnosis:
**"You're sick!"**
