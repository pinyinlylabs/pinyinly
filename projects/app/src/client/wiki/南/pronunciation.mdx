**🗣️ Pronunciation of 南 (nán)**

- **Pinyin:** nán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "new"
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** but with second tone → rise up like a question
- **nán** sounds like **"nahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone: Start low and rise up, like you're asking "Where?" —
that's the tone pattern of **nán**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 南 (nán) - "south"
- 南方 (nán fāng) - "the south"
- 南边 (nán biān) - "south side"
- 东南 (dōng nán) - "southeast"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

南 means "south" — when asking for directions, your voice naturally **rises** in question: "Which
way is south?"
