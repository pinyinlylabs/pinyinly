**🗣️ Pronunciation of 脸 (liǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** liǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **iǎn** sounds like **"ee-ahn"** with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **liǎn** sounds like **"lee-ahn"** with a gentle dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're expressing mild concern about someone's face: **"liǎn..."** — that's the gentle
tone pattern of **liǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 脸 (liǎn) - "face"
- 脸色 (liǎn sè) - "facial expression; complexion"
- 洗脸 (xǐ liǎn) - "wash one's face"
- 笑脸 (xiào liǎn) - "smiling face"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

脸 refers to the face, and the third tone's gentle curve reflects the soft, expressive nature of
facial features and expressions!
