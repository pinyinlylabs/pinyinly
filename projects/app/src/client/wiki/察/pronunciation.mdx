**🗣️ Pronunciation of 察 (chá)**

- **Pinyin:** chá
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair" but with more aspiration
- **á** sounds like **"ah"** but with a rising tone
- **chá** sounds like **"cha"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"chá?"** — that's the tone pattern of **chá**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 察 (chá) - "examine"
- 观察 (guān chá) - "observe"
- 察看 (chá kàn) - "examine"
- 检察 (jiǎn chá) - "inspect"
- 警察 (jǐng chá) - "police"
