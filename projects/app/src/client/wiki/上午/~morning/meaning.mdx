The period of time between midnight and noon.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                      |
| -------------- | ------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | shàng wǔ                  |
| Core meaning   | morning; forenoon; A.M.   |
| Part of speech | noun (time expression)    |
| Tone           | fourth + third tone       |

## Visual Breakdown

上午 combines **up/above** (上) with **noon** (午) to mean "before noon."

| Component | Visual Description                                          |
| --------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **上**    | "Up/above" - shows upward movement or higher position      |
| **午**    | "Noon" - represents the midday sun at its highest point    |

## Mnemonic

Think of 上午 as **"before the sun reaches up to noon"**:

- 上 suggests the sun is still climbing upward
- 午 represents the peak noon position
- Together: the time when the sun is ascending toward noon
- Like the "first half" of the sun's daily journey

Imagine watching the sun climb higher and higher until it reaches its noon peak - that climbing period is 上午.

## Usage Examples

- **上午九点** (shàng wǔ jiǔ diǎn) - "9 A.M."
- **上午好** (shàng wǔ hǎo) - "good morning"
- **上午的会议** (shàng wǔ de huìyì) - "morning meeting"
- **每天上午** (měi tiān shàng wǔ) - "every morning"

## Time System

上午 is part of the Chinese time division system:

- **上午**: Morning (6:00 AM - 12:00 PM)
- **下午**: Afternoon (12:00 PM - 6:00 PM)  
- **晚上**: Evening (6:00 PM - 12:00 AM)
- **夜里**: Night (12:00 AM - 6:00 AM)

## Cultural Context

上午 represents the Chinese approach to time:
- **Clear divisions**: Each part of day has distinct character
- **Solar reference**: Time tied to sun's position
- **Productive period**: Morning traditionally viewed as most productive time
- **Formal meetings**: Important business often scheduled 上午
