**🗣️ Pronunciation of 也 (yě)**

- **Pinyin:** yě
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ě** sounds like **"eh"** in "meh" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yě** sounds like **"yeh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're adding something thoughtfully — **"yě..."** — like **"also..."** or **"too..."**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 也 (yě) - "also, too"
- 我也是 (wǒ yě shì) - "I am too"
- 也许 (yě xǔ) - "perhaps, maybe"
- 也好 (yě hǎo) - "that's fine too"

**📍 Usage Note:**

也 is extremely common and usually comes **before the verb**:

- 我**也**去 (wǒ yě qù) - "I **also** go"
- 他**也**有 (tā yě yǒu) - "He **also** has"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**"Also..."** — the thoughtful third tone is perfect for adding something to a conversation!
