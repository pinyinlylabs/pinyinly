**🗣️ Pronunciation of 武 (wǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** wǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **wǔ** sounds like **"woo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're contemplating martial arts: **"wǔ..."** — that thoughtful dip-then-rise pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 武术 (wǔ shù) - "martial arts"
- 武器 (wǔ qì) - "weapon"
- 武力 (wǔ lì) - "military force"
- 文武 (wén wǔ) - "civil and military"
- 武装 (wǔ zhuāng) - "armed; military equipment"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

武 means "military/martial" — say it with that **contemplative dip-and-rise** like considering
martial strategy!
