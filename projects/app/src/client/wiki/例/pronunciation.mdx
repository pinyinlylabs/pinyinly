**🗣️ Pronunciation of 例 (lì)**

- **Pinyin:** lì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "like"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **lì** sounds like **"lee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"lì!"** — that's the tone pattern of **lì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 例 (lì) - "example; instance"
- 例如 (lì rú) - "for example; such as"
- 例子 (lì zi) - "example; case"
- 举例 (jǔ lì) - "to give an example"
- 病例 (bìng lì) - "medical case"
