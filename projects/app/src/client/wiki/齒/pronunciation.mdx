**🗣️ Pronunciation of 齒 (chǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** chǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church" (aspirated)
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **chǐ** sounds like **"chee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Start low, dip even lower, then rise up: **"chǐ..."** — imagine the sound you make when you're
thinking about something.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 齒 (chǐ) - "tooth"
- 牙齒 (yá chǐ) - "teeth"
- 齒輪 (chǐ lún) - "gear; cogwheel"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the sound when you **bite down** with your teeth - that thoughtful **dip-then-rise** of
**"chǐ"**!
