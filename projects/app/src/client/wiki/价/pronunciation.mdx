**🗣️ Pronunciation of 价 (jià)**

- **Pinyin:** jià
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **ià** sounds like **"yah"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **jià** sounds like **"jyah!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're stating a firm price: **"jià!"** — that's the decisive, falling tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 价格 (jià gé) - "price"
- 价钱 (jià qián) - "price; cost"
- 价值 (jià zhí) - "value; worth"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

价 means "price" — like firmly stating "that's the **price!**" with that decisive fourth tone!
