**🗣️ Pronunciation of 困 (kùn)**

- **Pinyin:** kùn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **ùn** sounds like **"oon"** in "moon", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **kùn** sounds like **"koon!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're stating you're tired decisively: **"kùn!"** — that's the falling tone of **kùn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 困 (kùn) - "sleepy; tired"
- 困难 (kùn nán) - "difficult"
- 困扰 (kùn rǎo) - "troubled; bothered"
- 贫困 (pín kùn) - "poor; impoverished"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of being so **困** (sleepy) that your energy just drops — that's the **fourth tone** falling
motion!
