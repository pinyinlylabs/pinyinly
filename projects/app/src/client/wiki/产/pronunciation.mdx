**🗣️ Pronunciation of 产 (chǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** chǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"**, but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **chǎn** sounds like **"chahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 产 (chǎn) - "give birth", "produce"
- 生产 (shēng chǎn) - "produce", "manufacture"
- 产品 (chǎn pǐn) - "product"
- 产生 (chǎn shēng) - "arise", "come into being"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 产 as the thoughtful **"chahn"** sound (third tone) - like pondering the process of
creating or producing something!
