**🗣️ Pronunciation of 留 (liú)**

- **Pinyin:** liú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"What?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **iú** sounds like **"yo"** in "yo-yo", but with second tone → rising upward
- **liú** sounds like **"lyo?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 留 (liú) - "to stay; to remain; to keep"
- 留下 (liú xià) - "to leave behind; to stay"
- 留学 (liú xué) - "to study abroad"
- 留学生 (liú xué shēng) - "international student"
- 保留 (bǎo liú) - "to retain; to keep"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Second tone rises like asking **"Stay?"** - your voice goes **up** throughout **liú**.
