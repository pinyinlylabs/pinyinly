**🗣️ Pronunciation of 晚 (wǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** wǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"an"** in "can" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **wǎn** sounds like **"wan"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully acknowledging the evening: **"wǎn..."** — that's the contemplative
tone pattern of **wǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 晚 (wǎn) - "evening; late"
- 晚上 (wǎn shang) - "evening"
- 晚餐 (wǎn cān) - "dinner"
- 晚安 (wǎn ān) - "good night"
