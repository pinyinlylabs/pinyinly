**🗣️ Pronunciation of 防 (fáng)**

- **Pinyin:** fáng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fun"
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with second tone → rising pitch
- **fáng** sounds like **"fahng?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Really?" in surprise: **"fáng?"** — that rising, questioning tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 防 (fáng) - "prevent"
- 防止 (fáng zhǐ) - "prevent, stop"
- 国防 (guó fáng) - "national defense"
- 预防 (yù fáng) - "prevention"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of raising an alert or concern when preventing something - that rising second tone matches the
alertness of **防** meaning "prevent"!
