**🗣️ Pronunciation of 超 (chāo)**

- **Pinyin:** chāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Chao"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church" (with tongue curled back, aspirated)
- **āo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but held steady and high
- **chāo** sounds like **"chow"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (First tone: ˉ)**

The **first tone** is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like you're announcing something super: **"chāo—"** — that's the tone pattern of **chāo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 超市 (chāo shì) - "supermarket"
- 超过 (chāo guò) - "exceed; surpass"
- 超级 (chāo jí) - "super; ultra"
- 超越 (chāo yuè) - "transcend; surpass"
