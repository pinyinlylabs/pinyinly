**🗣️ Pronunciation of 掉 (diào)**

- **Pinyin:** diào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "day"
- **iào** sounds like **"yow"** in "yowl", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **diào** sounds like **"dee-yow"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like something just dropped: **"diào!"** — that's the sharp falling tone of **diào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 掉 (diào) - "drop; fall"
- 掉了 (diào le) - "dropped; fell"
- 掉下来 (diào xià lái) - "fall down"
- 丢掉 (diū diào) - "throw away; discard"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"掉"** as **"dee-yow"** with a sharp drop — just like the sound of something falling!
