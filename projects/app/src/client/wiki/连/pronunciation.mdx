**🗣️ Pronunciation of 连 (lián)**

- **Pinyin:** lián
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "light"
- **ián** sounds like **"yen"** but with a rising tone
- **lián** sounds like **"lee-yen"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up, like saying **"What?"** when surprised: **"lián?"** — that's the tone pattern
of **lián**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 连 (lián) - "link; connect"
- 连接 (lián jiē) - "to connect"
- 连续 (lián xù) - "continuous"
- 连忙 (lián máng) - "hastily"
- 连环 (lián huán) - "chain; series"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 连 as **linking** things together — the rising tone connects the beginning to a higher end,
just like linking!
