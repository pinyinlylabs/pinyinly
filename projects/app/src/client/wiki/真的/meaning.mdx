Emphasizes the truthfulness of a statement; really; truly; indeed.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                  |
| -------------- | --------------------- |
| Pinyin         | zhēnde                |
| Core meaning   | really; truly; indeed |
| Part of speech | adverb, exclamation   |
| Tone           | first + neutral tone  |

## Core Meaning & Usage

真的 emphasizes **truthfulness, authenticity, or surprise**. It's used for:

1. **Emphasizing truth**: 真的很好 (zhēnde hěn hǎo) - "really very good"
2. **Expressing surprise**: 真的吗？(zhēnde ma?) - "really? is that true?"
3. **Confirming facts**: 真的是这样 (zhēnde shì zhèyàng) - "it really is like this"
4. **Intensifying emotion**: 真的谢谢 (zhēnde xièxie) - "really thank you"

## Examples

- **真的不知道** (zhēnde bù zhīdào) - "really don't know"
- **真的很棒** (zhēnde hěn bàng) - "really awesome"
- **真的假的** (zhēnde jiǎde) - "true or false? really?"
- **真的没事** (zhēnde méishì) - "really no problem"
- **是真的** (shì zhēnde) - "it's true; it's real"

## Emotional and Social Functions

真的 serves important conversational roles:

- **Shows sincerity** - demonstrates you're being honest
- **Expresses surprise** - reacts to unexpected information
- **Provides reassurance** - confirms something is genuinely true
- **Intensifies emotion** - makes feelings stronger and more authentic

## Grammar Patterns

- **真的 + Adjective**: "really [description]"
- **真的 + Verb**: "really [action]"
- **真的吗？**: "really? is that true?"
- **真的是**: "it really is"

真的 is essential for expressing genuine emotion and confirming authenticity in Chinese
conversation.
