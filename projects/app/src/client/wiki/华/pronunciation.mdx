**🗣️ Pronunciation of 华 (huá)**

- **Pinyin:** huá
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uá** sounds like **"wah"** but with the **second tone** → rising up
- **huá** sounds like **"hwah?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're admiringly asking: **"huá?"** — that upward, impressed inflection.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 华 (huá) - "magnificent; splendid"
- 华人 (huá rén) - "Chinese person"
- 中华 (zhōng huá) - "China"
- 华丽 (huá lì) - "gorgeous; magnificent"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

华 means "magnificent" — the rising second tone sounds like you're in awe, with that upward "wow!"
inflection!
