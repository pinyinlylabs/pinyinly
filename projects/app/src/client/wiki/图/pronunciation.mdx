**🗣️ Pronunciation of 图 (tú)**

- **Pinyin:** tú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "too", but with second tone → rising pitch
- **tú** sounds like **"too?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) starts **low** and rises **up**:

Say it like you're asking about a picture: **"tú?"** — that's the rising tone of **tú**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 图 (tú) - "picture; image; diagram"
- 图片 (tú piàn) - "picture; image"
- 图书馆 (tú shū guǎn) - "library"
- 地图 (dì tú) - "map"
- 意图 (yì tú) - "intention; plan"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **图** (picture) like asking "tú?" — questioning what's in the image!
