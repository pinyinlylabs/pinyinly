**🗣️ Pronunciation of 会**

会 has **two different pronunciations** depending on its meaning:

**📍 huì (fourth tone) - "can, will, meeting"**

- **Pinyin:** huì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Will!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uì** sounds like **"way"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **huì** sounds like **"hway!"** with a sharp drop

**📍 kuài (fourth tone) - "accounting, to account"**

- **Pinyin:** kuài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, same as above

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "kite"
- **uài** sounds like **"why"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **kuài** sounds like **"kwhy!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

Both pronunciations use **fourth tone** (ˋ) - a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving a command — that's the energy of both **huì** and
**kuài**.

**📝 Usage Guide:**

**会 (huì) - "can, will, meeting" (most common):**

- 我**会**说中文 (wǒ huì shuō zhōng wén) - "I can speak Chinese"
- **会**议 (huì yì) - "meeting"
- **会**员 (huì yuán) - "member"
- 他**会**来吗？(tā huì lái ma) - "Will he come?"

**会 (kuài) - "accounting" (less common):**

- **会**计 (kuài jì) - "accountant, accounting"
- 财**会** (cái kuài) - "finance and accounting"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**huì** is the main pronunciation you'll use 99% of the time for "can/will/meeting" **kuài** is
mainly used in the word 会计 (accountant) - think "**k**ounting" money
