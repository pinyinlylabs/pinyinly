**🗣️ Pronunciation of 追 (zhuī)**

- **Pinyin:** zhuī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "jam" but with tongue curled back
- **uī** sounds like **"way"** but with a high flat tone
- **zhuī** sounds like **"jway"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Keep it steady and high, like singing a sustained note: **"zhuīīī"** — that's the tone pattern of
**zhuī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 追 (zhuī) - "to chase; to pursue"
- 追求 (zhuī qiú) - "to pursue; to seek"
- 追赶 (zhuī gǎn) - "to catch up with"
- 追逐 (zhuī zhú) - "to chase after"
- 追问 (zhuī wèn) - "to question closely"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When **chasing** something, you maintain steady focus — just like the steady first tone!
