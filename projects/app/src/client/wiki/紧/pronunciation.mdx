**🗣️ Pronunciation of 紧 (jǐn)**

- **Pinyin:** jǐn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **ǐn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **jǐn** sounds like **"jeen"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being cautious: **"jǐn..."** — that's the tone pattern of **jǐn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 紧 (jǐn) - "tight"
- 紧张 (jǐn zhāng) - "nervous"
- 紧急 (jǐn jí) - "urgent"
- 赶紧 (gǎn jǐn) - "hurry up"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of something being **tightly** squeezed — the third tone's dip and rise mimics the tension and
release of something tight!
