**🗣️ Pronunciation of 医 (yī)**

- **Pinyin:** yī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **yī** sounds like **"yee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're calmly and confidently saying: **"yī"** — that steady, professional tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 医 (yī) - "medicine"
- 医生 (yī shēng) - "doctor"
- 医院 (yī yuàn) - "hospital"
- 中医 (zhōng yī) - "traditional Chinese medicine"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

医 means "medicine" — the steady, calm first tone is like a doctor speaking with professional
confidence!
