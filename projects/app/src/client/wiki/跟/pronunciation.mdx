**🗣️ Pronunciation of 跟 (gēn)**

- **Pinyin:** gēn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ēn** sounds like **"un"** in "gun" but with first tone → steady and high
- **gēn** sounds like **"gun"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — like confidently following along **with** someone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 跟 (gēn) - "with; follow"
- 跟着 (gēn zhe) - "follow; go along with"
- 跟上 (gēn shàng) - "keep up with"
- 跟前 (gēn qián) - "in front of; before"
- 跟随 (gēn suí) - "follow; accompany"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

跟 means "with" — the **steady** first tone suggests the consistency of staying with someone!
