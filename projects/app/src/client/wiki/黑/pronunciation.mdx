**🗣️ Pronunciation of 黑 (hēi)**

- **Pinyin:** hēi
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hay"
- **ēi** sounds like **"ay"** in "hay" but held steady and high
- **hēi** sounds like **"hey"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're calling out in the dark: **"hēi!"** — that steady, high-pitched call.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 黑 (hēi) - "black"
- 黑色 (hēi sè) - "black color"
- 黑板 (hēi bǎn) - "blackboard"
- 黑夜 (hēi yè) - "dark night"
- 黑头发 (hēi tóu fā) - "black hair"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

黑 sounds like "hey" with a steady high tone — imagine calling out **"hēi!"** in the darkness, that
clear, sustained call that cuts through the black night.
