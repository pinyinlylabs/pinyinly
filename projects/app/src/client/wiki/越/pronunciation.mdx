**🗣️ Pronunciation of 越 (yuè)**

- **Pinyin:** yuè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Yway!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **uè** sounds like **"way"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **yuè** sounds like **"yway!"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're emphasizing going beyond: **"yuè!"** — that's the sharp falling tone of **yuè**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 越来越 (yuè lái yuè) - "more and more"
- 超越 (chāo yuè) - "transcend; surpass"
- 跨越 (kuà yuè) - "cross over; span"
- 越过 (yuè guò) - "pass over; cross"
