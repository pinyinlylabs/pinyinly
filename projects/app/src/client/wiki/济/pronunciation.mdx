**🗣️ Pronunciation of 济 (jì)**

- **Pinyin:** jì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but softer, more like "gy")
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **jì** sounds like **"jee!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"jì!"** — that's the tone pattern of **jì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 济 (jì) - "help/aid"
- 经济 (jīng jì) - "economy"
- 救济 (jiù jì) - "relief/aid"
- 济南 (jǐ nán) - "Jinan" (city name)
- 无济于事 (wú jì yú shì) - "of no help"
- 同舟共济 (tóng zhōu gòng jì) - "pull together in times of trouble"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

济 (jì) contains the water radical (氵) on the left. Think of water as life-giving help — just like
how 济 means to help or aid others!
