**🗣️ Pronunciation of 务 (wù)**

- **Pinyin:** wù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Woo!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "wood"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **wù** sounds like **"woo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm instruction about business: **"wù!"** — decisive and authoritative.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 务 (wù) - "business, affairs"
- 服务 (fú wù) - "service"
- 任务 (rèn wù) - "task, mission"
- 公务员 (gōng wù yuán) - "civil servant"
- 业务 (yè wù) - "business operations"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **wù** as "woo!" said firmly — you're taking care of serious business!
