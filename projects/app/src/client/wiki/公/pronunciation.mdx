**🗣️ Pronunciation of 公 (gōng)**

- **Pinyin:** gōng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ōng** sounds like **"ohng"** with a high flat tone
- **gōng** sounds like **"gohng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're singing a steady, high note: **"gōng..."** — that's the tone pattern of **gōng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 公 (gōng) - "public; duke"
- 公司 (gōng sī) - "company"
- 公园 (gōng yuán) - "park"
- 公共 (gōng gòng) - "public; common"
- 公开 (gōng kāi) - "public; open"
- 公里 (gōng lǐ) - "kilometer"
- 办公室 (bàn gōng shì) - "office"

**💡 Special Notes:**

公 consistently uses the first tone (gōng) and relates to concepts of "public," "official," or
"collective." It's commonly used in words related to public services, business, and formal
institutions.
