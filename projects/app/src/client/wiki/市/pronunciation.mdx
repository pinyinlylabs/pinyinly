**🗣️ Pronunciation of 市 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Shh!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she" but with tongue curled back slightly
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** with a sharp falling tone
- **shì** sounds like **"shee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're announcing a place: **"shì!"** — that's the decisive energy of **shì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 市 (shì) - "city; market"
- 城市 (chéng shì) - "city"
- 市场 (shì chǎng) - "market"
- 市长 (shì zhǎng) - "mayor"
- 超市 (chāo shì) - "supermarket"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **市** as the bustling center of commerce — that sharp **fourth tone** shows the
definitive, authoritative nature of a city!

**📍 Cultural Note:**

市 can mean both "city" as an administrative unit and "market" as a place of commerce, reflecting
the historical connection between cities and trade.
