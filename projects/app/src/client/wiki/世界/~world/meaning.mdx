The earth with all its inhabitants and all things upon it; the world; the universe.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                   |
| -------------- | ---------------------- |
| Pinyin         | shìjiè                 |
| Core meaning   | world; earth; universe |
| Part of speech | noun                   |
| Tone           | fourth + fourth tone   |

## Visual Breakdown

世界 combines temporal and spatial concepts:

| Component | Visual Description                                                 |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **世**    | Generation/era - represents time, generations, and temporal scope  |
| **界**    | Boundary/realm - represents spatial limits and defined territories |

## Mnemonic

Think of 世界 as **all generations across all boundaries**:

- 世 (generation/era) + 界 (boundary/realm) = "all generations in all realms"
- Like all the time periods (世) within all the boundaries (界) of existence
- The combination of all eras and all places together
- Everything that exists across time and space

This creates the comprehensive meaning: **the entire world encompassing all time and space**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

世界 represents **the entire world, global scope, or universal realm**. It's used for:

1. **Global context**: 世界和平 (shìjiè hépíng) - "world peace"
2. **International affairs**: 世界大战 (shìjiè dàzhàn) - "world war"
3. **Universal scope**: 全世界 (quán shìjiè) - "the whole world"
4. **Fields of knowledge**: 科学世界 (kēxué shìjiè) - "world of science"

## Examples

- **世界杯** (shìjiè bēi) - "World Cup"
- **世界地图** (shìjiè dìtú) - "world map"
- **世界语** (shìjiè yǔ) - "Esperanto" (world language)
- **世界各地** (shìjiè gèdì) - "all over the world"
- **新世界** (xīn shìjiè) - "new world"

## Cultural and Philosophical Context

世界 carries deep meaning in Chinese thought:

- **Buddhist influence**: Represents the realm of existence and suffering
- **Confucian scope**: The domain of human civilization and moral order
- **Modern usage**: Global interconnectedness and international perspective
- **Universal concepts**: The broadest possible scope of experience

## Common Expressions

- **世界观** (shìjiè guān) - "worldview; outlook on life"
- **世界性** (shìjiè xìng) - "global; worldwide; international"
- **小世界** (xiǎo shìjiè) - "small world" (expressing coincidence)
