**🗣️ Pronunciation of 吹 (chuī)**

- **Pinyin:** chuī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church", but with a stronger aspiration
- **uī** sounds like **"way"** but held steady and high
- **chuī** sounds like **"chwee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady musical note: **"chuī..."** — keep the pitch high and level.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 吹 (chuī) - "blow"
- 吹风 (chuī fēng) - "blow-dry; wind blows"
- 吹牛 (chuī niú) - "brag; boast"
- 吹气 (chuī qì) - "blow air"
- 吹口哨 (chuī kǒu shào) - "whistle"
