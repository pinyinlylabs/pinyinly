**🗣️ Pronunciation of 院 (yuàn)**

- **Pinyin:** yuàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **yu** like **"you"** but quicker
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **yuàn** sounds like **"you-ahn"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're announcing arrival at an important place: **"yuàn!"** — that authoritative,
falling tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 院 (yuàn) - "courtyard, institution"
- 院子 (yuàn zi) - "courtyard"
- 医院 (yī yuàn) - "hospital"
- 学院 (xué yuàn) - "college, academy"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of entering through the gates of a grand courtyard or institution - that formal fourth tone
matches the dignity of **院** meaning "courtyard/institution"!
