**🗣️ Pronunciation of 气 (qì)**

- **Pinyin:** qì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but with more air flow)
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with the **fourth tone** → sharp fall down
- **qì** sounds like **"chee!"** with a sharp dropping pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (Fourth tone: ˋ)**

The **fourth tone** is **sharp and falling**, like making a firm statement:

Say it like you're taking a deep breath or expressing energy: **"qì!"** — that sharp, falling tone
is the **fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 气 (qì) - "air; gas; breath; energy"
- 空气 (kōng qì) - "air"
- 天气 (tiān qì) - "weather"
- 生气 (shēng qì) - "angry; to get angry"
- 气候 (qì hòu) - "climate"
- 氧气 (yǎng qì) - "oxygen"
- 勇气 (yǒng qì) - "courage"

**💡 Special Notes:**

气 is a versatile character representing air, breath, energy, and even emotions. It's essential in
weather-related vocabulary and appears in many compound words related to atmosphere, mood, and
vitality.
