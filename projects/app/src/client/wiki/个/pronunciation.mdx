**🗣️ Pronunciation of 个 (gè)**

- **Pinyin:** gè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Go!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** in "meh" but with a sharp falling tone
- **gè** sounds like **"geh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it decisively and firmly: **"gè!"** — like you're making a definitive statement.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 个 (gè) - measure word for general use
- 一个 (yí gè) - "one" (thing/item)
- 个人 (gè rén) - "individual person"
- 个子 (gè zi) - "height/stature"
- 这个 (zhè gè) - "this one"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**个** is the most common measure word in Chinese — think of it as pointing to **"that one!"** with
a firm, falling tone!
