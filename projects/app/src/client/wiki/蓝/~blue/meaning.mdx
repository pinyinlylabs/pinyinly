Of the color intermediate between green and violet, as of the sky or sea on a sunny day; blue; azure.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                        |
| -------------- | --------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | lán                         |
| Core meaning   | blue; azure; sapphire      |
| Part of speech | adjective, noun             |
| Tone           | second tone (rising)        |

## Visual Breakdown

蓝 represents **blue dye made from plants** through pictographic elements.

| Component | Visual Description                                        |
| --------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| **艹**    | Grass/plant radical (艹) - representing natural sources  |
| **监**    | Observe/supervise (监) - showing careful dye preparation |

The character suggests blue color created from plant-based dyes through careful observation and processing.

## Mnemonic

Think of 蓝 as **"carefully watching plants to extract beautiful blue dye"**:

- The grass radical (艹) represents indigo plants and other blue dye sources
- The observation component (监) shows the careful attention needed for dye-making
- Like ancient craftspeople watching plants to create perfect blue color
- Shows that beautiful blue requires careful observation of natural processes
- Combines natural materials with skilled human attention

This creates the image: **attentive observation creating brilliant blue from plants**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

蓝 represents **the color blue and associated concepts**. It's used:

1. **Basic color**: 蓝色 (lán sè) - "blue color"
2. **Natural blue**: 蓝天 (lán tiān) - "blue sky"
3. **Objects**: 蓝莓 (lán méi) - "blueberry"
4. **Materials**: 蓝宝石 (lán bǎo shí) - "sapphire" (blue gemstone)

## Examples

- **蓝天白云** (lán tiān bái yún) - "blue sky and white clouds"
- **深蓝** (shēn lán) - "dark blue; navy blue"
- **浅蓝** (qiǎn lán) - "light blue"
- **蓝牙** (lán yá) - "Bluetooth" (literally "blue tooth")
- **蓝领** (lán lǐng) - "blue collar worker"

## Shades and Variations

- **天蓝** (tiān lán) - "sky blue"
- **海蓝** (hǎi lán) - "sea blue; ocean blue"
- **湛蓝** (zhàn lán) - "azure; deep blue"
- **蔚蓝** (wèi lán) - "azure; deep blue"

## Cultural Significance

蓝 in Chinese culture represents:

**Positive Associations:**
- **宁静** (níng jìng) - Tranquility and peace
- **智慧** (zhì huì) - Wisdom and knowledge
- **无限** (wú xiàn) - Infinity and vastness
- **高贵** (gāo guì) - Nobility and elegance

**Natural Connections:**
- **天空** (tiān kōng) - Sky and heavens
- **海洋** (hǎi yáng) - Ocean and water
- **清晨** (qīng chén) - Clear morning sky
- **宁静** (níng jìng) - Peaceful environments

## Common Expressions

## Grammar Functions

- **Adjective**: 蓝裙子 (lán qún zi) - "blue skirt"
- **Noun**: 这个蓝很漂亮 (zhè ge lán hěn piào liang) - "this blue is beautiful"
- **Modifier**: 蓝色的海洋 (lán sè de hǎi yáng) - "blue ocean"

## Cultural Context

蓝 reflects important Chinese concepts:

- **天人合一** (tiān rén hé yī) - Harmony between heaven and humanity
- **水文化** (shuǐ wén huà) - Water culture and philosophy
- **宁静致远** (níng jìng zhì yuǎn) - Tranquility leads to distant goals
- **高远理想** (gāo yuǎn lǐ xiǎng) - Lofty ideals and aspirations

## Learning Notes

蓝 is essential because:

- Fundamental color term for describing nature and objects
- Important in environmental and technological vocabulary
- Key to understanding Chinese aesthetic and cultural concepts
- Demonstrates the connection between natural materials and human craft
- Represents peace, wisdom, and infinite possibilities

蓝 shows how Chinese characters capture both the physical beauty of color and its deeper symbolic meanings in culture and emotion!
