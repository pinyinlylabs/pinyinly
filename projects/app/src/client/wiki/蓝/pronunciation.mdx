**🗣️ Pronunciation of 蓝 (lán)**

- **Pinyin:** lán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "lake"
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"**, but with second tone → rising pitch
- **lán** sounds like **"lahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about a color: **"lán?"** — that's the tone pattern of **lán**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 蓝 (lán) - "blue"
- 蓝色 (lán sè) - "blue color"
- 天蓝色 (tiān lán sè) - "sky blue"
- 蓝天 (lán tiān) - "blue sky"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **lán** as pointing to the sky and asking: "Is that blue?" — the rising tone sounds like
you're admiring the beautiful blue color.
