**🗣️ Pronunciation of 碗 (wǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** wǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"** in "want" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **wǎn** sounds like **"wahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"wǎn..."** — that's the tone pattern of **wǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 碗 (wǎn) - "bowl"
- 饭碗 (fàn wǎn) - "rice bowl"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you're thoughtfully looking at a **bowl** wondering what to put in it, that contemplative tone
pattern matches perfectly with the third tone of **wǎn**!
