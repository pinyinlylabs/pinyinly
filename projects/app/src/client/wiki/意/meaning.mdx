## Core Meaning & Usage

意 represents **thoughts, intentions, meanings, and mental concepts**. It's used:

1. **Meaning**: 意思 (yìsi) - "meaning; significance"
2. **Intention**: 故意 (gùyì) - "deliberately; on purpose"
3. **Opinion**: 意见 (yìjiàn) - "opinion; suggestion"
4. **Attention**: 注意 (zhùyì) - "pay attention; notice"

## Examples

- **意思** (yìsi) - "meaning; significance; interesting"
- **意见** (yìjiàn) - "opinion; suggestion; objection"
- **注意** (zhùyì) - "pay attention; be careful"
- **故意** (gùyì) - "deliberately; on purpose"
- **满意** (mǎnyì) - "satisfied; pleased"

## Cultural Context

意 is central to Chinese philosophy and communication. In Confucian thought, understanding
someone's 意 (true intention) is crucial for proper relationships. The concept of 意境 (artistic
conception) in Chinese arts emphasizes the expression of deep meaning beyond surface
appearances. 意 represents the bridge between inner thought and outer expression, fundamental to
Chinese concepts of understanding and communication.
