**🗣️ Pronunciation of 意 (yì)**

- **Pinyin:** yì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like making a decisive point

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **yì** sounds like **"yee!"** with a decisive falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a clear, decisive statement: **"yì!"** — that sharp drop down expresses
the clarity of thought or intention.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 意 (yì) - "thought; idea; intention; meaning"
- 意思 (yì si) - "meaning; significance"
- 意见 (yì jiàn) - "opinion; suggestion"
- 注意 (zhù yì) - "to pay attention; to notice"
- 主意 (zhǔ yì) - "idea; plan"
- 满意 (mǎn yì) - "satisfied; pleased"

**🎭 Usage Note:**

意 (yì) is fundamental in expressing mental concepts like thoughts, ideas, and intentions. It
appears in many compound words related to consciousness and decision-making.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the moment when you get a clear idea — **"yì!"** — that sharp, definitive tone captures the
instant when a thought crystallizes!
