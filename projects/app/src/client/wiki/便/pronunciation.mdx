**🗣️ Pronunciation of 便 (biàn)**

- **Pinyin:** biàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "ball"
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** in "yen", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **biàn** sounds like **"byen!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"biàn!"** — that's the tone pattern of **biàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 便 (biàn) - "convenient; then"
- 方便 (fāng biàn) - "convenient"
- 便宜 (pián yi) - "cheap; inexpensive"
- 便是 (biàn shì) - "that is; namely"
- 方便面 (fāng biàn miàn) - "instant noodles"

**📝 Special Note:**

便 has two main pronunciations:

- **便 (biàn)** - "convenient; then" (fourth tone)
- **便 (pián)** - used in 便宜 (pián yi) "cheap" (second tone)
