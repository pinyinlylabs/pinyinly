**🗣️ Pronunciation of 床 (chuáng)**

- **Pinyin:** chuáng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church" but with stronger aspiration
- **uáng** sounds like **"wahng"** with second tone → rising pitch
- **chuáng** sounds like **"chwahng?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start mid-low and rise up, like when you ask "Really?" in surprise. **chuáng** should have that
upward questioning intonation.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 床 (chuáng) - "bed"
- 床单 (chuáng dān) - "bed sheet"
- 床垫 (chuáng diàn) - "mattress"
- 上床 (shàng chuáng) - "go to bed"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

床 means "bed" — imagine asking "Bed?" with that **rising intonation** when you're tired and want to
sleep!
