**🗣️ Pronunciation of 菜 (cài)**

- **Pinyin:** cài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats" (unaspirated)
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"**, but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **cài** sounds like **"tsai!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're announcing decisively: **"cài!"** — that's the tone pattern of **cài**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 菜 (cài) - "dish/food/vegetables"
- 菜单 (cài dān) - "menu"
- 白菜 (bái cài) - "Chinese cabbage"
- 做菜 (zuò cài) - "cook food"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **cài** as confidently ordering food: "I'll have this dish!" — the falling tone sounds
decisive and clear.
