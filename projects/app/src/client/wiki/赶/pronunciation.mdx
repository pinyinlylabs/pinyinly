**🗣️ Pronunciation of 赶 (gǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** gǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"** in "John" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **gǎn** sounds like **"gahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Third tone: ˇ)**

The **third tone** is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're urging someone to hurry: **"gǎn..."** — that's the contemplative tone pattern of
**gǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 赶快 (gǎn kuài) - "hurry up; quickly"
- 赶紧 (gǎn jǐn) - "hurry; make haste"
- 赶到 (gǎn dào) - "rush to; arrive in time"
- 赶上 (gǎn shàng) - "catch up with"
