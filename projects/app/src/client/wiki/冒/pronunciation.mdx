**🗣️ Pronunciation of 冒 (mào)**

- **Pinyin:** mào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "more"
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with a sharp falling tone
- **mào** sounds like **"mow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is sharp and **falling**:

Say it like you're warning someone about danger — **"mào!"** — urgent and decisive.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 冒 (mào) - "to risk; to emit"
- 冒险 (mào xiǎn) - "to take risks; adventure"
- 感冒 (gǎn mào) - "cold (illness)"
- 冒烟 (mào yān) - "to emit smoke"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you take a risk, you might shout out in alarm — that sharp falling tone captures the urgency of
**"mào!"**
