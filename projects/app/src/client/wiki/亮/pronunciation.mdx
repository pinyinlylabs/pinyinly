**🗣️ Pronunciation of 亮 (liàng)**

- **Pinyin:** liàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "light"
- **iàng** sounds like **"yahng"**, but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **liàng** sounds like **"lyahng!"** with a commanding drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 亮 (liàng) - "bright", "light"
- 明亮 (míng liàng) - "bright", "brilliant"
- 漂亮 (piào liang) - "beautiful", "pretty"
- 亮度 (liàng dù) - "brightness"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

亮 means **bright** - imagine someone exclaiming **"lyahng!"** (fourth tone) when suddenly seeing a
bright light!
