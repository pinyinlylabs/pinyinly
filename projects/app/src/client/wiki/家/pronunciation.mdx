**🗣️ Pronunciation of 家 (jiā)**

- **Pinyin:** jiā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but softer
- **iā** sounds like **"ya"** in "yard" but held steady and high
- **jiā** sounds like **"jya"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like warmly saying "home!": **"jiā"** — that steady, comforting tone represents the warmth of
**family**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 家 (jiā) - "family; home; house"
- 家人 (jiā rén) - "family members"
- 家庭 (jiā tíng) - "family; household"
- 在家 (zài jiā) - "at home"
- 回家 (huí jiā) - "to go home"
- 大家 (dà jiā) - "everyone; all of us"
- 国家 (guó jiā) - "country; nation"
- 专家 (zhuān jiā) - "expert; specialist"
- 作家 (zuò jiā) - "writer; author"

**📍 Cultural Note:**

家 is one of the most important concepts in Chinese culture. Family ties and the home are central to
Chinese values and social structure.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Family** — the steady first tone is like the warm, constant feeling of being with **family** at
**home**!
