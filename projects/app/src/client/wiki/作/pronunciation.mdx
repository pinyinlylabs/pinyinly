**🗣️ Pronunciation of 作 (zuò)**

- **Pinyin:** zuò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"ds"** in "beds" (but lighter)
- **uò** sounds like **"woh"**, but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **zuò** sounds like **"dzwoh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"zuò!"** — that's the tone pattern of **zuò**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 作 (zuò) - "to do; to make; to work"
- 工作 (gōng zuò) - "work; job"
- 作业 (zuò yè) - "homework; assignment"
- 作家 (zuò jiā) - "writer; author"
- 作用 (zuò yòng) - "function; effect; role"
