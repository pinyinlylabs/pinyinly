> **“A flag planted on the ground, pointing upward — rising up, beginning something new.”**

**🧱 Character breakdown:**

**上** has a **horizontal line** (the ground) and a **short stroke above it**, like a flag or marker
going up.

Visually, it **points upward**, matching the meanings:

- **Up** (direction)
- **Above** (position)
- **Start** (as in start work or get on)

**🗯️ How to remember it:**

“Imagine you’re starting a race — you plant a flag in the ground and step up to the starting line.
That’s the beginning — that’s 上.”
