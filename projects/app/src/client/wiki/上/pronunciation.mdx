**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **“sh”** in “shush”
- **ang** like **“ahng”** in “song” (but more nasal and farther back)
- **shàng** sounds like **“shahng!”** with a falling tone

⚠️ Don’t round the “a” like in “shank” — keep it open and farther back in your throat.

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you’re annoyed or shouting **“Hey!”** — that’s the energy of **shàng**.
