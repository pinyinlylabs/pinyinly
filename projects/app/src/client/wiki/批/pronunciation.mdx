**🗣️ Pronunciation of 批 (pī)**

- **Pinyin:** pī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pat" (aspirated, with puff of air)
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **pī** sounds like **"pee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — like the sustained tone when criticizing:
**"pī..."**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 批 (pī) - "criticize; batch; approve"
- 批评 (pī píng) - "criticize; criticism"
- 批准 (pī zhǔn) - "approve; ratify"
- 一批 (yī pī) - "a batch; a group"
- 批发 (pī fā) - "wholesale"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Criticize** — the steady first tone is like maintaining a consistent critical stance!
