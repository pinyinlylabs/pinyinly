**🗣️ Pronunciation of 假 (jiǎ)**

- **Pinyin:** jiǎ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep", but with the tongue closer to the hard palate and no puff of air
- **iǎ** sounds like **"yah"** but with the third tone → dip down and rise up
- **jiǎ** sounds like **"jyah"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"jiǎ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **jiǎ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 假 (jiǎ) - "false"
- 假如 (jiǎ rú) - "if"
- 假的 (jiǎ de) - "fake"
- 真假 (zhēn jiǎ) - "true or false"
