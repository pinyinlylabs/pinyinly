**🗣️ Pronunciation of 真 (zhēn)**

- **Pinyin:** zhēn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **ēn** sounds like **"en"** in "pen" but held steady and high
- **zhēn** sounds like **"jen"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like stating a fact steadily: **"zhēn—"** — that's the confident tone pattern of **zhēn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 真 (zhēn) - "real; true; genuine"
- 真的 (zhēn de) - "really; truly"
- 真正 (zhēn zhèng) - "genuine; authentic"
- 真实 (zhēn shí) - "real; true; authentic"
- 认真 (rèn zhēn) - "serious; earnest"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"jen"** with a steady, confident tone — when something is **real** or **true**, you state
it with certainty!
