**🗣️ Pronunciation of 阳 (yáng)**

- **Pinyin:** yáng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **áng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with second tone → rising pitch
- **yáng** sounds like **"yahng?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're looking up at bright light and asking "Is that the sun?": **"yáng?"** — that
upward, rising tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 阳 (yáng) - "light, sun, yang"
- 阳光 (yáng guāng) - "sunlight, sunshine"
- 太阳 (tài yáng) - "sun"
- 阳台 (yáng tái) - "balcony"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Picture the sun rising in the sky - that upward movement matches the rising second tone of **阳**
meaning "light/sun"!
