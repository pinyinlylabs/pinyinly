**🗣️ Pronunciation of 队 (duì)**

- **Pinyin:** duì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "do"
- **uì** sounds like **"way"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **duì** sounds like **"dway"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving an order: **"duì!"** — that's the confident, falling
tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 队 (duì) - "team"
- 队长 (duì zhǎng) - "team captain"
- 球队 (qiú duì) - "ball team"
- 排队 (pái duì) - "line up, queue"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a team captain shouting orders with authority - that sharp, commanding fourth tone fits
perfectly with **队** meaning "team"!
