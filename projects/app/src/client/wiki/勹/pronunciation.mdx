**🗣️ Pronunciation of 勹 (bāo)**

- **Pinyin:** bāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Baooo"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bow"
- **āo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but held steady and high
- **bāo** sounds like **"bow"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like calling out steadily: **"bāo"** — maintain that high pitch throughout.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 勹 (bāo) - "wrap" (radical)
- 包 (bāo) - "bag, wrap"
- 抱 (bào) - "hug, hold"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **bāo** as "bow" held steady — like wrapping something with a continuous motion!

**📍 Special Note:**

勹 is primarily used as a radical (component) in other characters and is rarely used independently.
It represents the concept of wrapping or enclosing.
