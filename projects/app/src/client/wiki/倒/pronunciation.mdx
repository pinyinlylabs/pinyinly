**🗣️ Pronunciation of 倒**

倒 has **two different pronunciations** depending on its meaning:

**📍 dǎo (third tone) - "to fall down, to collapse"**

- **Pinyin:** dǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **dǎo** sounds like **"dow"** with a dip-then-rise

**📍 dào (fourth tone) - "to pour, upside down"**

- **Pinyin:** dào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **dào** sounds like **"dow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

**dǎo** (third tone) = **fall-then-rise** — like something falling and bouncing back up **dào**
(fourth tone) = **sharp drop** — like pouring water down

**📝 Usage Guide:**

**倒 (dǎo) - "to fall down, to collapse":**

- 倒下 (dǎo xià) - "to fall down"
- 摔倒 (shuāi dǎo) - "to fall; to trip"

**倒 (dào) - "to pour, upside down":**

- 倒水 (dào shuǐ) - "to pour water"
- 倒车 (dào chē) - "to reverse (a car)"
- 颠倒 (diān dào) - "upside down"
