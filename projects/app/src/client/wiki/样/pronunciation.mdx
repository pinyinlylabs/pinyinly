**🗣️ Pronunciation of 样 (yàng)**

- **Pinyin:** yàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **àng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung" but with a sharp falling tone
- **yàng** sounds like **"yahng!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're definitively showing what something looks like: **"yàng!"** — sharp and clear.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 样 (yàng) - "shape/appearance"
- 样子 (yàng zi) - "appearance"
- 一样 (yí yàng) - "same"
- 怎么样 (zěn me yàng) - "how about"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **yàng** as pointing to show "this is what it looks like" — the falling tone emphasizes the
demonstration!
