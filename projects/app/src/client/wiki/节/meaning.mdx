## Grammar Patterns

- **过节**: Celebrate a festival
- **节日**: Festival/holiday (more formal compound)
- **传统节**: Traditional festival
- **节 + 快乐**: Happy + [festival name]

## Cultural Context

节 embodies the deep Chinese connection between seasonal cycles, cultural memory, and community
celebration. Traditional Chinese festivals align with agricultural seasons and lunar calendar,
representing harmony between human society and natural rhythms. Each 节 carries historical,
spiritual, and social significance that reinforces cultural identity and family bonds.
