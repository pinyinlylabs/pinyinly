**🗣️ Pronunciation of 节 (jié)**

- **Pinyin:** jié
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **ié** sounds like **"yeh"** but with second tone → rise up
- **jié** sounds like **"jyeh"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ´)**

The **second tone** is a **rising** tone, like asking a question:

Say it like you're asking about a festival: **"jié?"** — that questioning rise is the **second
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 节 (jié) - "festival, holiday"
- 节日 (jié rì) - "festival, holiday"
- 春节 (chūn jié) - "Spring Festival, Chinese New Year"
- 节目 (jié mù) - "program, show"
- 节约 (jié yuē) - "economical, to save"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **节** as excitedly asking about a festival — that upward, celebratory rise matches the
**second tone**!

**📍 Cultural Note:**

节 is essential in Chinese culture as it refers to traditional festivals like 春节 (Spring Festival)
and 中秋节 (Mid-Autumn Festival), which are central to Chinese celebrations.
