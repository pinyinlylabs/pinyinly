**🗣️ Pronunciation of 众 (zhòng)**

- **Pinyin:** zhòng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" (but with tongue curled back)
- **òng** sounds like **"ong"** in "song", but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **zhòng** sounds like **"jong"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making an announcement to a crowd: **"zhòng!"** — that sharp drop down is the
fourth tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 众 (zhòng) - "crowd; multitude"
- 众人 (zhòng rén) - "everyone; the crowd"
- 群众 (qún zhòng) - "masses; the people"
- 听众 (tīng zhòng) - "audience"
- 观众 (guān zhòng) - "audience; spectators"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"jong"** with a commanding tone — like addressing a crowd with authority!
