**🗣️ Pronunciation of 牙 (yá)**

- **Pinyin:** yá
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **á** sounds like **"ah"** but with a rising tone
- **yá** sounds like **"yah?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about a tooth: **"yá?"** — that questioning rise is the **second tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 牙 (yá) - "tooth"
- 牙齿 (yá chǐ) - "teeth"
- 刷牙 (shuā yá) - "brush teeth"
- 牙医 (yá yī) - "dentist"
- 牙疼 (yá téng) - "toothache"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **牙** like questioning a dental checkup: **"yá?"** — that rising intonation matches the
**second tone**!
