**🗣️ Pronunciation of 方 (fāng)**

- **Pinyin:** fāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Fahng"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fast"
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** in "hang" but with first tone → high and steady
- **fāng** sounds like **"fahng"** with a high, flat pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're describing a perfect square: **"fāng..."** — that steady, confident tone fits the
geometric precision of "square"!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 方 (fāng) - "square, direction, method"
- 方向 (fāng xiàng) - "direction"
- 方法 (fāng fǎ) - "method, way"
- 方面 (fāng miàn) - "aspect, side"
- 地方 (dì fāng) - "place, location"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Square** shapes are precise and steady — just like the steady first tone of **fāng**!

**📍 Usage Note:**

方 has multiple meanings: it can refer to geometric squares, directions (north/south/east/west),
methods, or general locations. Context determines the specific meaning.
