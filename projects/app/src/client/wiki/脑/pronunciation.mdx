**🗣️ Pronunciation of 脑 (nǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** nǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "how", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **nǎo** sounds like **"now"** with a thoughtful dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're pondering something: **"nǎo..."** — that's the contemplative tone pattern of
**nǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 脑 (nǎo) - "brain; mind"
- 头脑 (tóu nǎo) - "mind; brains"
- 脑子 (nǎo zi) - "brain; mind"
- 电脑 (diàn nǎo) - "computer"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

脑 refers to the brain or mind, and the thoughtful third tone with its dip-and-rise pattern mimics
the process of thinking and reflection!
