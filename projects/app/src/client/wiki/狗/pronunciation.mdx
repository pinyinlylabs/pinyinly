**🗣️ Pronunciation of 狗 (gǒu)**

- **Pinyin:** gǒu
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ǒu** sounds like **"oh"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **gǒu** sounds like **"goh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking about a dog: **"gǒu..."** — that thoughtful dip-and-rise is the **third
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 狗 (gǒu) - "dog"
- 小狗 (xiǎo gǒu) - "puppy; small dog"
- 狗狗 (gǒu gou) - "doggy" (cute/informal)
- 热狗 (rè gǒu) - "hot dog"
- 看门狗 (kān mén gǒu) - "watchdog"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **狗** as thoughtfully petting a dog: **"gǒu..."** — that gentle, contemplative tone
matches the **third tone**!
