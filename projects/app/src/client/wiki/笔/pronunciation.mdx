**🗣️ Pronunciation of 笔 (bǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** bǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **bǐ** sounds like **"bee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"bǐ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **bǐ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 笔 (bǐ) - "pen; writing brush"
- 笔记 (bǐ jì) - "notes"
- 笔记本 (bǐ jì běn) - "notebook"
- 铅笔 (qiān bǐ) - "pencil"
- 毛笔 (máo bǐ) - "writing brush"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"bee"** with the thoughtful dip-rise tone — like pondering what to write with your pen!
