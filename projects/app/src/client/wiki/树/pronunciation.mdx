**🗣️ Pronunciation of 树 (shù)**

- **Pinyin:** shù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **shù** sounds like **"shoo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly pointing at a tree: **"shù!"** — sharp and decisive.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 树 (shù) - "tree"
- 树木 (shù mù) - "trees/vegetation"
- 果树 (guǒ shù) - "fruit tree"
- 大树 (dà shù) - "big tree"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **shù** as pointing decisively at a tall tree — the falling tone is like your finger
pointing downward!
