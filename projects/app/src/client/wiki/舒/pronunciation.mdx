**🗣️ Pronunciation of 舒 (shū)**

- **Pinyin:** shū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady musical note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "cool", held steady and high
- **shū** sounds like **"shoo"** with a calm, steady pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and flat**:

Keep it steady and peaceful, like a long **"ahhhh"** when relaxing: **"shūūū"** — smooth and even.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 舒 (shū) - "relax; comfortable"
- 舒服 (shū fu) - "comfortable; feeling good"
- 舒适 (shū shì) - "comfortable; cozy"
- 舒缓 (shū huǎn) - "slow and gentle; soothing"

**💡 Feeling Note:**

舒 (shū) conveys a sense of comfort, relaxation, and ease. It's often used to describe physical or
mental comfort and well-being.
