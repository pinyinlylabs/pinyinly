**🗣️ Pronunciation of 毛 (máo)**

- **Pinyin:** máo
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "meow"
- **áo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with a **rising** tone
- **máo** sounds like **"meow?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is **rising**, like asking a question:

Say it like you're asking about a cat's fur: **"máo?"** — that questioning rise is the **second
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 毛 (máo) - "fur; hair; feather"
- 毛病 (máo bìng) - "problem; defect"
- 羊毛 (yáng máo) - "wool"
- 头发毛 (tóu fā máo) - "hair (on head)"
- 毛衣 (máo yī) - "sweater; woolen garment"
- 毛巾 (máo jīn) - "towel"

**💡 Special Notes:**

毛 refers to fine hair, fur, or feathers on animals and can also mean rough or unfinished. It's
commonly used to describe texture and is found in many everyday items like clothing and household
goods.
