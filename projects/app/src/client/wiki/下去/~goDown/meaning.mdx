To descend or move to a lower place away from the speaker.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                            |
| -------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | xià qù                          |
| Core meaning   | go down; move downward; descend |
| Part of speech | verb (directional)              |
| Tone           | fourth + fourth tone            |

## Visual Breakdown

下去 combines **down** (下) with **go** (去) to create downward movement.

| Component | Visual Description                                     |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **下**    | Down/below - shows downward movement or lower position |
| **去**    | Go - person (人) moving through earth/ground (土)      |

## Mnemonic

Think of 下去 as **"going toward the lower direction"**:

- 下 points downward like a falling arrow
- 去 shows the action of going/moving
- Together: moving in the downward direction
- Like going downstairs, walking downhill, or descending to a lower floor

The combination creates clear directional movement from higher to lower position.

## Usage Examples

- **下去看看** (xià qù kàn kan) - "go down and take a look"
- **走下去** (zǒu xià qù) - "walk down (there)"
- **跑下去** (pǎo xià qù) - "run down"
- **能下去吗？** (néng xià qù ma?) - "can (you) go down?"

## Directional Contrast

下去 directly contrasts with 上去:

- **上去** (shàng qù) - go up (toward higher position)
- **下去** (xià qù) - go down (toward lower position)
- Perfect mirror pattern using directional opposites

## Grammar Patterns

下去 functions as a **directional complement**:

- **[verb] + 下去**: [verb] + downward (走下去, 滑下去)
- **下去 + [verb]**: go down + [verb] (下去找, 下去买)
- **能/可以 + 下去**: can/may go down
- **不能 + 下去**: cannot go down

## Extended Meanings

下去 also means "continue" in certain contexts:

- **说下去** (shuō xià qù) - "continue speaking"
- **做下去** (zuò xià qù) - "continue doing"
- **坚持下去** (jiānchí xià qù) - "persist; keep going"

## Cultural Context

下去 reflects Chinese spatial and temporal thinking:

- **Physical descent**: Essential for navigation in multi-level spaces
- **Continuation**: Metaphor of "going down a path" = continuing
- **Hierarchy movement**: Moving to positions of less authority
- **Natural flow**: Downward movement as natural progression
