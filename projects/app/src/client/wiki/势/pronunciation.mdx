**🗣️ Pronunciation of 势 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Shih!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **ì** sounds like **"ih"** in "bit" but with a sharp falling tone
- **shì** sounds like **"shih!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're demonstrating power or authority: **"shì!"** — firm and commanding.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 势 (shì) - "power, force"
- 势力 (shì lì) - "power, influence"
- 优势 (yōu shì) - "advantage"
- 形势 (xíng shì) - "situation"
- 气势 (qì shì) - "momentum, imposing manner"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **shì** like "sheesh!" said with authority — expressing the power or force of something!
