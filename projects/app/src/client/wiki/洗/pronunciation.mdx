**🗣️ Pronunciation of 洗 (xǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** xǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (but softer, more like "hs")
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **xǐ** sounds like **"shee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"xǐ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **xǐ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 洗 (xǐ) - "wash"
- 洗手 (xǐ shǒu) - "wash hands"
- 洗澡 (xǐ zǎo) - "take a bath/shower"
- 洗衣服 (xǐ yī fu) - "wash clothes"
- 洗碗 (xǐ wǎn) - "wash dishes"
- 洗手间 (xǐ shǒu jiān) - "bathroom/restroom"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

洗 (xǐ) contains the water radical (氵) on the left, which makes perfect sense since washing
requires water!
