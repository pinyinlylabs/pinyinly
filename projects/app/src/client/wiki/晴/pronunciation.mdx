**🗣️ Pronunciation of 晴 (qíng)**

- **Pinyin:** qíng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question with enthusiasm

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" but with a slight puff of air
- **íng** sounds like **"ing"** in "ring", but with rising tone → goes up like asking a question
- **qíng** sounds like **"ching?"** with an upward rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up high, like asking "Really?" with excitement. Perfect for expressing the joy of
sunny weather!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 晴天 (qíng tiān) - "sunny day; clear day"
- 晴朗 (qíng lǎng) - "sunny and clear"
- 放晴 (fàng qíng) - "clear up (weather)"
- 转晴 (zhuǎn qíng) - "turn sunny"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

晴 means "sunny" - when the weather clears up and becomes sunny, you naturally say **"qíng!"** with
that bright, rising tone of happiness!
