**🗣️ Pronunciation of 韦 (wéi)**

- **Pinyin:** wéi
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **éi** sounds like **"way"** but with rising tone
- **wéi** sounds like **"way?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone, like when you ask "Which way?" Say **"wéi"** with a
curious, upward inflection.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 韦 (wéi) - "tanned leather"
- 韦编三绝 (wéi biān sān jué) - classical expression about studying diligently
- Used mainly in traditional contexts and compounds

**💡 Memory Tip:**

韦 is an ancient character related to leather working — imagine asking "What **way** do we tan this
leather?" with a rising tone!
