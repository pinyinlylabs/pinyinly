**🗣️ Pronunciation of 验 (yàn)**

- **Pinyin:** yàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **yàn** sounds like **"yahn!"** with a sharp falling pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone: Say it like you're giving a firm command:
**"yàn!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 验 (yàn) - "test; examine"
- 验证 (yàn zhèng) - "verify; validate"
- 检验 (jiǎn yàn) - "inspect; test"
- 体验 (tǐ yàn) - "experience; try out"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you **test** something, you make a firm, decisive judgment — that's the sharp, falling tone
of 验!
