**🗣️ Pronunciation of 突 (tū)**

- **Pinyin:** tū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "too", but with first tone → steady and high
- **tū** sounds like **"too"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and steady** tone:

Keep your voice high and flat throughout: **"tū"** — like holding a steady musical note.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 突 (tū) - "突出" only used in compounds
- 突出 (tū chū) - "stand out; prominent"
- 突然 (tū rán) - "suddenly"
- 突破 (tū pò) - "breakthrough"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"too"** sudden — the steady first tone represents something that stands out consistently!
