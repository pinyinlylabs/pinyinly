**🗣️ Pronunciation of 石 (shí)**

- **Pinyin:** shí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: "eh?"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with second tone → rising up
- **shí** sounds like **"shee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"shí?"** — that's the tone pattern of **shí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 石 (shí) - "stone"
- 石头 (shí tou) - "stone; rock"
- 石油 (shí yóu) - "petroleum; oil"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a **stone** that's so solid and reliable, you'd ask **"Really?"** (with that rising tone)
when someone says they can break it!
