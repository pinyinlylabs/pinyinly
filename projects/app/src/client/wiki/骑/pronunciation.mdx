**🗣️ Pronunciation of 骑 (qí)**

- **Pinyin:** qí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheap" (but more crisp)
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with rising tone
- **qí** sounds like **"chee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone: Say it like you're asking "Really?" — **"qí?"** —
that's the upward questioning tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 骑 (qí) - "ride"
- 骑车 (qí chē) - "ride a bike"
- 骑马 (qí mǎ) - "ride a horse"
- 骑手 (qí shǒu) - "rider; jockey"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you **ride** up a hill, your voice rises with excitement — that matches the rising tone of 骑!
