**🗣️ Pronunciation of 工 (gōng)**

- **Pinyin:** gōng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Gong"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ōng** sounds like **"ong"** in "song" but with first tone → high and steady
- **gōng** sounds like **"gong"** with a high, flat pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're singing a steady high note: **"gōng..."** — that's the tone pattern of **gōng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 工 (gōng) - "work"
- 工人 (gōng rén) - "worker"
- 工作 (gōng zuò) - "work; job"
- 工厂 (gōng chǎng) - "factory"
- 工程师 (gōng chéng shī) - "engineer"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **工** as looking like a construction beam or tool — that's the steady, strong **first
tone** quality of work!
