**🗣️ Pronunciation of 化 (huà)**

- **Pinyin:** huà
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uà** sounds like **"wah"** but with the **fourth tone** → sharp drop down
- **huà** sounds like **"hwah!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly stating something: **"huà!"** — that confident, decisive tone is the
fourth tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 化 (huà) - "transform"
- 变化 (biàn huà) - "change"
- 文化 (wén huà) - "culture"
- 化学 (huà xué) - "chemistry"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 化 as "transformation" — the sharp falling tone sounds decisive, like making a definitive
change!
