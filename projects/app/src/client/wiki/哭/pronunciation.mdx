**🗣️ Pronunciation of 哭 (kū)**

- **Pinyin:** kū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Kuuu"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key" (but softer, unaspirated)
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "moon", held steady and high
- **kū** sounds like **"koo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and level**:

Say it like the steady wail of crying: **"kū..."** — that sustained high pitch mimics a cry.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 哭 (kū) - "to cry; to weep"
- 哭泣 (kū qì) - "to weep; to sob"
- 大哭 (dà kū) - "to cry loudly; to wail"
- 哭声 (kū shēng) - "crying sound; wail"
- 想哭 (xiǎng kū) - "want to cry"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **mouth (口)** with **tears** — when you **cry**, the sound comes steadily from your mouth with
that long, sustained "kū" wail!
