**🗣️ Pronunciation of 白 (bái)**

- **Pinyin:** bái
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **ái** sounds like **"eye"** but with a **rising tone**
- **bái** sounds like **"buy?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) **rises** like asking a question:

Say it like you're asking "Is it white?": **"bái?"** — that's the rising energy of **bái**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 白 (bái) - "white"
- 白色 (bái sè) - "white color"
- 白天 (bái tiān) - "daytime"
- 白菜 (bái cài) - "Chinese cabbage"
- 明白 (míng bái) - "understand; clear"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

白 means "white" — the rising second tone sounds like someone exclaiming in wonder at something
bright white!
