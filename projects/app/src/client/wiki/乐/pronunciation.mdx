**🗣️ Pronunciation of 乐 (lè)**

- **Pinyin:** lè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like exclaiming: **"Yeah!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **è** sounds like **"eh"** in "met" but with a sharp falling tone
- **lè** sounds like **"leh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is **enthusiastic and definitive**:

Say it like you're excited about being happy — **"Lè!"** — that sharp tone matches the joy!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 乐 (lè) - "happy, joyful"
- 快乐 (kuài lè) - "happy"
- 欢乐 (huān lè) - "joyful"
- 乐观 (lè guān) - "optimistic"

**📍 Note on Multiple Pronunciations:**

乐 also has another pronunciation **yuè** (fourth tone) when it means **"music"**:

- 音乐 (yīn yuè) - "music"
- 乐器 (yuè qì) - "musical instrument"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Happy** = **lè** with a sharp, decisive tone — like declaring your joy with confidence!
