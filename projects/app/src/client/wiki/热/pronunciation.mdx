**🗣️ Pronunciation of 热 (rè)**

- **Pinyin:** rè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **r** like **"r"** in "red" (but softer, almost like "zh" sound)
- **è** sounds like **"uh"** in "duh" but with a sharp falling tone
- **rè** sounds like **"ruh!"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Say it like you're exclaiming "Hot!" when you touch something burning — **"rè!"** — that sharp,
definitive drop is the fourth tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 热 (rè) - "hot"
- 热水 (rè shuǐ) - "hot water"
- 热情 (rè qíng) - "enthusiastic"
- 发热 (fā rè) - "have a fever"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When something is really hot, you react with a sharp, quick exclamation — just like the fourth
tone's sharp falling pattern in 热!
