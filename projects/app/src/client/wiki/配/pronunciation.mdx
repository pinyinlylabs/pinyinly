**🗣️ Pronunciation of 配 (pèi)**

- **Pinyin:** pèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pay"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way" but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **pèi** sounds like **"pay!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying "Match!" decisively: **"pèi!"** — that's the tone pattern
of **pèi**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 配 (pèi) - "to match; to pair; to deserve"
- 配合 (pèi hé) - "to cooperate; to coordinate"
- 配菜 (pèi cài) - "side dish"
- 配音 (pèi yīn) - "dubbing; voice-over"
- 配偶 (pèi ǒu) - "spouse; partner"

**💡 Note:**

配 contains the 酉 (wine) radical, originally related to mixing or blending alcoholic beverages. The
meaning has expanded to general concepts of matching, pairing, and coordination.
