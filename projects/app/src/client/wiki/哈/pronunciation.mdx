**🗣️ Pronunciation of 哈 (hā)**

- **Pinyin:** hā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Haaa"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ā** sounds like **"ah"** in "father", held steady and high
- **hā** sounds like **"hah"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and level**:

Say it like you're laughing heartily: **"hā!"** — maintain that high, steady pitch throughout.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 哈 (hā) - exclamation of laughter
- 哈哈 (hā hā) - "haha" (laughter)
- 哈尔滨 (Hā ěr bīn) - "Harbin" (city name)
- 哈密瓜 (hā mì guā) - "cantaloupe"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **mouth radical (口)** plus **合** suggests the sound that comes from your mouth when you laugh
— **"hā hā!"**
