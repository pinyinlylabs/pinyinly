**🗣️ Pronunciation of 历 (lì)**

- **Pinyin:** lì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "like"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **lì** sounds like **"lee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Say it with conviction, like making a statement: **"lì!"** — that's the sharp falling pattern of
**lì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 历史 (lì shǐ) - "history"
- 日历 (rì lì) - "calendar"
- 经历 (jīng lì) - "experience"
- 履历 (lǚ lì) - "resume"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **历** as marking important moments in **history** — each pronunciation drops down sharply
like marking a definitive point in time!
