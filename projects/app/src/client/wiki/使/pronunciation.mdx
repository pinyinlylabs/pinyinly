**🗣️ Pronunciation of 使 (shǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** shǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ih"** in "ship", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **shǐ** sounds like **"shih"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"shǐ..."** — that's the tone pattern of **shǐ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 使 (shǐ) - "to make; to cause; to use"
- 使用 (shǐ yòng) - "to use; to employ"
- 使得 (shǐ dé) - "to make; to cause"
- 大使 (dà shǐ) - "ambassador"
- 大使馆 (dà shǐ guǎn) - "embassy"
