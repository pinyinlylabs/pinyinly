**🗣️ Pronunciation of 根 (gēn)**

- **Pinyin:** gēn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ēn** sounds like **"un"** in "fun" but held steady and high
- **gēn** sounds like **"gun"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like stating the foundation of something: **"gēn—"** — steady and firm.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 根 (gēn) - "root"
- 根本 (gēn běn) - "fundamental"
- 树根 (shù gēn) - "tree root"
- 根据 (gēn jù) - "according to"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **gēn** as the steady foundation of a tree — the high flat tone is like the solid,
unchanging root!
