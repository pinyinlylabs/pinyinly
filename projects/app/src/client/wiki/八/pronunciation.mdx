**🗣️ Pronunciation of 八 (bā)**

- **Pinyin:** bā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bay"
- **ā** sounds like **"ah"** in "father" but with a high flat tone
- **bā** sounds like **"bah"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're singing a steady, high note: **"bā..."** — that's the tone pattern of **bā**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 八 (bā) - "eight"
- 八月 (bā yuè) - "August"
- 十八 (shí bā) - "eighteen"
- 八十 (bā shí) - "eighty"
- 八点 (bā diǎn) - "eight o'clock"

**💡 Special Notes:**

八 consistently uses the first tone (bā) as the number "eight." In Chinese culture, eight is
considered a lucky number because it sounds similar to 发 (fā), which means "to prosper" or "to
become wealthy."
