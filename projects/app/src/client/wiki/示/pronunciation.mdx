**🗣️ Pronunciation of 示 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: "Look!"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **shì** sounds like **"shee!"** with a sharp falling command

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're pointing something out: **"shì!"** — that's the tone pattern of **shì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 示 (shì) - "show; to indicate"
- 表示 (biǎo shì) - "to express; to show"
- 显示 (xiǎn shì) - "to display; to show"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you **show** something to someone, you often speak with authority and definiteness — that
commanding fourth tone matches **shì** perfectly!
