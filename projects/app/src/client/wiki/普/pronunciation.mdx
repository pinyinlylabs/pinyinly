**🗣️ Pronunciation of 普 (pǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** pǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "put"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **pǔ** sounds like **"poo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Start mid-level, drop down low, then rise back up. Think of it as a thoughtful "hmm..." sound.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 普通 (pǔ tōng) - "ordinary; common"
- 普通话 (pǔ tōng huà) - "Mandarin Chinese"
- 普及 (pǔ jí) - "popularize; spread"
- 普遍 (pǔ biàn) - "universal; widespread"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

普 means "universal" or "common" - imagine something being **universally** available with that
thoughtful third tone: **"pǔ..."**
