**🗣️ Pronunciation of 满 (mǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** mǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "man"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **mǎn** sounds like **"mahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're satisfied after a full meal: **"mǎn..."** — that satisfied, contemplative tone
pattern of **mǎn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 满 (mǎn) - "full"
- 满意 (mǎn yì) - "satisfied"
- 满足 (mǎn zú) - "satisfied; content"
- 充满 (chōng mǎn) - "filled with"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When something is **full**, you say **"man, that's full"** with a satisfied, contemplative tone —
just like the third tone of **mǎn**!
