**🗣️ Pronunciation of 封 (fēng)**

- **Pinyin:** fēng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fun"
- **ēng** sounds like **"ung"** in "sung" but with a steady high tone
- **fēng** sounds like **"fung"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like a steady note: **"fēng—"** — that's the tone pattern of **fēng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 封 (fēng) - "seal"
- 封信 (fēng xìn) - "seal a letter"
- 封闭 (fēng bì) - "close off"
- 密封 (mì fēng) - "seal tightly"
- 解封 (jiě fēng) - "unseal"
