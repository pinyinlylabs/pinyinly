**🗣️ Pronunciation of 付 (fù)**

- **Pinyin:** fù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "father"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **fù** sounds like **"foo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"fù!"** — that's the decisive, falling tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 付钱 (fù qián) - "pay money"
- 付款 (fù kuǎn) - "make payment"
- 支付 (zhī fù) - "payment"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

付 means "pay" — like firmly saying "pay **up!**" with that decisive fourth tone!
