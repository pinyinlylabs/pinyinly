**🗣️ Pronunciation of 何 (hé)**

- **Pinyin:** hé
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **é** sounds like **"eh"** in "bet", but with second tone → rising up
- **hé** sounds like **"heh?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"hé?"** — that's the tone pattern of **hé**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 何 (hé) - "what; which"
- 如何 (rú hé) - "how; in what way"
- 为何 (wèi hé) - "why; for what reason"
- 何时 (hé shí) - "when; what time"
- 何处 (hé chù) - "where; what place"
