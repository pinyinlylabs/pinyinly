**🗣️ Pronunciation of 果 (guǒ)**

- **Pinyin:** guǒ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **uǒ** sounds like **"wo"** in "wo!", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **guǒ** sounds like **"gwo"** with a dip-then-rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 果 (guǒ) - "fruit; result"
- 苹果 (píng guǒ) - "apple"
- 水果 (shuǐ guǒ) - "fruit"
- 结果 (jié guǒ) - "result; outcome"
- 如果 (rú guǒ) - "if"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Fruit** hangs down then bounces up on a branch — that's the dip-then-rise third tone of **guǒ**!
