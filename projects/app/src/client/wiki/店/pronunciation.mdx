**🗣️ Pronunciation of 店 (diàn)**

- **Pinyin:** diàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a firm command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **diàn** sounds like **"dyen!"** with a sharp drop in pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly and decisively, like when you say "Stop!" firmly. **diàn** should sound
crisp and definitive.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 店 (diàn) - "shop; store"
- 商店 (shāng diàn) - "shop; store"
- 饭店 (fàn diàn) - "restaurant; hotel"
- 书店 (shū diàn) - "bookstore"
- 药店 (yào diàn) - "pharmacy"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

店 means "shop" — the **sharp, decisive** fourth tone sounds like a shopkeeper firmly calling out
"Shop!" to attract customers!
