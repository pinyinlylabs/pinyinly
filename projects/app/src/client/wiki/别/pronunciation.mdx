**🗣️ Pronunciation of 别 (bié)**

- **Pinyin:** bié
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **ié** sounds like **"yeh"**, but with second tone → rising
- **bié** sounds like **"byeh?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're telling someone "don't!" with concern: **"bié?"** — that's the tone pattern of
**bié**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 别 (bié) - "don't; do not"
- 别人 (bié rén) - "other people"
- 别的 (bié de) - "other; else"
- 分别 (fēn bié) - "separate; part"
- 特别 (tè bié) - "especially; particularly"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

别 means "don't" — imagine the **rising** tone as the concerned inflection when warning someone not
to do something!
