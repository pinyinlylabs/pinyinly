**🗣️ Pronunciation of 打 (dǎ)**

- **Pinyin:** dǎ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ǎ** sounds like **"ah"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **dǎ** sounds like **"dah"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're taking aim before hitting: **"dǎ..."** — that's the focused tone pattern of
**dǎ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 打 (dǎ) - "hit; strike; play"
- 打电话 (dǎ diàn huà) - "make a phone call"
- 打球 (dǎ qiú) - "play ball"
- 打开 (dǎ kāi) - "open; turn on"
- 打工 (dǎ gōng) - "work part-time"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Hit** — the dipping third tone is like the windup before striking something!
