**🗣️ Pronunciation of 甘 (gān)**

- **Pinyin:** gān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ān** sounds like **"an"** in "can", but with first tone → steady and high
- **gān** sounds like **"gahn"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 甘 (gān) - "sweet"
- 甘蔗 (gān zhè) - "sugar cane"
- 甘甜 (gān tián) - "sweet and pleasant"
- 甘心 (gān xīn) - "willing; resigned"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

First tone is like singing a steady high note - keep your voice **flat and high** throughout
**gān**.
