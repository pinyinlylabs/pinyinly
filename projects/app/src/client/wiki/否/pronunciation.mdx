**🗣️ Pronunciation of 否 (fǒu)**

- **Pinyin:** fǒu
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "father"
- **ǒu** sounds like **"oh"** but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up
- **fǒu** sounds like **"foh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"fǒu..."** — that's the tone pattern of **fǒu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 是否 (shì fǒu) - "whether or not"
- 能否 (néng fǒu) - "whether able to"
- 否则 (fǒu zé) - "otherwise"
- 否定 (fǒu dìng) - "deny, negate"
- 否认 (fǒu rèn) - "deny, disavow"
