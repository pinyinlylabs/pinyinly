**🗣️ Pronunciation of 同 (tóng)**

- **Pinyin:** tóng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **óng** sounds like **"ong"** in "song" but with a rising tone
- **tóng** sounds like **"tong?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) **rises** like asking a question:

Say it like you're asking "Really?" — that's the energy of **tóng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 同学 (tóng xué) - "classmate"
- 同事 (tóng shì) - "colleague"
- 同意 (tóng yì) - "agree"
- 同时 (tóng shí) - "at the same time"
- 不同 (bù tóng) - "different"
