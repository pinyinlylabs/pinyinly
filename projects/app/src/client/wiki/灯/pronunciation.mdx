**🗣️ Pronunciation of 灯 (dēng)**

- **Pinyin:** dēng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ēng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung" but with first tone → high and steady
- **dēng** sounds like **"dung"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're announcing you're turning on a light: **"dēng!"** — keep that high, steady tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 灯 (dēng) - "lamp; light"
- 电灯 (diàn dēng) - "electric light"
- 台灯 (tái dēng) - "desk lamp"
- 路灯 (lù dēng) - "street light"
- 红绿灯 (hóng lǜ dēng) - "traffic light"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you turn on a **lamp**, there's that steady, bright illumination — just like the steady, high
tone of **dēng**!
