**🗣️ Pronunciation of 词 (cí)**

- **Pinyin:** cí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"tsuh?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats" — a sharp burst of air with tongue tip touching teeth
- **í** sounds like **"uh"** in "but" but with a rising tone
- **cí** sounds like **"tsuh?"** with a questioning rise

**🔊 Key Sound Tips:**

The **c** in Chinese is **not** like English "c":

1. **Make a "ts" sound** — like the end of "cats" or "sits"
2. **Tongue tip touches** the back of your upper teeth
3. **Sharp release of air** — like a small explosion of sound
4. **Aspirated** — feel a puff of air on your hand

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start **low and rise up** like you're asking a question: **"cí?"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 词 (cí) - "word; term"
- 词典 (cí diǎn) - "dictionary"
- 词语 (cí yǔ) - "word; term"
- 生词 (shēng cí) - "new word"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think **"tsuh?"** with that rising questioning tone — like asking "what's that word?" when you
encounter a new term!
