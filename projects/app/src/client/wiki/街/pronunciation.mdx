**🗣️ Pronunciation of 街 (jiē)**

- **Pinyin:** jiē
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but more like "gee" with tongue forward)
- **iē** sounds like **"yeah"**, but with first tone → high and steady
- **jiē** sounds like **"gee-yay"** held steady and high

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're clearly naming a street: **"jiē"** — that's the tone pattern of **jiē**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 街 (jiē) - "street"
- 大街 (dà jiē) - "main street"
- 街道 (jiē dào) - "street/road"
- 商业街 (shāng yè jiē) - "commercial street"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **jiē** as pointing down a street and saying: "This street!" — the high flat tone sounds
clear and directional.
