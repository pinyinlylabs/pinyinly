**🗣️ Pronunciation of 遍 (biàn)**

- **Pinyin:** biàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **biàn** sounds like **"bee-yen!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying **"All!"** when emphasizing completeness: **"biàn!"** —
that's the tone pattern of **biàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 遍 (biàn) - "everywhere; all over"
- 到处 (dào chù) + 遍 (biàn) - "everywhere"
- 普遍 (pǔ biàn) - "universal; widespread"
- 走遍 (zǒu biàn) - "to travel all over"
- 看遍 (kàn biàn) - "to see everything"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Everywhere** means complete coverage — the decisive falling tone emphasizes that total,
comprehensive reach!
