**🗣️ Pronunciation of 对 (duì)**

- **Pinyin:** duì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **uì** sounds like **"way"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **duì** sounds like **"dway!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"duì!"** — that's the tone pattern of **duì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 对 (duì) - "correct"
- 对不起 (duì bu qǐ) - "sorry"
- 对了 (duì le) - "that's right"
- 面对 (miàn duì) - "face"
- 对手 (duì shǒu) - "opponent"
