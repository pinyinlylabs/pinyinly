**🗣️ Pronunciation of 调 (tiáo)**

- **Pinyin:** tiáo
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea"
- **iáo** sounds like **"ee-ow"** blended together with a rising tone
- **tiáo** sounds like **"tee-ow?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is **rising**, like when you're adjusting something and checking:

Say it like you're asking **"Is this the right adjustment?"**: **"tiáo?"** — that upward tone is the
**second tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 调 (tiáo) - "adjust; tune; regulate"
- 调节 (tiáo jié) - "adjust; regulate"
- 调整 (tiáo zhěng) - "adjust; readjust"
- 调查 (tiáo chá) - "investigate; survey"

**💡 Multiple Pronunciations:**

调 has different pronunciations for different meanings:

- **tiáo** (2nd tone) - "adjust; tune; investigate"
- **diào** (4th tone) - "tone; melody; key (in music)"

The pronunciation **tiáo** (second tone) is used when 调 means "to adjust" or "to investigate".
