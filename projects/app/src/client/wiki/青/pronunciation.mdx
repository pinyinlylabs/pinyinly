**🗣️ Pronunciation of 青 (qīng)**

- **Pinyin:** qīng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but with a puff of air)
- **īng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing", held steady and high
- **qīng** sounds like **"ching"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 青 (qīng) - "green or blue"
- 青年 (qīng nián) - "young person; youth"
- 青菜 (qīng cài) - "green vegetables"
- 青少年 (qīng shào nián) - "teenager; adolescent"

**💡 Note:**

青 can mean both "green" and "blue" depending on context. In traditional Chinese culture, the
distinction between green and blue was less rigid than in modern usage.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **high flat tone** represents the fresh, vibrant color of young green leaves — **qīng**
