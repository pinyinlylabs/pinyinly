**🗣️ Pronunciation of 辶 (chuò)**

- **Pinyin:** chuò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Stop!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair"
- **uò** sounds like **"woh"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling tone
- **chuò** sounds like **"chwoh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"chuò!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone pattern of **chuò**.

**📝 Note on Usage:**

辶 is the **"walking" radical** (also called 走之底 "walking bottom"). It appears at the bottom of
many characters related to movement:

- 过 (guò) - "to pass"
- 这 (zhè) - "this"
- 进 (jìn) - "to enter"
- 远 (yuǎn) - "far"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

辶 represents **walking/movement** — pronounce it with a sharp, decisive **chuò** like taking a firm
step forward!
