**🗣️ Pronunciation of 漂 (piào)**

- **Pinyin:** piào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "park"
- **iào** sounds like **"yow"** in "yowl" but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **piào** sounds like **"pyow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're impressed by something floating gracefully: **"piào!"** — that definitive, sharp
falling tone pattern of **piào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 漂 (piào) - "float; drift"
- 漂亮 (piào liang) - "beautiful; pretty"

**💡 Special Note:**

漂 has different pronunciations for different meanings:

- **piào** (4th tone) - "float; drift"
- **piāo** (1st tone) - "float; drift" (alternative pronunciation)
- **piǎo** (3rd tone) - "bleach" (as in bleaching clothes)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When something **floats** beautifully, you exclaim **"Wow!"** with that sharp, falling tone — just
like **piào**!
