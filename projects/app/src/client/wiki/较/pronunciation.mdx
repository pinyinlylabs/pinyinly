**🗣️ Pronunciation of 较 (jiào)**

- **Pinyin:** jiào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **iào** sounds like **"yow"** (as in "ouch") but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **jiào** sounds like **"j-yow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a definitive comparison: **"jiào!"** — that's the tone pattern of
**jiào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 较 (jiào) - "relatively; comparatively; rather"
- 比较 (bǐ jiào) - "compare; comparison; relatively"
- 较好 (jiào hǎo) - "relatively good; better"
- 较多 (jiào duō) - "relatively many; more"
- 较少 (jiào shǎo) - "relatively few; less"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

较 means "relatively" — the **sharp falling** tone emphasizes the decisive nature of making
comparisons!
