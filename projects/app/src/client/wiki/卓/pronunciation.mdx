**🗣️ Pronunciation of 卓 (zhuó)**

- **Pinyin:** zhuó
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "jump" but with tongue curled back
- **uó** sounds like **"wore"** but with second tone → rise up like a question
- **zhuó** sounds like **"joor"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone: Start low and rise up, like you're asking "Really?" —
that's the tone pattern of **zhuó**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 卓 (zhuó) - "outstanding"
- 卓越 (zhuó yuè) - "excellent"
- 卓有成效 (zhuó yǒu chéng xiào) - "remarkably effective"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

卓 means "outstanding" — imagine someone **rising up** above the crowd, which matches the **rising
tone**!
