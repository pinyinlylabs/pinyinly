**🗣️ Pronunciation of 绍 (shào)**

- **Pinyin:** shào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **shào** sounds like **"shaow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"shào!"** — start high and drop down decisively.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 绍兴 (Shào xīng) - "Shaoxing" (city name)
- 介绍 (jiè shào) - "to introduce"
- 绍介 (shào jiè) - "to introduce" (formal)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"show"** but with a falling tone — you're **showing** or **continuing** something with
authority!
