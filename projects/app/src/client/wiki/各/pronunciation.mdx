**🗣️ Pronunciation of 各 (gè)**

- **Pinyin:** gè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **è** sounds like **"uh"** with a sharp falling tone
- **gè** sounds like **"guh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving a command — that's the energy of **gè**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 各种 (gè zhǒng) - "various kinds"
- 各位 (gè wèi) - "everyone"
- 各自 (gè zì) - "respectively"
- 各国 (gè guó) - "various countries"
