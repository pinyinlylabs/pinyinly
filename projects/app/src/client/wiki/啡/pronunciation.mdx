**🗣️ Pronunciation of 啡 (fēi)**

- **Pinyin:** fēi
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Feee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fee"
- **ēi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way", held steady and high
- **fēi** sounds like **"fay"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and level**:

Say it like naming an ingredient: **"fēi"** — that steady, clear pronunciation.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 啡 (fēi) - appears mainly in compound words
- 咖啡 (kā fēi) - "coffee"
- 吗啡 (mǎ fēi) - "morphine"

**🔟 Special Note:**

啡 is **rarely used alone** in modern Chinese. It's almost always found as part of **咖啡 (coffee)**
where it represents the second syllable of the borrowed word "coffee."

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **mouth (口)** plus **非** — think of the **coffee** you drink through your mouth, making that
smooth "fēi" sound!
