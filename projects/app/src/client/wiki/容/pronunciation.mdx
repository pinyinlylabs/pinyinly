**🗣️ Pronunciation of 容 (róng)**

- **Pinyin:** róng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **r** like **"r"** in "rang" but softer
- **óng** sounds like **"ong"** in "gong" but with a rising tone
- **róng** sounds like **"rong"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"róng?"** — that's the tone pattern of **róng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 容 (róng) - "contain"
- 容易 (róng yì) - "easy"
- 内容 (nèi róng) - "content"
- 容器 (róng qì) - "container"
- 容忍 (róng rěn) - "tolerate"
