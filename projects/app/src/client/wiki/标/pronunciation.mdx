**🗣️ Pronunciation of 标 (biāo)**

- **Pinyin:** biāo
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **iāo** sounds like **"yow"** in "yowl" but held steady and high
- **biāo** sounds like **"bee-yow"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like announcing a mark or label: **"biāo!"** — keep that high, steady tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 标 (biāo) - "mark"
- 标准 (biāo zhǔn) - "standard"
- 标题 (biāo tí) - "title"
- 目标 (mù biāo) - "goal"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **biāo** as marking something important — say it with a clear, steady high tone like
pointing to a sign!
