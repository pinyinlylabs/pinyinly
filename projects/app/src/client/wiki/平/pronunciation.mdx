**🗣️ Pronunciation of 平 (píng)**

- **Pinyin:** píng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "ping"
- **íng** sounds like **"eeng"** but with rising tone
- **píng** sounds like **"peeng"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is a **rising** tone, like asking a question:

Say it like asking if something is **flat**: **"píng?"** — that rising, curious tone is the **second
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 平 (píng) - "flat; level"
- 平常 (píng cháng) - "ordinary; usual"
- 平安 (píng ān) - "safe; peaceful"
- 平时 (píng shí) - "usually; normally"
- 水平 (shuǐ píng) - "level; standard"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **平** as asking if a surface is **flat** with that rising, questioning tone — that's the
**second tone** inquiry!
