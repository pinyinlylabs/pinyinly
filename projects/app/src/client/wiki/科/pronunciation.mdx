**🗣️ Pronunciation of 科 (kē)**

- **Pinyin:** kē
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **ē** sounds like **"uh"** in "duh", but with first tone → steady and high
- **kē** sounds like **"kuh"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're stating something clearly: **"kē"** — hold that high, steady pitch like
**"kēēē"**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 科 (kē) - "branch of study; department"
- 科学 (kē xué) - "science"
- 科技 (kē jì) - "technology"
- 学科 (xué kē) - "academic subject"
- 外科 (wài kē) - "surgery"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 科 as "course" or "class" — the steady first tone sounds confident and academic, perfect
for talking about fields of study.
