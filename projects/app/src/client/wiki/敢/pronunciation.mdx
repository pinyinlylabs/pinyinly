**🗣️ Pronunciation of 敢 (gǎn)**

- **Pinyin:** gǎn
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ǎn** sounds like **"ahn"**, but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up
- **gǎn** sounds like **"gahn"** with a dip-then-rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 敢 (gǎn) - "dare"
- 敢于 (gǎn yú) - "dare to"
- 不敢 (bù gǎn) - "don't dare"
- 敢说 (gǎn shuō) - "dare to say"
- 勇敢 (yǒng gǎn) - "brave"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The third tone's hesitation (dip) then confidence (rise) perfectly captures the moment of gathering
courage to **dare** do something.
