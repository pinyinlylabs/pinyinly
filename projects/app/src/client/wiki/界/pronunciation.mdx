**🗣️ Pronunciation of 界 (jiè)**

- **Pinyin:** jiè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Stop!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but softer)
- **iè** sounds like **"yeh"** in "yeah", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **jiè** sounds like **"jyeh!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 界 (jiè) - "boundary; world; field"
- 世界 (shì jiè) - "world"
- 边界 (biān jiè) - "border; boundary"
- 界限 (jiè xiàn) - "boundary; limit"
- 学界 (xué jiè) - "academic circles"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Fourth tone is like firmly setting a **boundary** - your voice **drops sharply** throughout **jiè**.
