**🗣️ Pronunciation of 衣 (yī)**

- **Pinyin:** yī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like holding a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **yī** sounds like **"yee"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 衣 (yī) - "clothes"
- 衣服 (yī fu) - "clothing"
- 上衣 (shàng yī) - "upper garment"
- 大衣 (dà yī) - "overcoat"
- 洗衣机 (xǐ yī jī) - "washing machine"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the steady, high tone like the sound you make when showing off a nice piece of clothing:
"Eeeee, look at this!"
