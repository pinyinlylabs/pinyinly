**🗣️ Pronunciation of 搬 (bān)**

- **Pinyin:** bān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "ban"
- **ān** sounds like **"ahn"**, but held steady and high
- **bān** sounds like **"bahn"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like singing a steady high note: **"bān~~~"** — that's the flat, steady tone of **bān**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 搬 (bān) - "move; carry"
- 搬家 (bān jiā) - "move house; relocate"
- 搬运 (bān yùn) - "transport; carry"
- 搬走 (bān zǒu) - "move away"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"搬"** as **"bahn"** held steady — like steadily moving or carrying heavy things!
