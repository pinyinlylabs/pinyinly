**🗣️ Pronunciation of 周 (zhōu)**

- **Pinyin:** zhōu
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge", but with tongue tip curled back
- **ōu** sounds like **"oh"** but held steady and high
- **zhōu** sounds like **"joe"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady musical note: **"zhōu..."** — keep the pitch high and level.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 周 (zhōu) - "week; circumference"
- 周末 (zhōu mò) - "weekend"
- 周围 (zhōu wéi) - "around; surrounding"
- 周年 (zhōu nián) - "anniversary"
- 一周 (yí zhōu) - "one week"
- 上周 (shàng zhōu) - "last week"
- 下周 (xià zhōu) - "next week"
