**🗣️ Pronunciation of 当 (dāng)**

- **Pinyin:** dāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Daaa"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "day"
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with first tone → steady and high
- **dāng** sounds like **"dahng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high flat** tone:

Say it like you're making a confident statement: **"dāng"** — that's the steady, authoritative tone
of **dāng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 当 (dāng) - "serve as/act as"
- 当时 (dāng shí) - "at that time"
- 当然 (dāng rán) - "of course"
- 当作 (dāng zuò) - "regard as"
- 当中 (dāng zhōng) - "in the middle"

**💡 Special Notes:**

当 can also be pronounced **dàng** (fourth tone) meaning "proper/suitable":

- 恰当 (qià dàng) - "appropriate"
- 上当 (shàng dàng) - "be fooled"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

当 means "serve as" — the steady first tone sounds confident and authoritative, like someone
**taking charge** of a situation!
