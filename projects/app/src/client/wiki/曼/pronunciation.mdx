**🗣️ Pronunciation of 曼 (màn)**

- **Pinyin:** màn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "man"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"**, but with fourth tone → sharp fall down
- **màn** sounds like **"mahn!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop down sharply, like making a definitive statement. The tone matches the graceful,
flowing meaning of the character.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 曼妙 (màn miào) - "graceful; enchanting"
- 曼延 (màn yán) - "extend; stretch"
- 浪漫 (làng màn) - "romantic"
- 曼哈顿 (Màn hā dùn) - "Manhattan"

**📚 Special Notes:**

曼 often appears in names and poetic expressions. It conveys a sense of graceful extension or
flowing beauty.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

曼 means "long" or "graceful" - imagine something gracefully extending with that definitive fourth
tone: **"màn!"**
