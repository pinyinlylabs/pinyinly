**🗣️ Pronunciation of 算 (suàn)**

- **Pinyin:** suàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"That's it!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **uàn** sounds like **"wan"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **suàn** sounds like **"swan"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is **authoritative and falling**:

Say it like you're making a calculation and stating the result — that's the decisive tone of
**suàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 算 (suàn) - "calculate; count"
- 算数 (suàn shù) - "arithmetic"
- 计算 (jì suàn) - "calculate; compute"
- 算了 (suàn le) - "forget it; never mind"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **suàn** as the sound of dropping a calculator after finishing a calculation — that sharp
fourth tone drop!
