**🗣️ Pronunciation of 租 (zū)**

- **Pinyin:** zū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"dz"** in "suds"
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "zoo", but with first tone → steady and high
- **zū** sounds like **"zoo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're stating something clearly: **"zū"** — hold that high, steady pitch like
**"zūūū"**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 租 (zū) - "to rent; to lease"
- 房租 (fáng zū) - "rent (for housing)"
- 出租 (chū zū) - "to rent out"
- 出租车 (chū zū chē) - "taxi"
- 租金 (zū jīn) - "rental fee"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the steady first tone as representing the **regular monthly payments** you make when you
rent something — consistent and reliable like **"zū, zū, zū"** each month.
