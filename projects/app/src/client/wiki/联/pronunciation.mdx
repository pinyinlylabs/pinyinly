**🗣️ Pronunciation of 联 (lián)**

- **Pinyin:** lián
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question "really?"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **ián** sounds like **"yen"** (the currency), but with second tone → rise up
- **lián** sounds like **"yen"** with a questioning rise

**🔤 Alternative breakdown:**

- **li** like **"lee"**
- **án** like **"ahn"** with rising tone
- **lián** = **"lee-ahn"** with second tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up, like you're asking "Connected?" — that's the linking tone pattern of
**lián**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 联 (lián) - "connect, unite, alliance"
- 联合 (lián hé) - "unite, combine"
- 联系 (lián xì) - "contact, connection"
- 联合国 (lián hé guó) - "United Nations"
- 联盟 (lián méng) - "alliance, league"
- 联络 (lián luò) - "contact, liaison"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **lián** as the sound of things coming together — that rising second tone suggests
connection and joining!
