**🗣️ Pronunciation of 挺 (tǐng)**

- **Pinyin:** tǐng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top" (with a puff of air)
- **ǐng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **tǐng** sounds like **"ting"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"tǐng..."** — that's the tone pattern of
**tǐng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 挺 (tǐng) - "to straighten; quite; rather"
- 挺好 (tǐng hǎo) - "quite good; pretty good"
- 挺直 (tǐng zhí) - "to straighten up; upright"
- 挺身 (tǐng shēn) - "to stand up bravely"
- 很挺 (hěn tǐng) - "very; quite" (colloquial)

**💡 Usage Note:**

挺 is commonly used as an adverb meaning "quite" or "rather":

- 挺不错 (tǐng bù cuò) - "quite good; not bad"
