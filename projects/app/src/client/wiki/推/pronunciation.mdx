**🗣️ Pronunciation of 推 (tuī)**

- **Pinyin:** tuī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea" (but with a slight puff of air)
- **uī** sounds like **"way"**, but held steady and high
- **tuī** sounds like **"tway"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like singing a steady high note: **"tuī~~~"** — that's the flat, steady tone of **tuī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 推 (tuī) - "push"
- 推广 (tuī guǎng) - "promote; popularize"
- 推进 (tuī jìn) - "advance; push forward"
- 推迟 (tuī chí) - "postpone; delay"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"推"** as **"tway"** held steady — like steadily pushing something forward!
