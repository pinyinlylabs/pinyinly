**🗣️ Pronunciation of 令 (lìng)**

- **Pinyin:** lìng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "long"
- **ìng** sounds like **"ing"** in "ring" but with a sharp falling tone
- **lìng** sounds like **"ling!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"lìng!"** — that's the decisive, falling tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 命令 (mìng lìng) - "command; order"
- 令人 (lìng rén) - "causing someone to..."
- 法令 (fǎ lìng) - "decree; statute"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

令 means "command" — perfect match with the fourth tone's commanding, authoritative sound!
