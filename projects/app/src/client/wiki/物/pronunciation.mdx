**🗣️ Pronunciation of 物 (wù)**

- **Pinyin:** wù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "wood"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **wù** sounds like **"woo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're pointing out an object: **"wù!"** — that sharp, decisive drop is the **fourth
tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 物 (wù) - "thing; object"
- 东西 (dōng xi) - "thing" (note: 西 in compound is neutral tone)
- 动物 (dòng wù) - "animal"
- 植物 (zhí wù) - "plant"
- 物品 (wù pǐn) - "goods; items"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **物** as definitively naming an object: **"wù!"** — that sharp downward tone matches the
**fourth tone**!
