**🗣️ Pronunciation of 云 (yún)**

- **Pinyin:** yún
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ún** sounds like **"oon"** in "moon" but with a rising tone
- **yún** sounds like **"yoon?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is a **rising** tone, like floating upward:

Say it like you're gazing up at the sky in wonder: **"yún?"** — that's the upward floating energy of
**yún**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 云 (yún) - "cloud"
- 白云 (bái yún) - "white cloud"
- 乌云 (wū yún) - "dark cloud"
- 云彩 (yún cǎi) - "colorful clouds"
- 多云 (duō yún) - "cloudy"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **云** as clouds rising up in the sky — that's the **second tone** upward movement!

**📍 Modern Usage:**

云 is also commonly used in modern technology terms:

- 云计算 (yún jì suán) - "cloud computing"
- 云存储 (yún cún chǔ) - "cloud storage"
