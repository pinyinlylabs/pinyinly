**🗣️ Pronunciation of 章 (zhāng)**

- **Pinyin:** zhāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "junk" but with tongue curled back
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** in "bang", but with first tone → steady and high
- **zhāng** sounds like **"jahng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and steady** tone:

Keep your voice high and flat throughout: **"zhāng"** — like holding a steady musical note.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 章 (zhāng) - "chapter; section"
- 文章 (wén zhāng) - "article; essay"
- 印章 (yìn zhāng) - "seal; stamp"
- 规章 (guī zhāng) - "rules; regulations"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"jahng"** as the sound of reading a chapter steadily — the consistent first tone
represents organized, structured content!
