**🗣️ Pronunciation of 弓 (gōng)**

- **Pinyin:** gōng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Gooo"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ōng** sounds like **"ohng"** but with first tone → steady and high
- **gōng** sounds like **"gohng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high flat** tone:

Say it like you're drawing a bow string taut: **"gōng"** — that's the steady, tense tone of
**gōng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 弓 (gōng) - "bow" (weapon)
- 弓箭 (gōng jiàn) - "bow and arrow"
- 拉弓 (lā gōng) - "draw a bow"
- 弓形 (gōng xíng) - "arch-shaped"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

弓 means "bow" (the weapon) — the steady first tone is like holding a bow string **taut and ready**
to shoot!
