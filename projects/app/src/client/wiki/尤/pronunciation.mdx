**🗣️ Pronunciation of 尤 (yóu)**

- **Pinyin:** yóu
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking "you?"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **óu** sounds like **"oh"** but with a rising tone, similar to "go" but rising
- **yóu** sounds like **"yo"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is **rising like a question**:

Start **low and rise up** — like asking "Really?" The tone pattern is similar to the English
intonation in "you?" when surprised.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 尤 (yóu) - "especially"
- 尤其 (yóu qí) - "especially; particularly"
- 尤为 (yóu wéi) - "especially; particularly"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of someone asking **"you?"** in surprise — that rising tone is exactly the second tone pattern
for **yóu**!
