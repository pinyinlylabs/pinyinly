**🗣️ Pronunciation of 富 (fù)**

- **Pinyin:** fù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fun"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **fù** sounds like **"foo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"fù!"** — that's the tone pattern of **fù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 富 (fù) - "rich"
- 富人 (fù rén) - "rich person"
- 富有 (fù yǒu) - "wealthy"
- 丰富 (fēng fù) - "abundant"
- 财富 (cái fù) - "wealth"
