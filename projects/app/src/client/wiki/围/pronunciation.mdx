**🗣️ Pronunciation of 围 (wéi)**

- **Pinyin:** wéi
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **éi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way", but with second tone → rising pitch
- **wéi** sounds like **"way?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) starts **low** and rises **up**:

Say it like you're asking "围?" (surround?) — that's the rising tone of **wéi**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 围 (wéi) - "surround; encircle"
- 围绕 (wéi rào) - "around; surrounding"
- 围巾 (wéi jīn) - "scarf"
- 包围 (bāo wéi) - "surround; encircle"
- 范围 (fàn wéi) - "scope; range"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **围** (surrounding) like asking "围?" — questioning what's all around you!
