**🗣️ Pronunciation of 代 (dài)**

- **Pinyin:** dài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **dài** sounds like **"die!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"dài!"** — that's the decisive, falling tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 代表 (dài biǎo) - "represent"
- 代替 (dài tì) - "replace; substitute"
- 时代 (shí dài) - "era; age"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

代 means "replace" — like decisively saying "**take** their place!" with that firm fourth tone!
