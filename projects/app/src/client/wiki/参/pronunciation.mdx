**🗣️ Pronunciation of 参 (cān)**

- **Pinyin:** cān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats"
- **ān** sounds like **"ahn"** in "yarn", held steady and high
- **cān** sounds like **"tsahn"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high flat** tone:

Say it evenly and confidently: **"cān"** — that's the steady, high pattern of **cān**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 参加 (cān jiā) - "participate"
- 参观 (cān guān) - "visit"
- 人参 (rén shēn) - "ginseng" (note: tone change to shēn)
- 参考 (cān kǎo) - "reference"

**💡 Special Note:**

参 has multiple pronunciations:

- **参 (cān)** - "participate" (first tone)
- **参 (shēn)** - in 人参 "ginseng" (first tone, different sound)
