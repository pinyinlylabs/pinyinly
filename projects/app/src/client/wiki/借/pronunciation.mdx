**🗣️ Pronunciation of 借 (jiè)**

- **Pinyin:** jiè
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep", but with the tongue closer to the hard palate and no puff of air
- **iè** sounds like **"yeh"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **jiè** sounds like **"jyeh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"jiè!"** — that's the tone pattern of **jiè**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 借 (jiè) - "borrow"
- 借用 (jiè yòng) - "borrow for use"
- 借钱 (jiè qián) - "borrow money"
- 借书 (jiè shū) - "borrow books"
