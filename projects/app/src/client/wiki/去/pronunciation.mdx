**🗣️ Pronunciation of 去 (qù)**

- **Pinyin:** qù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" but more aspirated
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **qù** sounds like **"choo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Say it decisively, like giving a direction: **"qù!"** — that's the commanding, falling pattern of
**qù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 去年 (qù nián) - "last year"
- 去世 (qù shì) - "pass away"
- 上去 (shàng qù) - "go up"
- 出去 (chū qù) - "go out"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **去** as **go** — the sharp falling tone is like pointing decisively in the direction you
want to go!
