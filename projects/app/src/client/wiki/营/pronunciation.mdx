**🗣️ Pronunciation of 营 (yíng)**

- **Pinyin:** yíng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **íng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing", but with second tone → rising pitch
- **yíng** sounds like **"ying?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about something: **"yíng?"** — that's the tone pattern of **yíng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 营 (yíng) - "camp"
- 营养 (yíng yǎng) - "nutrition"
- 经营 (jīng yíng) - "operate/manage"
- 夏令营 (xià lìng yíng) - "summer camp"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **yíng** as asking about a camp: "Is this the camp?" — the rising tone sounds inquisitive
and hopeful.
