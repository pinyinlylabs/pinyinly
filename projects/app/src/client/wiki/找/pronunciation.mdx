**🗣️ Pronunciation of 找 (zhǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** zhǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" (with tongue curled back)
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **zhǎo** sounds like **"jow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're searching thoughtfully: **"zhǎo..."** — that's the contemplative tone pattern of
**zhǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 找 (zhǎo) - "look for; find; seek"
- 找到 (zhǎo dào) - "find; locate"
- 找出 (zhǎo chū) - "find out; discover"
- 找人 (zhǎo rén) - "look for someone"
- 寻找 (xún zhǎo) - "search for; seek"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Look for** — the dipping third tone is like the thoughtful process of searching!
