**🗣️ Pronunciation of 亿 (yì)**

- **Pinyin:** yì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **yì** sounds like **"yee!"** with a commanding drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 亿 (yì) - "hundred million"
- 一亿 (yī yì) - "one hundred million"
- 几亿 (jǐ yì) - "several hundred million"
- 亿万 (yì wàn) - "hundreds of millions"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

亿 represents an enormous number (hundred million) - say **"yee!"** with the sharp, definitive
fourth tone to express the magnitude of such a huge number!
