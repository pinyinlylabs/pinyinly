**🗣️ Pronunciation of 鹿 (lù)**

- **Pinyin:** lù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "look"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "food" but with a sharp falling tone
- **lù** sounds like **"loo"** with a sharp drop in pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly pointing out a deer in the distance: **"lù!"** — that definitive, falling
tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 鹿 (lù) - "deer"
- 梅花鹿 (méi huā lù) - "sika deer"
- 驯鹿 (xùn lù) - "reindeer"
- 麋鹿 (mí lù) - "elk"
- 鹿角 (lù jiǎo) - "deer antlers"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

鹿 with its sharp falling tone sounds like you're calling out to spot a deer: **"lù!"** — like
"Look!" but with that decisive fourth tone drop.
