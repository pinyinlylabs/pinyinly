An institution that provides medical or surgical care and treatment for the sick or injured; hospital; medical facility.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                                        |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | yīyuàn                                      |
| Core meaning   | hospital; medical facility; healthcare center |
| Part of speech | noun                                        |
| Tone           | yī (1st), yuàn (4th)                        |

## Word Breakdown

医院 combines concepts of medicine and institutional courtyard to represent medical facilities.

| Component | Meaning                                                |
| --------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| **医**    | Medicine; doctor; heal - representing medical practice |
| **院**    | Courtyard; institution; compound - representing organized facility |

Together they create: "medical institution" or "healing compound" - an organized facility dedicated to medical care.

## Mnemonic

Think of 医院 as **"a medical compound where healing is organized and systematic"**:

- 医 (yī) represents medicine, doctors, and the healing arts
- 院 (yuàn) shows the institutional courtyard, organized facility structure
- Together: a complex of buildings organized around medical care
- Like a traditional Chinese courtyard designed for healing
- The combination of medical expertise with institutional organization

This creates the image: **an organized compound dedicated to systematic medical care and healing**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

医院 represents **hospitals and medical care facilities**. It's used:

1. **Medical facility**: 去医院 (qù yīyuàn) - "go to the hospital"
2. **Healthcare institution**: 大医院 (dà yīyuàn) - "large hospital"
3. **Treatment location**: 在医院 (zài yīyuàn) - "in the hospital"
4. **Medical system**: 医院治疗 (yīyuàn zhìliáo) - "hospital treatment"

## Examples

- **住医院** (zhù yīyuàn) - "be hospitalized"
- **医院医生** (yīyuàn yīshēng) - "hospital doctor"
- **急救医院** (jíjiù yīyuàn) - "emergency hospital"
- **出医院** (chū yīyuàn) - "leave the hospital; be discharged"
- **医院床位** (yīyuàn chuángwèi) - "hospital bed"

## Types of Hospitals

Different kinds of 医院:

- **综合医院** (zōnghé yīyuàn) - "general hospital"
- **专科医院** (zhuānkē yīyuàn) - "specialty hospital"
- **儿童医院** (értóng yīyuàn) - "children's hospital"
- **中医院** (zhōngyī yuàn) - "traditional Chinese medicine hospital"

## Hospital Levels

医院 by scale and capability:

- **三甲医院** (sānjiǎ yīyuàn) - "Grade 3A hospital" (top tier)
- **二级医院** (èrjí yīyuàn) - "Grade 2 hospital"
- **社区医院** (shèqū yīyuàn) - "community hospital"
- **乡镇医院** (xiāngzhèn yīyuàn) - "township hospital"

## Hospital Departments

医院 sections:

- **急诊科** (jízhěn kē) - "emergency department"
- **内科** (nèikē) - "internal medicine"
- **外科** (wàikē) - "surgery department"
- **儿科** (érkē) - "pediatrics"

## Medical Activities

医院 procedures:

- **看病** (kàn bìng) - "see a doctor; get medical treatment"
- **手术** (shǒushù) - "surgery; operation"
- **检查** (jiǎnchá) - "medical examination"
- **治疗** (zhìliáo) - "treatment; therapy"

## Cultural Significance

医院 in Chinese culture represents:

**Healthcare Values:**
- **救死扶伤** (jiù sǐ fú shāng) - Save lives and heal the wounded
- **仁心仁术** (rén xīn rén shù) - Benevolent heart and skillful medicine
- **医者父母心** (yīzhě fùmǔ xīn) - Doctors have parents' hearts
- **健康第一** (jiànkāng dì yī) - Health comes first

**Social Institution:**
- **公共服务** (gōnggòng fúwù) - Public service for society
- **生命守护** (shēngmìng shǒuhù) - Guardian of life
- **科技进步** (kējì jìnbù) - Hub of medical technology advancement
- **人文关怀** (rénwén guānhuái) - Humanistic care

## Grammar Functions

- **Subject**: 医院很大 (yīyuàn hěn dà) - "the hospital is big"
- **Object**: 建设医院 (jiànshè yīyuàn) - "build a hospital"
- **Location**: 在医院工作 (zài yīyuàn gōngzuò) - "work at the hospital"

## Regional Healthcare

## Learning Notes

医院 is essential because:

- Fundamental institution for health and medical care
- Essential for discussing health, illness, and medical treatment
- Key to understanding Chinese healthcare system and values
- Important for emergency situations and daily health concerns
- Demonstrates how compound words express institutional concepts

医院 reflects the Chinese understanding that healing requires both medical expertise (医) and organized institutional support (院) to provide comprehensive care that addresses both physical and social aspects of health!
