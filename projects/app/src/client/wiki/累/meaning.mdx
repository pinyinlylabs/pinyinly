## Core Meaning & Usage

累 represents **tiredness, exhaustion, and fatigue from effort**. It's used:

1. **Physical tiredness**: 很累 (hěn lèi) - "very tired"
2. **Mental fatigue**: 脑子累 (nǎozi lèi) - "mentally tired"
3. **Exhaustion**: 累坏了 (lèi huài le) - "exhausted"
4. **Weariness**: 感到累 (gǎndào lèi) - "feel tired"

## Examples

- **很累** (hěn lèi) - "very tired"
- **太累了** (tài lèi le) - "too tired"
- **不累** (bù lèi) - "not tired"
- **累得不行** (lèi de bù xíng) - "extremely tired"
- **累死了** (lèi sǐ le) - "dead tired" (colloquial)

## Types of Tiredness

Different kinds of 累:

- **体力累** (tǐlì lèi) - "physically tired"
- **精神累** (jīngshén lèi) - "mentally tired"
- **眼睛累** (yǎnjīng lèi) - "eye strain; eyes tired"

## Causes of Tiredness

累 from various activities:

累 in communication:

Different degrees of 累:

## Recovery from Tiredness

Dealing with 累:

## Learning Notes

- Essential for expressing personal condition and needs
- Key to understanding work culture and life balance

- Demonstrates how characters connect work, accumulation, and physical effects

累 reflects the Chinese understanding that tiredness comes from the accumulation of many efforts and
tasks, requiring balance between dedication to work and care for one's health and well-being!
