**🗣️ Pronunciation of 累 (lèi)**

- **Pinyin:** lèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "low"
- **èi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **lèi** sounds like **"lay!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're exhausted and sighing: **"lèi!"** — that's the sharp falling pattern of **lèi**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 累 (lèi) - "tired"
- 疲累 (pí lèi) - "exhausted"
- 劳累 (láo lèi) - "weary"
- 累了 (lèi le) - "tired" (completed action)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you're **tired**, your energy **drops** sharply — just like the fourth tone's falling pattern!
