**🗣️ Pronunciation of 系 (xì)**

- **Pinyin:** xì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"Connect!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **xì** sounds like **"shee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is **authoritative and falling**:

Say it like you're establishing a connection or system: **"xì!"** — that decisive tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 系 (xì) - "system; department; tie"
- 系统 (xì tǒng) - "system"
- 关系 (guān xì) - "relationship; connection"
- 联系 (lián xì) - "contact; connection"

**💡 Special Note:**

系 has multiple pronunciations:

- **xì** (4th tone) - "system; tie; connect" (most common)
- **jì** (4th tone) - used in some compound words

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **系** as connecting things together — that sharp fourth tone drop is like clicking two
pieces firmly into place!
