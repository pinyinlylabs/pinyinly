**🗣️ Pronunciation of 台 (tái)**

- **Pinyin:** tái
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **ái** sounds like **"eye"** but with second tone → rising upward
- **tái** sounds like **"tie"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "tie?": **"tái?"** — that's the questioning tone pattern of **tái**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 台 (tái) - "platform; stage; Taiwan"
- 电台 (diàn tái) - "radio station"
- 舞台 (wǔ tái) - "stage; platform"
- 台湾 (tái wān) - "Taiwan"
- 讲台 (jiǎng tái) - "podium; platform"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"tie"** with a rising question — when asking about a **platform** or **stage**, you might
raise your voice questioningly!
