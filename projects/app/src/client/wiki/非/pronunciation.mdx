**🗣️ Pronunciation of 非 (fēi)**

- **Pinyin:** fēi
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fish"
- **ēi** sounds like **"ay"** in "hay", held steady and high
- **fēi** sounds like **"fay"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 非 (fēi) - "non-; not"
- 非常 (fēi cháng) - "very; extremely"
- 是非 (shì fēi) - "right and wrong"
- 非洲 (fēi zhōu) - "Africa"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **high flat tone** sounds definitive and firm, like a clear "NO!" — **fēi**
