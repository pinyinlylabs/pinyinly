**🗣️ Pronunciation of 爸 (bà)**

- **Pinyin:** bà
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "baby"
- **à** sounds like **"ah"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **bà** sounds like **"bah!"** with a sharp downward drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **falling** tone:

Say it like you're calling "Dad!" when you want his attention — **"bà!"** — that strong, definitive
drop is the fourth tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 爸 (bà) - "dad; father"
- 爸爸 (bà ba) - "daddy; father" (second 爸 is neutral tone)
- 爸妈 (bà mā) - "mom and dad"

**💡 Special Note:**

In 爸爸 (daddy), the first 爸 is pronounced **bà** (fourth tone) and the second 爸 is **ba**
(neutral tone). This is the most common way to say "father" in everyday speech.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When calling your dad, you use a clear, strong voice to get his attention — just like the fourth
tone's commanding pattern in 爸!
