**🗣️ Pronunciation of 响 (xiǎng)**

- **Pinyin:** xiǎng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" but with tongue tip down
- **iǎng** sounds like **"yahng"** with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **xiǎng** sounds like **"shyahng"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're pondering the sound you just heard: **"xiǎng..."** — that's the tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 响 (xiǎng) - "sound; loud"
- 响声 (xiǎng shēng) - "sound; noise"
- 影响 (yǐng xiǎng) - "influence; effect"
- 响应 (xiǎng yìng) - "respond"
- 巨响 (jù xiǎng) - "loud sound; boom"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **sound radical (音)** suggests something that makes noise, and when you hear a loud **sound**,
you often react with that falling-rising "xiǎng" tone!
