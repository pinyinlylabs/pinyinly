**🗣️ Pronunciation of 起 (qǐ)**

- **Pinyin:** qǐ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" but with more air (aspirated)
- **ǐ** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **qǐ** sounds like **"chee"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Third tone: ˇ)**

The **third tone** is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're asking someone to get up: **"qǐ..."** — that's the contemplative tone pattern of
**qǐ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 起床 (qǐ chuáng) - "get up; get out of bed"
- 起来 (qǐ lái) - "get up; stand up"
- 起飞 (qǐ fēi) - "take off (airplane)"
- 一起 (yì qǐ) - "together"
