**🗣️ Pronunciation of 乱 (luàn)**

- **Pinyin:** luàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Stop!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **uàn** sounds like **"wahn"** in "want" but with a sharp falling tone
- **luàn** sounds like **"lwahn!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is **sharp and decisive**:

Say it with frustration, like exclaiming about a **mess** — **"Luàn!"** — that abrupt tone matches
the meaning of **chaos**!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 乱 (luàn) - "chaos, messy, confused"
- 乱七八糟 (luàn qī bā zāo) - "in a complete mess"
- 打乱 (dǎ luàn) - "to disrupt, mess up"
- 乱说 (luàn shuō) - "talk nonsense"

**📍 Usage Note:**

乱 can be used as:

- **Adjective**: 很乱 (hěn luàn) - "very messy"
- **Adverb**: 乱跑 (luàn pǎo) - "run around randomly"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Chaos** — the sharp falling tone captures the sudden, disruptive nature of **luàn**!
