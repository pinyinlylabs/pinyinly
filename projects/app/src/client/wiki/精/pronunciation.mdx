**🗣️ Pronunciation of 精 (jīng)**

- **Pinyin:** jīng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Jing"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but **lighter** and **more forward** in the mouth
- **īng** sounds like **"ing"** but with first tone → high and steady
- **jīng** sounds like **"jing"** with a steady high pitch

**🎯 Mastering the "j" sound:**

The **j** in Chinese is **different** from English "j":

1. **Keep your tongue forward** — tip behind lower teeth
2. **Make it lighter** — less buzzy than English "j"
3. **More like "jee"** in "gee whiz" but softer

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're expressing something refined: **"jīng..."** — that steady, elevated tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 精 (jīng) - "essence; refined; precise"
- 精彩 (jīng cǎi) - "wonderful; brilliant"
- 精神 (jīng shén) - "spirit; energy"
- 精确 (jīng què) - "precise; accurate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **精** as something refined and pure — the first tone stays high and steady, like
maintaining quality standards!
