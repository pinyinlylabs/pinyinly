**🗣️ Pronunciation of 术 (shù)**

- **Pinyin:** shù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp downward fall
- **shù** sounds like **"shoo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're confidently declaring mastery: **"shù!"** — that firm downward drop is the fourth
tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 术 (shù) - "art, skill, technique"
- 技术 (jì shù) - "technology, technique"
- 艺术 (yì shù) - "art"
- 手术 (shǒu shù) - "surgery, operation"
- 武术 (wǔ shù) - "martial arts"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **术** as mastering an "art" or skill — the decisive falling tone matches the confident
execution of a practiced technique!
