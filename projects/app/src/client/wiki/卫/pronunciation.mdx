**🗣️ Pronunciation of 卫 (wèi)**

- **Pinyin:** wèi
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **èi** sounds like **"way"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **wèi** sounds like **"way!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a sharp **falling** tone: Start high and drop sharply down, like a
guard's firm command: **"wèi!"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 卫 (wèi) - "guard; defend"
- 卫生 (wèi shēng) - "hygiene; health"
- 卫兵 (wèi bīng) - "guard; sentry"
- 保卫 (bǎo wèi) - "defend; safeguard"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

卫 means "guard" — guards give sharp, **commanding** orders with a falling tone to maintain order!
