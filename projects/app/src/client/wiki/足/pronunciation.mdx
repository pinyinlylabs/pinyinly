**🗣️ Pronunciation of 足 (zú)**

- **Pinyin:** zú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"dz"** in "adze" (a buzzing sound)
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "zoo" but with second tone → rising up
- **zú** sounds like **"zoo"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "foot?" — that's the tone pattern of **zú**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 足 (zú) - "foot"
- 足够 (zú gòu) - "enough; sufficient"
- 足球 (zú qiú) - "soccer; football"
- 满足 (mǎn zú) - "satisfied; content"
- 不足 (bù zú) - "insufficient; not enough"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

足 means "foot" — the **rising** tone is like lifting your foot to take a step forward!
