**🗣️ Pronunciation of 齐 (qí)**

- **Pinyin:** qí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (aspirated)
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with second tone → rising up
- **qí** sounds like **"chee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and go up, like asking a question: **"qí?"** — that's the tone pattern of **qí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 齐 (qí) - "together; even; neat"
- 整齐 (zhěng qí) - "tidy; neat"
- 齐全 (qí quán) - "complete; comprehensive"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of people coming **together** and asking **"qí?"** with that rising tone — like they're
checking if everyone is ready!
