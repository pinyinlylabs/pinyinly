**🗣️ Pronunciation of 光 (guāng)**

- **Pinyin:** guāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **gu** like **"goo"** in "goose"
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** with a high flat tone
- **guāng** sounds like **"gwahng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're singing a steady, high note: **"guāng..."** — that's the tone pattern of
**guāng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 光 (guāng) - "light"
- 阳光 (yáng guāng) - "sunlight"
- 光明 (guāng míng) - "bright; light"
- 光线 (guāng xiàn) - "ray of light"
- 月光 (yuè guāng) - "moonlight"

**💡 Special Notes:**

光 consistently uses the first tone (guāng) in all its common meanings and compounds. The character
represents both physical light and metaphorical concepts of brightness or clarity.
