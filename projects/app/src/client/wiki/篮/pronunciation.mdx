**🗣️ Pronunciation of 篮 (lán)**

- **Pinyin:** lán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Lan?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** but with second tone → rising
- **lán** sounds like **"lahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "which basket?" — **"lán?"** — that rising questioning tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 篮 (lán) - "basket"
- 篮球 (lán qiú) - "basketball"
- 篮子 (lán zi) - "basket"
- 购物篮 (gòu wù lán) - "shopping basket"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **篮** with the second tone rising like a basketball going up into the basket — that upward
motion matches the rising tone!
