**🗣️ Pronunciation of 笑 (xiào)**

- **Pinyin:** xiào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" but with tongue lower
- **iào** sounds like **"yow"** in "yowl", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **xiào** sounds like **"shyow!"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop sharply: **"xiào!"** — like the sudden burst of laughter.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 笑 (xiào) - "smile; laugh"
- 笑话 (xiào hua) - "joke"
- 笑话儿 (xiào huār) - "joke" (Beijing dialect)
- 微笑 (wēi xiào) - "smile"
- 大笑 (dà xiào) - "laugh loudly"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"shyow!"** as the sudden sound of laughter bursting out — the sharp fourth tone captures
the explosive nature of laughter!
