**🗣️ Pronunciation of 兴 (xìng)**

- **Pinyin:** xìng (main pronunciation)
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "she" (but more like "hs" sound)
- **ìng** sounds like **"ing"** with a sharp falling tone
- **xìng** sounds like **"hsing!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"xìng!"** — that's the tone pattern of **xìng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

**xìng (fourth tone):**

- 兴趣 (xìng qù) - "interest; hobby"
- 高兴 (gāo xìng) - "happy; glad"
- 兴奋 (xìng fèn) - "excited"

**💡 Special Notes:**

兴 has two main pronunciations:

- **兴 (xìng)** - fourth tone: used in words like 高兴 (happy), 兴趣 (interest)
- **兴 (xīng)** - first tone: used in words like 兴起 (to rise), 复兴 (revival)

The fourth tone pronunciation (xìng) is more commonly encountered in everyday vocabulary.
