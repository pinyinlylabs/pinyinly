**🗣️ Pronunciation of 能 (néng)**

- **Pinyin:** néng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **éng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung", but with second tone → rise up
- **néng** sounds like **"nung?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "Can you?": **"néng?"** — that's the inquiring tone pattern of **néng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 能 (néng) - "can; be able to"
- 能力 (néng lì) - "ability; capability"
- 能够 (néng gòu) - "be able to; can"
- 可能 (kě néng) - "possible; maybe"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

能 expresses ability or capability, and the rising second tone suggests the upward potential and
possibility inherent in being able to do something!
