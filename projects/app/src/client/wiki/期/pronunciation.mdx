**🗣️ Pronunciation of 期 (qī)**

- **Pinyin:** qī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but more aspirated)
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **qī** sounds like **"chee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like singing a steady high note: **"qī"** — that even, high pitch is the first tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 期 (qī) - "period, time period"
- 学期 (xué qī) - "semester, term"
- 期待 (qī dài) - "to expect, to look forward to"
- 日期 (rì qī) - "date"
- 星期 (xīng qī) - "week"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **期** as marking a steady "period" of time — the flat, consistent first tone matches the
steady flow of time periods!
