**🗣️ Pronunciation of 地 (dì)**

- **Pinyin:** dì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "day"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **dì** sounds like **"dee!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) starts **high** and drops **fast**:

Say it like you're pointing to the ground: **"dì!"** — that's the falling tone of **dì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 地 (dì) - "ground; earth; place"
- 地方 (dì fāng) - "place; location"
- 地图 (dì tú) - "map"
- 土地 (tǔ dì) - "land; territory"
- 地址 (dì zhǐ) - "address"
- 地球 (dì qiú) - "Earth (planet)"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **地** (ground) like firmly pointing down to the earth: **"dì!"** — that's the **fourth
tone** downward motion!
