**🗣️ Pronunciation of 湖 (hú)**

- **Pinyin:** hú
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "home"
- **ú** sounds like **"oo"** in "food" but with second tone → rising up
- **hú** sounds like **"hoo?"** with a rising pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking someone about a body of water: **"hú?"** — that questioning, upward tone
pattern of **hú**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 湖 (hú) - "lake"
- 湖水 (hú shuǐ) - "lake water"
- 西湖 (xī hú) - "West Lake" (famous lake in Hangzhou)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you see a beautiful **lake**, you might ask **"Hoo, is that a lake?"** — the rising tone in
**"Hoo?"** matches the second tone of **hú**!
