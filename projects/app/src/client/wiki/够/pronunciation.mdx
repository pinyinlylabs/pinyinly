**🗣️ Pronunciation of 够 (gòu)**

- **Pinyin:** gòu
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — **falling** tone, like stating a fact: **"Go!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **òu** sounds like **"oh"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **gòu** sounds like **"go!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're decisively saying "that's sufficient": **"gòu!"** — that definitive tone is the
**fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 够 (gòu) - "enough; sufficient"
- 够了 (gòu le) - "that's enough"
- 不够 (bù gòu) - "not enough"
- 够用 (gòu yòng) - "sufficient for use"
- 足够 (zú gòu) - "adequate; sufficient"
- 够格 (gòu gé) - "qualified; up to standard"

**💡 Special Notes:**

够 expresses sufficiency or adequacy. It's commonly used to indicate when something meets the
required amount or standard. The word is essential for expressing satisfaction with quantity or
quality, or conversely, for stating that something is insufficient.
