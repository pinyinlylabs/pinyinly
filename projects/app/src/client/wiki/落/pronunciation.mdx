**🗣️ Pronunciation of 落 (luò)**

- **Pinyin:** luò
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "lake"
- **uò** sounds like **"woh"**, but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **luò** sounds like **"lwoh!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like describing something dropping: **"luò!"** — that's the tone pattern of **luò**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 落 (luò) - "drop/fall"
- 落后 (luò hòu) - "fall behind"
- 日落 (rì luò) - "sunset"
- 降落 (jiàng luò) - "land/descend"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **luò** as watching something fall down: "It drops!" — the falling tone mirrors the action
of dropping or falling.
