**🗣️ Pronunciation of 加 (jiā)**

- **Pinyin:** jiā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Jiaaa"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but softer, like "gee"
- **iā** sounds like **"ya"** in "yard" but held steady and high
- **jiā** sounds like **"jya"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like announcing something important: **"jiā!"** — keep that high, steady tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 加 (jiā) - "add"
- 加油 (jiā yóu) - "add fuel, come on!" (encouragement)
- 加工 (jiā gōng) - "processing"
- 加强 (jiā qiáng) - "strengthen"
- 加快 (jiā kuài) - "speed up"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **jiā** as "yeah!" with enthusiasm — you're adding energy or excitement!
