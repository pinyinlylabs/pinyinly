**🗣️ Pronunciation of 女 (nǚ)**

- **Pinyin:** nǚ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **ǚ** sounds like **"ü"** (like German "ü" or "ee" with rounded lips), with third tone → dip down
  and rise up
- **nǚ** sounds like **"nyü"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it thoughtfully, like you're considering: **"nǚ..."** — that contemplative dip-and-rise is the
**third tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 女 (nǚ) - "woman, female"
- 女人 (nǚ rén) - "woman"
- 女孩 (nǚ hái) - "girl"
- 女儿 (nǚ ér) - "daughter"
- 女朋友 (nǚ péng yǒu) - "girlfriend"
- 女生 (nǚ shēng) - "female student"

**💡 Special Notes:**

女 is a fundamental character meaning "woman" or "female." It's also an important radical that
appears in many characters related to women, family, or feminine concepts. The character originally
depicted a kneeling female figure.

**📍 As a Radical:**

When 女 appears as a radical (女部), it often indicates characters related to women, family
relationships, or traditionally feminine roles: 妈 (mother), 她 (she), 妹 (younger sister).
