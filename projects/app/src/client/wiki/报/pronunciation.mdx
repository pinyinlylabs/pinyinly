**🗣️ Pronunciation of 报 (bào)**

- **Pinyin:** bào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bat"
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **bào** sounds like **"bow!"** (as in taking a bow) with a sharp downward fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're announcing important news: **"bào!"** — start high and drop down decisively.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 报 (bào) - "to report; newspaper"
- 报告 (bào gào) - "report; to report"
- 报名 (bào míng) - "to sign up; to register"
- 报纸 (bào zhǐ) - "newspaper"
- 报道 (bào dào) - "to report (news)"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a news anchor **boldly** announcing breaking news — that's the decisive, falling tone of
**bào**.
