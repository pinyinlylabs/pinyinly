**🗣️ Pronunciation of 巧 (qiǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** qiǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheap" but with more aspiration (puff of air)
- **iǎo** sounds like **"ee-ow"** with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **qiǎo** sounds like **"chee-ow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're discovering something clever: **"qiǎo..."** — that's the thoughtful tone of
finding a coincidence!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 巧 (qiǎo) - "coincidence; skillful"
- 巧克力 (qiǎo kè lì) - "chocolate"
- 技巧 (jì qiǎo) - "skill; technique"
- 巧妙 (qiǎo miào) - "ingenious; clever"
- 恰巧 (qià qiǎo) - "by chance; coincidentally"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **巧** as a skillful discovery — that **third tone** dip and rise shows the surprise of a
happy coincidence!
