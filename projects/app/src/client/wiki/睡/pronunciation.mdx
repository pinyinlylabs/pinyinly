**🗣️ Pronunciation of 睡 (shuì)**

- **Pinyin:** shuì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **uì** sounds like **"way"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **shuì** sounds like **"shway!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a sleepy command: **"shuì!"** — that's the decisive tone pattern of **shuì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 睡 (shuì) - "sleep"
- 睡觉 (shuì jiào) - "sleep; go to bed"
- 睡着 (shuì zháo) - "fall asleep"
- 午睡 (wǔ shuì) - "nap; siesta"
- 睡眠 (shuì mián) - "sleep" (formal)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"shway!"** with a sharp drop — when it's time to **sleep**, you decisively drop into bed!
