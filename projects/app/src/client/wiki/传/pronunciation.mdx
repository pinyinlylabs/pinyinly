**🗣️ Pronunciation of 传 (chuán)**

- **Pinyin:** chuán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair" (but with tongue curled back)
- **uán** sounds like **"wan"** in "wander", but with second tone → rising upward
- **chuán** sounds like **"chwan"** with a rising tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking if something should be passed along: **"chuán?"** — that upward rise is
the second tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 传 (chuán) - "pass; transmit"
- 传说 (chuán shuō) - "legend; tradition"
- 传播 (chuán bō) - "spread; broadcast"
- 传统 (chuán tǒng) - "tradition"
- 宣传 (xuān chuán) - "publicity; propaganda"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"chwan"** with a questioning rise — like asking if you should pass something on!
