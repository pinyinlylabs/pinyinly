**🗣️ Pronunciation of 红 (hóng)**

- **Pinyin:** hóng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hot"
- **óng** sounds like **"ong"** in "song", but with second tone → rising up
- **hóng** sounds like **"hong?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're surprised by something red: **"hóng?"** — that's the rising pattern of **hóng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 红 (hóng) - "red"
- 红色 (hóng sè) - "red color"
- 红茶 (hóng chá) - "black tea" (lit. "red tea")
- 红酒 (hóng jiǔ) - "red wine"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of seeing something bright **red** and asking in surprise: **"hóng?"** — the rising tone
matches your surprised reaction!
