**🗣️ Pronunciation of 胖 (pàng)**

- **Pinyin:** pàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pat" (with a puff of air)
- **àng** sounds like **"ahng"** with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **pàng** sounds like **"pahng!"** with a firm falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're stating a fact clearly: **"pàng!"** — that's the direct tone pattern of **pàng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 胖 (pàng) - "fat; plump"
- 胖子 (pàng zi) - "fat person"
- 发胖 (fā pàng) - "gain weight; get fat"
- 肥胖 (féi pàng) - "obese; obesity"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

胖 describes being fat or plump, and the solid fourth tone reflects the substantial, round nature
associated with being plump!
