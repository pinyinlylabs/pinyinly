**🗣️ Pronunciation of 雨 (yǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** yǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "boot", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **yǔ** sounds like **"you"** with a dip-then-rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 雨 (yǔ) - "rain"
- 下雨 (xià yǔ) - "to rain" (literally "fall rain")
- 雨水 (yǔ shuǐ) - "rainwater"
- 雨天 (yǔ tiān) - "rainy day"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **third tone** sounds thoughtful, like when you look up and wonder: "Is it going to rain?" —
**yǔ**
