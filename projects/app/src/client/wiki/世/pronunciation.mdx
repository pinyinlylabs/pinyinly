**🗣️ Pronunciation of 世 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **shì** sounds like **"shee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a firm statement: **"shì!"** — that's the tone pattern of **shì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 世 (shì) - "generation; world; era"
- 世界 (shì jiè) - "world"
- 世纪 (shì jì) - "century"
- 世代 (shì dài) - "generation"
- 去世 (qù shì) - "to pass away"

**💡 Special Notes:**

世 refers to generations, eras, or the world in general. It's a fundamental character for discussing
time periods, family lineages, and global concepts. The character appears in many important compound
words related to time, society, and existence.
