**🗣️ Pronunciation of 到 (dào)**

- **Pinyin:** dào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dow"
- **ào** sounds like **"ow"** in "now", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **dào** sounds like **"dow!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're announcing arrival with certainty: **"dào!"** — that's the tone pattern of
**dào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 到 (dào) - "arrive; reach"
- 到处 (dào chù) - "everywhere"
- 到底 (dào dǐ) - "after all; in the end"
- 到达 (dào dá) - "arrive; reach"
- 做到 (zuò dào) - "achieve; accomplish"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

到 means "arrive" — imagine the decisive **falling** tone as the moment you finally **reach** your
destination!
