**🗣️ Pronunciation of 卜 (bǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** bǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "book"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **bǔ** sounds like **"boo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone: Say it like you're thinking or being
thoughtful: **"bǔ..."** — that's the tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 卜 (bǔ) - "divine; predict"
- 占卜 (zhàn bǔ) - "divination"
- 萝卜 (luó bo) - "radish" (note: neutral tone in compound)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

卜 means "divine" — fortune tellers often use a **thoughtful, contemplative** tone when making
predictions!
