**🗣️ Pronunciation of 我 (wǒ)**

- **Pinyin:** wǒ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ǒ** sounds like **"oh"** but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **wǒ** sounds like **"whoa"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're pointing to yourself thoughtfully: **"wǒ..."** — that's the contemplative tone
pattern of **wǒ**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 我 (wǒ) - "I; me"
- 我们 (wǒ men) - "we; us"
- 我的 (wǒ de) - "my; mine"
- 我是 (wǒ shì) - "I am"
- 我叫 (wǒ jiào) - "my name is"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**I** — the dipping third tone is like pointing to yourself with emphasis!
