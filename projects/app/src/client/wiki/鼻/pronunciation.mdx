**🗣️ Pronunciation of 鼻 (bí)**

- **Pinyin:** bí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bee"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with second tone → rising up
- **bí** sounds like **"bee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and go up, like asking a question: **"bí?"** — that's the tone pattern of **bí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 鼻 (bí) - "nose"
- 鼻子 (bí zi) - "nose"
- 鼻音 (bí yīn) - "nasal sound"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Imagine pointing to your nose and asking **"bí?"** with that rising intonation — like you're
confirming it's your nose!
