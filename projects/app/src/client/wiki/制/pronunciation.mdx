**🗣️ Pronunciation of 制 (zhì)**

- **Pinyin:** zhì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "jeer" (retroflex sound)
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **zhì** sounds like **"jee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're taking firm control: **"zhì!"** — that's the tone pattern of **zhì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 制 (zhì) - "control; make"
- 制作 (zhì zuò) - "make; produce"
- 制定 (zhì dìng) - "formulate; establish"
- 制度 (zhì dù) - "system; institution"
- 制造 (zhì zào) - "manufacture; create"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

制 means "control" — imagine the authoritative **falling** tone as taking firm control over
something!
