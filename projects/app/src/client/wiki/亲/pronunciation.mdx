**🗣️ Pronunciation of 亲 (qīn)**

- **Pinyin:** qīn
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheap" (but with more air)
- **īn** sounds like **"een"** in "seen", but with first tone → steady and high
- **qīn** sounds like **"cheen"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 亲 (qīn) - "dear", "close", "親"
- 亲人 (qīn rén) - "relative", "family member"
- 亲切 (qīn qiè) - "kind", "cordial"
- 亲自 (qīn zì) - "personally", "in person"
- 父亲 (fù qīn) - "father"
- 母亲 (mǔ qīn) - "mother"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

亲 means **dear** or **close** - say **"cheen"** with warmth and steadiness (first tone), like
addressing someone dear to you!
