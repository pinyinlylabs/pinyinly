**🗣️ Pronunciation of 坚 (jiān)**

- **Pinyin:** jiān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Jeean"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but more forward tongue position)
- **iān** sounds like **"yen"** but held steady and high
- **jiān** sounds like **"jee-ahn"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like holding a steady, high musical note: **"jiān"** — maintain that high pitch throughout.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 坚 (jiān) - "firm"
- 坚决 (jiān jué) - "resolute"
- 坚强 (jiān qiáng) - "strong"
- 坚持 (jiān chí) - "persist"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **坚** like being "firm as steel" — the high, steady tone reflects unwavering strength and
determination!
