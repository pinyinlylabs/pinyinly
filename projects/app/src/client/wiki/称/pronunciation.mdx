**🗣️ Pronunciation of 称 (chēng)**

- **Pinyin:** chēng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church"
- **ēng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung", but with first tone → steady and high
- **chēng** sounds like **"chung"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're stating something clearly: **"chēng"** — hold that high, steady pitch like
**"chēnggg"**.

**📝 Note on Pronunciation:**

称 has multiple pronunciations:

- **称 (chēng)** - "to call; to say; to be called" (first tone)
- **称 (chèn)** - "to weigh; scales" (fourth tone)

**📝 Common Examples with chēng:**

- 称 (chēng) - "to call; to say"
- 称为 (chēng wéi) - "to be called; to be known as"
- 名称 (míng chēng) - "name; title"
- 称呼 (chēng hu) - "to address; form of address"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

For the "to call/say" meaning, the steady first tone sounds like you're **clearly stating** or
**calling out** something with confidence.
