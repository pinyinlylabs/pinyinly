**🗣️ Pronunciation of 养 (yǎng)**

- **Pinyin:** yǎng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ǎng** sounds like **"ahng"** but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up
- **yǎng** sounds like **"yahng"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're nurturing or caring for something: **"yǎng..."** — that gentle, thoughtful tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 养 (yǎng) - "to raise; to keep"
- 养成 (yǎng chéng) - "to develop (a habit)"
- 营养 (yíng yǎng) - "nutrition"
- 养家 (yǎng jiā) - "to support a family"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of raising a child — your voice goes down then up, like the ups and downs of parenting!
