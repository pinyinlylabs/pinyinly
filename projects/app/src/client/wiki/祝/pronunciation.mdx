**🗣️ Pronunciation of 祝 (zhù)**

- **Pinyin:** zhù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: "Bless!"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "jump" (but with tongue curled back)
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **zhù** sounds like **"joo!"** with a sharp falling blessing

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a heartfelt blessing: **"zhù!"** — that's the tone pattern of **zhù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 祝 (zhù) - "wish; to bless"
- 祝贺 (zhù hè) - "to congratulate"
- 祝福 (zhù fú) - "blessing; to wish well"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you give someone a **blessing** or **wish**, you speak with sincerity and conviction — that
definitive fourth tone captures the earnestness of **zhù**!
