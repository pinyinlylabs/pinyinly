**🗣️ Pronunciation of 安 (ān)**

- **Pinyin:** ān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ā** sounds like **"ah"** in "father" but held steady and high
- **n** like **"n"** in "noon"
- **ān** sounds like **"ahn"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like a calm, steady sigh of relief: **"ān..."** — that peaceful, steady tone matches the
meaning of **peaceful**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 安 (ān) - "peaceful; safe; calm"
- 安全 (ān quán) - "safe; secure"
- 安静 (ān jìng) - "quiet; peaceful"
- 平安 (píng ān) - "safe and sound"
- 安排 (ān pái) - "to arrange"
- 安装 (ān zhuāng) - "to install"

**📍 Cultural Note:**

安 is a very important concept in Chinese culture, representing peace, safety, and tranquility. It's
often used in blessings and well-wishes.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Peaceful** — the steady, flat first tone is like the calm feeling of being **safe and peaceful**!
