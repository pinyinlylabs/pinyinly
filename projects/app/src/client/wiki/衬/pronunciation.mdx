**🗣️ Pronunciation of 衬 (chèn)**

- **Pinyin:** chèn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "church"
- **èn** sounds like **"en"** in "pen" but with sharp falling tone
- **chèn** sounds like **"chen!"** with a sharp drop in pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 衬 (chèn) - "inner fabric layers; lining"
- 衬衫 (chèn shān) - "shirt; dress shirt"
- 衬衣 (chèn yī) - "shirt; underwear"
- 陪衬 (péi chèn) - "serve as a foil; set off"
- 反衬 (fǎn chèn) - "contrast; set off by contrast"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sharp, decisive fourth tone matches how you firmly put on an undershirt or lining - it's a
definitive action underneath your outer clothes!
