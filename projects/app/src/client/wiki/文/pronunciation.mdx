**🗣️ Pronunciation of 文 (wén)**

- **Pinyin:** wén
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **én** sounds like **"un"** in "fun", but with a rising tone
- **wén** sounds like **"wun?"** with a questioning rise

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 文 (wén) - "script, writing, culture"
- 文字 (wén zì) - "written characters"
- 文化 (wén huà) - "culture"
- 文学 (wén xué) - "literature"
- 文件 (wén jiàn) - "document"
- 中文 (zhōng wén) - "Chinese language"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The rising second tone suggests the upward progression of learning and **culture** that comes
through **writing** and **script**.
