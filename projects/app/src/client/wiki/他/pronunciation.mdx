**🗣️ Pronunciation of 他 (tā)**

- **Pinyin:** tā
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **ā** sounds like **"ah"** in "father" but held steady and high
- **tā** sounds like **"tah"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ā) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're stating a fact clearly: **"tā"** — that's the steady, high tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 他们 (tā men) - "they; them"
- 他的 (tā de) - "his"
- 他是 (tā shì) - "he is"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

他 is the most common pronoun for "he" — steady and straightforward, just like its flat first tone!
