**🗣️ Pronunciation of 啤 (pí)**

- **Pinyin:** pí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pea" (unaspirated, softer than English "p")
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with rising tone
- **pí** sounds like **"pee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about a drink: **"pí?"** — that rising intonation fits perfectly.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 啤 (pí) - appears mainly in compound words
- 啤酒 (pí jiǔ) - "beer"

**🔟 Special Note:**

啤 is **rarely used alone** in modern Chinese. It's almost exclusively found in **啤酒 (beer)**
where it represents the first syllable of the borrowed word "beer."

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The **mouth (口)** plus **卑** — when you taste **beer**, you might make that rising "pí?" sound of
pleasant surprise!
