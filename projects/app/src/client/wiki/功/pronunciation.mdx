**🗣️ Pronunciation of 功 (gōng)**

- **Pinyin:** gōng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Gooong"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ōng** sounds like **"ong"** in "long" but held steady and high
- **gōng** sounds like **"gong"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like holding a steady, high musical note: **"gōng"** — maintain that high pitch throughout.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 功 (gōng) - "achievement"
- 功夫 (gōng fu) - "kung fu, skill"
- 功能 (gōng néng) - "function"
- 功课 (gōng kè) - "homework"
- 成功 (chéng gōng) - "success"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **gōng** like a "gong" — the sound rings high and steady, just like the first tone!
