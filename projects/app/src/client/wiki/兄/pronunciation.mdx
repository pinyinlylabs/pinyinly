**🗣️ Pronunciation of 兄 (xiōng)**

- **Pinyin:** xiōng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like a soft **"sh"** sound, but your tongue is much closer to your teeth
- **iōng** sounds like **"yohng"** held steady and high
- **xiōng** sounds like **"shyohng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like a steady note: **"xiōng—"** — that's the tone pattern of **xiōng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 兄 (xiōng) - "elder brother"
- 兄弟 (xiōng dì) - "brothers"
- 师兄 (shī xiōng) - "senior fellow student"
- 大兄 (dà xiōng) - "big brother"
