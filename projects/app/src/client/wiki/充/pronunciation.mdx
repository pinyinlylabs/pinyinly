**🗣️ Pronunciation of 充 (chōng)**

- **Pinyin:** chōng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like the **"ch"** in "chat", but with your **tongue curled back** more, and a stronger puff
  of air
- **ōng** sounds like **"ohng"** held steady and high
- **chōng** sounds like **"chohng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like a steady note: **"chōng—"** — that's the tone pattern of **chōng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 充 (chōng) - "fill"
- 充满 (chōng mǎn) - "full of"
- 充电 (chōng diàn) - "charge (battery)"
- 补充 (bǔ chōng) - "supplement"
