**🗣️ Pronunciation of 赢 (yíng)**

- **Pinyin:** yíng
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Ying?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **íng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing" but with a rising tone
- **yíng** sounds like **"ying?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Second tone: ˊ)**

The **second tone** is **rising**, like the end of a question:

Say it like you're asking "we won?": **"yíng?"** — that's the rising tone pattern of **yíng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 赢 (yíng) - "win; gain"
- 赢得 (yíng dé) - "win; gain; earn"
- 输赢 (shū yíng) - "win or lose"
- 双赢 (shuāng yíng) - "win-win"
