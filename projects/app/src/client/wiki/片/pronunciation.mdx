**🗣️ Pronunciation of 片 (piàn)**

- **Pinyin:** piàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "piece" but with a slight puff of air
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **piàn** sounds like **"pyen!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're cutting something into slices: **"piàn!"** — that sharp, decisive drop is the
**fourth tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 片 (piàn) - "slice; piece"
- 片子 (piàn zi) - "slice; film"
- 照片 (zhào piàn) - "photograph"
- 电影片 (diàn yǐng piàn) - "movie film"
- 药片 (yào piàn) - "tablet; pill"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **片** as sharply cutting a slice: **"piàn!"** — that sharp downward motion matches the
**fourth tone**!
