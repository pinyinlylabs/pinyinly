Precipitation in the form of ice crystals falling from clouds.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                  |
| -------------- | --------------------- |
| Pinyin         | xià xuě               |
| Core meaning   | to snow; it's snowing |
| Part of speech | verb (weather)        |
| Tone           | fourth + third tone   |

## Usage Examples

- **今天下雪** (jīntiān xià xuě) - "it's snowing today"
- **要下雪了** (yào xià xuě le) - "it's going to snow"
- **下大雪** (xià dà xuě) - "heavy snowfall"
- **下雪天** (xià xuě tiān) - "snowy day"

## Weather Pattern with 下雨

下雪 parallels 下雨 in structure:

- **下雨** (xià yǔ) - to rain (liquid precipitation)
- **下雪** (xià xuě) - to snow (frozen precipitation)
- Both use 下 to show precipitation falling downward

## Grammar Patterns

下雪 functions as an **intransitive verb**:

- **正在 + 下雪**: currently snowing
- **开始 + 下雪**: starting to snow
- **不 + 下雪**: not snowing
- **下雪 + 了**: it snowed / it's snowing now

## Related Winter Words

- **雪花** (xuě huā) - snowflakes
- **大雪** (dà xuě) - heavy snow
- **小雪** (xiǎo xuě) - light snow
- **雪人** (xuě rén) - snowman

## Cultural Context

下雪 holds special meaning in Chinese culture:

- **Seasonal beauty**: Snow represents winter's quiet elegance
- **Purity symbol**: White snow symbolizes cleanliness and new beginnings
- **Poetry inspiration**: Snow scenes frequent in classical Chinese literature
- **Regional significance**: More meaningful in northern China where snow is common
