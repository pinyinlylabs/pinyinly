**🗣️ Pronunciation of 升 (shēng)**

- **Pinyin:** shēng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **ēng** sounds like **"uhng"** but held steady and high
- **shēng** sounds like **"shuhng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're steadily announcing: **"shēng!"** — that level, sustained tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 升 (shēng) - "rise; liter"
- 上升 (shàng shēng) - "rise up"
- 升级 (shēng jí) - "upgrade"
- 一升 (yī shēng) - "one liter"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

升 means "rise" — the steady, high first tone is like something steadily rising to a high level!
