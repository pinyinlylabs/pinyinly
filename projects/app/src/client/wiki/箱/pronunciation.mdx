**🗣️ Pronunciation of 箱 (xiāng)**

- **Pinyin:** xiāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Sheang"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **iāng** sounds like **"yang"** but with first tone → high and steady
- **xiāng** sounds like **"shyang"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady note: **"xiāng..."** — that's the tone pattern of **xiāng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 箱 (xiāng) - "box; case"
- 箱子 (xiāng zi) - "box; case"
- 邮箱 (yóu xiāng) - "mailbox"
- 行李箱 (xíng li xiāng) - "suitcase"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **xiāng** as the sound of opening a steady, reliable box — that high, flat first tone is
like the satisfying "click" of a well-made container!
