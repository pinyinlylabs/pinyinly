**🗣️ Pronunciation of 团 (tuán)**

- **Pinyin:** tuán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Group?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top"
- **uán** sounds like **"wahn"** but with a rising tone
- **tuán** sounds like **"twahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about a group: **"tuán?"** — that's the tone pattern of **tuán**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 团 (tuán) - "group; team; organization"
- 团体 (tuán tǐ) - "group; organization"
- 团队 (tuán duì) - "team"
- 集团 (jí tuán) - "group; corporation"
- 团结 (tuán jié) - "unity; solidarity"

**💡 Special Notes:**

团 represents a group, team, or organization of people working together. It emphasizes collective
action and unity. The character is commonly used in organizational contexts, from small teams to
large corporations, and is essential for describing social and professional groupings in Chinese
society.
