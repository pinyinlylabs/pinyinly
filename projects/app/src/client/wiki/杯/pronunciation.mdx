**🗣️ Pronunciation of 杯 (bēi)**

- **Pinyin:** bēi
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bay"
- **ēi** sounds like **"ay"** in "bay", but with first tone → stay high and flat
- **bēi** sounds like **"bay"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 杯 (bēi) - "cup; glass"
- 杯子 (bēi zi) - "cup"
- 干杯 (gān bēi) - "cheers!"
- 一杯茶 (yī bēi chá) - "a cup of tea"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

A **cup** sits steadily on a table — that's the steady first tone of **bēi**!
