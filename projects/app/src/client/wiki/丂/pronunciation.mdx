**🗣️ Pronunciation of 丂 (kǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** kǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **kǎo** sounds like **"cow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"kǎo..."** — that's the tone pattern of **kǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 丂 (kǎo) - "axe handle" (archaic/rare character)

**💡 Special Notes:**

丂 is an archaic character rarely used in modern Chinese. It historically referred to an axe handle
or the curved part of a tool handle. This character appears mainly in classical texts and is not
commonly encountered in everyday usage.
