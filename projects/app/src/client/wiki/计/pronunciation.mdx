**🗣️ Pronunciation of 计 (jì)**

- **Pinyin:** jì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" but softer
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **jì** sounds like **"jee"** with a decisive drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a calculation: **"jì!"** — that decisive drop shows the precision of
counting.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 计 (jì) - "calculate; count"
- 计划 (jì huà) - "plan"
- 计算 (jì suàn) - "calculate"
- 设计 (shè jì) - "design"
- 预计 (yù jì) - "estimate; predict"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 计 as "calculating" — when you announce the final result of a calculation, you state it
with decisive authority, just like the sharp falling fourth tone!
