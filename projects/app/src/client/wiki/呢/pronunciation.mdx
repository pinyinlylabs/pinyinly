**🗣️ Pronunciation of 呢 (ne)**

- **Pinyin:** ne
- **Tone: Neutral tone** — **light and short**, unstressed

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **n** like **"n"** in "no"
- **e** sounds like **"uh"** in "about", but very light and quick
- **ne** sounds like **"nuh"** said quickly and lightly

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **neutral tone** (no mark) is **light and unstressed**:

Say it quickly and lightly, like **"nuh"** — don't emphasize it, just let it flow naturally after
the main word.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 呢 (ne) - question particle (used at end of sentences)
- 你好呢 (nǐ hǎo ne) - "How about you?"
- 他呢？(tā ne) - "What about him?"
- 这个呢？(zhè ge ne) - "What about this one?"
- 你在干什么呢？(nǐ zài gàn shén me ne) - "What are you doing?"

**💡 Usage Note:**

呢 (ne) is a **sentence-final particle** that:

- Makes questions softer and more casual
- Implies "what about..." or "how about..."
- Should always be pronounced with neutral tone
