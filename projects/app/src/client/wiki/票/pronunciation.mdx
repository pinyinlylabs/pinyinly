**🗣️ Pronunciation of 票 (piào)**

- **Pinyin:** piào
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: "Here!"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **p** like **"p"** in "pot"
- **iào** sounds like **"ee-ow"** but with fourth tone → sharp drop on the "ow"
- **piào** sounds like **"pee-ow!"** with a sharp falling end

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're handing over something important: **"piào!"** — that's the tone pattern of
**piào**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 票 (piào) - "ticket"
- 票价 (piào jià) - "ticket price"
- 机票 (jī piào) - "airplane ticket"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you hand someone a **ticket**, you often speak decisively — that definitive fourth tone matches
the importance and finality of presenting a **piào**!
