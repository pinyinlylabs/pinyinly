**🗣️ Pronunciation of 等 (děng)**

- **Pinyin:** děng
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **ěng** sounds like **"ung"** in "lung", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **děng** sounds like **"dung"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"děng..."** — that's the tone pattern of **děng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 等 (děng) - "wait; and so on"
- 等等 (děng děng) - "wait a moment"
- 等到 (děng dào) - "wait until"
- 等待 (děng dài) - "wait for"
- 等于 (děng yú) - "equal to"
- 我等 (wǒ děng) - "I wait"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"dung"** with the thoughtful dip-rise tone — like pondering while waiting for something!
