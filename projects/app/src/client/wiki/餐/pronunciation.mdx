**🗣️ Pronunciation of 餐 (cān)**

- **Pinyin:** cān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats" (a sharp "ts" sound)
- **ān** sounds like **"ahn"** but held at a high, steady pitch
- **cān** sounds like **"tsahn"** at a constant high tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**. Keep your voice at the same high pitch throughout the
sound, like humming a single musical note.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 餐 (cān) - "meal"
- 餐厅 (cān tīng) - "restaurant"
- 中餐 (zhōng cān) - "Chinese food"
- 西餐 (xī cān) - "Western food"
- 快餐 (kuài cān) - "fast food"
- 晚餐 (wǎn cān) - "dinner"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the steady, regular rhythm of eating a meal - that consistent pace matches the flat first
tone of **cān**.
