**🗣️ Pronunciation of 麦 (mài)**

- **Pinyin:** mài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "my"
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **mài** sounds like **"my"** with a sharp drop in pitch, similar to "my!" said emphatically

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're proudly declaring ownership of your wheat field: **"mài!"** — that emphatic,
falling tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 麦 (mài) - "wheat"
- 小麦 (xiǎo mài) - "wheat"
- 燕麦 (yàn mài) - "oats"
- 麦子 (mài zi) - "wheat grain"
- 麦田 (mài tián) - "wheat field"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

麦 sounds like "my!" with a falling tone — imagine a farmer proudly saying **"mài!"** (my wheat!)
when showing off their golden wheat field.
