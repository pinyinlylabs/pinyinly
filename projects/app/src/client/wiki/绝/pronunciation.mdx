**🗣️ Pronunciation of 绝 (jué)**

- **Pinyin:** jué
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but slightly softer)
- **ué** sounds like **"weh"** with a rising tone
- **jué** sounds like **"jweh?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and go up, like expressing surprise: **"jué?!"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 绝对 (jué duì) - "absolutely; definitely"
- 绝不 (jué bù) - "absolutely not"
- 拒绝 (jù jué) - "to refuse; to reject"
- 绝望 (jué wàng) - "despair; hopeless"
- 决绝 (jué jué) - "resolute; decisive"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **cutting** something completely — the sharp, decisive action matches the rising tone's
energy!
