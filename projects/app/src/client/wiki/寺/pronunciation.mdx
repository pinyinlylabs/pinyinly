**🗣️ Pronunciation of 寺 (sì)**

- **Pinyin:** sì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **s** like **"s"** in "see"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **sì** sounds like **"see!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm command: **"sì!"** — that's the tone pattern of **sì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 寺 (sì) - "temple"
- 寺庙 (sì miào) - "temple"
- 佛寺 (fó sì) - "Buddhist temple"
- 古寺 (gǔ sì) - "ancient temple"
- 名寺 (míng sì) - "famous temple"
