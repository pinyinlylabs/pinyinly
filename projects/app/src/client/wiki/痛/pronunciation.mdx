**🗣️ Pronunciation of 痛 (tòng)**

- **Pinyin:** tòng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Down!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "top" but **stronger and more aspirated** — with a clear puff of air
- **òng** sounds like **"ong"** in "song", but with a sharp falling tone
- **tòng** sounds like **"tong!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're crying out in pain: **"tòng!"** — that sharp, falling cry of distress.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 痛 (tòng) - "pain; ache; hurt"
- 痛苦 (tòng kǔ) - "painful; suffering"
- 疼痛 (téng tòng) - "pain; ache"
- 头痛 (tóu tòng) - "headache"
- 心痛 (xīn tòng) - "heartache"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

痛 means "pain" — the sharp falling fourth tone sounds like someone crying out sharply when they're
hurt: **"Ow!"**
