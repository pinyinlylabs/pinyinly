**🗣️ Pronunciation of 完 (wán)**

- **Pinyin:** wán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "wan"
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** but with second tone → rising upward
- **wán** sounds like **"wahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking "finished?" with anticipation: **"wán?"** — that's the rising tone pattern
of **wán**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 完 (wán) - "to finish; complete"
- 完成 (wán chéng) - "to complete; accomplish"
- 完全 (wán quán) - "completely; entirely"
- 完整 (wán zhěng) - "complete; intact"
- 完美 (wán měi) - "perfect; flawless"
- 完善 (wán shàn) - "to perfect; improve"

**📍 Usage Note:**

完 often appears in compound words related to completion and perfection. It can function as both a
verb ("to finish") and as part of adverbs ("completely").

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Finish** — the rising second tone is like the satisfaction rising when you **complete** something!
