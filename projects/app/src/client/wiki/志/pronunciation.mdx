**🗣️ Pronunciation of 志 (zhì)**

- **Pinyin:** zhì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Jir!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **ì** sounds like **"ir"** in "sir" but shorter and with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **zhì** sounds like **"jir!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're declaring your determination: **"zhì!"** — that's the resolute tone pattern of
**zhì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 志 (zhì) - "will; purpose; ambition"
- 志愿 (zhì yuàn) - "volunteer; aspiration"
- 志愿者 (zhì yuàn zhě) - "volunteer"
- 杂志 (zá zhì) - "magazine"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"jir!"** with a sharp fall — when declaring your **purpose** or **ambition**, you often
speak with firm determination, just like the fourth tone's pattern!
