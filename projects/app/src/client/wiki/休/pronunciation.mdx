**🗣️ Pronunciation of 休 (xiū)**

- **Pinyin:** xiū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep" (but softer)
- **iū** sounds like **"yo"** in "yo-yo", but with first tone → high and steady
- **xiū** sounds like **"sh-yo"** held high and flat

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like singing a high steady note: **"xiū~~"** — that high, level pitch is the first tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 休 (xiū) - "rest; cease"
- 休息 (xiū xi) - "rest; take a break"
- 休假 (xiū jià) - "take vacation"
- 退休 (tuì xiū) - "retire"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"she-yo"** said calmly and steadily — like settling down for a peaceful rest!
