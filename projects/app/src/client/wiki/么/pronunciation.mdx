**🗣️ Pronunciation of 么 (me)**

- **Pinyin:** me
- **Tone: Fifth tone (neutral)** — **light and unstressed**, like the "uh" at the end of English
  words

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "me"
- **e** sounds like **"uh"** in "the" (unstressed)
- **me** sounds like a light **"muh"** with no particular tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **neutral tone** (marked with no tone mark) is **light and quick**:

Say it casually and unstressed — like the ending sound in **"What-uh?"** when you're questioning
something.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 什么 (shén me) - "what"
- 怎么 (zěn me) - "how"
- 这么 (zhè me) - "so, this way"
- 那么 (nà me) - "so, that way"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

么 is almost always used as a question particle — think of it as the **"huh?"** at the end of
questions!
