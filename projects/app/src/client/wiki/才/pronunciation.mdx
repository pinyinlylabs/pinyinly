**🗣️ Pronunciation of 才 (cái)**

- **Pinyin:** cái
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Cái?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **c** like **"ts"** in "cats" (aspirated)
- **ái** sounds like **"eye"** but with rising tone
- **cái** sounds like **"tsai"** with a rising pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you just realized something: **"cái!"** (Oh, just now!) — that's the sudden realization
tone pattern of **cái**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 才 (cái) - "just now; only then; talent"
- 刚才 (gāng cái) - "just now; a moment ago"
- 人才 (rén cái) - "talent; talented person"
- 才能 (cái néng) - "ability; talent"
- 天才 (tiān cái) - "genius; talent"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Just now** — the rising second tone captures the "aha!" moment of sudden realization!
