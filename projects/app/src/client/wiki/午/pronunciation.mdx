**🗣️ Pronunciation of 午 (wǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** wǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up
- **wǔ** sounds like **"woo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're drowsily thinking about lunch: **"wǔ..."** — that contemplative, mid-day tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 午 (wǔ) - "noon"
- 中午 (zhōng wǔ) - "noon; midday"
- 上午 (shàng wǔ) - "morning"
- 下午 (xià wǔ) - "afternoon"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

午 means "noon" — the dipping third tone is like the sun reaching its peak and then starting to
descend!
