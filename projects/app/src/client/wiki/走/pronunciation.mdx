**🗣️ Pronunciation of 走 (zǒu)**

- **Pinyin:** zǒu
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"dz"** in "adze" (voiced consonant)
- **ǒu** sounds like **"oh"** in "so" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **zǒu** sounds like **"dzoh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip (Third tone: ˇ)**

The **third tone** is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're contemplating movement: **"zǒu..."** — that's the thoughtful tone pattern of
**zǒu**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 走 (zǒu) - "walk; go"
- 走路 (zǒu lù) - "walk; go on foot"
- 走开 (zǒu kāi) - "go away"
- 走过 (zǒu guò) - "walk past; go through"
