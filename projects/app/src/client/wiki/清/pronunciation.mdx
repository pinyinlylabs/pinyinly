**🗣️ Pronunciation of 清 (qīng)**

- **Pinyin:** qīng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheese" (but with more air)
- **īng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing", but with first tone → high and steady
- **qīng** sounds like **"ching"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady note: **"qīng"** — that's the tone pattern of **qīng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 清 (qīng) - "clear"
- 清楚 (qīng chu) - "clear/distinct"
- 清洁 (qīng jié) - "clean"
- 清水 (qīng shuǐ) - "clear water"
- 清晨 (qīng chén) - "early morning"
- 清香 (qīng xiāng) - "fragrant/fresh scent"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

清 (qīng) contains the water radical (氵) on the left. Think of crystal clear water — when water is
pure and transparent, it's 清 (clear)!
