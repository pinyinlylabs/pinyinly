**🗣️ Pronunciation of 实 (shí)**

- **Pinyin:** shí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "she"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with second tone → rising upward
- **shí** sounds like **"shee?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're confirming something is real: **"shí?"** — that's the rising tone pattern of
**shí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 实 (shí) - "real; actual; solid"
- 实际 (shí jì) - "actual; practical; reality"
- 实现 (shí xiàn) - "to realize; achieve"
- 实验 (shí yàn) - "experiment"
- 实习 (shí xí) - "internship; practice"
- 实力 (shí lì) - "strength; capability"
- 确实 (què shí) - "indeed; really"
- 现实 (xiàn shí) - "reality"
- 真实 (zhēn shí) - "true; real; authentic"

**📍 Usage Note:**

实 emphasizes genuineness, reality, and substance. It's the opposite of 虚 (xū, "false/empty").

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Real** — the rising second tone is like confirming "is it **real**?" with curiosity!
