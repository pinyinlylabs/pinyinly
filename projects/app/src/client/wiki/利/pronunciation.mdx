**🗣️ Pronunciation of 利 (lì)**

- **Pinyin:** lì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "lee"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **lì** sounds like **"lee!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a decisive point about profit: **"lì!"** — that's the tone pattern of
**lì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 利 (lì) - "benefit; profit"
- 利用 (lì yòng) - "utilize; make use of"
- 有利 (yǒu lì) - "favorable; beneficial"
- 胜利 (shèng lì) - "victory; triumph"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

利 means "benefit" — imagine the sharp **falling** tone as the decisive advantage you gain!
