**🗣️ Pronunciation of 育 (yù)**

- **Pinyin:** yù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp drop down
- **yù** sounds like **"yoo!"** with a decisive falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making an important declaration: **"yù!"** — that's the authoritative tone
pattern of **yù**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 育 (yù) - "have children; raise; nurture"
- 教育 (jiào yù) - "education"
- 体育 (tǐ yù) - "physical education; sports"
- 培育 (péi yù) - "cultivate; foster"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

育 means to nurture or raise children, and the strong fourth tone conveys the decisive commitment
involved in raising and educating!
