**🗣️ Pronunciation of 开 (kāi)**

- **Pinyin:** kāi
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Kaaa"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **āi** sounds like **"eye"** but with first tone → steady and high
- **kāi** sounds like **"kye"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high flat** tone:

Say it like you're giving a clear announcement: **"kāi"** — that's the steady, confident tone of
**kāi**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 开 (kāi) - "open"
- 开门 (kāi mén) - "open the door"
- 开始 (kāi shǐ) - "begin/start"
- 开车 (kāi chē) - "drive a car"
- 开学 (kāi xué) - "start school"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

开 means "open" — imagine the steady, confident first tone like confidently **opening** a door!
