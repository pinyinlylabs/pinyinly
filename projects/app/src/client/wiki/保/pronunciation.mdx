**🗣️ Pronunciation of 保 (bǎo)**

- **Pinyin:** bǎo
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "ball"
- **ǎo** sounds like **"ow"** in "cow", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **bǎo** sounds like **"bow"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being thoughtful: **"bǎo..."** — that's the tone pattern of **bǎo**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 保 (bǎo) - "to protect; to ensure; to keep"
- 保护 (bǎo hù) - "to protect"
- 保持 (bǎo chí) - "to maintain; to keep"
- 保证 (bǎo zhèng) - "to guarantee"
- 保险 (bǎo xiǎn) - "insurance"
