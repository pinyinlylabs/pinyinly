**🗣️ Pronunciation of 员 (yuán)**

- **Pinyin:** yuán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **uán** sounds like **"wan"** but with a rising tone
- **yuán** sounds like **"ywan?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking a question: **"yuán?"** — let your voice rise at the end.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 员 (yuán) - "member; staff"
- 员工 (yuán gōng) - "employee; staff"
- 会员 (huì yuán) - "member"
- 服务员 (fú wù yuán) - "waiter; service staff"
- 公务员 (gōng wù yuán) - "civil servant"
- 演员 (yǎn yuán) - "actor; performer"
