Individual; person; private; personal; a single person as distinct from a group or collective
entity.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                                         |
| -------------- | -------------------------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | gèrén                                        |
| Core meaning   | individual; personal; private; single person |
| Part of speech | noun, adjective                              |
| Tone           | fourth + second tone                         |

## Word Breakdown

个人 combines the universal measure word with the concept of humanity.

| Component | Meaning                                            |
| --------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **个**    | Individual unit; general measure word for counting |
| **人**    | Person; human being; people                        |

## Understanding

Think of 个人 as **"one individual unit of humanity"**:

- 个 emphasizes the "single" or "individual" nature
- 人 specifies that we're talking about a human being
- Together they contrast individual vs. collective identity
- Highlights personal agency, responsibility, and uniqueness
- Often opposed to group, organization, or societal perspectives

## Core Meanings & Usage

### **1. Individual Person (个体)**

- **每个人** (měi gè rén) vs **个人** (gèrén) - "each person" vs "the individual"
- **个人权利** (gèrén quánlì) - "individual rights"
- **个人选择** (gèrén xuǎnzé) - "personal choice"

### **2. Personal/Private (私人)**

- **个人信息** (gèrén xìnxī) - "personal information"
- **个人生活** (gèrén shēnghuó) - "private life"
- **个人空间** (gèrén kōngjiān) - "personal space"

### **3. Individual vs. Collective (对比)**

- **个人利益** (gèrén lìyì) - "individual interests" (vs. collective interests)
- **个人主义** (gèrén zhǔyì) - "individualism"
- **个人与社会** (gèrén yǔ shèhuì) - "individual and society"

## Common Expressions

### **Personal Attributes**

- **个人能力** (gèrén nénglì) - "individual ability; personal capability"
- **个人经验** (gèrén jīngyàn) - "personal experience"
- **个人风格** (gèrén fēnggé) - "personal style"

### **Privacy and Autonomy**

- **个人隐私** (gèrén yǐnsī) - "personal privacy"
- **个人自由** (gèrén zìyóu) - "individual freedom"
- **个人决定** (gèrén juédìng) - "personal decision"

### **Professional Context**

- **个人简历** (gèrén jiǎnlì) - "personal resume; CV"
- **个人发展** (gèrén fāzhǎn) - "personal development"
- **个人品牌** (gèrén pǐnpái) - "personal brand"

## Cultural Context

个人 reflects evolving Chinese social concepts:

### **Traditional Collectivism**

- Historically, Chinese culture emphasized group harmony over individual desires
- 个人 was often seen as secondary to family (家庭) and society (社会)
- The concept carried potential negative connotations of selfishness

### **Modern Individualism**

- Contemporary China increasingly values individual rights and choices
- 个人 now has more positive associations with autonomy and self-realization
- Balance between collective responsibility and individual expression

### **Philosophical Tension**

- **个人与集体** (gèrén yǔ jítǐ) - "individual vs. collective" remains a key cultural discussion
- Modern Chinese people navigate between personal ambitions and social obligations
- 个人 represents the modern challenge of self-actualization within social context

## Grammar Notes

个人 can function as:

- **Noun**: 作为一个个人 (zuòwéi yīgè gèrén) - "as an individual"
- **Adjective**: 个人观点 (gèrén guāndiǎn) - "personal opinion"
- **Modifier**: 个人的 (gèrén de) - "personal; individual"

## Contrast with Related Terms

| Term     | Focus                     | Usage                    |
| -------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| **个人** | Individual vs. collective | Individual rights/choice |

Understanding 个人 shows your grasp of the **individual-collective dynamic** that shapes modern
Chinese society.
