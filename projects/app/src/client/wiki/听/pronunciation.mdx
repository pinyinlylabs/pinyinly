**🗣️ Pronunciation of 听 (tīng)**

- **Pinyin:** tīng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "tea", but **unaspirated** — no strong puff of air
- **īng** sounds like **"eeng"** in "seeing" but held steady and high
- **tīng** sounds like **"teeng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're holding a steady musical note: **"tīng..."** — keep the pitch high and level.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 听 (tīng) - "hear; listen"
- 听说 (tīng shuō) - "heard; I heard that"
- 听见 (tīng jiàn) - "hear"
- 听众 (tīng zhòng) - "audience"
- 听课 (tīng kè) - "attend class"
- 听音乐 (tīng yīn yuè) - "listen to music"
