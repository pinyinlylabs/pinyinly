**🗣️ Pronunciation of 须 (xū)**

- **Pinyin:** xū
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "ship"
- **ū** sounds like **"oo"** in "zoo" but held steady and high
- **xū** sounds like **"shoo"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and steady** tone. Say **"xū"** with a firm, level tone when
expressing necessity: "You **must**!"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 须 (xū) - "must/beard"
- 必须 (bì xū) - "must/have to"
- 须要 (xū yào) - "need to"
- 胡须 (hú xū) - "beard/whiskers"
- 须知 (xū zhī) - "need to know"
- 不须 (bù xū) - "need not"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you say someone **must** do something, use a high, steady tone to show certainty — that's
**xū**!
