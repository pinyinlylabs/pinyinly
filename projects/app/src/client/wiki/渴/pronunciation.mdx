**🗣️ Pronunciation of 渴 (kě)**

- **Pinyin:** kě
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **k** like **"k"** in "key"
- **ě** sounds like **"uh"** in "duh" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **kě** sounds like **"kuh"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're expressing that you're really thirsty: **"kě..."** — that contemplative, longing
tone pattern of **kě**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 渴 (kě) - "thirsty"
- 渴望 (kě wàng) - "yearn; long for"
- 口渴 (kǒu kě) - "thirsty"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you're really **thirsty**, you naturally say "uh..." with that longing, dip-then-rise tone —
just like **kě**!
