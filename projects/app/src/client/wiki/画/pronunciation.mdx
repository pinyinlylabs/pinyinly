**🗣️ Pronunciation of 画 (huà)**

- **Pinyin:** huà
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Stop!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **uà** sounds like **"wa"** in "water", but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **huà** sounds like **"hwah!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 画 (huà) - "to draw; painting"
- 画家 (huà jiā) - "painter; artist"
- 画画 (huà huà) - "to draw pictures"
- 图画 (tú huà) - "picture; drawing"
- 画面 (huà miàn) - "picture; scene"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Fourth tone is like the sharp stroke of a **brush** - your voice **drops sharply** throughout
**huà**.
