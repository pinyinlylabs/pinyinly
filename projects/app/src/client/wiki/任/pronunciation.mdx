**🗣️ Pronunciation of 任 (rèn)**

- **Pinyin:** rèn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **r** like **"r"** in "run" (but softer, almost like "zh")
- **èn** sounds like **"un"** in "fun" but with a sharp falling tone
- **rèn** sounds like **"run!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"rèn!"** — that's the decisive, falling tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 任何 (rèn hé) - "any; whatever"
- 任务 (rèn wù) - "task; mission"
- 责任 (zé rèn) - "responsibility"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

任 means "any" — like firmly stating "**any** one will do!" with that decisive fourth tone!
