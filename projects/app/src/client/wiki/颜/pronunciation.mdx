**🗣️ Pronunciation of 颜 (yán)**

- **Pinyin:** yán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: "yan?"

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **án** sounds like **"ahn"** but with a rising tone
- **yán** sounds like **"yahn"** with an upward inflection

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone. Start low and rise up, like when you're asking
"Really?" in English.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 颜色 (yán sè) - "color"
- 颜料 (yán liào) - "paint, pigment"
- 颜面 (yán miàn) - "face, dignity"
- 红颜 (hóng yán) - "beautiful woman"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of asking "What color (颜色) is that?" - the questioning tone matches the rising second tone
of **yán**.
