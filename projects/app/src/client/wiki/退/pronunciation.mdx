**🗣️ Pronunciation of 退 (tuì)**

- **Pinyin:** tuì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **t** like **"t"** in "take"
- **uì** sounds like **"way"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **tuì** sounds like **"tway!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying **"No!"** firmly: **"tuì!"** — that's the tone pattern of
**tuì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 退 (tuì) - "to retreat; to withdraw"
- 退休 (tuì xiū) - "to retire"
- 退出 (tuì chū) - "to exit; to quit"
- 退步 (tuì bù) - "to regress"
- 退款 (tuì kuǎn) - "refund"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

**Retreating** is often a decisive action — the sharp falling tone captures that firm decision to
withdraw!
