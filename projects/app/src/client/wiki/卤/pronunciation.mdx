**🗣️ Pronunciation of 卤 (lǔ)**

- **Pinyin:** lǔ
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "look"
- **ǔ** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **lǔ** sounds like **"loo"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone: Say it like you're contemplating the taste:
**"lǔ..."** — that's the tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 卤 (lǔ) - "salt; brine"
- 卤水 (lǔ shuǐ) - "brine; salt water"
- 卤菜 (lǔ cài) - "braised dishes"
- 卤蛋 (lǔ dàn) - "braised egg"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

卤 relates to salt and braising — the **thoughtful tone** reflects the slow, contemplative process
of braising food!
