**🗣️ Pronunciation of 绿 (lǜ)**

- **Pinyin:** lǜ
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "look"
- **ǜ** sounds like **"ü"** in German (or "ue" sound), but with fourth tone → sharp falling
- **lǜ** sounds like **"lyue!"** with a sharp drop

**🔤 Special Note on ǜ:**

The **ǜ** sound is made by:

1. Round your lips like saying "oo"
2. But position your tongue like saying "ee"
3. Then add the sharp falling fourth tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like pointing out something green: **"lǜ!"** — start high and drop down.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 绿色 (lǜ sè) - "green color"
- 绿茶 (lǜ chá) - "green tea"
- 绿豆 (lǜ dòu) - "mung bean"
- 绿灯 (lǜ dēng) - "green light"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the vibrant **green** of nature — that bold, definitive color matches the sharp tone!
