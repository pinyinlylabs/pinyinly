**🗣️ Pronunciation of 但 (dàn)**

- **Pinyin:** dàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **d** like **"d"** in "dog"
- **àn** sounds like **"ahn"** (like "ah" + "n"), but with fourth tone → sharp downward drop
- **dàn** sounds like **"dahn"** with a sharp falling tone

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like making a contrast: **"dàn!"** — that sharp drop down shows the fourth tone pattern,
perfect for "but"!

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 但 (dàn) - "but; however"
- 但是 (dàn shì) - "but; however"
- 不但 (bù dàn) - "not only"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"dahn"** with a decisive falling tone — like making a strong contrast or objection!
