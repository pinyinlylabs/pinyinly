**🗣️ Pronunciation of 纪 (jì)**

- **Pinyin:** jì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **jì** sounds like **"jee!"** with a sharp fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're firmly stating a record: **"jì!"** — that's the sharp falling pattern of **jì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 纪 (jì) - "record/discipline"
- 纪录 (jì lù) - "record"
- 纪念 (jì niàn) - "commemorate"
- 世纪 (shì jì) - "century"
- 年纪 (nián jì) - "age"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When stating a **record** or important **discipline**, you speak with authority and finality — just
like the sharp, falling fourth tone of **jì**!
