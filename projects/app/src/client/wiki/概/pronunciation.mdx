**🗣️ Pronunciation of 概 (gài)**

- **Pinyin:** gài
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ài** sounds like **"eye"** with a sharp falling tone
- **gài** sounds like **"guy"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a firm statement: **"gài!"** — that's the sharp downward tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 概括 (gài kuò) - "to summarize"
- 大概 (dà gài) - "probably; approximately"
- 概念 (gài niàn) - "concept"
- 概率 (gài lǜ) - "probability"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

概 means "approximately" — like making a **sharp, definitive** estimate with that fourth tone!
