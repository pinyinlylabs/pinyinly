**🗣️ Pronunciation of 辰 (chén)**

- **Pinyin:** chén
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **ch** like **"ch"** in "chair"
- **én** sounds like **"uhn"** but with second tone → starts low and rises up
- **chén** sounds like **"chen"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking for confirmation: **"chén?"** — that's the rising tone pattern of
**chén**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 时辰 (shí chén) - "time period"
- 生辰 (shēng chén) - "birthday"
- 良辰 (liáng chén) - "auspicious time"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

辰 relates to **time** — pronounce it with a rising **chén** like asking "what time is it?"
