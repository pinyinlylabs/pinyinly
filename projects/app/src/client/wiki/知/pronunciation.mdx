**🗣️ Pronunciation of 知 (zhī)**

- **Pinyin:** zhī
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **zh** like **"j"** in "judge" but with tongue curled back
- **ī** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but held steady and high
- **zhī** sounds like **"jee"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like stating knowledge confidently: **"zhī—"** — that's the confident tone pattern of
**zhī**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 知 (zhī) - "know; be aware of"
- 知道 (zhī dào) - "know; be aware of"
- 知识 (zhī shí) - "knowledge"
- 不知道 (bù zhī dào) - "don't know"
- 知名 (zhī míng) - "well-known; famous"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **"jee"** with a steady, confident tone — when you **know** something, you state it with
certainty and confidence!
