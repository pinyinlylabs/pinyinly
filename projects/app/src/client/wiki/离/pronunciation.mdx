**🗣️ Pronunciation of 离 (lí)**

- **Pinyin:** lí
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "love"
- **í** sounds like **"ee"** in "see", but with second tone → rise up
- **lí** sounds like **"lee"** with a rising tone, like asking "Lee?"

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (´) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking: **"lí?"** — that's the questioning rise of **lí**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 离 (lí) - "to leave; from"
- 离开 (lí kāi) - "to leave; to depart"
- 离婚 (lí hūn) - "to divorce"
- 距离 (jù lí) - "distance"
- 分离 (fēn lí) - "to separate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of 离 as "leaving" — the rising tone sounds like you're asking "Are you leaving?" which fits
the meaning perfectly.
