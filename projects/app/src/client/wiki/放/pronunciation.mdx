**🗣️ Pronunciation of 放 (fàng)**

- **Pinyin:** fàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fun"
- **àng** sounds like **"ahng"**, but with a sharp falling tone
- **fàng** sounds like **"fahng!"** with a sharp drop

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 放 (fàng) - "put, place"
- 放下 (fàng xià) - "put down"
- 放学 (fàng xué) - "school is out"
- 放心 (fàng xīn) - "be at ease"
- 放假 (fàng jià) - "have a holiday"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The sharp falling fourth tone matches the decisive action of **putting** or **placing** something
down firmly.
