**🗣️ Pronunciation of 架 (jià)**

- **Pinyin:** jià
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep" (but softer, like "gee")
- **ià** sounds like **"yah"**, but with fourth tone → sharp fall
- **jià** sounds like **"jyah!"** with a sharp falling tone

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 架 (jià) - "frame; shelf; to put up"
- 书架 (shū jià) - "bookshelf"
- 衣架 (yī jià) - "clothes hanger"
- 打架 (dǎ jià) - "to fight"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Setting up a **frame** requires firm, decisive action — that's the sharp fourth tone of **jià**!
