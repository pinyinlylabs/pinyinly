**🗣️ Pronunciation of 银 (yín)**

- **Pinyin:** yín
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ín** sounds like **"een"** in "seen" but with a rising tone
- **yín** sounds like **"yeen"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Start low and rise up: **"yín?"** — like asking "Silver?" with curiosity.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 银 (yín) - "silver"
- 银行 (yín háng) - "bank"
- 银牌 (yín pái) - "silver medal"
- 银行卡 (yín háng kǎ) - "bank card"

**📝 Special Notes:**

The character 银 contains the metal radical 钅, which is appropriate since silver is a precious
metal. Notice how 银行 (bank) literally means "silver business," reflecting the historical
connection between banks and precious metals.

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you see something **silver** and shiny, you might ask "Silver?" with a rising tone of
curiosity!
