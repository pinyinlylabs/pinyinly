**🗣️ Pronunciation of 匚 (fāng)**

- **Pinyin:** fāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "far"
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** but held steady and high
- **fāng** sounds like **"fahng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like you're singing a steady high note: **"fāng~~~"** — that level, sustained tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 匚 (fāng) - "box" (radical)
- 区 (qū) - uses this radical (originally from 匚)
- 匡 (kuāng) - "correct; rectify" (contains 匚)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

匚 is a "box" radical — the steady, flat first tone is like the stable, level base of a box!

**📝 Note:**

匚 is primarily used as a radical in other characters and is rarely used independently in modern
Chinese.
