**🗣️ Pronunciation of 孩 (hái)**

- **Pinyin:** hái
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Really?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ái** sounds like **"eye"** but with a rising tone
- **hái** sounds like **"hi?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is **rising like a question**:

Start **low and rise up** — like calling to a child with affection: **"hái?"** — that's the warm,
rising second tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 孩 (hái) - "child"
- 孩子 (hái zi) - "child; kid"
- 小孩 (xiǎo hái) - "small child"
- 女孩 (nǚ hái) - "girl"
- 男孩 (nán hái) - "boy"
- 孩童 (hái tóng) - "children" (formal)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The second tone's rising pattern is like affectionately calling to a **child** — that warm,
questioning rise shows care and attention!
