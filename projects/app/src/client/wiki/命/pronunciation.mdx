**🗣️ Pronunciation of 命 (mìng)**

- **Pinyin:** mìng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Ming!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **m** like **"m"** in "man"
- **ìng** sounds like **"eeng"** in "seeing", but with a sharp falling tone
- **mìng** sounds like **"meeng!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're being decisive or giving a command: **"mìng!"** — let your voice drop sharply.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 命 (mìng) - "life; fate; destiny"
- 生命 (shēng mìng) - "life"
- 命运 (mìng yùn) - "fate; destiny"
- 救命 (jiù mìng) - "save a life; help!"
- 性命 (xìng mìng) - "life"
- 要命 (yào mìng) - "terrible; awful"
