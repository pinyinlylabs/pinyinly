**🗣️ Pronunciation of 些 (xiē)**

- **Pinyin:** xiē
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Ahhhh"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **iē** sounds like **"yeah"** but held steady and high
- **xiē** sounds like **"shyeah"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip (First tone: ˉ)**

The **first tone** is **high and flat**, like holding a steady note:

Say it like you're casually mentioning some things: **"xiē..."** — that steady, relaxed tone is the
**first tone**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 些 (xiē) - "some; a few" (measure word)
- 一些 (yī xiē) - "some; a few"
- 这些 (zhè xiē) - "these"
- 那些 (nà xiē) - "those"
- 有些 (yǒu xiē) - "some; somewhat"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **些** as casually indicating "some things" with a steady tone — that's the **first tone**
relaxed quality!

**📍 Usage Note:**

些 is primarily used as a measure word for indefinite quantities. It's almost always used with other
words like 一些, 这些, 那些 rather than alone.
