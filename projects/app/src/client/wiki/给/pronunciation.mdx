**🗣️ Pronunciation of 给 (gěi)**

- **Pinyin:** gěi
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **g** like **"g"** in "go"
- **ěi** sounds like **"ay"** in "hey", but with third tone → dip down and rise up
- **gěi** sounds like **"gay"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thinking or being considerate: **"gěi..."** — that thoughtful dip and rise.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 给你 (gěi nǐ) - "give you"
- 给我 (gěi wǒ) - "give me"
- 送给 (sòng gěi) - "to give as a gift"
- 给钱 (gěi qián) - "to give money; to pay"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you **give** something, you often pause thoughtfully before handing it over — that's the third
tone pattern!
