To give; to provide; to offer; to hand over; for; to; in favor of.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                        |
| -------------- | --------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | gěi                         |
| Core meaning   | give; provide; for; to      |
| Part of speech | verb, preposition           |
| Tone           | third tone (falling-rising) |

## Visual Breakdown

给 combines **silk + cooperation** to represent offering and providing.

| Component | Visual Description                                   |
| --------- | ---------------------------------------------------- |
| **纟**    | Silk radical (纟) - represents valuable materials    |
| **合**    | Cooperation (合) - shows joining and giving together |

## Mnemonic

Think of 给 as **offering valuable silk through cooperation**:

- The silk component (纟) represents something precious to give
- The cooperation component (合) shows the collaborative act of giving
- Like communities coming together to share valuable resources
- Shows giving as a cooperative, valuable exchange
- Combines material value with social cooperation

This creates the image: **sharing precious resources through collaborative giving**.

## Core Meaning & Usage

给 represents **giving, providing, and benefiting others**. It's used:

1. **Direct giving**: 给钱 (gěi qián) - "give money"
2. **Providing for**: 给孩子买书 (gěi háizi mǎi shū) - "buy books for children"
3. **Indirect object**: 给我看 (gěi wǒ kàn) - "show me"
4. **Beneficiary**: 为了给你 (wèile gěi nǐ) - "in order to give you"

## Examples

- **送给** (sòng gěi) - "give as a gift; present to"
- **交给** (jiāo gěi) - "hand over to; entrust to"
- **让给** (ràng gěi) - "yield to; give way to"
- **分给** (fēn gěi) - "distribute to; allocate to"
- **卖给** (mài gěi) - "sell to"
- **说给** (shuō gěi) - "tell to; say to"

## Acts of Giving

给 in generous actions:

- **给予** (jǐyǔ) - "give; grant; bestow"
- **赠给** (zèng gěi) - "present as a gift"
- **献给** (xiàn gěi) - "dedicate to; offer to"
- **捐给** (juān gěi) - "donate to"

## Providing Services

给 in service contexts:

- **给人帮助** (gěi rén bāngzhù) - "help people"
- **给人治病** (gěi rén zhì bìng) - "treat people's illnesses"
- **给人上课** (gěi rén shàng kè) - "teach people"
- **给人修理** (gěi rén xiūlǐ) - "repair for people"

## Indirect Object Usage

给 marking beneficiary:

- **给我买** (gěi wǒ mǎi) - "buy for me"
- **给他做** (gěi tā zuò) - "do for him"
- **给她说** (gěi tā shuō) - "tell her"
- **给孩子读书** (gěi háizi dú shū) - "read to children"

## Common Expressions

- **给力** (gěi lì) - "awesome; powerful" (internet slang)
- **给面子** (gěi miànzi) - "show respect; give face"
- **给机会** (gěi jīhuì) - "give an opportunity"
- **自给自足** (zì gěi zì zú) - "self-sufficient"

## Reciprocal Giving

给 in mutual exchange:

- **给来给去** (gěi lái gěi qù) - "give back and forth"
- **互相给** (hùxiāng gěi) - "give to each other"
- **给予回报** (gěiyǔ huíbào) - "give in return"
- **给人方便** (gěi rén fāngbiàn) - "make things convenient for others"

## Modern Usage

给 in contemporary contexts:

- **给赞** (gěi zàn) - "give a like" (social media)
- **给评论** (gěi pínglùn) - "give comments"
- **给打赏** (gěi dǎshǎng) - "give a tip/reward"
- **给关注** (gěi guānzhù) - "give attention/follow"

## Cultural Context

给 reflects Chinese values of:

- **礼尚往来** (lǐ shàng wǎng lái) - Reciprocity in gift-giving
- **助人为乐** (zhù rén wéi lè) - Helping others brings happiness
- **慷慨大方** (kāngkǎi dàfāng) - Generosity and magnanimity
- **社会和谐** (shèhuì héxié) - Social harmony through giving

## Philosophy of Giving

给 in Chinese thought:

- **施比受更有福** (shī bǐ shòu gèng yǒu fú) - It's more blessed to give than receive
- **赠人玫瑰，手有余香** (zèng rén méiguì, shǒu yǒu yú xiāng) - Giving roses leaves fragrance on
  your hands
- **予人方便，自己方便** (yǔ rén fāngbiàn, zìjǐ fāngbiàn) - Making things convenient for others
  benefits yourself
- **给人以鱼，不如教人以渔** (gěi rén yǐ yú, bùrú jiāo rén yǐ yú) - Give a fish vs. teach to fish

The character represents the fundamental social act of sharing resources, services, and care with
others, emphasizing the cooperative nature of human society.
