**🗣️ Pronunciation of 式 (shì)**

- **Pinyin:** shì
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shoe"
- **ì** sounds like **"ee"** in "see" but with a sharp falling tone
- **shì** sounds like **"she!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're making a formal announcement: **"shì!"** — that's the sharp, falling tone of
**shì**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 式 (shì) - "style/type/ceremony"
- 方式 (fāng shì) - "method/way"
- 形式 (xíng shì) - "form/format"
- 正式 (zhèng shì) - "formal/official"
- 仪式 (yí shì) - "ceremony"
- 公式 (gōng shì) - "formula"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

式 means "style/ceremony" — the sharp fourth tone sounds formal and official, like announcing a
**ceremonial style**!
