**🗣️ Pronunciation of 护 (hù)**

- **Pinyin:** hù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **h** like **"h"** in "hat"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book", but with fourth tone → sharp drop
- **hù** sounds like **"hoo!"** with a sharp downward fall

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"hù!"** — start high and drop down decisively.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 护 (hù) - "to protect; to guard"
- 护照 (hù zhào) - "passport"
- 保护 (bǎo hù) - "to protect"
- 护士 (hù shì) - "nurse"
- 护理 (hù lǐ) - "to nurse; nursing care"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of a guardian **firmly** saying "Halt!" to protect someone — that's the sharp, decisive tone
of **hù**.
