**🗣️ Pronunciation of 缺 (quē)**

- **Pinyin:** quē
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **qu** like **"ch"** in "choose" (but with rounded lips)
- **ē** sounds like **"eh"** but held steady and high
- **quē** sounds like **"cheh"** held high and flat

**🔤 Special Note on qu:**

The **qu** sound is made by:

1. Start with "ch" sound
2. Round your lips slightly
3. It's like "ch" + "w" blended together

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and flat**:

Hold your voice steady and high, like stating a fact: **"quē~~~"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 缺少 (quē shǎo) - "to lack; to be short of"
- 缺点 (quē diǎn) - "shortcoming; weakness"
- 缺席 (quē xí) - "to be absent"
- 缺乏 (quē fá) - "to lack; to be deficient in"
- 缺钱 (quē qián) - "to be short of money"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When something is **lacking**, you state it clearly and directly — that straightforward tone matches
the flat first tone!
