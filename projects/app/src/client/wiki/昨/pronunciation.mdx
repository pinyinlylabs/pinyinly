**🗣️ Pronunciation of 昨 (zuó)**

- **Pinyin:** zuó
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **z** like **"ds"** in "kids" — a buzzing sound
- **uó** sounds like **"wo"** in "woke" but with second tone → rising upward
- **zuó** sounds like **"dzwo?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about yesterday: **"zuó?"** — that upward rise is the second tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 昨 (zuó) - "yesterday"
- 昨天 (zuó tiān) - "yesterday"
- 昨日 (zuó rì) - "yesterday" (more formal)
