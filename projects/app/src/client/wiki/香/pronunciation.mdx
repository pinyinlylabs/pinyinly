**🗣️ Pronunciation of 香 (xiāng)**

- **Pinyin:** xiāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note held high

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like **"sh"** in "sheep"
- **iāng** sounds like **"yang"** but with first tone → steady and high
- **xiāng** sounds like **"shyang"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone: Keep your voice steady and high, like singing a
sustained note: **"xiāng"** — that's the tone pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 香 (xiāng) - "fragrant"
- 香蕉 (xiāng jiāo) - "banana"
- 香水 (xiāng shuǐ) - "perfume"
- 香味 (xiāng wèi) - "fragrance; aroma"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of the sweet, **high** scent of flowers rising up — that's the steady, high tone of 香!
