**🗣️ Pronunciation of 丰 (fēng)**

- **Pinyin:** fēng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **f** like **"f"** in "fun"
- **ēng** sounds like **"ung"** in "hung" but held steady and high
- **fēng** sounds like **"fung"** with steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — like humming a single high note: **"fēēēng"**

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 丰 (fēng) - "abundant; rich"
- 丰富 (fēng fù) - "abundant; rich"
- 风 (fēng) - "wind" (different character, same sound)
- 封 (fēng) - "to seal" (different character, same sound)

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **abundance** flowing like a steady **wind** — the high, flat tone of **fēng** is like a
constant, abundant breeze!
