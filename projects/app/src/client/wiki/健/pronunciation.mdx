**🗣️ Pronunciation of 健 (jiàn)**

- **Pinyin:** jiàn
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jeep", but with the tongue closer to the hard palate and no puff of air
- **iàn** sounds like **"yen"** but with a sharp falling tone
- **jiàn** sounds like **"jyen!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (`) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like you're giving a firm command: **"jiàn!"** — that's the tone pattern of **jiàn**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 健 (jiàn) - "healthy"
- 健康 (jiàn kāng) - "health"
- 健身 (jiàn shēn) - "fitness"
- 保健 (bǎo jiàn) - "health care"
