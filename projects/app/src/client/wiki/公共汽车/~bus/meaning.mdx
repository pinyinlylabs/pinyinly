A large motor vehicle carrying passengers by road, typically one serving the public on a fixed route
and for a fare.

## Quick Reference

| Aspect         | Info                       |
| -------------- | -------------------------- |
| Pinyin         | gōng gòng qì chē           |
| Core meaning   | public bus; city bus       |
| Part of speech | noun                       |
| Tone           | 1st + 4th + 4th + 1st tone |

## Visual Breakdown

公共汽车 combines **public + shared + steam + vehicle** to represent public transportation.

| Component | Meaning                   | Contribution to 公共汽车             |
| --------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| **公共**  | public; common; shared    | Shows this serves the general public |
| **汽车**  | automobile; motor vehicle | Represents the motorized transport   |

## Character Analysis

### 公共 (public)

公共 shows **resources shared by everyone**:

- **公** (public) represents what belongs to all
- **共** (together) shows collective sharing and use
- Together: services and facilities available to entire community

### 汽车 (automobile)

汽车 depicts **steam-powered vehicle**:

- **汽** (steam/gas) represents the power source
- **车** (vehicle) shows wheeled transportation
- Together: motorized vehicle powered by combustion engine

## Mnemonic

Think of 公共汽车 as **"a steam vehicle that everyone in the public shares"**:

- 公共 (public) shows this isn't private transportation but for everyone
- 汽车 (automobile) shows it's a motorized vehicle, not horse-drawn
- Picture a large vehicle that stops to pick up anyone who pays the fare
- Like a shared car that follows fixed routes through the city

## Usage Examples

- **坐公共汽车** (zuò gōng gòng qì chē) - "take the bus"
- **公共汽车站** (gōng gòng qì chē zhàn) - "bus stop"
- **等公共汽车** (děng gōng gòng qì chē) - "wait for the bus"
- **公共汽车票** (gōng gòng qì chē piào) - "bus ticket"
- **公共汽车司机** (gōng gòng qì chē sī jī) - "bus driver"

## Grammar Patterns

公共汽车 typically:

- **Transportation**: 乘坐公共汽车 - "ride the bus"
- **Locations**: 公共汽车路线 - "bus route"
- **Services**: 公共汽车服务 - "bus service"
- **Infrastructure**: 公共汽车系统 - "bus system"

## Cultural Context

公共汽车 reflects Chinese urban development and social values:

- **Public service**: 公共汽车 represents government commitment to public transportation
- **Environmental consciousness**: Using 公共汽车 considered more environmentally responsible
- **Economic accessibility**: 公共汽车 provides affordable transportation for working people
- **Urban planning**: 公共汽车 routes essential part of Chinese city development
- **Social equality**: 公共汽车 serves all social classes, promoting mobility for everyone
