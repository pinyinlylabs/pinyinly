**🗣️ Pronunciation of 商 (shāng)**

- **Pinyin:** shāng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Shaang"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **sh** like **"sh"** in "shop"
- **āng** sounds like **"ahng"** in "gong", held steady and high
- **shāng** sounds like **"shahng"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is **high and level**:

Say it like confidently discussing business: **"shāng"** — that steady, professional tone.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 商 (shāng) - "commerce; business; merchant"
- 商业 (shāng yè) - "business; commerce"
- 商店 (shāng diàn) - "shop; store"
- 商场 (shāng chǎng) - "shopping mall"
- 商人 (shāng rén) - "merchant; businessman"
- 商品 (shāng pǐn) - "commodity; goods"
- 商量 (shāng liang) - "to discuss; to deliberate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

The character suggests a **building structure** — like the steady, organized world of **commerce**
and **business**!
