**🗣️ Pronunciation of 京 (jīng)**

- **Pinyin:** jīng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **j** like **"j"** in "jar"
- **īng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing", but with first tone → steady and high
- **jīng** sounds like **"jing"** with a steady high pitch

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 京 (jīng) - "capital city"
- 北京 (běi jīng) - "Beijing"
- 京剧 (jīng jù) - "Peking opera"
- 京城 (jīng chéng) - "capital city"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

京 represents the **capital** - say **"jing"** with the authority and steadiness (first tone)
befitting a great capital city!
