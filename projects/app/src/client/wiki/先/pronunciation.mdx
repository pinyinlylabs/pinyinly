**🗣️ Pronunciation of 先 (xiān)**

- **Pinyin:** xiān
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **x** like a soft **"sh"** sound, but your tongue is much closer to your teeth
- **iān** sounds like **"yen"** held steady and high
- **xiān** sounds like **"shyen"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (ˉ) is a **high and flat** tone:

Say it like a steady note: **"xiān—"** — that's the tone pattern of **xiān**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 先 (xiān) - "first"
- 先生 (xiān sheng) - "Mr./sir"
- 首先 (shǒu xiān) - "first of all"
- 祖先 (zǔ xiān) - "ancestors"
