**🗣️ Pronunciation of 量 (liàng)**

- **Pinyin:** liàng
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **l** like **"l"** in "long"
- **iàng** sounds like **"yang"** but with fourth tone → sharp falling pitch
- **liàng** sounds like **"lyang!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Start high and drop quickly, like saying "Measure!" decisively: **"liàng!"** — that's the tone
pattern of **liàng**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 量 (liàng) - "quantity; amount; measure"
- 数量 (shù liàng) - "quantity; amount"
- 质量 (zhì liàng) - "quality"
- 重量 (zhòng liàng) - "weight"
- 大量 (dà liàng) - "large quantity; lots of"
- 力量 (lì liàng) - "strength; power"

**💡 Special Note:**

量 has two pronunciations:

- **量 (liàng)** - "quantity; measure" (fourth tone) - as a noun
- **量 (liáng)** - "to measure" (second tone) - as a verb

The pronunciation here focuses on the noun meaning "quantity/amount".
