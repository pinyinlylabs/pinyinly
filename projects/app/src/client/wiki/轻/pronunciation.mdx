**🗣️ Pronunciation of 轻 (qīng)**

- **Pinyin:** qīng
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady note: **"Eeee"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **q** like **"ch"** in "cheat" (aspirated)
- **īng** sounds like **"ing"** in "sing" but with first tone → steady and high
- **qīng** sounds like **"ching"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is **high and flat**:

Keep your voice **steady and high** throughout — like the gentle, consistent quality of something **light**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 轻 (qīng) - "light; gentle; soft"
- 轻松 (qīng sōng) - "relaxed; easy"
- 年轻 (nián qīng) - "young"
- 轻声 (qīng shēng) - "soft voice; whisper"
- 轻易 (qīng yì) - "easily; lightly"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

轻 means "light" — the **steady, high** tone floats like something weightless and light!
