**🗣️ Pronunciation of 园 (yuán)**

- **Pinyin:** yuán
- **Tone: Second tone** — **rising** tone, like asking a question: **"Garden?"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **uán** sounds like **"wahn"** but with a rising tone
- **yuán** sounds like **"ywahn?"** with a questioning rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **second tone** (ˊ) is a **rising** tone:

Say it like you're asking about a garden: **"yuán?"** — that's the tone pattern of **yuán**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 园 (yuán) - "garden; park; yard"
- 公园 (gōng yuán) - "park"
- 花园 (huā yuán) - "flower garden"
- 动物园 (dòng wù yuán) - "zoo"
- 幼儿园 (yòu ér yuán) - "kindergarten"

**💡 Special Notes:**

园 refers to a garden, park, or enclosed outdoor space, often cultivated or designed for specific
purposes. It appears in many compound words related to recreational spaces, educational institutions
(like kindergarten), and specialized facilities (like zoos). The character emphasizes organized,
purposeful outdoor spaces.
