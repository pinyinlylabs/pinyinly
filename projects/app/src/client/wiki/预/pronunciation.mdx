**🗣️ Pronunciation of 预 (yù)**

- **Pinyin:** yù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **y** like **"y"** in "yes"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "good" but with sharp falling tone
- **yù** sounds like **"you!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone. Say **"yù"** like you're decisively saying "Get
ready!" — that commanding tone fits the meaning "prepare."

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 预 (yù) - "prepare/advance"
- 预习 (yù xí) - "preview/prepare lessons"
- 预报 (yù bào) - "forecast"
- 预备 (yù bèi) - "prepare/get ready"
- 预订 (yù dìng) - "book/reserve"
- 预料 (yù liào) - "expect/anticipate"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

When you command someone to **prepare**, you use a firm, falling tone — that's the fourth tone of
**yù**!
