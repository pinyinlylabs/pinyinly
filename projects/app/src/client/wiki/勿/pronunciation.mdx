**🗣️ Pronunciation of 勿 (wù)**

- **Pinyin:** wù
- **Tone: Fourth tone** — sharp **falling** tone, like giving a command: **"Woo!"**

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "wood"
- **ù** sounds like **"oo"** in "book" but with a sharp falling tone
- **wù** sounds like **"woo!"** with a sharp drop

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **fourth tone** (ˋ) is a **sharp falling** tone:

Say it like giving a firm prohibition: **"wù!"** — decisive and commanding, like "don't!"

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 勿 (wù) - "must not, don't"
- 勿忘 (wù wàng) - "don't forget"
- 请勿 (qǐng wù) - "please don't"
- 切勿 (qiè wù) - "never, must not"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

Think of **wù** as "whoa!" said firmly — you're telling someone to stop or not do something!

**📍 Special Note:**

勿 is a formal/literary way to say "don't" or "must not" — more commonly seen in written signs and
formal contexts than everyday speech.
