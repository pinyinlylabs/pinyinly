**🗣️ Pronunciation of 北 (běi)**

- **Pinyin:** běi
- **Tone: Third tone** — **falling-rising** tone, like saying "uh-huh" when nodding

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **b** like **"b"** in "bet"
- **ěi** sounds like **"ay"** in "way", but with the **third tone** → dip down and rise up
- **běi** sounds like **"bay"** with a dip-then-rise

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **third tone** (ˇ) is a **fall–then-rise** tone:

Say it like you're thoughtfully considering: **"běi..."** — that questioning, contemplative tone
pattern.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 北 (běi) - "north"
- 北京 (běi jīng) - "Beijing"
- 北方 (běi fāng) - "the north"
- 北边 (běi biān) - "north side"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

北 means "north" — imagine looking up north thoughtfully, with that rising tone like you're
pondering which direction to go!
