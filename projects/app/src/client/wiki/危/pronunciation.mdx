**🗣️ Pronunciation of 危 (wēi)**

- **Pinyin:** wēi
- **Tone: First tone** — **high and flat** tone, like a steady musical note

**🔤 Breakdown:**

- **w** like **"w"** in "way"
- **ēi** sounds like **"way"** but with first tone → high and steady
- **wēi** sounds like **"way"** with a steady high pitch

**🎧 Tone tip:**

The **first tone** (¯) is a **high flat** tone: Keep it steady and high, like a warning siren's
steady tone: **"wēi"**.

**📝 Common Examples:**

- 危 (wēi) - "dangerous; perilous"
- 危险 (wēi xiǎn) - "dangerous"
- 危害 (wēi hài) - "harm; endanger"
- 危机 (wēi jī) - "crisis"

**💡 Memory Tip:**

危 means "dangerous" — danger warnings often use a **steady, high-pitched** alarm tone to get
attention!
